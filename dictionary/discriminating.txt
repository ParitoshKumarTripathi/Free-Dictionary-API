DISCRIMINATING
Dis*crim"i*na`ting, a.

Defn: Marking a difference; distinguishing.
 -- Dis*crim"i*na`ting*ly, adv.
And finds with keen discriminating sight, Black's not so black; --
nor white so very white. Canning.
