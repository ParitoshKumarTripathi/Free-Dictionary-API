PENTA-
Pen"ta-. Etym: [Gr. Five.]

1. A combining form denoting five; as, pentacapsular; pentagon.

2. (Chem.)

Defn: Denoting the degree of five, either as regards quality,
property, or composition; as, pentasulphide; pentoxide, etc. Also
used adjectively.
