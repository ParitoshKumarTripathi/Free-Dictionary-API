TRIFALLOW
Tri"fal`low, v. t. [imp. & p. p. Trifallowed; p. pr. & vb. n.
Trifallowing.] Etym: [Pref. tri- + fallow. Cf. Thryfallow.]

Defn: To plow the third time before sowing, as land. Mortimer.
