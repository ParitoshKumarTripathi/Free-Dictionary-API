UNSTRAINED
Un*strained", a. Etym: [Pref. un- not + strain.]

1. Not strained; not cleared or purified by straining; as, unstrained
oil or milk.

2. Not forced; easy; natural; as, a unstrained deduction or
inference. Hakewill.
