VEGETIVE
Veg"e*tive, a. Etym: [See Vegetate, and Vegetative.]

Defn: Having the nature of a plant; vegetable; as, vegetive life.
[R.] Tusser.
VEGETIVE

=====
Veg"e*tive, n.

Defn: A vegetable. [Obs.]
The blest infusions That dwell in vegetives, in metals, stones. Shak.
