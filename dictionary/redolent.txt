REDOLENT
Red"o*lent (-lent), a. Etym: [L. redolens, -entis, p. pr. of redolere
to emit a scent, diffuse an odor; pref. red-, re-, re- + olere to
emit a smell. See Odor.]

Defn: Diffusing odor or fragrance; spreading sweet scent; scented;
odorous; smelling; -- usually followed by of. "Honey redolent of
spring." Dryden.
 -- Red"o*lent*ly, adv.
Gales . . . redolent of joy and youth. Gray.
