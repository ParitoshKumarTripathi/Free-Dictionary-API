TOLUENE
Tol"u*ene, n. Etym: [Tolu + benzene.] (Chem.)

Defn: A hydrocarbon, C6H5.CH3, of the aromatic series, homologous
with benzene, and obtained as a light mobile colorless liquid, by
distilling tolu balsam, coal tar, etc.; -- called also methyl
benzene, phenyl methane, etc.
