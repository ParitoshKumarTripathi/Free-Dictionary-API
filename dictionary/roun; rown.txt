ROUN; ROWN
Roun, Rown, v. i. & t. Etym: [AS. r, fr. r a rune, secret, mystery;
akin to G. raunen to whisper. See Rune.]

Defn: To whisper. [obs.] Gower.
Another rouned to his fellow low. Chaucer.
