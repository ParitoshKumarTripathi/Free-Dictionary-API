TUBICORN
Tu"bi*corn, n. Etym: [L. tubus tube + cornu horn: cf. F. tubicorne.]
(Zoöl.)

Defn: Any ruminant having horns composed of a bony axis covered with
a horny sheath; a hollow-horned ruminant.
