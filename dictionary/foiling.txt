FOILING
Foil"ing, n. (Arch.)

Defn: A foil. Simmonds.
FOILING

=====
Foil"ing, n. Etym: [Cf. F. foulées. See 1st Foil.] (Hunting)

Defn: The track of game (as deer) in the grass.
