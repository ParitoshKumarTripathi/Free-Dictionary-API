TURANIANS
Tu*ra"ni*ans, n. pl. (Ethnol.)
(a) An extensive division of mankind including the Mongols and allied
races of Asia, together with the Malays and Polynesians.
(b) A group of races or tribes inhabiting Asia and closely related to
the Mongols.
