WAYWISER
Way"wis`er, n. Etym: [Cf. G. wegweiser a waymark, a guide; weg way +
weisen to show, direct.]

Defn: An instrument for measuring the distance which one has traveled
on the road; an odometer, pedometer, or perambulator.
The waywiser to a coach, exactly measuring the miles, and showing
them by an index. Evelyn.
