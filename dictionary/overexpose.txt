OVEREXPOSE
O`ver*ex*pose", v. t.

Defn: To expose excessively; specif. (Photog.),

Defn: to subject (a plate or film) too long to the actinic action of
the light used in producing a picture. -- O`ver*ex*po"sure (#), n.
