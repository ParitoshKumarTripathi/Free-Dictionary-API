THROMBOSIS
Throm*bo"sis, n. Etym: [NL. See Thrombus.] (Med.)

Defn: The obstruction of a blood vessel by a clot formed at the site
of obstruction; -- distinguished from embolism, which is produced by
a clot or foreign body brought from a distance.
 -- Throm*bot"ic, a.
