HETEROSOMATI
Het`e*ro*so"ma*ti, n. pl. Etym: [NL., fr. Gr. (Zoöl.)

Defn: An order of fishes, comprising the flounders, halibut, sole,
etc., having the body and head asymmetrical, with both eyes on one
side. Called also Heterosomata, Heterosomi.
