REFLET
Re*flet" (re*fle"), n. [F., reflection. See Reflect.]

Defn: Luster; special brilliancy of surface; -- used esp. in ceramics
to denote the peculiar metallic brilliancy seen in lustered pottery
such as majolica; as, silver reflet; gold reflet.
