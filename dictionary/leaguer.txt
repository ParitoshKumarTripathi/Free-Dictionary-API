LEAGUER
Lea"guer, n. Etym: [D.leger camp, bed, couch, lair. See Lair, and
cf.Beleaguer.]

1. The camp of a besieging army; a camp in general. b. Jonson.

2. A siege or beleaguering. [R.] Sir W. Scott.
LEAGUER

=====
Lea"guer, v. t.

Defn: To besiege; to beleaguer. [Obs.]
