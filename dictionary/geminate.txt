GEMINATE
Gem"i*nate, a. Etym: [L. geminatus, p.p. of genimare to double. See
Gemini.] (Bot.)

Defn: In pairs or twains; two together; binate; twin; as, geminate
flowers. Gray.
GEMINATE

=====
Gem"i*nate, v. t.

Defn: To double. [R.] B. Jonson.
