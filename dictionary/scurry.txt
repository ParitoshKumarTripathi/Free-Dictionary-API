SCURRY
Scur"ry, v. i. Etym: [Cf. Scur, Skirr.]

Defn: To hasten away or along; to move rapidly; to hurry; as, the
rabbit scurried away.
SCURRY

=====
Scur"ry, n.

Defn: Act of scurring; hurried movement.
