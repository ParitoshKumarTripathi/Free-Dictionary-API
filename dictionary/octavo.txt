OCTAVO
Oc*ta"vo, n.;pl. Octavos. Etym: [L. in octavo; in in + octavo, abl.
of octavus. See Octave.]

Defn: A book composed of sheets each of which is folded into eight
leaves; hence, indicating more or less definitely a size of book so
made; -- usually written 8vo or 8ยบ.
OCTAVO

=====
Oc*ta"vo, a.

Defn: Having eight leaves to a sheet; as, an octavo form, book, leaf,
size, etc.
