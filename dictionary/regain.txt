REGAIN
Re*gain" (r*gn"), v. t. Etym: [Pref. re- + gain: cf. F. regagner.]

Defn: To gain anew; to get again; to recover, as what has escaped or
been lost; to reach again.

Syn.
 -- To recover; reobtain; repossess; retrieve.
