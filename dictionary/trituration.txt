TRITURATION
Trit`u*ra"tion, n. Etym: [Cf. F. trituration, L. trituratio a
thrashing of grain.]

Defn: The act of triturating, or reducing to a fine or impalpable
powder by grinding, rubbing, bruising, etc. Paley.
