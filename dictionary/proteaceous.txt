PROTEACEOUS
Pro`te*a"ceous, a. Etym: [From Proteus.] (Bot.)

Defn: Of or pertaining to the Proteaceæ, an order of apetalous
evergreen shrubs, mostly natives of the Cape of Good Hope or of
Australia.
