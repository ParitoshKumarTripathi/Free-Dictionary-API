SEA CRAWFISH; SEA CRAYFISH
Sea" craw"fish`. Sea" cray"fish`. (Zoöl.)

Defn: Any crustacean of the genus Palinurus and allied genera, as the
European spiny lobster (P. vulgaris), which is much used as an
article of food. See Lobster.
