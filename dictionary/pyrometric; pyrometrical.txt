PYROMETRIC; PYROMETRICAL
Pyr`o*met"ric, Pyr`o*met"ric*al a. Etym: [Cf. F. pyrométrique.]
(Physics)

Defn: Pertaining to, or obtained by, the pyrometer; as, pyrometrical
instruments; pyrometrical measurements.
