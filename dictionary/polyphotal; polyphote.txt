POLYPHOTAL; POLYPHOTE
Pol`y*pho"tal, Pol"y*phote, a.  [Poly- + Gr., , light.] (Elec.)

Defn: Pertaining to or designating arc lamps so constructed that more
than one can be used on a single circuit.
