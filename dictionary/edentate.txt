EDENTATE
E*den"tate, a.

1. Destitute of teeth; as, an edentate quadruped; an edentate leaf.

2. (Zoöl.)

Defn: Belonging to the Edentata.
EDENTATE

=====
E*den"tate, n. (Zoöl.)

Defn: One of the Edentata.
