STEVEDORE
Ste"ve*dore`, n. Etym: [Sp. estivador a packer, a stower, fr. estivar
to pack, to stow, L. stipare to press, compress, probably akin to E.
stiff. See Stiff, Stive to stuff.]

Defn: One whose occupation is to load and unload vessels in port; one
who stows a cargo in a hold.
