PULMONATA
Pul`mo*na"ta, n. pl. Etym: [NL., from L. pulmo, -onis, a lung.]
(Zoöl.)

Defn: An extensive division, or sub-class, of hermaphrodite
gastropods, in which the mantle cavity is modified into an air-
breathing organ, as in Helix, or land snails, Limax, or garden slugs,
and many pond snails, as Limnæa and Planorbis.
