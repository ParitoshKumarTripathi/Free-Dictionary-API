ELEVATORY
El"e*va`to*ry, a.

Defn: Tending to raise, or having power to elevate; as, elevatory
forces.
ELEVATORY

=====
El"e*va`to*ry, n. Etym: [Cf. F. élévatoire.] (Surg.)

Defn: See Elevator, n. (e). Dunglison.
