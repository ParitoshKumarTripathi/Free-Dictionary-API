PHRYGANEIDES
Phryg`a*ne"i*des, n. pl. Etym: [NL., fr. Phryganea, the typical
genus, fr. Gr. (Zoöl.)

Defn: A tribe of neuropterous insects which includes the caddice
flies; -- called also Trichoptera. See Trichoptera. [Written also
Phryganides.]
