FOOLHARDY
Fool"har`dy, a. Etym: [OF. folhardi. See Fool idiot, and Hardy.]

Defn: Daring without judgment; foolishly adventurous and bold.
Howell.

Syn.
 -- Rash; venturesome; venturous; precipitate; reckless; headlong;
incautious. See Rash.
