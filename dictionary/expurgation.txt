EXPURGATION
Ex`pur*ga"tion, n. Etym: [L. expurgatio justification, excuse: cf. F.
expurgation.]

Defn: The act of expurgating, purging, or cleansing; purification
from anything noxious, offensive, sinful, or erroneous. Milton.
