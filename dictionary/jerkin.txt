JERKIN
Jer"kin, n. Etym: [Dim. of D. jurk a frock.]

Defn: A jacket or short coat; a close waistcoat. Shak.
JERKIN

=====
Jer"kin, n. (Zoöl.)

Defn: A male gyrfalcon.
