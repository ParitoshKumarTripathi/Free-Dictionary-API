YARWHIP
Yar"whip`, n. Etym: [So called from its sharp cry uttered when taking
wing.] (Zoöl.)

Defn: The European bar-tailed godwit; -- called also yardkeep, and
yarwhelp. See Godwit. [Prov. Eng.]
