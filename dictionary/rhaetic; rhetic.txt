RHAETIC; RHETIC
Rhae"tic, Rhe"tic, a. Etym: [L. Rhaeticus Rhetian.] (Geol.)

Defn: Pertining to, or of the same horizon as, certain Mesozoic
strata of the Rhetian Alps. These strata are regarded as closing the
Triassic period. See the Chart of Geology.
