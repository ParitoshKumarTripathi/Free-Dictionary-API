MENSAL
Men"sal, a. Etym: [L. mensalis, fr. mensa table.]

Defn: Belonging to the table; transacted at table; as, mensa
conversation.
MENSAL

=====
Men"sal, a. Etym: [L. mensis month.]

Defn: Occurring once in a month; monthly.
