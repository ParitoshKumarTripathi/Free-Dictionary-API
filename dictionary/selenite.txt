SELENITE
Sel"e*nite, n. (Chem.)

Defn: A salt of selenious acid.
SELENITE

=====
Sel"e*nite, n. Etym: [L. selenites, Gr. (Min.)

Defn: A variety of gypsum, occuring in transparent crystals or
crystalline masses.
