-LING
-ling. Etym: [AS. -ling.]

Defn: A noun suffix, commonly having a diminutive or a depreciatory
force; as in duck-ling, dosling, hireling, fosterling, firstling,
underling.
-LING

=====
-ling

Defn: . An adverbial suffix; as, darkling, flatling.
