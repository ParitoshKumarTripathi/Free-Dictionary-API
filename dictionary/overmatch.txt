OVERMATCH
O`ver*match", v. t.

1. To be more than equal to or a match for; hence, to vanquish.
Drayton.

2. To marry (one) to a superior. [Obs.] Burton.
OVERMATCH

=====
O"ver*match`, n.

Defn: One superior in power; also, an unequal match; a contest in
which one of the opponents is overmatched. Milton. D. Webster.
