OSTEOCOMMA
Os`te*o*com"ma, n.; pl. L. Osteocommata, E. Osteocommas. Etym: [NL.
See Osteo-, and Comma.] (Anat.)

Defn: A metamere of the vertebrate skeleton; an osteomere; a
vertebra. Owen.
