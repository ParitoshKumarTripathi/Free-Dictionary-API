PODURA
Po*du"ra, n.; pl. L. Poduræ, E. Poduras. Etym: [NL.; Gr. poy`s,
podo`s, foot +

Defn: Any small leaping thysanurous insect of the genus Podura and
related genera; a springtail. Podura scale (Zoöl.), one of the minute
scales with which the body of a podura is covered. They are used as
test objects for the microscope.
