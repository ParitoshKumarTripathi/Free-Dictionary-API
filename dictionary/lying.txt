LYING
Ly"ing, p. pr. & vb. n.

Defn: of Lie, to tell a falsehood.
LYING

=====
Ly"ing, p. pr. & vb. n.

Defn: of Lie, to be supported horizontally. Lying panel (Arch.), a
panel in which the grain of the wood is horizontal. [R.] -- Lying to
(Naut.), having the sails so disposed as to counteract each other.
