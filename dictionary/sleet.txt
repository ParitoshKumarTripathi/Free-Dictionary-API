SLEET
Sleet, n. (Gun.)

Defn: The part of a mortar extending from the chamber to the
trunnions.
SLEET

=====
Sleet, n. Etym: [OE. sleet; akin to MHG. sl, sl hailstone, G.
schlosse; of uncertain origin.]

Defn: Hail or snow, mingled with rain, usually falling, or driven by
the wind, in fine particles.
SLEET

=====
Sleet, v. i. [imp. & p. p. Sleeted; p. pr. & vb. n. Sleeting.]

Defn: To snow or hail with a mixture of rain.
