PLEDGEOR; PLEDGOR
Pledge*or", Pledg*or", n. (Law)

Defn: One who pledges, or delivers anything in pledge; a pledger; --
opposed to Ant: pledgee.

Note: This word analogically requires the e after g, but the spelling
pledgor is perhaps commoner.
