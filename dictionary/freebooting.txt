FREEBOOTING
Free"boot`ing, n.

Defn: Robbery; plunder; a pillaging.
FREEBOOTING

=====
Free"boot`ing, a.

Defn: Acting the freebooter; practicing freebootery; robbing.
Your freebooting acquaintance. Sir W. Scott.
