TRACHEATA
Tra`che*a"ta, n.pl. Etym: [NL.] (Zoöl.)

Defn: An extensive division of arthropods comprising all those which
breathe by tracheæ, as distinguished from Crustacea, which breathe by
means of branchiæ.
