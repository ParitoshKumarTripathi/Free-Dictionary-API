GLORIATION
Glo`ri*a"tion, n. Etym: [L. gloriatio, from gloriari to glory, boast,
fr. gloria glory. See Glory, n.]

Defn: Boast; a triumphing. [Obs.] Bp. Richardson.
Internal gloriation or triumph of the mind. Hobbes.
