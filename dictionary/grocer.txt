GROCER
Gro"cer, n. Etym: [Formerly written grosser, orig., one who sells by
the gross, or deals by wholesale, fr. F. grossier, marchand grossier,
fr. gros large, great. See Gross.]

Defn: A trader who deals in tea, sugar, spices, coffee, fruits, and
various other commodities. Grocer's itch (Med.), a disease of the
akin, caused by handling sugar and treacle.
