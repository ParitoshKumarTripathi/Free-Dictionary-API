SEMINOSE
Sem"i*nose`, n. Etym: [L. semen seed + glucose.] (Chem.)

Defn: A carbohydrate of the glucose group found in the thickened
endosperm of certain seeds, and extracted as yellow sirup having a
sweetish-bitter taste.
