UNSWEAR
Un*swear", v. t. Etym: [1st pref. un- + swear.]

Defn: To recant or recall, as an oath; to recall after having sworn;
to abjure. J. Fletcher.
UNSWEAR

=====
Un*swear", v. i.

Defn: To recall an oath. Spenser.
