HOMOMORPHY
Ho"mo*mor`phy, n. Etym: [Homo- + Gr. (Biol.)

Defn: Similarity of form; resemblance in external characters, while
widely different in fundamental structure; resemblance in geometric
ground form. See Homophyly, Promorphology.
