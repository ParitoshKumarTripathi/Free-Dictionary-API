TREMOLITE
Trem"o*lite, n. Etym: [From Tremola, a valley in the Alps, where it
was discovered.] (Min.)

Defn: A white variety of amphibole, or hornblende, occurring in long,
bladelike crystals, and coarsely fibrous masses.
