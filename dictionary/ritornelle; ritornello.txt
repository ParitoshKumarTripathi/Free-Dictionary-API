RITORNELLE; RITORNELLO
Rit`or*nelle", Ri`tor*nel"lo, n. Etym: [It. ritornello, dim. of
ritorno return, fr. ritornare to return: cf. F. ritournelle.] (Mus.)
(a) A short return or repetition; a concluding symphony to an air,
often consisting of the burden of the song.
(a) A short intermediate symphony, or instrumental passage, in the
course of a vocal piece; an interlude.
