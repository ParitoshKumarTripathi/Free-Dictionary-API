LOAF

=====
Loaf, v. i. [imp. & p. p. Loafed; p. pr. & vb. n. Loafing.] Etym: [G.
laufen to run, Prov. G. loofen. See Leap.]

Defn: To spend time in idleness; to lounge or loiter about. " Loafing
vagabonds." W. Black.
LOAF

=====
Loaf, v. t.

Defn: To spend in idleness; -- with away; as, to loaf time away.
