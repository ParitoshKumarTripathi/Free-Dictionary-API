TETRABRANCHIATE
Tet`ra*bran`chi*ate, a. Etym: [Tetra + branchiate.] (Zoöl.)

Defn: Of or pertaining to the Tetrabranchiata.
 -- n.

Defn: One of the Tetrabranchiata.
