SEA BREAM
Sea" bream`. (Zoöl.)

Defn: Any one of several species of sparoid fishes, especially the
common European species (Pagellus centrodontus), the Spanish (P.
Oweni), and the black sea bream (Cantharus lineatus); -- called also
old wife.
