TERACRYLIC
Ter`a*cryl"ic, a. Etym: [Terpene + acrylic.] (Chem.)

Defn: Of, pertaining to, or designating, an acid of the acrylic
series, obtained by the distillation of terpenylic acid, as an only
substance having a peculiar cheesy odor.
