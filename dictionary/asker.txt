ASKER
Ask"er, n.

Defn: One who asks; a petitioner; an inquirer. Shak.
ASKER

=====
Ask"er, n. Etym: [A corruption of AS. a lizard, newt.] (Zoöl.)

Defn: An ask; a water newt. [Local Eng.]
