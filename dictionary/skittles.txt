SKITTLES
Skit"tles, n. pl. Etym: [Of Scand. origin. sq. root159. See Shoot, v.
t., and cf. Shuttle, Skit, v. t.]

Defn: An English game resembling ninepins, but played by throwing
wooden disks, instead of rolling balls, at the pins.
