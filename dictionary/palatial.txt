PALATIAL
Pa*la"tial, a. Etym: [L. palatium palace. See Palace.]

Defn: Of or pertaining to a palace; suitable for a palace; resembling
a palace; royal; magnificent; as, palatial structures. "Palatial
style." A. Drummond.
PALATIAL

=====
Pa*la"tial, a. [From Palate.] (Anat.)

Defn: Palatal; palatine. [Obs.] Barrow.
PALATIAL

=====
Pa*la"tial, n.

Defn: A palatal letter. [Obs.] Sir W. Jones.
