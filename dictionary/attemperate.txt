ATTEMPERATE
At*tem"per*ate, a. Etym: [L. attemperatus, p. p. of attemperare. See
Attemper.]

Defn: Tempered; proportioned; properly adapted.
Hope must be . . . attemperate to the promise. Hammond.
ATTEMPERATE

=====
At*tem"per*ate, v. t.

Defn: To attemper. [Archaic]
