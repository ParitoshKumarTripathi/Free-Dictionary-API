WOLVERENE; WOLVERINE
Wol`ver*ene", Wol`ver*ine", n. Etym: [From Wolf, with a dim suffix;
prob. so called from its supposed wolfish qualities.]

1. (Zoöl.)

Defn: The glutton.

2. A nickname for an inhabitant of Michigan. [U. S.]
