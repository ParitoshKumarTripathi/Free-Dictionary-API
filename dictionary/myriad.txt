MYRIAD
Myr"i*ad, n. Etym: [Gr. myriade.]

1. The number of ten thousand; ten thousand persons or things.

2. An immense number; a very great many; an indefinitely large
number.
MYRIAD

=====
Myr"i*ad, a.

Defn: Consisting of a very great, but indefinite, number; as, myriad
stars.
