OUTSHINE
Out*shine", v. i.

Defn: To shine forth. "Bright, outshining beams." Shak.
OUTSHINE

=====
Out*shine", v. t.

Defn: To excel in splendor.
A throne of royal state, which far Outshone the wealth of Ormus and
of Ind. Milton.
