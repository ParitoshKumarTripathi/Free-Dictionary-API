RADIOLI
Ra*di"o*li, n. pl.; sing. Radiolus (. Etym: [NL., dim. of L. radius
radius: cf. L. radiolus a feeble sunbeam.] (Zoöl.)

Defn: The barbs of the radii of a feather; barbules.
