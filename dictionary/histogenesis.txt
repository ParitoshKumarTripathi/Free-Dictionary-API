HISTOGENESIS
His`to*gen"e*sis, n. Etym: [Gr. "isto`s tissue + E. genesis.] (Biol.)
(a) The formation and development of organic tissues; histogeny; --
the opposite of histolysis.
(b) Germ history of cells, and of the tissues composed of cells.
Haeckel.
