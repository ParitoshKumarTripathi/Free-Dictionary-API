HOMOPOLIC
Ho`mo*pol"ic, a. Etym: [Homo- + pole.] (Biol.)

Defn: In promorphology, pertaining to or exhibiting that kind of
organic form, in which the stereometric ground form is a pyramid,
with similar poles. See Promorphology.
