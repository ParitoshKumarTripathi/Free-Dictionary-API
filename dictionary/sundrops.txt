SUNDROPS
Sun"drops`, n.  [Sun + drop.] (Bot.)

Defn: Any one of the several species of Kneiffia, esp. K. fruticosa
(syn. Onothera fruticosa), of the Evening-primrose family, having
flowers that open by daylight.
