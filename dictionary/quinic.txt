QUINIC
Quin"ic, a. Etym: [See Quinine, and cf. Kinic.] (Chem.)

Defn: Pertaining to, derived from, or connected with, quinine and
related compounds; specifically, designating a nonnitrogenous acid
obtained from cinchona bark, coffee, beans, etc., as a white
crystalline substance. [Written also chinic, kinic.]
