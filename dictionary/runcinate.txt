RUNCINATE
Run"ci*nate, a. Etym: [L. runcinatus, p.p. of runcinareto plane off,
fr. runcina a plane.] (Bot.)

Defn: Pinnately cut with the lobes pointing downwards, as the leaf of
the dandelion.
