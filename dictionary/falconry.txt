FALCONRY
Fal"con*ry, n. Etym: [Cf. F. fauconnerie. See Falcon.]

1. The art of training falcons or hawks to pursue and attack wild
fowl or game.

2. The sport of taking wild fowl or game by means of falcons or
hawks.
