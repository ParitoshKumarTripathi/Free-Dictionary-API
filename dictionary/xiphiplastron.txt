XIPHIPLASTRON
Xiph"i*plas"tron, n.; pl. Xiphiplastra. Etym: [NL., fr. Gr. xi`fos a
sword + plastron.] (Anat.)

Defn: The posterior, or fourth, lateral plate in the plastron of
turtles; -- called also xiphisternum.
