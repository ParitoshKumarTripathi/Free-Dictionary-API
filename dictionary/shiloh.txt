SHILOH
Shi"loh (shi\'b6lo), n. Etym: [Heb. shiloh, literally, quiet, rest,
fr. shalah to rest.] (Script.)

Defn: A word used by Jacob on his deathbed, and interpreted
variously, as "the Messiah," or as the city "Shiloh," or as "Rest."
