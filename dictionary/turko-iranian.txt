TURKO-IRANIAN
Tur`ko-I*ra"ni*an, a. (Ethnol.)

Defn: Designating, or pert. to, a mixed racial type including the
Afghans, and characterized chiefly by stature above mean, fair
complexion, dark, or sometimes gray, eyes, brachycephaly, and very
long, prominent, and moderately narrow nose.
TURKO-IRANIAN

=====
Tur`ko-I*ra"ni*an, n.

Defn: A member of any race of the Turko-Iranian type.
