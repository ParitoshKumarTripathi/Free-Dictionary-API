PYGOSTYLE
Py"go*style, n. Etym: [Gr. (Anat.)

Defn: The plate of bone which forms the posterior end of the
vertebral column in most birds; the plowshare bone; the vomer. It is
formed by the union of a number of the last caudal vertebræ, and
supports the uropigium.
