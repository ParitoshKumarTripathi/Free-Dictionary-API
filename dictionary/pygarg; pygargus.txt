PYGARG; PYGARGUS
Py"garg, Py*gar"gus,Etym: [L. pygargus, Gr. pygargue.]

1. (Zoöl.)

Defn: A quadruped, probably the addax, an antelope having a white
rump. Deut. xiv. 5.

2. (Zoöl.)
(a) The female of the hen harrier.
(b) The sea eagle.
