OVERSTOCK
O"ver*stock`, n.

Defn: Stock in excess. Tatler.
OVERSTOCK

=====
O`ver*stock", v. t. [imp. & p. p. Overstocked; p. pr. & vb. n.
Overstocking.]

Defn: To fill too full; to supply in excess; as, to overstock a
market with goods, or a farm with cattle.
