SEPTENARY
Sep"ten*a*ry, a. Etym: [L. septenairus, from septeni seven each,
septem seven: cf. F. septénaire. See Seven. ]

1. Consisting of, or relating to, seven; as, a septenary number. I.
Watts.

2. Lasting seven years; continuing seven years. "Septenary penance."
Fuller.
SEPTENARY

=====
Sep"ten*a*ry, n.

Defn: The number seven. [R.] Holinshed.
