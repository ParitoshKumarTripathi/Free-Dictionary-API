REFLUX
Re"flux` (r"flks`), a.

Defn: Returning, or flowing back; reflex; as, reflux action.
REFLUX

=====
Re"flux`, n. Etym: [F. reflux. See Refluent, Flux.]

Defn: A flowing back, as the return of a fluid; ebb; reaction; as,
the flux and reflux of the tides.
All from me Shall with a fierce reflux on me redound. Milton.
