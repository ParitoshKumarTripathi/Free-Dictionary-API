SARMENT
Sar"ment, n. Etym: [L. sarmentum a twig, fr. sarpere to cut off, to
trim: cf. F. sarment.] (Bot.)

Defn: A prostrate filiform stem or runner, as of the strawbwrry. See
Runner.
