QUADRUPED
Quad"ru*ped, a. Etym: [L. quadrupes, -pedis; quattuor four + pes,
pedis, a foot: cf. F. quadrupède. See Quadrate, and Foot.]

Defn: Having four feet.
QUADRUPED

=====
Quad"ru*ped, n. (Zoöl.)

Defn: An animal having four feet, as most mammals and reptiles; --
often restricted to the mammals.
