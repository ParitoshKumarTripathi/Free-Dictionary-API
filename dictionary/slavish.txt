SLAVISH
Slav"ish, a.

Defn: Of or pertaining to slaves; such as becomes or befits a slave;
servile; excessively laborious; as, a slavish life; a slavish
dependance on the great.
 -- Slav"ish*ly, adv.
 -- Slav"ish*ness, n.
