PHENOMENAL
Phe*nom"e*nal, a. Etym: [Cf. F. phénoménal.]

Defn: Relating to, or of the nature of, a phenomenon; hence,
extraordinary; wonderful; as, a phenomenal memory.
 -- Phe*nom"e*nal*ly, adv.
