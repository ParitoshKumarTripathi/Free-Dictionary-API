JOUNCE
Jounce, v. t. & i. [imp. & p. p. Jounced; p. pr. & vb. n. Jouncing.]
Etym: [Cf. Jaunce.]

Defn: To jolt; to shake, especially by rough riding or by driving
over obstructions.
JOUNCE

=====
Jounce, n.

Defn: A jolt; a shake; a hard trot.
