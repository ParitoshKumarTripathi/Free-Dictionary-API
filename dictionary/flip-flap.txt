FLIP-FLAP
Flip"-flap`, n. Etym: [See Flip, and Flap.]

Defn: The repeated stroke of something long and loose. Johnson.
FLIP-FLAP

=====
Flip"-flap`, adv.

Defn: With repeated strokes and noise, as of something long and
loose. Ash.
