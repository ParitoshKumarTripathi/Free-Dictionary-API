STEREOGRAPHIC; STEREOGRAPHICAL
Ste`re*o*graph"ic, Ste`re*o*graph"ic*al, a. Etym: [Cf. F.
stéréographique.]

Defn: Made or done according to the rules of stereography; delineated
on a plane; as, a stereographic chart of the earth. Stereographic
projection (Geom.), a method of representing the sphere in which the
center of projection is taken in the surface of the sphere, and the
plane upon which the projection is made is at right andles to the
diameter passing through the center of projection.
