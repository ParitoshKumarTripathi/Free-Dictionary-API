HEARTBURNING
Heart"burn`ing, a.

Defn: Causing discontent.
HEARTBURNING

=====
Heart"burn`ing, n.

1. (Med.)

Defn: Same as Heartburn.

2. Discontent; secret enmity. Swift.
The transaction did not fail to leave heartburnings. Palfrey.
