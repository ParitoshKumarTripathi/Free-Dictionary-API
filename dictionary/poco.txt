POCO
Po"co, adv. Etym: [It.] (Mus.)

Defn: A little; -- used chiefly in phrases indicating the time or
movement; as, poco più allegro, a little faster; poco largo, rather
slow. Poco a poco Etym: [It.] (Mus.) Little by little; as, poco a
poco crescendo, gradually increasing in loudness.
