SULK
Sulk, n. Etym: [L. sulcus.]

Defn: A furrow. [Obs.]
SULK

=====
Sulk, v. i. Etym: [See Sulkiness.]

Defn: To be silently sullen; to be morose or obstinate. T. Hook.
