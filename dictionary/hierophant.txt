HIEROPHANT
Hi*er"o*phant, n. Etym: [L. hierophanta, hierophantes, Gr.
hiérophante.]

Defn: The presiding priest who initiated candidates at the Eleusinian
mysteries; hence, one who teaches the mysteries and duties of
religion. Abp Potter.
