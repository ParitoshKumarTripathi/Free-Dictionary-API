MIDWAY
Mid"way`, n.

Defn: The middle of the way or distance; a middle way or course.
Shak.
Paths indirect, or in the midway faint. Milton.
MIDWAY

=====
Mid"way`, a.

Defn: Being in the middle of the way or distance; as, the midway air.
Shak.
MIDWAY

=====
Mid"way`, adv.

Defn: In the middle of the way or distance; half way. "She met his
glance midway." Dryden.
