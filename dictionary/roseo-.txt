ROSEO-
Ro"se*o-. (Chem.)

Defn: A prefix (also used adjectively) signifying rose-red;
specifically used to designate certain rose-red compounds (called
roseo-cobaltic compounds) of cobalt with ammonia. Cf. Luteo-.
