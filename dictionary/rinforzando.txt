RINFORZANDO
Rin`for*zan"do, a. Etym: [It., fr. rinfor to reënforce, strengthen.]
(Mus.)

Defn: Increasing; strengthening; -- a direction indicating a sudden
increase of force (abbreviated rf., rfz.) Cf. Forzando, and
Sforzando.
