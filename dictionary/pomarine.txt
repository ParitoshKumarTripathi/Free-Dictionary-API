POMARINE
Po"ma*rine, a. Etym: [Gr. (Zoöl.)

Defn: Having the nostril covered with a scale. Pomarine jager
(Zoöl.), a North Atlantic jager (Stercorarius pomarinus) having the
elongated middle tail feathers obtuse. The adult is black.
