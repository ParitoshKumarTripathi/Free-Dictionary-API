PABULATION
Pab`u*la"tion, n. [L. pabulatio, fr. pabulari to feed, fr. pabulum
food. See Pabulum.]

1. The act of feeding, or providing food. [Obs.]  Cockeram.

2. Food; fodder; pabulum. [Obs.]
