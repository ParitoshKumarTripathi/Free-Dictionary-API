POLYZONAL
Pol`y*zon"al, a. Etym: [Poly- + zonal.]

Defn: Consisting of many zones or rings. Polyzonal lens (Opt.), a
lens made up of pieces arranged zones or rings, -- used in the
lanterns of lighthouses.
