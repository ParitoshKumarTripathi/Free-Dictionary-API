SKUNKHEAD
Skunk"head`, n. (Zoöl.)
(a) The surf duck.
(b) A duck (Camptolaimus Labradorus) which formerly inhabited the
Atlantic coast of New England. It is now supposed to be extinct.
Called also Labrador duck, and pied duck.
