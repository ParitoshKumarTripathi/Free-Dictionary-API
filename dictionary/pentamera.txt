PENTAMERA
Pen*tam"e*ra, n. pl. Etym: [NL. See Pentamerous.] (Zoöl.)

Defn: An extensive division of Coleoptera, including those that
normally have five-jointed tarsi. It embraces about half of all the
known species of the Coleoptera.
