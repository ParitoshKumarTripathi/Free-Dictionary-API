SCORPENE
Scor"pene, n. Etym: [F. scorpène, fr. L. scorpaena a kind of fish,
Gr. (Zoöl.)

Defn: A marine food fish of the genus Scorpæna, as the European
hogfish (S. scrofa), and the California species (S. guttata).
