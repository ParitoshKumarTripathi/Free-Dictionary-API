ACEROUS
Ac"er*ous, a.

Defn: Same as Acerose.
ACEROUS

=====
Ac"er*ous, a. Etym: [Gr. &a; priv. + keras a horn.] (Zoöl.)
(a) Destitute of tentacles, as certain mollusks.
(b) Without antennæ, as some insects.
