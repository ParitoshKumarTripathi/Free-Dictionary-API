PLATINOID
Plat"i*noid, a. Etym: [Platinum + -oid.]

Defn: Resembling platinum.
PLATINOID

=====
Plat"i*noid, n. (Chem.)

Defn: An alloy of German silver containing tungsten; -- used for
forming electrical resistance coils and standards.
