VIRGATE
Vir"gate, a. Etym: [L. virgatus made of twigs, fr. virga a twig, rod.
See Verge a rod.] (Bot.)

Defn: Having the form of a straight rod; wand-shaped; straight and
slender.
VIRGATE

=====
Vir"gate, n. Etym: [LL. virgata, virgata terrae, so much land as
virga terrae, a land measure, contains, fr. L. virga a twig, rod.]

Defn: A yardland, or measure of land varying from fifteen to forty
acres. [Obs.] T. Warton.
