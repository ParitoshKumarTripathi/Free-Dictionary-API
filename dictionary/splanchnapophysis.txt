SPLANCHNAPOPHYSIS
Splanch`napoph"y*sis, n.; pl. Splanchnapophyses. Etym: [NL. See
Splanchnic, and Apophysis.] (Anat.)

Defn: Any element of the skeleton in relation with the alimentary
canal, as the jaws and hyoidean apparatus.
 -- Splanch`nap`o*phys"i*al, a. Mivart.
