CALLER
Call"er, n.

Defn: One who calls.
CALLER

=====
Cal"ler, a. [Scot.]

1. Cool; refreshing; fresh; as, a caller day; the caller air.
Jamieson.

2. Fresh; in good condition; as, caller berrings.
