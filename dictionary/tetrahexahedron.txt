TETRAHEXAHEDRON
Tet`ra*hex`a*he"dron, n. Etym: [Tetra- + hexahedron.] (Crystallog.)

Defn: A solid in the isometric system, bounded by twenty-four equal
triangular faces, four corresponding to each face of the cube.
