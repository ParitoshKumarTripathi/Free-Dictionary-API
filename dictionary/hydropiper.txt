HYDROPIPER
Hy"dro*pi`per, n. Etym: [NL., fr. Gr. "y`dwr water + L. piper a
pepper.] (Bot.)

Defn: A species (Polygonum Hydropiper) of knotweed with acrid
foliage; water pepper; smartweed.
