REDTOP
Red"top` (-tp`), n. (Bot.)

Defn: A kind of grass (Agrostis vulgaris) highly valued in the United
States for pasturage and hay for cattle; -- called also English
grass, and in some localities herd's grass. See Illustration in
Appendix. The tall redtop is Triodia seslerioides.
