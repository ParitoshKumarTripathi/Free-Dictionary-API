RODOMONTADE
Rod`o*mon*tade", n. Etym: [F., fr. It. rodomontana. See Rodomont, n.]

Defn: Vain boasting; empty bluster or vaunting; rant.
I could show that the rodomontades of Almanzor are neither so
irrational nor impossible. Dryden.
