PERSEVERANT
Per`se*ver"ant, a. Etym: [L. perseverans, -antis, p.pr.: cf. F.
persévérant.]

Defn: Persevering. [R.] "Perseverant faith." Whitby.
 -- Per`se*ver"ant*ly, adv. [R.]
