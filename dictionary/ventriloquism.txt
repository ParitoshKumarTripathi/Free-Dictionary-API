VENTRILOQUISM
Ven*tril"o*quism, n. Etym: [See Ventriloquous.]

Defn: The act, art, or practice of speaking in such a manner that the
voice appears to come, not from the person speaking, but from some
other source, as from the opposite side of the room, from the cellar,
etc.
