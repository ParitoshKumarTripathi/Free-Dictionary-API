REVE
Reve, v. t.

Defn: To reave. [Obs.] Chaucer.
REVE

=====
Reve, n. Etym: [See Reeve.]

Defn: An officer, steward, or governor. [Usually written reeve.]
[Obs.] Piers Plowman.
