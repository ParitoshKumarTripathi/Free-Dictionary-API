REPTANT
Rep"tant (rp"tant), a. Etym: [L. reptans, -antis, p. pr. of reptare,
v. intens. from repere to creep. See Reptile.]

1. (Bot.)

Defn: Same as Repent.

2. (Zoöl.)

Defn: Creeping; crawling; -- said of reptiles, worms, etc.
