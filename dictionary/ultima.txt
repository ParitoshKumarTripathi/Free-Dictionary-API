ULTIMA
Ul"ti*ma, a. Etym: [L., fem. ultimus last.]

Defn: Most remote; furthest; final; last. Ultima ratio Etym: [L.],
the last reason or argument; the last resort.
 -- Ultima Thule. [L.] See Thule.
ULTIMA

=====
Ul"ti*ma, n. Etym: [L., fem. of ultimus last.] (Gram. & Pros.)

Defn: The last syllable of a word.
