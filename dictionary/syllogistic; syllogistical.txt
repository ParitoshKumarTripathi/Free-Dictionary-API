SYLLOGISTIC; SYLLOGISTICAL
Syl`lo*gis"tic, Syl`lo*gis"tic*al, a. Etym: [L. syllogisticus, Gr.
syllogistique.]

Defn: Of or pertaining to a syllogism; consisting of a syllogism, or
of the form of reasoning by syllogisms; as, syllogistic arguments or
reasoning.
