RUBERYTHRINIC
Ru`ber*y*thrin"ic, a. Etym: [L. ruber red + erythrin.] (Chem.)

Defn: Pertaining to, or designating, an acid extracted from madder
root. It is a yellow crystalline substance from which alizarin is
obtained.
