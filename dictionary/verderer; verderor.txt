VERDERER; VERDEROR
Ver"der*er, Ver"der*or, n. Etym: [F. verdier, LL. viridarius, fr. L.
viridis green.] (Eng. Forest Law)

Defn: An officer who has the charge of the king's forest, to preserve
the vert and venison, keep the assizes, view, receive, and enroll
attachments and presentments of all manner of trespasses. Blackstone.
