TROCHIL
Tro"chil, n. Etym: [Cf. F. trochile. See Trochilus.] (Zoöl.)

Defn: The crocodile bird.
The crocodile . . . opens his chaps to let the trochil in to pick his
teeth, which gives it the usual feeding. Sir T. Herbert.
