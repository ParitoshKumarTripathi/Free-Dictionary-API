INCLINNOMETER
In`clin*nom"e*ter, n. Etym: [Incline + -meter.] (Magnetism)

Defn: An apparatus to determine the inclination of the earth's
magnetic force to the plane of the horizon; -- called also
inclination compass, and dip circle.
