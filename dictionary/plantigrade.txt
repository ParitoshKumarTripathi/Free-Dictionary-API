PLANTIGRADE
Plan"ti*grade, a. Etym: [L. planta sole of the foot + gradi to walk:
cf. F. plantigrade.] (Zoöl.)
(a) Walking on the sole of the foot; pertaining to the plantigrades.
(b) Having the foot so formed that the heel touches the ground when
the leg is upright.
PLANTIGRADE

=====
Plan"ti*grade, n. (Zoöl.)

Defn: A plantigrade animal, or one that walks or steps on the sole of
the foot, as man, and the bears.
