SCLERODERMIC; SCLERODERMOUS
Scler`o*der"mic, Scler`o*der"mous, (Zoöl.)
(a) Having the integument, or skin, hard, or covered with hard
plates.
(b) Of or pertaining to the Sclerodermata.
