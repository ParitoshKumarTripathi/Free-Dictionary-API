DISACCORD
Dis`ac*cord", v. i. Etym: [Cf. F. désaccorder to cause discord.]

Defn: To refuse to assent. [Obs.] Spenser.
DISACCORD

=====
Dis`ac*cord", n.

Defn: Disagreement. Pop. Sci. Monthly.
