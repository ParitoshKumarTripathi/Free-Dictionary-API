PROCELEUSMATIC
Proc`e*leus*mat"ic, a. Etym: [L. proceleusmaticus, Gr.
procéleusmatique.]

1. Inciting; animating; encouraging. [R.] Johnson.

2. (Pros.)

Defn: Consisting of four short syllables; composed of feet of four
short syllables each.
