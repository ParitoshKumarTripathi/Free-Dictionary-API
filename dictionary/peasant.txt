PEASANT
Peas"ant, n. Etym: [OF. païsant (the i being perh. due to confusion
with the p.pr. of verbs), païsan, F. paysan, fr. OF. & F. pays
country, fr. L. pagus the country. See Pagan.]

Defn: A countryman; a rustic; especially, one of the lowest class of
tillers of the soil in European countries.

Syn.
 -- Countryman; rustic; swain; hind.
PEASANT

=====
Peas"ant, a.

Defn: Rustic, rural. Spenser.
