UNEQUIVOCAL
Un`e*quiv"o*cal, a.

Defn: Not equivocal; not doubtful; not ambiguous; evident; sincere;
plain; as, unequivocal evidence; unequivocal words.
 -- Un`e*quiv"o*cal*ly, adv.
 -- Un`e*quiv"o*cal*ness, n.
