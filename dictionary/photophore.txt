PHOTOPHORE
Pho"to*phore, n.  [Photo- + Gr.  to bear.]

1. (Med.) A form of endoscope using an electric light.

2.  (Zoöl.) A light-emitting organ; specif., one of the luminous
spots on certain marine (mostly deep-sea) fishes.
