GEANTICLINAL
Ge`an*ti*cli"nal, n. Etym: [Gr. anticlinal.] (Geol.)

Defn: An upward bend or flexure of a considerable portion of the
earth's crust, resulting in the formation of a class of mountain
elevations called anticlinoria; -- opposed to geosynclinal.
