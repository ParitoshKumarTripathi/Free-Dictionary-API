SIXTEEN
Six"teen`, a. Etym: [AS. sixt, sixt. See Six, and Ten, and cf.
Sixty.]

Defn: Six and ten; consisting of six and ten; fifteen and one more.
SIXTEEN

=====
Six"teen`, n.

1. The number greater by a unit than fifteen; the sum of ten and six;
sixteen units or objects.

2. A symbol representing sixteen units, as 16, or xvi.
