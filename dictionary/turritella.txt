TURRITELLA
Tur`ri*tel"la, n. Etym: [NL., dim fr. L. turris tower.] (Zoöl.)

Defn: Any spiral marine gastropod belonging to Turritella and allied
genera. These mollusks have an elongated, turreted shell, composed of
many whorls. They have a rounded aperture, and a horny multispiral
operculum.
