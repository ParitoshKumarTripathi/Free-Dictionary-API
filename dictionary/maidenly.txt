MAIDENLY
Maid"en*ly, a.

Defn: Like a maid; suiting a maid; maiden-like; gentle, modest,
reserved.
Must you be blushing . . . What a maidenly man-at-arms are you become
! Shak.
MAIDENLY

=====
Maid"en*ly, adv.

Defn: In a maidenlike manner. "Maidenly demure." Skelton.
