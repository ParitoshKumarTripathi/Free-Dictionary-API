SPORTULE
Spor"tule, n. Etym: [L. sportula a little basket, a gift, dim. of
sporta a basket: cf. F. sortule.]

Defn: A charitable gift or contribution; a gift; an alms; a dole; a
largess; a sportula. [Obs.] Ayliffe.
