PROTOORGANISM; PROTOOERGANISM
Pro`to*ör"gan*ism, n. Etym: [Proto- + organism.] (Biol.)

Defn: An organism whose nature is so difficult to determine that it
might be referred to either the animal or the vegetable kingdom.
