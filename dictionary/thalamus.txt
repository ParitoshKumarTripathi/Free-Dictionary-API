THALAMUS
Thal"a*mus, n.; pl. Thalami. Etym: [L. thalamus chamber, Gr.

1. (Anat.)

Defn: A mass of nervous matter on either side of the third ventricle
of the brain; -- called also optic thalamus.

2. (Bot.)
(a) Same as Thallus.
(b) The receptacle of a flower; a torus.
