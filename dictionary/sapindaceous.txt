SAPINDACEOUS
Sap`in*da"ceous, a. (Bot.)

Defn: Of or pertaining to an order of trees and shrubs (Sapindaceæ),
including the (Typical) genus Sapindus, the maples, the margosa, and
about seventy other genera.
