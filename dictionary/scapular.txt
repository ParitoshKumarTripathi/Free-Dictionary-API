SCAPULAR
Scap"u*lar, a. Etym: [Cf. F. scapulaire. Cf. Scapulary.]

Defn: Of or pertaining to the scapula or the shoulder Scapular arch
(Anat.), the pectoral arch. See under pectoral.
 -- Scapular region, or Scapular tract (Zoöl.), a definite
longitudinal area over the shoulder and along each side of the back
of a bird, from which the scapular feathers arise.
SCAPULAR

=====
Scap"u*lar, n. (Zoöl.)

Defn: One of a special group of feathers which arise from each of the
scapular regions and lie along the sides of the back.
