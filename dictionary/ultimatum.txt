ULTIMATUM
Ul`ti*ma"tum, n.; pl. E. Ultimatums, L. Ultimata. Etym: [NL. See
Ultimate.]

Defn: A final proposition, concession, or condition; especially, the
final propositions, conditions, or terms, offered by either of the
parties in a diplomatic negotiation; the most favorable terms a
negotiator can offer, the rejection of which usually puts an end to
the hesitation.
