PELLIBRANCHIATA
Pel`li*bran`chi*a"ta, n. pl. Etym: [NL., fr. pellis garment +
branchia a gill.] (Zoöl.)

Defn: A division of Nudibranchiata, in which the mantle itself serves
as a gill.
