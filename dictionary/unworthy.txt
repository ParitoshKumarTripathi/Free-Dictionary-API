UNWORTHY
Un*wor"thy, a.

Defn: Not worthy; wanting merit, value, or fitness; undeserving;
worthless; unbecoming; -- often with of.
 -- Un*wor"thi*ly, adv.
 -- Un*wor"thi*ness, n.
