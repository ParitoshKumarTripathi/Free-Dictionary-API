UNVEIL
Un*veil", v. t. Etym: [1st pref. un- + veil.]

Defn: To remove a veil from; to divest of a veil; to uncover; to
disclose to view; to reveal; as, she unveiled her face.
UNVEIL

=====
Un*veil", v. i.

Defn: To remove a veil; to reveal one's self.
