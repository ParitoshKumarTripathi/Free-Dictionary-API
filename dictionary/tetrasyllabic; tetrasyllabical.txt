TETRASYLLABIC; TETRASYLLABICAL
Tet`ra*syl*lab"ic, Tet`ra*syl*lab"ic*al, a. Etym: [Cf. F.
tétrasyllabique.]

Defn: Consisting of, or having, four syllables; quadrisyllabic.
