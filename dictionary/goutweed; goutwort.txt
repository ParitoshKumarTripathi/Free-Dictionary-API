GOUTWEED; GOUTWORT
Gout"weed` (, Gout"wort` n. Etym: [So called from having been
formerly used in assuaging the pain of the gout.] (Bot.)

Defn: A coarse umbelliferous plant of Europe (Ægopodium Podagraria);
-- called also bishop's weed, ashweed, and herb gerard.
