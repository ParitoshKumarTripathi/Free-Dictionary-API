GYNECOCRACY
Gyn`e*coc"ra*cy, n. Etym: [Gr. gynh`, gynaiko`s, a woman +
gynécocratie. Cf. Gynocracy.]

Defn: Government by a woman, female power; gyneocracy. Bailey.
