FETID
Fet"id ( or ; 277), a. Etym: [L. fetidus, foetidus, fr. fetere,
foetere, to have an ill smell, to stink: cf. F. fétide.]

Defn: Having an offensive smell; stinking.
Most putrefactions . . . smell either fetid or moldy. Bacon.
