UROPYGIUM
U`ro*pyg"i*um, n. Etym: [NL., fr. Gr. (Anat.)

Defn: The prominence at the posterior extremity of a bird's body,
which supports the feathers of the tail; the rump; -- sometimes
called pope's nose.
