FILICOID
Fil"i*coid, a. Etym: [L. filix, -icis, fern + -oid: cf. F.
filicoiïde.] (Bot.)

Defn: Fernlike, either in form or in the nature of the method of
reproduction.
FILICOID

=====
Fil"i*coid, n. (Bot.)

Defn: A fernlike plant. Lindley.
