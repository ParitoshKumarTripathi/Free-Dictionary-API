BRAGGART
Brag"gart, n. Etym: [OF. bragard flaunting, vain, bragging. See Brag,
v. i.]

Defn: A boaster.
O, I could play the woman with mine eyes, And braggart with my
tongue. Shak.
BRAGGART

=====
Brag"gart, a.

Defn: Boastful.
 -- Brag"gart*ly, adv.
