PEPTOHYDROCHLORIC
Pep`to*hy`dro*chlo"ric, a. Etym: [See Peptone, and Hydrochloric.]
(Physiol. Chem.)

Defn: Designating a hypothetical acid (called peptohydrochloric acid,
pepsinhydrochloric acid, and chloropeptic acid) which is supposed to
be formed when pepsin and dilute (0.1-0.4 per cent) hydrochloric acid
are mixed together.
