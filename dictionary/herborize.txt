HERBORIZE
Her"bo*rize, v. i. [imp. & p. p. Herborized; p. pr. & vb. n.
Herborizing.] Etym: [F. herboriser, for herbariser, fr. L. herbarium.
See Hebrarium.]

Defn: To search for plants, or new species of plants, with a view to
classifying them.
He herborized as he traveled. W. Tooke.
HERBORIZE

=====
Her"bo*rize, v. t.

Defn: To form the figures of plants in; -- said in reference to
minerals. See Arborized.
Herborized stones contain fine mosses. Fourcroy (Trans.)
