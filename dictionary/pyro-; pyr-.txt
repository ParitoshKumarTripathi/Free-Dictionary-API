PYRO-; PYR-
Pyro-, Pyr-. Etym: [Gr.

Defn: Combining forms designating fire or heat; specifically (Chem.),
used to imply an actual or theoretical derivative by the action of
heat; as in pyrophosphoric, pyrosulphuric, pyrotartaric,
pyrotungstic, etc.
