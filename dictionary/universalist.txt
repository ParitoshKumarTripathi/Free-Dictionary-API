UNIVERSALIST
U`ni*ver"sal*ist, n. Etym: [Cf. F. universaliste.]

1. (Theol.)

Defn: One who believes in Universalism; one of a denomination of
Christians holding this faith.

2. One who affects to understand all the particulars in statements or
propositions. [Obs.] Bentley.
UNIVERSALIST

=====
U`ni*ver"sal*ist, a.

Defn: Of or pertaining to Unversalists of their doctrines.
