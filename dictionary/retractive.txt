RETRACTIVE
Re*tract"ive, a.

Defn: Serving to retract; of the nature of a retraction.
 -- Re*tract"ive*ly, adv.
RETRACTIVE

=====
Re*tract"ive, n.

Defn: That which retracts, or withdraws.
