RIAL
Ri"al (, n.

Defn: A Spanish coin. See Real. [Obs.]
RIAL

=====
Ri*al", a.

Defn: Royal. [Obs.] Chaucer.
RIAL

=====
Ri"al, n. Etym: [From Royal.]

Defn: A gold coin formerly current in England, of the value of ten
shillings sterling in the reign of Henry VI., and of fifteen
shillings in the reign of Elizabeth. [Spelt also ryal.] Brande & C.
