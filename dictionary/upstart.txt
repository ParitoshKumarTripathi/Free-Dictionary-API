UPSTART
Up*start", v. i.

Defn: To start or spring up suddenly. Spenser. Tennyson.
UPSTART

=====
Up"start`, n.

1. One who has risen suddenly, as from low life to wealth, power, or
honor; a parvenu. Bacon.

2. (Bot.)

Defn: The meadow saffron. Dr. Prior.
UPSTART

=====
Up"start`, a.

Defn: Suddenly raised to prominence or consequence. "A race of
upstart creatures." Milton.
