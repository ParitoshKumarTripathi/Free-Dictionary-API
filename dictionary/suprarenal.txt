SUPRARENAL
Su`pra*re"nal, a. (Anat.)

Defn: Situated above, or anterior to, the kidneys.
 -- n.

Defn: A suprarenal capsule. Suprarenal capsules (Anat.), two small
bodies of unknown function in front of, or near, the kidneys in most
vertebrates. Also called renal capsules, and suprarenal bodies.
