HAUSTELLATA
Haus`tel*la"ta, n. pl. Etym: [NL., fr. haustellum, fr. L. haurire,
haustum, to draw water, to swallow. See Exhaust.] (Zoöl.)

Defn: An artificial division of insects, including all those with a
sucking proboscis.
