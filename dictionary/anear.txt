ANEAR
A*near", prep. & adv. Etym: [Pref. a- + near.]

Defn: Near. [R.] "It did not come anear." Coleridge.
The measure of misery anear us. I. Taylor.
ANEAR

=====
A*near", v. t. & i.

Defn: To near; to approach. [Archaic]
