PLANET-STRICKEN; PLANET-STRUCK
Plan"et-strick`en, Plan"et-struck`, a.

Defn: Affected by the influence of planets; blasted. Milton.
Like planet-stricken men of yore He trembles, smitten to the core By
strong compunction and remorse. Wordsworth.
