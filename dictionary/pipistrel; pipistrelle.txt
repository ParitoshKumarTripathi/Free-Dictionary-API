PIPISTREL; PIPISTRELLE
Pi*pis"trel, Pip`i*strelle", n. Etym: [F. pipistrelle, It.
pipistrello.] (Zoöl.)

Defn: A small European bat (Vesperugo pipistrellus); -- called also
flittermouse.
