DECIMETER; DECIMETRE
Dec"i*me`ter, Dec"i*me`tre, n. Etym: [F. décimètre; pref. déci- tenth
(fr. L. decimus) + mètre. See Meter.]

Defn: A measure of length in the metric system; one tenth of a meter,
equal to 3.937 inches.
