RHOMBOID
Rhom"boid, n. Etym: [Gr. rhomboïde.] (Geom.)

Defn: An oblique-angled parallelogram like a rhomb, but having only
the opposite sides equal, the length and with being different.
RHOMBOID

=====
Rhom"boid, a.

Defn: Same as Rhomboidal.
