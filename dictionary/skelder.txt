SKELDER
Skel"der, v. t. & i. Etym: [Etymol. uncertain.]

Defn: To deceive; to cheat; to trick. [Obs.] B. Jonson.
SKELDER

=====
Skel"der, n.

Defn: A vagrant; a cheat. [Obs.] B. Jonson.
