DISSLANDER
Dis*slan"der, v. t. Etym: [Pref. dis- (intens.) + slander.]

Defn: To slander. [Obs.] Legend of Dido.
DISSLANDER

=====
Dis*slan"der, n.

Defn: Slander. [Obs.] E. Hall.
