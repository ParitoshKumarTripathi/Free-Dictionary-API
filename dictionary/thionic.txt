THIONIC
Thi*on"ic, a. Etym: [Gr. (Chem.)

Defn: Of or pertaining to sulphur; containing or resembling sulphur;
specifically, designating certain of the thio compounds; as, the
thionic acids. Cf. Dithionic, Trithionic, Tetrathionic, etc.
