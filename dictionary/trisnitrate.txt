TRISNITRATE
Tris*ni"trate, n. Etym: [Gr. nitrate.] (Chem.)

Defn: A nitrate formed from three molecules of nitric acid; also,
less properly, applied to certain basic nitrates; as, trisnitrate of
bismuth.
