GLUM
Glum, n. Etym: [See Gloom.]

Defn: Sullenness. [Obs.] Skelton.
GLUM

=====
Glum, a.

Defn: Moody; silent; sullen.
I frighten people by my glun face. Thackeray.
GLUM

=====
Glum, v. i.

Defn: To look sullen; to be of a sour countenance; to be glum. [Obs.]
Hawes.
