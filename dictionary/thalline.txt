THALLINE
Thal"line, a. (Bot.)

Defn: Consisting of a thallus.
THALLINE

=====
Thal"line, n. Etym: [Gr. (Chem.)

Defn: An artificial alkaloid of the quinoline series, obtained as a
white crystalline substance, C10H13NO, whose salts are valuable as
antipyretics; -- so called from the green color produced in its
solution by certain oxidizing agents.
