JESTING
Jest"ing, a.

Defn: Sportive; not serious; fit for jests.
He will find that these are no jesting matters. Macaulay
.
JESTING

=====
Jest"ing, n.

Defn: The act or practice of making jests; joking; pleasantry. Eph.
v. 4.
