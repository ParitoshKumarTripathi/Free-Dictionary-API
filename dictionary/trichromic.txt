TRICHROMIC
Tri*chro"mic, a. Etym: [Pref. tri- + Gr. (Opt.)

Defn: If, pertaining to, or consisting of, three colors or color
sensations.
TRICHROMIC

=====
Tri*chro"mic, a. Etym: [Pref. tri- + chromic.] (Chem.)

Defn: Containing three atoms of chromium.
