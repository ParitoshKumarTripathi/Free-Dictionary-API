PUTREFACTIVE
Pu`tre*fac"tive, a. Etym: [Cf. putréfactif. See Putrefy.]

1. Of or pertaining to putrefaction; as, the putrefactive smell or
process. Wiseman.

2. Causing, or tending to promote, putrefaction.
 -- Pu``tre*fac"tive*ness, n.
