OUTLET
Out"let`, n.

Defn: The place or opening by which anything is let out; a passage
out; an exit; a vent.
Receiving all, and having no outlet. Fuller.
OUTLET

=====
Out*let", v. t.

Defn: To let out; to emit. [R.] Daniel.
