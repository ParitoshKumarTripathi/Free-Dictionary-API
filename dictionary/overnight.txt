OVERNIGHT
O"ver*night`, n.

Defn: The fore part of the night last past; the previous evening.
[R.] Shak.
OVERNIGHT

=====
O"ver*night", adv.

Defn: In the fore part of the night last past; in the evening before;
also, during the night; as, the candle will not last overnight.
I had been telling her all that happened overnight. Dickens.
