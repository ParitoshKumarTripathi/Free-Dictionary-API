FRIGORIFIC; FRIGORIFICAL
Frig"o*rif"ic, Frig"o*rif`ic*al a. Etym: [L. frigorificus; frigus,
frigoris, cold + facere to make: cf. F. frigorifique.]

Defn: Causing cold; producing or generating cold. Quincy.
