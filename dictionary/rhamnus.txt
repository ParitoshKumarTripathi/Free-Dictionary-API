RHAMNUS
Rham"nus, n. Etym: [NL., from Gr. rhamnos.] (Bot.)

Defn: A genus of shrubs and small trees; buckthorn. The California
Rhamnus Purchianus and the European R. catharticus are used in
medicine. The latter is used for hedges.
