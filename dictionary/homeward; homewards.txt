HOMEWARD; HOMEWARDS
Home"ward, Home"wards, adv. Etym: [AS. hamweard.]

Defn: Toward home; in the direction of one's house, town, or country.
Homeward bound, bound for home; going homeward; as, the homeward
bound fleet.
