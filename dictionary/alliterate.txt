ALLITERATE
Al*lit"er*ate, v. t.

Defn: To employ or place so as to make alliteration. Skeat.
ALLITERATE

=====
Al*lit"er*ate, v. i.

Defn: To compose alliteratively; also, to constitute alliteration.
