SPAN-NEW
Span"-new`, a. Etym: [Icel. spann, properly, new as a ship just
split; spann chip + n new. See Spoon, and New.]

Defn: Quite new; brand-new; fire-new. "A span-new archbishop's
chair." Fuller.
