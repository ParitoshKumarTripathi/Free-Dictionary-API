THERMOMETRIC; THERMOMETRICAL
Ther`mo*met"ric, Ther`mo*met"ric*al, a. Etym: [Cf. F.
thermométrique.]

1. Of or pertaining to a thermometer; as, the thermometrical scale or
tube.

2. Made, or ascertained, by means of a thermometer; as,
thermometrical observations.
