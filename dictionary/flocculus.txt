FLOCCULUS
Floc"cu*lus, n.; pl. Flocculi. Etym: [NL., dim. of L. floccus a lock
or flock of wool.] (Anat.)

Defn: A small lobe in the under surface of the cerebellum, near the
middle peduncle; the subpeduncular lobe.
