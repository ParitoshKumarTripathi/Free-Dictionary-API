ALSATIAN
Al*sa"tian, a.

Defn: Pertaining to Alsatia.
ALSATIAN

=====
Al*sa"tian, n.

Defn: An inhabitant of Alsatia or Alsace in Germany, or of Alsatia or
White Friars (a resort of debtors and criminals) in London.
