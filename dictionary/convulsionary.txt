CONVULSIONARY
Con*vul"sion*a*ry (, a. Etym: [Cf. F. convulsionnaire.]

Defn: Pertaining to convulsion; convulsive. "Convulsionary
struggles." Sir W. Scott.
CONVULSIONARY

=====
Con*vul"sion*a*ry, n.

Defn: A convulsionist.
