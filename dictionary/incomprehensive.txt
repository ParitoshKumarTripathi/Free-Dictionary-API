INCOMPREHENSIVE
In*com`pre*hen"sive, a.

Defn: Not comprehensive; not capable of including or of
understanding; not extensive; limited.
 -- In*com`pre*hen"sive*ly, a. Sir W. Hamilton.
 -- In*com`pre*hen"sive*ness, n. T. Warton.
