UMBILICATE; UMBILICATED
Um*bil"i*cate, Um*bil"i*ca`ted, a. Etym: [L. umbilicatus. See
Umbilic.]
(a) Depressed in the middle, like a navel, as a flower, fruit, or
leaf; navel-shaped; having an umbilicus; as, an umbilicated smallpox
vesicle.
(b) (Bot.) Supported by a stalk at the central point.
