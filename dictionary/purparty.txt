PURPARTY
Pur"par`ty, n. Etym: [OF. pourpartie; pour for + partie a part; cf.
OF. purpart a respective part.] (Law)

Defn: A share, part, or portion of an estate allotted to a
coparcener. [Written also purpart, and pourparty.]
I am forced to eat all the game of your purparties, as well as my own
thirds. Walpole.
