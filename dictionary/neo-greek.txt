NEO-GREEK
Ne`o-Greek", n.

Defn: A member of a body of French painters (F. les néo-Grecs) of the
middle 19th century. The term is rather one applied by outsiders to
certain artists of grave and refined style, such as Hamon and Aubert,
than a name adopted by the artists themselves.
