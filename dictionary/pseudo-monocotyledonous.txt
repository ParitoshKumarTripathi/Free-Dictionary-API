PSEUDO-MONOCOTYLEDONOUS
Pseu`do-mon`o*cot`y*led"on*ous, a. Etym: [Pseudo- +
monocotyledonous.] (Bot.)

Defn: Having two coalescent cotyledons, as the live oak and the
horse-chestnut.
