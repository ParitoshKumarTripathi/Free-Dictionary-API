DEUTEROPATHIA; DEUTEROPATHY
Deu`ter*o*pa*thi"a, Deu`ter*op"a*thy, n. Etym: [NL. deuteropathia,
fr. Gr. deutéropathie.] (Med.)

Defn: A sympathetic affection of any part of the body, as headache
from an overloaded stomach.
