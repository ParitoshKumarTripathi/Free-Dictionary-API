YTTRO-COLUMBITE; YTTRO-TANTALITE
Yt`tro-co*lum"bite, Yt`tro-tan"ta*lite, n. (Min.)

Defn: A tantalate of uranium, yttrium, and calcium, of a brown or
black color.
