VILLOUS
Vil"lous, a. Etym: [L. villosus: cf. F. villeux. Cf. Velvet.]

1. Abounding in, or covered with, fine hairs, or a woolly substance;
shaggy with soft hairs; nappy.

2. (Anat.)

Defn: Furnished or clothed with villi.
