VIPEROIDEA; VIPEROIDES
Vi`per*oi"de*a, Vi`per*oi"des, n. pl. Etym: [NL. See Viper, and -
oid.] (Zoöl.)

Defn: A division of serpents which includes the true vipers of the
Old World and the rattlesnakes and moccasin snakes of America; --
called also Viperina.
