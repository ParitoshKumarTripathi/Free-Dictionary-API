NIGHTLY
Night"ly, a.

Defn: Of or pertaining to the night, or to every night; happening or
done by night, or every night; as, nightly shades; he kept nightly
vigils.
NIGHTLY

=====
Night"ly, adv.

Defn: At night; every night.
