RECIPIENT
Re*cip"i*ent, n. Etym: [L. recipiens, -entis, receiving, p. pr. of
recipere to receive: cf. F. récipient. See Receive.]

Defn: A receiver; the person or thing that receives; one to whom, or
that to which, anything is given or communicated; specifically, the
receiver of a still.
RECIPIENT

=====
Re*cip"i*ent, a.

Defn: Receiving; receptive.
