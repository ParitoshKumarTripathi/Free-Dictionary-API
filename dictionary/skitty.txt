SKITTY
Skit"ty, n. Etym: [Cf. Skittish.] (Zoöl.)

Defn: A rail; as, the water rail (called also skitty cock, and skitty
coot); the spotted crake (Porzana maruetta), and the moor hen. [Prov.
Eng.]
