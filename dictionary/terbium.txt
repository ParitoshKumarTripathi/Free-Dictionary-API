TERBIUM
Ter"bi*um, n. Etym: [NL., fr. Ytterby, in Sweden. See Erbium.]
(Chem.)

Defn: A rare metallic element, of uncertain identification, supposed
to exist in certain minerals, as gadolinite and samarskite, with
other rare ytterbium earth. Symbol Tr or Tb. Atomic weight 150.
