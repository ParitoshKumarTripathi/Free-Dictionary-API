HOWSOEVER
How`so*ev"er, adj. & conj. Etym: [How + so + ever.]

1. In what manner soever; to whatever degree or extent; however.
I am glad he's come, howsoever he comes. Shak.

2. Although; though; however. [Obs.] Shak.
