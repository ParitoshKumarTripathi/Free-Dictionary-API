PLUTO
Plu"to, n. Etym: [L., fr. Gr. (Class. Myth.)

Defn: The son of Saturn and Rhea, brother of Jupiter and Neptune; the
dark and gloomy god of the Lower World. Pluto monkey (Zoöl.), a long-
tailed African monkey (Cercopithecus pluto), having side whiskers.
The general color is black, more or less grizzled; the frontal band
is white.
