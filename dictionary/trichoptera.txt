TRICHOPTERA
Tri*chop"te*ra, n. pl. Etym: [NL., fr. Gr. (Zoöl.)

Defn: A suborder of Neuroptera usually having the wings covered with
minute hairs. It comprises the caddice flies, and is considered by
some to be a distinct order.
