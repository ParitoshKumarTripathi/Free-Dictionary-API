SOMNIAL
Som"ni*al, a. Etym: [L. spmnialis dream bringing, fr. somnium dream,
fr. somnus sleep.]

Defn: Of or pertaining to sleep or dreams.
The somnial magic superinducted on, without suspending, the active
powers of the mind. Coleridge.
