PSEUDOBRANCHIA
Pseu`do*bran"chi*a, n.; pl. Pseudobranchiæ. Etym: [NL. See Pseudo-,
and Branchia.] (Anat.)

Defn: A rudimentary branchia, or gill.
 -- Pseu`do*bran"chi*al, a.
