VISITANT
Vis"it*ant, n. Etym: [L. visitans, -antis; p. pr.: cf. F. visitant.]

Defn: One who visits; a guest; a visitor.
When the visitant comes again, he is no more a stranger. South.
VISITANT

=====
Vis"it*ant, a.

Defn: Visiting. Wordsworth.
