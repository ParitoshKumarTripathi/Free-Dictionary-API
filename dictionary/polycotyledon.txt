POLYCOTYLEDON
Pol`y*cot`y*le"don, n. Etym: [Poly- + cotyledon: cf. F.
polycotylédone.] (Bot.)

Defn: A plant that has many, or more than two, cotyledons in the
seed.
 -- Pol`y*cot`y*led"on*ous, a.
