QUINQUARTICULAR
Quin`quar*tic"u*lar, a. Etym: [Quinque- + article.] (Theol.)

Defn: Relating to the five articles or points; as, the
quinquarticular controversy between Arminians and Calvinists. [Obs.]
Bp. Sanderson.
