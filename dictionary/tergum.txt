TERGUM
Ter"gum, n.; pl. Terga. Etym: [L., the back.] (Zoöl.)
(a) The back of an animal.
(b) The dorsal piece of a somite of an articulate animal.
(c) One of the dorsal plates of the operculum of a cirriped.
