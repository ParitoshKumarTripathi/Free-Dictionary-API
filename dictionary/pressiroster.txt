PRESSIROSTER
Pres`si*ros"ter, n. Etym: [L. presssus pressed (p. p. of premere) +
rostrum beak: cf. F. pressirostre. See 4th Press.] (Zoöl.)

Defn: One of a tribe of wading birds (Pressirostres) including those
which have a compressed beak, as the plovers.
