METASTOMA; METASTOME
Me*tas"to*ma, Met"a*stome, n. Etym: [NL. metastoma, from Gr. meta`
behind + sto`ma mouth.] (Zoöl.)

Defn: A median elevation behind the mouth in the arthropods.
