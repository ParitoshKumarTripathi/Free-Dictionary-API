SOCIATE
So"ci*ate, a. Etym: [L. sociatus, p. p. of sociare to associate, fr.
socius companion.]

Defn: Associated. [Obs.]
SOCIATE

=====
So"ci*ate, n.

Defn: An associate. [Obs.]
As for you, Dr. Reynolds, and your sociates. Fuller.
SOCIATE

=====
So"ci*ate, v. i.

Defn: To associate. [Obs.] Shelford.
