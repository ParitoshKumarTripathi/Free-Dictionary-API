RAPPROCHEMENT
Rap`proche`ment", n. [F., fr. rapprocher to cause to approach again.
See Re-; Approach.]

Defn: Act or fact of coming or being drawn near or together;
establishment or state of cordial relations.

He had witnessed the gradual rapprochement between the papacy and
Austria.
Wilfrid Ward.
