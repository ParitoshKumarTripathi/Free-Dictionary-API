HOMOPTERA
Ho*mop"te*ra, n. pl. Etym: [NL., fr. Gr. (Zoöl.)

Defn: A suborder of Hemiptera, in which both pairs of wings are
similar in texture, and do not overlap when folded, as in the cicada.
See Hemiptera.
