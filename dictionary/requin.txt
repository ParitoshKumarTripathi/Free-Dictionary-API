REQUIN
Re"quin (r"kwn), n. Etym: [F., fr. reqiem a Mass sung for the dead.
See Requiem.] (Zoöl.)

Defn: The man-eater, or white shark (Carcharodon carcharias); -- so
called on account of its causing requiems to be sung.
