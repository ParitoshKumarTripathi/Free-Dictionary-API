REMIPED
Rem"i*ped (rm"-pd), a. Etym: [L. remus oar + pes, pedis, foot: cf. F.
rémipède.] (Zoöl.)

Defn: Having feet or legs that are used as oars; -- said of certain
crustaceans and insects.
REMIPED

=====
Rem"i*ped, n. (Zoöl.)
(a) An animal having limbs like oars, especially one of certain
crustaceans.
(b) One of a group of aquatic beetles having tarsi adapted for
swimming. See Water beetle.
