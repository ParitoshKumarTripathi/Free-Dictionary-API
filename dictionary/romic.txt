ROMIC
Rom"ic, n.

Defn: A method of notation for all spoken sounds, proposed by Mr.
Sweet; -- so called because it is based on the common Roman-letter
alphabet. It is like the palæotype of Mr. Ellis in the general plan,
but simpler.
