HOMOEOMERIC; HOMOEOMERICAL
Ho`moe*o*mer"ic, Ho`moe*o*mer"ic*al, a.

Defn: Pertaining to, or characterized by, sameness of parts;
receiving or advocating the doctrine of homogeneity of elements or
first principles.
