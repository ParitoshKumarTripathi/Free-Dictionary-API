REDEMAND
Re`de*mand" (r`d*-mnd"), v. t. Etym: [Pref. re- back, again + demand:
cf. F. redemander.]

Defn: To demand back; to demand again.
REDEMAND

=====
Re`de*mand", n.

Defn: A demanding back; a second or renewed demand.
