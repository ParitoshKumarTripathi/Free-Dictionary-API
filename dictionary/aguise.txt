AGUISE
A*guise", n.

Defn: Dress. [Obs.] Dr. H. More.
AGUISE

=====
A*guise", v. t. Etym: [Pref a- + guise.]

Defn: To dress; to attire; to adorn. [Obs.]
Above all knights ye goodly seem aguised. Spenser.
