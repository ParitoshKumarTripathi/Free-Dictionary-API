REDRAW
Re*draw" (r*dr"), v. t. [imp. Redrew (-dr");p. p. Redrawn (-dr*n");
p. pr. & vb. n. Redrawing.]

Defn: To draw again; to make a second draft or copy of; to redraft.
REDRAW

=====
Re*draw", v. i. (Com.)

Defn: To draw a new bill of exchange, as the holder of a protested
bill, on the drawer or indorsers.
