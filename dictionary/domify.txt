DOMIFY
Dom"i*fy, v. t. Etym: [L. domus + -fy: cf. F. domifier.]

1. (Astrol.)

Defn: To divide, as the heavens, into twelve houses. See House, in
astrological sense. [Obs.]

2. To tame; to domesticate. [Obs.] Johnson.
