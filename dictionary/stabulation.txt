STABULATION
Stab`u*la"tion, n. Etym: [L. stabulatio, fr. stabulari to stable
cattle, fr. stabulum. See Stable, n.]

1. The act of stabling or housing beasts.

2. A place for lodging beasts; a stable. [Obs.]
