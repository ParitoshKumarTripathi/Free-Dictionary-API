NEGRITOS
Ne*gri"tos, n. pl.; sing Negrito (. Etym: [Sp., dim. of negro black.]
(Ethnol.)

Defn: A degraded Papuan race, inhabiting Luzon and some of the other
east Indian Islands. They resemble negroes, but are smaller in size.
They are mostly nomads.
