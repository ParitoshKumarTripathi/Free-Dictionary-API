SHARPER
Sharp"er, n.

Defn: A person who bargains closely, especially, one who cheats in
bargains; a swinder; also, a cheating gamester.
Sharpers, as pikes, prey upon their own kind. L'Estrange.

Syn.
 -- Swindler; cheat; deceiver; trickster; rogue. See Swindler.
