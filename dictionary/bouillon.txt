BOUILLON
Bou`illon" (, n. Etym: [F., fr. bouillir to boil.]

1. A nutritious liquid food made by boiling beef, or other meat, in
water; a clear soup or broth.

2. (Far.)

Defn: An excrescence on a horse's frush or frog.
