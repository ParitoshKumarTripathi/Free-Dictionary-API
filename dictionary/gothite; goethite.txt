GOTHITE; GOETHITE
Gö"thite, or Goe"thite (, n. Etym: [After the poet Göthe.] (Min.)

Defn: A hydrous oxide of iron, occurring in prismatic crystals, also
massive, with a fibrous, reniform, or stalactitic structure. The
color varies from yellowish to blackish brown.
