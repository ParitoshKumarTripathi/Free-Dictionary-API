PEDATE
Ped"ate, a. Etym: [L. pedatus, p.p. of pedare to furnish with feet,
fr. pes, pedis, a foot.] (Bot.)

Defn: Palmate, with the lateral lobes cleft into two or more
segments; -- said of a leaf.
 -- Ped"ate*ly, adv.
