TWAITE
Twaite, n. Etym: [Prov. E.] (Zoöl.)

Defn: A European shad; -- called also twaite shad. See Shad.
TWAITE

=====
Twaite, n. (O. Eng. Law)

Defn: A piece of cleared ground. See Thwaite.
