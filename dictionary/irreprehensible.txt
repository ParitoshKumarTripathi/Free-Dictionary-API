IRREPREHENSIBLE
Ir*rep`re*hen"si*ble, a. Etym: [L. irreprehensibilis: cf. F.
irrépréhensible. See Reprehensible.]

Defn: Not reprehensible; blameless; innocent.
 -- Ir*rep`re*hen"si*ble*ness, n.
 -- Ir*rep`re*hen"si*bly, adv.
