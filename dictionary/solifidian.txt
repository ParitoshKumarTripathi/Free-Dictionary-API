SOLIFIDIAN
Sol`i*fid"i*an, n. Etym: [L. solus alone + fides faith.] (Eccl.)

Defn: One who maintains that faith alone, without works, is
sufficient for justification; -- opposed to nullifidian. Hammond.
SOLIFIDIAN

=====
Sol`i*fid"i*an, a.

Defn: Holding the tenets of Solifidians; of or pertaining to the
solifidians.
