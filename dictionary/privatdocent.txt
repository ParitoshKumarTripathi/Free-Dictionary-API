PRIVATDOCENT
Pri*vat"do*cent`, n.; G. pl. -docenten (#). [Also Privatdozent.] [G.;
privat private + docent teacher. See Docent.]

Defn: In the universities of Germany and some other European
countries, a licensed teacher or lecturer having no share in the
university government and dependent upon fees for remuneration.
