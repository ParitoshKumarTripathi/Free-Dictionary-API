UNGUICULATA
Un*guic`u*la"ta, n. pl. Etym: [NL., fr. L. unguiculus a finger nail.]
(Zoöl.)

Defn: An extensive division of Mammalia including those having claws
or nails, as distinguished from the hoofed animals (Ungulata).
