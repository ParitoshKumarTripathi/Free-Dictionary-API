TRIPLICATE
Trip"li*cate, a. Etym: [L. triplicatus, p. p. of triplicare to
triple, treble; tri- (see Tri-) + plicare to fold. See Ply, v. t.]

Defn: Made thrice as much; threefold; tripled. Triplicate ratio
(Math.), the ratio of the cubes of two quantities; thus, the
triplicate ratio of a to b is a3: b3.
TRIPLICATE

=====
Trip"li*cate, n.

Defn: A third thing corresponding to two others of the same kind.
