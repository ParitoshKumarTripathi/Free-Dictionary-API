BARIC
Bar"ic, a. (Chem.)

Defn: Of or pertaining to barium; as, baric oxide.
BARIC

=====
Bar"ic, a. Etym: [Gr. weight.] (Physics)

Defn: Of or pertaining to weight, esp. to the weight or pressure of
the atmosphere as measured by the barometer.
