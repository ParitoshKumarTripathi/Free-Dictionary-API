PENTOSAN; PENTOSANE
Pen"to*san, n. Also -sane. [From Pentose.] (Chem.)

Defn: One of a class of substances (complex carbohydrates widely
distributed in plants, as in fruits, gums, woods, hay, etc.) which
yield pentoses on hydrolysis.
