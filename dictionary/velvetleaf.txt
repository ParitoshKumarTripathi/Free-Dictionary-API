VELVETLEAF
Vel"vet*leaf`, n. (Bot.)

Defn: A name given to several plants which have soft, velvety leaves,
as the Abutilon Avicennæ, the Cissampelos Pareira, and the Lavatera
arborea, and even the common mullein.
