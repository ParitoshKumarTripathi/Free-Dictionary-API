PROTEIDEA
Pro`te*id"e*a, n. pl. Etym: [NL. See Proteus, and -oid.] (Zoöl.)

Defn: An order of aquatic amphibians having prominent external gills
and four legs. It includes Proteus and Menobranchus (Necturus).
Called also Proteoidea, and Proteida.
