SNITE
Snite, n.

Defn: A snipe. [Obs. or Scot.] Carew.
SNITE

=====
Snite, v. t. Etym: [Icel. snifa. See Snout.]

Defn: To blow, as the nose; to snuff, as a candle. [Obs. or Scot.]
