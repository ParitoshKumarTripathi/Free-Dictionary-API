UROPOETIC
U`ro*po*et"ic, a. Etym: [1st uro- + Gr.

1. (Med.)

Defn: Producing, or favoring the production of, urine.

2. (Zoöl.)

Defn: Of, pertaining to, or designating, a system of organs which
eliminate nitrogenous waste matter from the blood of certain
invertebrates.
