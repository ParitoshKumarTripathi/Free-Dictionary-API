REDROOT
Red"root` (rd"rt`), n. (Bot.)

Defn: A name of several plants having red roots, as the New Jersey
tea (see under Tea), the gromwell, the bloodroot, and the Lachnanthes
tinctoria, an endogenous plant found in sandy swamps from Rhode
Island to Florida.
