USUFRUCT
U"su*fruct, n. Etym: [L. usufructus, ususfructus, usus et fructus;
usus use + fructus fruit.] (Law)

Defn: The right of using and enjoying the profits of an estate or
other thing belonging to another, without impairing the substance.
Burrill.
