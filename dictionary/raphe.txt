RAPHE
Ra"phe (ra"fe), n. Etym: [NL., fr. Gr.

1. (Anat.)

Defn: A line, ridge, furrow, or band of fibers, especially in the
median line; as, the raphe of the tongue.

2. (Bot.)

Defn: Same as Rhaph.
