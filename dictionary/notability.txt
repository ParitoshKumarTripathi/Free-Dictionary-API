NOTABILITY
Not`a*bil"i*ty, n.; pl. Notabilities. Etym: [Cf. F. notabilité .]

1. Quality of being notable.

2. A notable, or remarkable, person or thing; a person of note.
"Parisian notabilities" Carlyle.

3. A notable saying. [Obs.] Chaucer.
