TAPOA TAFA
Ta*po"a ta"fa. (Zoöl.)

Defn: A small carnivorous marsupial (Phascogale penicillata) having
long, soft fur, and a very long tail with a tuft of long hairs at the
end; -- called also brush-tailed phascogale.
