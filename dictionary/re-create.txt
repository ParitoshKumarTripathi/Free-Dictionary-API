RE-CREATE
Re`-cre*ate" (r`kr*t"), v. t. Etym: [Pref. re- + create.]

Defn: To create or form anew.
On opening the campaign of 1776, instead of reënforcing, it was
necessary to re-create, the army. Marshall.
