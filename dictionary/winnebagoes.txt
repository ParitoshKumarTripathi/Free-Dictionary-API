WINNEBAGOES
Win`ne*ba"goes, n.; sing. Winnebago (. (Ethnol.)

Defn: A tribe of North American Indians who originally occupied the
region about Green Bay, Lake Michigan, but were driven back from the
lake and nearly exterminated in 1640 by the IIlinnois.
