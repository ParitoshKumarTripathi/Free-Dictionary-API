YOUNKER
Youn"ker, n. Etym: [D. jonker, jonkeer; jong young + heer a lord,
sir, gentleman. See Young, a.]

Defn: A young person; a stripling; a yonker. [Obs. or Colloq.]
That same younker soon was overthrown. Spenser.
