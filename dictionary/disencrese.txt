DISENCRESE
Dis`en*crese", v. i. Etym: [Pref. dis- + OE. encrese, E. increase.]

Defn: To decrease. [Obs.] Chaucer.
DISENCRESE

=====
Dis`en*crese", n.

Defn: Decrease. [Obs.]
