GLASS-SPONGE
Glass"-sponge`, n. (Zoöl.)

Defn: A siliceous sponge, of the genus Hyalonema, and allied genera;
-- so called from their glassy fibers or spicules; -- called also
vitreous sponge. See Glass-rope, and Euplectella.
