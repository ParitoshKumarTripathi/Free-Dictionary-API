PYROMUCIC
Pyr`o*mu"cic, a. Etym: [Pyro- + mucic.] (Chem.)

Defn: Pertaining to, derived from, or designating, an acid obtained
as a white crystalline substance by the distillation of mucic acid,
or by the oxidation of furfurol.
