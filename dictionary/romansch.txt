ROMANSCH
Ro*mansch", n. Etym: [Grisons rumansch, rumonsch, romonsch. See
Romance.]

Defn: The language of the Grisons in Switzerland, a corruption of the
Latin. [Written also Romansch, and Rumonsch.]
