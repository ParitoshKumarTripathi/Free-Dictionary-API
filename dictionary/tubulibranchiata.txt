TUBULIBRANCHIATA
Tu`bu*li*bran`chi*a"ta, n. pl. Etym: [NL., from L. tubulus a little
tube + branchia a gill.] (Zoöl.)

Defn: A group of gastropod mollusks having a tubular shell. Vermetus
is an example.
