PERAMELES
Per`a*me"les, n. Etym: [NL., fr. Gr. meles a badger.] (Zoöl.)

Defn: Any marsupial of the genus Perameles, which includes numerous
species found in Australia. They somewhat resemble rabbits in size
and form. See Illust. under Bandicoot.
