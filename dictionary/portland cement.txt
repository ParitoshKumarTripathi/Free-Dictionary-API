PORTLAND CEMENT
Port"land ce*ment".

Defn: A cement having the color of the Portland stone of England,
made by calcining an artificial mixture of carbonate of lime and
clay, or sometimes certain natural limestones or chalky clays. It
contains a large proportion of clay, and hardens under water.
