RUNLET
Run"let, n. Etym: [Run + -let.]

Defn: A little run or stream; a streamlet; a brook.
To trace out to its marshy source every runlet that has cast in its
tiny pitcherful with the rest. Lowell.
RUNLET

=====
Run"let, n.

Defn: Same as Rundlet. "A stoup of sack, or a runlet of canary." Sir
W. Scott.
