DEIFIC; DEIFICAL
De*if"ic, De*if"ic*al, a. Etym: [L. deificus; deus god + facere to
make: cf. F. déifigue.]

Defn: Making divine; producing a likeness to God; god-making. "A
deifical communion." Homilies.
