PELTA
Pel"ta, n.; pl. Peltæ. Etym: [L., a shield, fr. Gr.

1. (Antiq.)

Defn: A small shield, especially one of an approximately elliptic
form, or crescent-shaped.

2. (Bot.)

Defn: A flat apothecium having no rim.
