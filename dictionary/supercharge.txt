SUPERCHARGE
Su`per*charge", v. t. [imp. & p. p. Supercharged; p. pr. & vb. n.
Supercharging.] Etym: [Pref. super- + charge. Cf. Surcharge.] (Her.)

Defn: To charge (a bearing) upon another bearing; as, to supercharge
a rose upon a fess.
SUPERCHARGE

=====
Su`per*charge", n. (Her.)

Defn: A bearing charged upon another bearing. [R.]
