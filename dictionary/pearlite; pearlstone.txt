PEARLITE; PEARLSTONE
Pearl"ite, Pearl"stone`, n. (Min.)

Defn: A glassy volcanic rock of a grayish color and pearly luster,
often having a spherulitic concretionary structure due to the curved
cracks produced by contraction in cooling. See Illust. under
Perlitic.
