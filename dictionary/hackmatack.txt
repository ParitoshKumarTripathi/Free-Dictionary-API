HACKMATACK
Hack"ma*tack`, n. Etym: [Of American Indian origin.] (Bot.)

Defn: The American larch (Larix Americana), a coniferous tree with
slender deciduous leaves; also, its heavy, close-grained timber.
Called also tamarack.
