TRACKER
Track"er, n.

1. One who, or that which, tracks or pursues, as a man or dog that
follows game.
And of the trackers of the deer Scarce half the lessening pack was
near. Sir W. Scott.

2. (Mus.)

Defn: In the organ, a light strip of wood connecting (in path) a key
and a pallet, to communicate motion by pulling.
