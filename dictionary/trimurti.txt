TRIMURTI
Tri*mur"ti, n. Etym: [Skr. trim; tri three + m body.] (Hindoo Myth.)

Defn: The triad, or trinity, of Hindoo gods, consisting of Brahma,
the Creator, Vishnu, the Preserver, and Siva, the Destroyer. [Spelled
also Trimurtti.]
