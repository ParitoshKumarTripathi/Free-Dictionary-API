RED DOG FLOUR; RED-DOG FLOUR
Red dog, or Red`-dog" flour.

Defn: The lowest grade of flour in milling. It is dark and of little
expansive power, is secured largely from the germ or embryo and
adjacent parts, and contains a relatively high percentage of protein.
It is chiefly useful as feed for farm animals.
