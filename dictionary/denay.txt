DENAY
De*nay", v. t. Etym: [See Deny.]

Defn: To deny. [Obs.]
That with great rage he stoutly doth denay. Spenser.
DENAY

=====
De*nay", n.

Defn: Denial; refusal. [Obs.] Shak.
