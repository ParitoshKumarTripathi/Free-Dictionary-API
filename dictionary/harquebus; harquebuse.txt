HARQUEBUS; HARQUEBUSE
Har"que*bus, Har"que*buse, n. Etym: [See Arquebus.]

Defn: A firearm with match holder, trigger, and tumbler, made in the
second half of the 15th century. the barrel was about forty inches
long. A form of the harquebus was subsequently called arquebus with
matchlock.
