PALAEOTYPE
Pa"læ*o*type, n. Etym: [Palæo- + -type.] (Phon.)

Defn: A system of representing all spoken sounds by means of the
printing types in common use. Ellis.
 -- Pa`læ*o*typ"ic*al, a.
 -- Pa`læ*o*typ"ic*al*ly, adv.
