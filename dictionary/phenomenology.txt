PHENOMENOLOGY
Phe*nom`e*nol"o*gy, n. Etym: [Phenomenon + -logy: cf. F.
phénoménologie.]

Defn: A description, history, or explanation of phenomena. "The
phenomenology of the mind." Sir W. Hamilton.
