ELIZABETHAN
E*liz"a*beth`an, a.

Defn: Pertaining to Queen Elizabeth or her times, esp. to the
architecture or literature of her reign; as, the Elizabethan writers,
drama, literature.
 -- n.

Defn: One who lived in England in the time of Queen Elizabeth.
Lowell.
