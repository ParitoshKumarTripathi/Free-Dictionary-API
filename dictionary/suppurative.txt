SUPPURATIVE
Sup"pu*ra*tive, a. Etym: [Cf. F. suppuratif.]

Defn: Tending to suppurate; promoting suppuration. Suppurative fever
(Med.), pyæmia.
SUPPURATIVE

=====
Sup"pu*ra*tive, n. (Med.)

Defn: A suppurative medicine.
