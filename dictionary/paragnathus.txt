PARAGNATHUS
Pa*rag"na*thus, n.; pl. Paragnathi. Etym: [NL. See Para-, and
Gnathic.] (Zoöl.)
(a) One of the two lobes which form the lower lip, or metastome, of
Crustacea.
(b) One of the small, horny, toothlike jaws of certain annelids.
