REFEL
Re*fel" (r*fl"), v. t. Etym: [L. refellere; pref. re- re- + fallere
to deceive.]

Defn: To refute; to disprove; as, to refel the tricks of a sophister.
[Obs.]
How he refelled me, and how I replied. Shak.
