SHANTY
Shan"ty, a.

Defn: Jaunty; showy. [Prov. Eng.]
SHANTY

=====
Shan"ty, n.;pl. Shanties. Etym: [Said to be fr. Ir. sean old + tig. a
house.]

Defn: A small, mean dwelling; a rough, slight building for temporary
use; a hut.
SHANTY

=====
Shan"ty, v. i.

Defn: To inhabit a shanty. S. H. Hammond.
