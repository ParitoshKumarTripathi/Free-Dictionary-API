PHOTO-EPINASTY
Pho`to-ep"i*nas`ty, n. Etym: [See Photo-, and Epinastic.] (Bot.)

Defn: A disproportionately rapid growth of the upper surface of
dorsiventral organs, such as leaves, through the stimulus of exposure
to light. Encyc. Brit.
