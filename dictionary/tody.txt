TODY
To"dy, n.; pl. Todies. Etym: [Cf. NL. todus, F. todier, G. todvogel.]
(Zoöl.)

Defn: Any one of several species of small insectivorous West Indian
birds of the genus Todus. They are allied to the kingfishers.
