STUMP-TAILED
Stump"-tailed`, a.

Defn: Having a short, thick tail. Stump-tailed lizard (Zoöl.), a
singular Australian scincoid lizard (Trachydosaurus rugosus) having a
short, thick tail resembling its head in form; -- called also
sleeping lizard.
