OFFPRINT
Off"print`, n.

Defn: A reprint or excerpt.
OFFPRINT

=====
Off`print", v. t.  [Off + print.]

Defn: To reprint (as an excerpt); as, the articles of some magazines
are offprinted from other magazines.
