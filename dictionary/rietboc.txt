RIETBOC
Riet"boc, n. Etym: [D. riet reed + bok buck.] (Zoöl.)

Defn: The reedbuck, a South African antelope (Cervicapra
arundinacea); -- so called from its frequenting dry places covered
with high grass or reeds. Its color is yellowish brown. Called also
inghalla, and rietbok.
