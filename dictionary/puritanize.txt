PURITANIZE
Pu"ri*tan*ize, v. i. [imp. & p. p. Puritanized; p. pr. & vb. n.
Puritanizing.]

Defn: To agree with, or teach, the doctrines of Puritans; to conform
to the practice of Puritans. Bp. Montagu.
