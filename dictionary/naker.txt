NAKER
Na"ker, n. (Zoöl.)

Defn: Same as Nacre.
NAKER

=====
Na"ker, n. Etym: [OE. nakere, F. nakaire, LL. nacara, Per. naqaret.]

Defn: A kind of kettledrum. [Obs.] Chaucer.
