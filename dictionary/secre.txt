SECRE
Se"cre ( or ), a.

Defn: Secret; secretive; faithful to a secret. [Obs.]
To be holden stable and secre. Chaucer.
SECRE

=====
Se"cre, n.

Defn: A secret. [Obs.] Chaucer.
