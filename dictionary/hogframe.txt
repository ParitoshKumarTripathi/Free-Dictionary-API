HOGFRAME
Hog"frame`, n. (Steam Vessels)

Defn: A trussed frame extending fore and aft, usually above deck, and
intended to increase the longitudinal strength and stiffness. Used
chiefly in American river and lake steamers. Called also hogging
frame, and hogback.
