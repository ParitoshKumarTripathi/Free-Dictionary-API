UNWIELDY
Un*wield"y, a.

Defn: Not easily wielded or carried; unmanageable; bulky; ponderous.
"A fat, unwieldy body of fifty-eight years old." Clarendon.
 -- Un*wield"i*ly, adv.
 -- Un*wield"i*ness, n.
