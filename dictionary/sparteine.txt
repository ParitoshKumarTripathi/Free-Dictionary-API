SPARTEINE
Spar"te*ine, n. (Chem.)

Defn: A narcotic alkaloid extracted from the tops of the common broom
(Cytisus scoparius, formerly Spartium scoparium), as a colorless oily
liquid of aniline-like odor and very bitter taste.
