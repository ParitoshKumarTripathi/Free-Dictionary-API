POMFRET
Pom"fret, n. Etym: [Perhaps corrupt. fr. Pg. pampano a kind of fish.]
(Zoöl.)
(a) One of two or more species of marine food fishes of the genus
Stromateus (S. niger, S. argenteus) native of Southern Europe and
Asia.
(b) A marine food fish of Bermuda (Brama Raji).
