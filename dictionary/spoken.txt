SPOKEN
Spo"ken, a. Etym: [p.p. of Speak.]

1. Uttered in speech; delivered by word of mouth; oral; as, a spoken
narrative; the spoken word.

2. Characterized by a certain manner or style in speaking; -- often
in composition; as, a pleasant-spoken man.
Methinks you 're better spoken. Shak.
