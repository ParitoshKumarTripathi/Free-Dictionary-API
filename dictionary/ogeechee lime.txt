OGEECHEE LIME
O*gee"chee lime`. Etym: [So named from the Ogeechee River in
Georgia.] (Bot.)
(a) The acid, olive-shaped, drupaceous fruit of a species of tupelo
(Nyssa capitata) which grows in swamps in Georgia and Florida.
(b) The tree which bears this fruit.
