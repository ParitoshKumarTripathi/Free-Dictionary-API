UNLEARNED
Un*learn"ed, a. Etym: [Pref. un- + learned.]

1. Not learned; untaught; uneducated; ignorant; illiterate.

2. Not gained by study; not known.

3. Not exhibiting learning; as, unlearned verses.
 -- Un*learn"ed*ly, adv.
 -- Un*learn"ed*ness, n.
