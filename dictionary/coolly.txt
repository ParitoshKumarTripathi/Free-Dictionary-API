COOLLY
Cool"ly, a.

Defn: Coolish; cool. [Obs.] Spenser.
COOLLY

=====
Cool"ly, adv.

Defn: In a cool manner; without heat or excessive cold; without
passion or ardor; calmly; deliberately; with indifference;
impudently.
