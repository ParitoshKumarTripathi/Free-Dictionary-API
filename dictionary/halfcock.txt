HALFCOCK
Half"cock`, v. t. [imp. & p. p. Halfcocked(); p. pr. & vb. n.
Halfcocking.]

Defn: To set the cock of (a firearm) at the first notch. To go off
halfcocked. (a) To be discharged prematurely, or with the trigger at
half cock; -- said of a firearm. (b) To do or say something without
due thought or care. [Colloq. or Low]
