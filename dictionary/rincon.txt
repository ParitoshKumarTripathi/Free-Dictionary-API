RINCON
Rin*con", n.; pl. Rincones (#). [Sp. rincón.]

Defn: An interior corner; a nook; hence, an angular recess or hollow
bend in a mountain, river, cliff, or the like. [Western & Southern U.
S.] D. S. Jordan.
