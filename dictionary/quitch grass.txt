QUITCH GRASS
Quitch" grass`. Etym: [Properly quick grass, being probably so called
from its vigorous growth, or from its tenacity of life. See Quick,
and cf. Couch grass.] (Bot.)

Defn: A perennial grass (Agropyrum repens) having long running
rootstalks, by which it spreads rapidly and pertinaciously, and so
becomes a troublesome weed. Also called couch grass, quick grass,
quick grass, twitch grass. See Illustration in Appendix.
