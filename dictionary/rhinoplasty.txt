RHINOPLASTY
Rhi"no*plas`ty, n. Etym: [Rhino- + -plasty: cf. F. rhinoplastie.]

Defn: Plastic surgery of the nose to correct deformity or to replace
lost tissue. Tissue may be transplanted from the patient's cheek,
forehead, arm, etc., or even from another person.
