TRIREME
Tri"reme, n. Etym: [L. triremis; tri- (see Tri-) + remus an oar, akin
to E. row. See Row to propel with an oar.] (Class. Antiq.)

Defn: An ancient galley or vessel with tree banks, or tiers, of oars.
