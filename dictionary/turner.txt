TURNER
Turn"er, n.

1. One who turns; especially, one whose occupation is to form
articles with a lathe.

2. (Zoöl.)

Defn: A variety of pigeon; a tumbler.
TURNER

=====
Tur"ner, n. Etym: [G.]

Defn: A person who practices athletic or gymnastic exercises.
