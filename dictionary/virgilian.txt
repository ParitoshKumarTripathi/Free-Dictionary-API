VIRGILIAN
Vir*gil"i*an, a. Etym: [L. Virgilianus, better Vergilianus.]

Defn: Of or pertaining to Virgil, the Roman poet; resembling the
style of Virgil. [Spelt also Vergilian.]
The rich Virgilian rustic measure Of Lari Maxume. Tennyson.
