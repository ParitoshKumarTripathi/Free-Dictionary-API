GLOMERULUS
Glo*mer"u*lus, n.; pl. Glomeruli. Etym: [NL., dim. of L. glomus. See
3d Glome.] (Anat.)

Defn: The bunch of looped capillary blood vessels in a Malpighian
capsule of the kidney.
