CONSTABULARY
Con*stab"u*la*ry, a. Etym: [LL. constabularius an equerry. See
Constable.]

Defn: Of or pertaining to constables; consisting of constables.
CONSTABULARY

=====
Con*stab"u*la*ry, n.

Defn: The collective body of constables in any town, district, or
country.
