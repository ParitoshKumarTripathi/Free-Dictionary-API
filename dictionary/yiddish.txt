YIDDISH
Yid"dish, n. [G. jüdisch, prop., Jewish, fr. Jude Jew. See Jew,
Jewish.]

Defn: A language used by German and other Jews, being a Middle German
dialect developed under Hebrew and Slavic influence. It is written in
Hebrew characters.
