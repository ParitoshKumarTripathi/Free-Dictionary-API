SHEET ANCHOR
Sheet" an"chor. Etym: [OE. scheten to shoot, AS. sceótan; cf. OE.
shoot anchor. See Shoot, v. t.]

1. (Naut.)

Defn: A large anchor stowed on shores outside the waist of a vessel;
-- called also waist anchor. See the Note under Anchor.

2. Anything regarded as a sure support or dependence in danger; the
best hope or refuge.
