STOND
Stond, n. Etym: [For stand.]

1. Stop; halt; hindrance. [Obs.] Bacon.

2. A stand; a post; a station. [Obs.] Spenser.
STOND

=====
Stond, v. i.

Defn: To stand. [Obs.] Chaucer.
