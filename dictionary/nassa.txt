NASSA
Nas"sa, n.; pl. E. Nassas, L. NassÆ. Etym: [From L. nassa a kind of
basket, in allusion to the reticulation of some species.] (Zoöl.)

Defn: Any species of marine gastropods, of the genera Nassa, Tritia,
and other allied genera of the family Nassidæ; a dog whelk. See
Illust. under Gastropoda.
 -- nas"soid, a.
