PLATINOUS
Plat"i*nous, a. (Chem.)

Defn: Of, pertaining to, or containing, platinum; -- used
specifically to designate those compounds in which the element has a
lower valence, as contrasted with the platinic compounds; as,
platinous chloride (PtCl2).
