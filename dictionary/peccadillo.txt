PECCADILLO
Pec`ca*dil"lo, n; pl. Peccadillos. Etym: [Sp. pecadillo, dim. of
pecado a sin, fr. L. peccatum. See Peccant.]

Defn: A slight trespass or offense; a petty crime or fault. Sir W.
Scott.
