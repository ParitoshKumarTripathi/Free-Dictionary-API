VOMITORY
Vom"i*to*ry, a. Etym: [L. vomitorious.]

Defn: Causing vomiting; emetic; vomitive.
VOMITORY

=====
Vom"i*to*ry, n.; pl. Vomitories (.

1. An emetic; a vomit. Harvey.

2. Etym: [L. vomitorium.] (Arch.)

Defn: A principal door of a large ancient building, as of an
amphitheater.
Sixty-four vomitories . . . poured forth the immense multitude.
Gibbon.
