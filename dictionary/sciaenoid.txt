SCIAENOID
Sci*æ"noid, a. Etym: [L. sciæna a kind of fish (fr. Gr. -oid.]
(Zoöl.)

Defn: Of or pertaining to the Sciænidæ, a family of marine fishes
which includes the meagre, the squeteague, and the kingfish.
