PYRETHRIN
Pyr"eth*rin, n. Etym: [NL. Pyrethrum, generic name of feverfew, Gr.
(Chem.)

Defn: A substance resembling, and isomeric with, ordinary camphor,
and extracted from the essential oil of feverfew; -- called also
Pyrethrum camphor.
