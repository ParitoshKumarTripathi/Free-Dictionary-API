UNDATED
Un"da*ted, a. Etym: [L. undatus, p. p. of undare to rise in waves, to
wave, to undulate, fr. unda a wave. See Undulate.] (Bot.)

Defn: Rising and falling in waves toward the margin, as a leaf;
waved.
UNDATED

=====
Un*dat"ed, a. Etym: [Pref. un- + dated.]

Defn: Not dated; having no date; of unknown age; as, an undated
letter.
