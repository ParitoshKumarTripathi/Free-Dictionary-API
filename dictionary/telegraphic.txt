TELEGRAPHIC
Tel`e*graph"ic, a. Etym: [Cf. F. télégraphique.]

Defn: Of or pertaining to the telegraph; made or communicated by a
telegraph; as, telegraphic signals; telegraphic art; telegraphic
intelligence.
