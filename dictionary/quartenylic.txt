QUARTENYLIC
Quar"ten*yl"ic, a. Etym: [Quartene + -yl + -ic.] (Chem.)

Defn: Pertaining to, or designating, an acid of the acrylic acid
series, metameric with crotonic acid, and obtained as a colorless
liquid; -- so called from having four carbon atoms in the molecule.
Called also isocrotonic acid.
