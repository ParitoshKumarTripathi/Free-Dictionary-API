VERTEBRAL
Ver"te*bral, a. Etym: [Cf. F. vertébral.]

1. (Anat.)

Defn: Of or pertaining to a vertebræ, or the vertebral column;
spinal; rachidian.

2. Vertebrate.
VERTEBRAL

=====
Ver"te*bral, n. (Zoöl.)

Defn: A vertebrate. [R.]
