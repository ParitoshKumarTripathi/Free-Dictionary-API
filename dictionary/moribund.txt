MORIBUND
Mor"i*bund, a. Etym: [L. moribundus, from moriri to die. See Mortal.]

Defn: In a dying state; dying; at the point of death.
The patient was comatose and moribund. Copland.
MORIBUND

=====
Mor"i*bund, n.

Defn: A dying person. [R.]
