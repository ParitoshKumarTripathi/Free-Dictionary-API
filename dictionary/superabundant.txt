SUPERABUNDANT
Su`per*a*bun"dant, a. Etym: [L. superabundans, p.pr. of
superabundare. See Superabound.]

Defn: Abounding to excess; being more than is sufficient; redundant;
as, superabundant zeal.
 -- Su`per*a*bun"dant*ly, adv.
