SCUTIBRANCHIATE
Scu`ti*bran"chi*ate, a. (Zoöl.)

Defn: Having the gills protected by a shieldlike shell; of or
pertaining to the Scutibranchiata.
 -- n.

Defn: One of the Scutibranchiata.
