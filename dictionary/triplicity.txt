TRIPLICITY
Tri*plic"i*ty, n. Etym: [Cf. F. triplicité, fr. L. triplex,
triplicis, threefold. See Triplicate, a.]

Defn: The quality or state of being triple, or threefold; trebleness.
In their trinal triplicities on high. Spenser.
