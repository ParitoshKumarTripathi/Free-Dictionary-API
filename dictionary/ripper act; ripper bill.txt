RIPPER ACT; RIPPER BILL
Rip"per act or bill.

Defn: An act or a bill conferring upon a chief executive, as a
governor or mayor, large powers of appointment and removal of heads
of departments or other subordinate officials. [Polit. Cant, U. S.]
