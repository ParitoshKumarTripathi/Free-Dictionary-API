GLOBULIMETER
Glob`u*lim"e*ter, n. Etym: [Globule + -meter.] (Physiol.)

Defn: An instrument for measuring the number of red blood corpuscles
in the blood.

Note: The method depends on the differences of tint obtained by
mixing a sample of the blood with sodium carbonate solution.
