MAMMOCK
Mam"mock, n. Etym: [Ir. & Gael. mam a round hill + -ock.]

Defn: A shapeless piece; a fragment. [Obs.]
MAMMOCK

=====
Mam"mock, v. t.

Defn: To tear to pieces. [Obs.] Milton.
