MISCREATE
Mis`cre*ate", a.

Defn: Miscreated; illegitimate; forged; as, miscreate titles. [Obs.
or Poet.] Shak.
MISCREATE

=====
Mis`cre*ate", v. t.

Defn: To create badly or amiss.
