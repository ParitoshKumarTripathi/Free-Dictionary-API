TOCORORO
To*co*ro"ro, n. Etym: [Probably from the native name through the
Spanish: cf. Sp. tocororo.] (Zoöl.)

Defn: A cuban trogon (Priotelus temnurus) having a serrated bill and
a tail concave at the end.
