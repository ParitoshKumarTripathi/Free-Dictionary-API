RESCAT
Res"cat (rs"kt), v. t. Etym: [Sp. rescattar.]

Defn: To ransom; to release; to rescue. [Obs.] Howell.
RESCAT

=====
Res"cat, n. Etym: [Sp. rescate.]

Defn: Ransom; release. [Obs.]
