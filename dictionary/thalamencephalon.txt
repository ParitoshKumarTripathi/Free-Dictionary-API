THALAMENCEPHALON
Thal`a*men*ceph"a*lon, n. Etym: [NL. See Thalamus, and Encephalon.]
(Anat.)

Defn: The segment of the brain next in front of the midbrain,
including the thalami, pineal gland, and pituitary body; the
diencephalon; the interbrain.
