SUPRACHOROID; SUPRACHOROIDAL
Su`pra*cho"roid, Su`pra*cho*roid"al, a. (Anat.)

Defn: Situated above the choroid; -- applied to the layer of the
choroid coat of the eyeball next to the sclerotic.
