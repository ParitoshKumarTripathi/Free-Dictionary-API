TENAILLE
Te*naille", n. Etym: [F., a pair of pincers or tongs, a tenaille, fr.
L. tenaculum. See Tenaculum.] (Fort.)

Defn: An outwork in the main ditch, in front of the curtain, between
two bastions. See Illust. of Ravelin.
