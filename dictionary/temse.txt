TEMSE
Temse, n. Etym: [F. tamis, or D. tems, teems. Cf. Tamine.]

Defn: A sieve. [Written also tems, and tempse.] [Prov. Eng.]
Halliwell. Temse bread, Temsed bread, Temse loaf, bread made of flour
better sifted than common fluor. [Prov. Eng.]
