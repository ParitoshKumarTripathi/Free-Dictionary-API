PSEUDOTETRAMERA
Pseu`do*te*tram"e*ra, n. pl. Etym: [NL. See Pseudo-, and
Tetramerous.] (Zoöl.)

Defn: A division of beetles having the fifth tarsal joint minute and
obscure, so that there appear to be but four joints.
 -- Pseu`do*te*tram"er*al, a.
