BOGUE
Bogue, v. i. (Naut.)

Defn: To fall off from the wind; to edge away to leeward; -- said
only of inferior craft.
BOGUE

=====
Bogue, n. (Zoöl.)

Defn: The boce; -- called also bogue bream. See Boce.
