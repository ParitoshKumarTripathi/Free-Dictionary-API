DREE
Dree, v. t. Etym: [AS. dreógan to bear, endure, complete.]

Defn: To endure; to suffer. [Scot.]
DREE

=====
Dree, v. i.

Defn: To be able to do or endure. [Obs.]
DREE

=====
Dree, a.

Defn: Wearisome; tedious. [Prov. Eng.]
