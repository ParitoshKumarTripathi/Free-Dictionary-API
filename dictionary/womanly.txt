WOMANLY
Wom"an*ly, a.

Defn: Becoming a woman; feminine; as, womanly behavior. Arbuthnot.
A blushing, womanly discovering grace. Donne.
WOMANLY

=====
Wom"an*ly, adv.

Defn: In the manner of a woman; with the grace, tenderness, or
affection of a woman. Gascoigne.
