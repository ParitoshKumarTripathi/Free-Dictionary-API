VALEDICTION
Val`e*dic"tion, n. Etym: [L., valedicere, valedictum, to say
farewell; vale farewell (imperative of valere to be strong or well) +
dicere to say. See Valiant, Diction.]

Defn: A farewell; a bidding farewell. Donne.
