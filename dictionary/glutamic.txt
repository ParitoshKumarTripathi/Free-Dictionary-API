GLUTAMIC
Glu*tam"ic, a. Etym: [Gluten + -amic.] (Chem.)

Defn: Of or pertaining to gluten. Glutamic acid, a nitrogenous
organic acid obtained from certain albuminoids, as gluten; -- called
also amido-glutaric acid.C5H9NO4.
