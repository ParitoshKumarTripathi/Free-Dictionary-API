FRESNEL LAMP; FRESNEL LANTERN
Fres`nel" lamp", Fres`nel" lan"tern. Etym: [From Fresnel the
inventor, a French physicist.]

Defn: A lantern having a lamp surrounded by a hollow cylindrical
Fresnel lens.
