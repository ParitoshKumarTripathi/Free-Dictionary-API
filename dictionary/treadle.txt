TREADLE
Trea"dle, n. Etym: [OE. tredyl a step, AS. tredel. See Tread.]

1. The part of a foot lathe, or other machine, which is pressed or
moved by the foot.

2. (Biol.)

Defn: The chalaza of a bird's egg; the tread.
