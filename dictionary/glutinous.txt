GLUTINOUS
Glu"ti*nous, a. Etym: [L. glutinosus, fr. gluten glue: cf. F.
glutineux. See Gluten.]

1. Of the nature of glue; resembling glue; viscous; viscid; adhesive;
gluey.

2. (Bot.)

Defn: Havig a moist and adhesive or sticky surface, as a leaf or
gland.
