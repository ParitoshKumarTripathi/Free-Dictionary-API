BLISSOM
Blis"som, v. i. Etym: [For blithesome: but cf. also Icel. bl of a
goat at heat.]

Defn: To be lustful; to be lascivious. [Obs.]
BLISSOM

=====
Blis"som, a.

Defn: Lascivious; also, in heat; -- said of ewes.
