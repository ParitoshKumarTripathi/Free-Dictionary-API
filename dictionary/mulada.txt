MULADA
Mu*la"da, n.

Defn: A moor. [Scot.] Lockhart.
MULADA

=====
Mu*la"da, n. Etym: [Sp. Amer., fr. Sp. mulo, mula, a mule.]

Defn: A drove of mules. [Southwest. U.S.]
