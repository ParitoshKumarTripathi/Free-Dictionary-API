STUPE
Stupe, n. Etym: [L. stupa, or better stuppa, tow. Cf. Stop, v. t.]
(Med.)

Defn: Cloth or flax dipped in warm water or medicaments and applied
to a hurt or sore.
STUPE

=====
Stupe, v. t. [imp. & p. p. Stuped; p. pr. & vb. n. Stuping.]

Defn: To foment with a stupe. Wiseman.
STUPE

=====
Stupe, n. Etym: [See Stupid.]

Defn: A stupid person. [Obs.]
