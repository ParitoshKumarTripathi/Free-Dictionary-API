ERECTO-PATENT
E*rec"to-pat"ent, a.

1. (Bot.)

Defn: Having a position intermediate between erect and patent, or
spreading.

2. (Zoöl.)

Defn: Standing partially spread and erect; -- said of the wings of
certain insects.
