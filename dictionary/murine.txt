MURINE
Mu"rine, a. Etym: [L. murinus, from mus, muris, mouse: cf. F. murin.]
(Zoöl.)

Defn: Pertaining to a family of rodents (Muridæ), of which the mouse
is the type.
MURINE

=====
Mu"rine, n. (Zoöl.)

Defn: One of a tribe of rodents, of which the mouse is the type.
