VERDIN
Ver"din, n. Etym: [Cf. Sp. verdino bright green, F. verdin the
yellow-hammer.] (Zoöl.)

Defn: A small yellow-headed bird (Auriparus flaviceps) of Lower
California, allied to the titmice; -- called also goldtit.
