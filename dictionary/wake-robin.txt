WAKE-ROBIN
Wake"-rob`in, n. (Bot.)

Defn: Any plant of the genus Arum, especially, in England, the
cuckoopint (Arum maculatum).

Note: In America the name is given to several species of Trillium,
and sometimes to the Jack-in-the-pulpit.
