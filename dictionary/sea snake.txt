SEA SNAKE
Sea" snake`. (Zoöl.)

Defn: Any one of many species of venomous aquatic snakes of the
family Hydrophidæ, having a flattened tail and living entirely in the
sea, especially in the warmer parts of the Indian and Pacific Oceans.
They feed upon fishes, and are mostly of moderate size, but some
species become eight or ten feet long and four inches broad.
