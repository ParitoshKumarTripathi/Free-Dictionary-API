REMEDIABLE
Re*me"di*a*ble (r-m"d--b'l), a. Etym: [L. remediabilis: cf. F.
remédiable.]

Defn: Capable of being remedied or cured.
 -- Re*me"di*a*ble*ness, n. -Re*me"di*a*bly, adv.
