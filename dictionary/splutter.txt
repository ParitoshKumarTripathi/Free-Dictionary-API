SPLUTTER
Splut"ter, v. i. [imp. & p. p. Spluttered; p. pr. & vb. n.
Spluttering.] Etym: [Prov. E. splutter, eqivalent to sputter. Cf.
Sputter.]

Defn: To speak hastily and confusedly; to sputter. [Colloq.]
Carleton.
SPLUTTER

=====
Splut"ter, n.

Defn: A confused noise, as of hasty speaking. [Colloq.]
