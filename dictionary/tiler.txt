TILER
Til"er, n.

Defn: A man whose occupation is to cover buildings with tiles.
Bancroft.
TILER

=====
Til"er, n. Etym: [Of uncertain origin, but probably from E. tile, n.]

Defn: A doorkeeper or attendant at a lodge of Freemasons. [Written
also tyler.]
