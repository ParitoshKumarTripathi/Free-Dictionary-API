DEFLAGRATION
Def`la*gra"tion, n. Etym: [L. deflagratio: cf. F. déflagration.]

1. A burning up; conflagration. "Innumerable deluges and
deflagrations." Bp. Pearson.

2. (Chem.)

Defn: The act or process of deflagrating.
