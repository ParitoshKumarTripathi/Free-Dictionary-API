UPRIST
Up*rist", n.

Defn: Uprising. [Obs.] Chaucer.
UPRIST

=====
Up*rist", obs.

Defn: imp. of Uprise. Uprose. Chaucer.
Nor dim nor red, like God's own head The glorious sun uprist.
Coleridge.
