UNAGREEABLE
Un`a*gree"a*ble, a.

1. Disagreeable.

2. Not agreeing or consistent; unsuitable. Shak.
 -- Un`a*gree"a*ble*ness, n.
 -- Un`a*gree"a*bly, adv.
