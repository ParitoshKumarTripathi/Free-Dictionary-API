VIRGINITY
Vir*gin"i*ty, n. Etym: [OE. virgintee, F. virginité, L. virginitas.]

1. The quality or state of being a virgin; undefiled purity or
chastity; maidenhood.

2. The unmarried life; celibacy. [Obs.] Chaucer.
