GRALLAE
Gral"læ, n. pl. Etym: [NL., fr. L. grallae stilts, for gradulae, fr.
gradus. See Grade.] (Zoöl.)

Defn: An order of birds which formerly included all the waders. By
later writers it is usually restricted to the sandpipers, plovers,
and allied forms; -- called also Grallatores.
