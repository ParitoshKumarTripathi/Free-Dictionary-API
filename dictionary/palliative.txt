PALLIATIVE
Pal"li*a*tive, a. Etym: [Cf. F. palliatif.]

Defn: Serving to palliate; serving to extenuate or mitigate.
PALLIATIVE

=====
Pal"li*a*tive, n.

Defn: That which palliates; a palliative agent. Sir W. Scott.
