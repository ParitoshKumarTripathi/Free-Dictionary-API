SPOROZOITE
Spo`ro*zo"ite, n. (Zoöl.)

Defn: In certain Sporozoa, a small active, usually elongate, sickle-
shaped or somewhat amoboid spore, esp. one of those produced by
division of the passive spores into which the zygote divides. The
sporozoites reproduce asexually.
