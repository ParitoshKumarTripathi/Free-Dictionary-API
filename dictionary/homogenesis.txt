HOMOGENESIS
Ho`mo*gen"e*sis, n. Etym: [Homo- + genesis.] (Biol.)

Defn: That method of reproduction in which the successive generations
are alike, the offspring, either animal or plant, running through the
same cycle of existence as the parent; gamogenesis; -- opposed to
heterogenesis.
