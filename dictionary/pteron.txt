PTERON
Pte"ron, n. Etym: [NL., fr. Gr. (Anat.)

Defn: The region of the skull, in the temporal fossa back of the
orbit, where the great wing of the sphenoid, the temporal, the
parietal, and the frontal hones approach each other.
