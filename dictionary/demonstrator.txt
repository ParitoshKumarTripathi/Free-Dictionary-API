DEMONSTRATOR
Dem"on*stra`tor, n. Etym: [L.: cf. F. démonstrateur.]

1. One who demonstrates; one who proves anything with certainty, or
establishes it by indubitable evidence.

2. (Anat.)

Defn: A teacher of practical anatomy.
