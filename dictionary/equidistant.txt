EQUIDISTANT
E`qui*dis"tant, a. Etym: [L. aequidistans, -antis; aequus equal +
distans distant: cf. F. équidistant.]

Defn: Being at an equal distance from the same point or thing.
 -- E`qui*dis"tant*ly, adv. Sir T. Browne.
