RETROMINGENT
Re`tro*min"gent, a. Etym: [Pref. retro- + L. mingens, p. pr. of
mingere to urinate.]

Defn: Organized so as to discharge the urine backward.
 -- n. (Zoöl.)

Defn: An animal that discharges its urine backward.
