SCHIZOGNATHOUS
Schi*zog"na*thous, a. Etym: [Schize- + Gr. (Zoöl.)

Defn: Having the maxillo-palatine bones separate from each other and
from the vomer, which is pointed in front, as in the gulls, snipes,
grouse, and many other birds.
