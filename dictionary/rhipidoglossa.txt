RHIPIDOGLOSSA
Rhi*pi`do*glos"sa, n.pl. Etym: [NL., fr. Gr. (Zoöl.)

Defn: A division of gastropod mollusks having a large number of long,
divergent, hooklike, lingual teeth in each transverse row. It
includes the scutibranchs. See Illustration in Appendix.
