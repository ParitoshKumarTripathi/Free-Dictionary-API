REPRINT
Re*print" (r-prnt"), v. t.

1. To print again; to print a second or a new edition of.

2. To renew the impression of.
The whole business of our redemption is . . . to reprint God's image
upon the soul. South.
REPRINT

=====
Re"print` (r"prnt`), n.

Defn: A second or a new impression or edition of any printed work;
specifically, the publication in one country of a work previously
published in another.
