REPUGN
Re*pugn" (r-pn"), v. t. Etym: [F. répugner, L. repugnare, repugnatum;
pref. re- + pugnare to fight. See Pugnacious.]

Defn: To fight against; to oppose; to resist. [R.]
Stubbornly he did repugn the truth. Shak.
