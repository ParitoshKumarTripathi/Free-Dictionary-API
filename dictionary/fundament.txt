FUNDAMENT
Fun"da*ment, n. Etym: [OE. fundament, fundement, fondement, OF.
fundement, fondement, F. fondement, fr. L. fundamentum foundation,
fr. fundare to lay the bottom, to found, fr. fundus bottom. See
Fund.]

1. Foundation. [Obs.] Chaucer.

2. The part of the body on which one sits; the buttocks; specifically
(Anat.), the anus. Hume.
