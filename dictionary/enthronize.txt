ENTHRONIZE
En*thron"ize, v. t. [imp. & p. p. Enthronized; p. pr. & vb. n.
Enthronizing.] Etym: [See Inthronize.]

Defn: To place on a throne; hence, to induct into office, as a
bishop.
There openly enthronized as the very elected king. Knolles.
