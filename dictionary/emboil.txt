EMBOIL
Em*boil", v. i.

Defn: To boil with anger; to effervesce. [Obs.] Spenser.
EMBOIL

=====
Em*boil", v. t.

Defn: To cause to boil with anger; to irritate; to chafe. [Obs.]
Spenser.
