VIRIDITY
Vi*rid"i*ty, n. Etym: [L. viriditas, fr. viridis green: cf. F.
viridité. See Verdant.]

1. Greenness; verdure; the color of grass and foliage.

2. Freshness; soundness. [Obs.] Evelyn.
