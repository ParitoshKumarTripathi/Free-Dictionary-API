PEPSINOGEN
Pep*sin"o*gen, n. Etym: [Pepsin + -gen.] (Physiol. Chem.)

Defn: The antecedent of the ferment pepsin. A substance contained in
the form of granules in the peptic cells of the gastric glands. It is
readily convertible into pepsin. Also called propepsin.
