POTICHOMANIA; POTICHOMANIE
Po`ti*cho*ma"ni*a, Po`ti*cho*ma"nie, n. Etym: [F. potichomanie;
potiche a porcelain vase + manie mania.]

Defn: The art or process of coating the inside of glass vessels with
engravings or paintings, so as to give them the appearance of painted
ware.
