LEUCOSOID
Leu"co*soid, a. Etym: [NL. Leucosia, the typical genus (fr. Gr. -
oid.] (Zoöl.)

Defn: Like or pertaining to the Leucosoidea, a tribe of marine crabs
including the box crab or Calappa.
