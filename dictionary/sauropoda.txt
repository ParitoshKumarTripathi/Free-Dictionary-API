SAUROPODA
Sau*rop"o*da, n. pl. Etym: [NL., fr. Gr. -poda.] (Paleon.)

Defn: An extinct order of herbivorous dinosaurs having the feet of a
saurian type, instead of birdlike, as they are in many dinosaurs. It
includes the Largest Known land animals, belonging to Brontosaurus,
Camarasaurus, and alied genera. See Illustration in Appendix.
