PEDICULAR
Pe*dic"u*lar, a. Etym: [L. pedicularis, fr. pediculus a louse: cf. F.
pédiculaire.]

Defn: Of or pertaining to lice; having the lousy distemper
(phthiriasis); lousy. Southey.
