INCEPTIVE
In*cep"tive, a.

Defn: Beginning; expressing or indicating beginning; as, an inceptive
proposition; an inceptive verb, which expresses the beginning of
action; -- called also inchoative.
 -- In*cep"tive*ly, adv.
INCEPTIVE

=====
In*cep"tive, n.

Defn: An inceptive word, phrase, or clause.
