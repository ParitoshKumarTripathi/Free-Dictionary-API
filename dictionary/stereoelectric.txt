STEREOELECTRIC
Ste`re*o*e*lec"tric, a. Etym: [Stereo- + electric.] (Physics)

Defn: Of or pertaining to the generation of electricity by means of
solid bodies alone; as, a stereoelectric current is one obtained by
means of solids, without any liquid.
