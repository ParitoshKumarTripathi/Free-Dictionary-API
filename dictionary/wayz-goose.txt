WAYZ-GOOSE
Wayz"-goose`, n. Etym: [Wase stubble + goose.]

1. A stubble goose. [Obs. or Prov. Eng.]

2. An annual feast of the persons employed in a printing office.
[Written also way-goose.] [Eng.]
