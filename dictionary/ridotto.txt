RIDOTTO
Ri*dot"to, n. Etym: [It., fr. LL. reductus a retreat. See Redoubt.]

Defn: A favorite Italian public entertainment, consisting of music
and dancing, -- held generally on fast eves. Brande & C.
There are to be ridottos at guinea tickets. Walpole.
RIDOTTO

=====
Ri*dot"to, v. i.

Defn: To hold ridottos. [R.] J. G. Cooper.
