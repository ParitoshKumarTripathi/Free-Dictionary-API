TRUNCATE
Trun"cate, v. t. [imp. & p. p. Truncated; p. pr. & vb. n.
Truncating.] Etym: [L. truncatus, p. p. of truncare to cut off,
mutilate, fr. truncus maimed, mutilated, cut short. See Trunk.]

Defn: To cut off; to lop; to maim.
TRUNCATE

=====
Trun"cate, a. Etym: [L. truncatus, p. p. ]

Defn: Appearing as if cut off at the tip; as, a truncate leaf or
feather.
