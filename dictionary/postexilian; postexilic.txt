POSTEXILIAN; POSTEXILIC
Post`ex*il"i*an, Post`ex*il"ic, a.

Defn: After the exile; specif. (Jewish Hist.),

Defn: belonging to a period subsequent to the Babylonian captivity or
exile (b. c. 597 or about 586-about 537).
