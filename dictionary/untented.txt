UNTENTED
Un*tent"ed, a. Etym: [Pref. un- not + tent a covering.]

Defn: Having no tent or tents, as a soldier or a field.
UNTENTED

=====
Un*tent"ed, a. Etym: [Pref. un- not + tented, p. p. of tent to
probe.]

Defn: Not tended; not dressed. See 4th Tent.
The untented woundings of a father's curse Pierce every sense about
thee! Shak.
