WHIP-TOM-KELLY
Whip"-tom`-kel"ly, n. Etym: [So called in imitation of its notes.]
(Zoöl.)

Defn: A vireo (Vireo altiloquus) native of the West Indies and
Florida; -- called also black-whiskered vireo.
