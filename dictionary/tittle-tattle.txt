TITTLE-TATTLE
Tit"tle-tat`tle, n. Etym: [A reduplication of tattle.]

1. Idle, trifling talk; empty prattle. Arbuthnot.

2. An idle, trifling talker; a gossip. [R.] Tatler.
TITTLE-TATTLE

=====
Tit"tle-tat`tle, v. i.

Defn: To talk idly; to prate. Shak.
