REDBREAST
Red"breast` (-brst`), n.

1. (Zoöl.)
(a) The European robin.
(b) The American robin. See Robin.
(c) The knot, or red-breasted snipe; -- called also robin breast, and
robin snipe. See Knot.

2. (Zoöl.)

Defn: The long-eared pondfish. See Pondfish.
