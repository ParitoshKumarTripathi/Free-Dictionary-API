USURE
U"sure, v. i. [imp. & p. p. Usured; p. pr. & vb. n. Usuring.] Etym:
[Cf. OF. usurer, LL. usurare.]

Defn: To practice usury; to charge unlawful interest. [Obs.] "The
usuringb senate." Shak.
I usured not ne to me usured any man. Wyclif (Jer. xv. 10).
USURE

=====
U"sure, n. Etym: [F.]

Defn: Usury. [Obs.] Wyclif.
Foul usure and lucre of villainy. Chaucer.
