PROCELLARIAN
Pro`cel*la"ri*an, n. Etym: [L. procella a storm.] (Zoöl.)

Defn: One of a family of oceanic birds (Procellaridæ) including the
petrels, fulmars, and shearwaters. They are often seen in great
abundance in stormy weather.
