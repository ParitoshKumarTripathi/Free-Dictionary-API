RESCISSION
Re*scis"sion (r-szh"n), n. Etym: [L. rescissio: cf. F. rescission.
See Rescind.]

Defn: The act of rescinding, abrogating, annulling, or vacating; as,
the rescission of a law, decree, or judgment.
