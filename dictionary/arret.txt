ARRET
Ar*rêt, n. Etym: [F. See Arrest, n.] (F. Law)
(a) A judgment, decision, or decree of a court or high tribunal;
also, a decree of a sovereign.
(b) An arrest; a legal seizure.
ARRET

=====
Ar*ret", v. t.

Defn: Same as Aret. [Obs.] Spenser.
