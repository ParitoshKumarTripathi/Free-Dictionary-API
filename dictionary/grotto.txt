GROTTO
Grot"to, n.; pl. Grottoes. Etym: [Formerly grotta, fr. It. grotta,
LL. grupta, fr. L. crypta a con cealed subterranran passage vault,
cavern, Gr. Grot, Crypt.]

Defn: A natural covered opening in the earth; a cave; also, an
artificial recess, cave, or cavernlike apartment.
