UNMERCIFUL
Un*mer"ci*ful, a.

Defn: Not merciful; indisposed to mercy or grace; cruel; inhuman;
merciless; unkind.
 -- Un*mer"ci*ful*ly, adv.
 -- Un*mer"ci*ful*ness, n.
