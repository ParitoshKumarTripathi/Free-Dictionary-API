PYRENEAN
Pyr`e*ne"an, a. Etym: [L. Pyrenaei (sc. montes) the Pyrenees, fr.
Pyrene, Gr.

Defn: Of or pertaining to the Pyrenees, a range of mountains
separating France and Spain.
 -- n.

Defn: The Pyrenees. Shak.
