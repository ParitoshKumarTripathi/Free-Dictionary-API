NEMATHECIUM
Nem`a*the"ci*um ( or , n.; pl. Nemathecia. Etym: [NL., fr. gr. (Bot.)

Defn: A peculiar kind of fructification on certain red algæ,
consisting of an external mass of filaments at length separating into
tetraspores.
