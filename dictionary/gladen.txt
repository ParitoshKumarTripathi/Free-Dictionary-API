GLADEN
Gla"den, n. Etym: [AS. glædene, cf. L. gladius a sword. Cf.
Gladiole.] (Bot.)

Defn: Sword grass; any plant with sword-shaped leaves, esp. the
European Iris foetidissima. [Written also gladwyn, gladdon, and
glader.]
