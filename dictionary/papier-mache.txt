PAPIER-MACHE
Pa`pier"-ma`ché", n. Etym: [F. papier mâché, lit., chewed or mashed
paper.]

Defn: A hard and strong substance made of a pulp from paper, mixed
with sise or glue, etc. It is formed into various articles, usually
by means of molds.
