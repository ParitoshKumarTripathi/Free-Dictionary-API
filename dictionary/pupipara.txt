PUPIPARA
Pu*pip"a*ra, n. pl. Etym: [NL. See Pupiparous.] (Zoöl.)

Defn: A division of Diptera in which the young are born in a stage
like the pupa. It includes the sheep tick, horse tick, and other
parasites. Called also Homaloptera.
