PENTAGON
Pen"ta*gon, n. Etym: [Gr. Penta-) + gwni`a angle: cf. L. pentagonium,
F. pentagone.] (Geom.)

Defn: A plane figure having five angles, and, consequently, five
sides; any figure having five angles. Regular pentagon, a pentagon in
which the angles are all equal, and the sides all equal.
