REPRIVE
Re*prive" (r-prv"), v. t. Etym: [Pref. re- + L. privare to deprive.]

Defn: To take back or away. [Obs.] Spenser.
REPRIVE

=====
Re*prive", v. t.

Defn: To reprieve. [Obs.] Howell.
