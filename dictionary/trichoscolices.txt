TRICHOSCOLICES
Trich`o*scol"i*ces, n. pl. Etym: [NL., from Gr. tri`x, tricho`s, hair
+ skw`lhx a worm.] (Zoöl.)

Defn: An extensive group of wormlike animals characterized by being
more or less covered with cilia.
