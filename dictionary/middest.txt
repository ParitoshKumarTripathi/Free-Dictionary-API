MIDDEST
Mid"dest, a.; superl. of Mid. Etym: [See Midst.]

Defn: Situated most nearly in the middle; middlemost; midmost. [Obs.]
" 'Mongst the middest crowd." Spenser.
MIDDEST

=====
Mid"dest, n.

Defn: Midst; middle. [Obs.] Fuller.
