SHALLOT
Shal*lot", n. Etym: [OF. eschalote (for escalone), F. échalote. See
Scallion, and cf. Eschalot.] (Bot.)

Defn: A small kind of onion (Allium Ascalonicum) growing in clusters,
and ready for gathering in spring; a scallion, or eschalot.
