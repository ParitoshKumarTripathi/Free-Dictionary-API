TARANIS
Tar"a*nis, n. Etym: [L. taranis, from the Celtic; cf. W. & Corn.
taran thunder.] (Myth.)

Defn: A Celtic divinity, regarded as the evil principle, but
confounded by the Romans with Jupiter.
