PARA-ANAESTHESIA; PARA-ANESTHESIA
Par`a-an`æs*the"si*a, -an`es*the"si*a, n. [NL.; para- + anæsthesia.]
(Med.)

Defn: Anæsthesia of both sides of the lower half of the body.
