PHYSICOCHEMICAL
Phys`i*co*chem"ic*al, a. Etym: [Physico- + chemical.]

Defn: Involving the principles of both physics and chemistry;
dependent on, or produced by, the joint action of physical and
chemical agencies. Huxley.
