TUSCHE; TUSHE; TOUSCHE
Tusch"e, n. Also Tushe, Tousche, etc.[G. tusche, fr. F. toucher to
touch.]

Defn: A lithographic drawing or painting material of the same nature
as lithographic ink. It is also used as a resistant in the biting-in
process.
