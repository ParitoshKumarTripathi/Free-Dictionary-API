THREE-LEAFED; THREE-LEAVED
Three"-leafed`, Three"-leaved`, a. (Bot.)
(a) Producing three leaves; as, three-leaved nightshade.
(b) Consisting of three distinct leaflets; having the leaflets
arranged in threes. Three-leaved nightshade. See Trillium.
