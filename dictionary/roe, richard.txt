ROE, RICHARD
Roe, Richard. (Law)

Defn: A fictious name for a party, real or fictious, to an act or
proceeding. Other names were formerly similarly used, as John-a-
Nokes, John o', or of the, Nokes, or Noakes, John-a-Stiles, etc.
