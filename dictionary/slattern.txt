SLATTERN
Slat"tern, n.

Defn: A woman who is negligent of her dress or house; one who is not
neat and nice.
SLATTERN

=====
Slat"tern, a.

Defn: Resembling a slattern; sluttish; slatterny. "The slattern air."
Gay.
SLATTERN

=====
Slat"tern, v. t.

Defn: To consume carelessly or wastefully; to waste; -- with away.
[R.] Chesterfield.
