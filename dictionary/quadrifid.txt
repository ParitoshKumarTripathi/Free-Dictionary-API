QUADRIFID
Quad"ri*fid, a. Etym: [L. quadrifidus; quattuor four + findere to
cleave: cf. F. quadrifide.]

Defn: Divided, or deeply cleft, into four parts; as, a quadrifid
perianth; a quadrifid leaf.
