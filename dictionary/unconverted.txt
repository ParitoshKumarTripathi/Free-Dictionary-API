UNCONVERTED
Un`con*vert"ed, a.

1. Not converted or exchanged.

2. Not changed in opinion, or from one faith to another.
Specifically: --
(a) Not persuaded of the truth of the Christian religion; heathenish.
Hooker.
(b) Unregenerate; sinful; impenitent. Baxter.
