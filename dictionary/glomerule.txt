GLOMERULE
Glom"er*ule, n. Etym: [Dim. fr. L. glomus ball.]

1. (Bot.)

Defn: A head or dense cluster of flowers, formed by condensation of a
cyme, as in the flowering dogwood.

2. (Anat.)

Defn: A glomerulus.
