INTERTWINE
In`ter*twine", v. t.

Defn: To unite by twining one with another; to entangle; to
interlace. Milton.
INTERTWINE

=====
In`ter*twine", v. i.

Defn: To be twined or twisted together; to become mutually involved
or enfolded.
INTERTWINE

=====
In`ter*twine", n.

Defn: The act intertwining, or the state of being intertwined.
Coleridge.
