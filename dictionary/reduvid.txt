REDUVID
Red"u*vid (rd"*vd), n. Etym: [L. reduvia a hangnail.] (Zoöl.)

Defn: Any hemipterous insect of the genus Redivius, or family
Reduvidæ. They live by sucking the blood of other insects, and some
species also attack man.
