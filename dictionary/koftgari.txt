KOFTGARI
Koft`ga*ri", a. [Hind. koft gar誰 goldbeating. fr. Per. koft beating +
gar誰 trade.]

Defn: Ornamental work produced by inlaying steel with gold, -- a
variety of damascening much used in the arts of India.
