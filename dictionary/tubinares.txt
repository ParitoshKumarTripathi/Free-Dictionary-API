TUBINARES
Tu`bi*na"res, n. pl. Etym: [NL., fr. L. tubus tube + nares the
nostrils.] (Zoöl.)

Defn: A tribe of sea birds comprising the petrels, shearwaters,
albatrosses, hagdons, and allied birds having tubular horny nostrils.
