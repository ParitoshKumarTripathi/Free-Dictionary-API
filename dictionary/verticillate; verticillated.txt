VERTICILLATE; VERTICILLATED
Ver*tic"il*late, Ver*tic"il*la`ted, a. Etym: [See Verticil.] (Bot. &
Zoöl.)

Defn: Arranged in a transverse whorl or whorls like the rays of a
wheel; as, verticillate leaves of a plant; a verticillate shell.
