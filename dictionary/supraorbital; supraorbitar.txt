SUPRAORBITAL; SUPRAORBITAR
Su`pra*or"bit*al, Su`pra*or"bit*ar, a. (Anat.)

Defn: Situated above the orbit of the eye. Supraorbital point
(Anat.), the middle point of the supraorbital line, which is a line
drawn across the narrowest part of the forehead, separating the face
from the cranium; the ophryon.
