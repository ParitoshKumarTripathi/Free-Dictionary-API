POSTORBITAL
Post*or"bit*al, a. Etym: [Pref. post- + orbital.] (Anat. & Zoöl.)

Defn: Situated behind the orbit; as, the postorbital scales of some
fishes and reptiles.
 -- n.

Defn: A postorbital bone or scale.
