FRANK-LAW
Frank"-law`, n. Etym: [Frank free + law.] (Eng. Law)

Defn: The liberty of being sworn in courts, as a juror or witness;
one of the ancient privileges of a freeman; free and common law; --
an obsolete expression signifying substantially the same as the
American expression civil rights. Abbot.
