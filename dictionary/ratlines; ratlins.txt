RATLINES; RATLINS
Rat"lines, rat"lins, n. pl. Etym: [Of uncertain origin.] (Naut.)

Defn: The small transverse ropes attached to the shrouds and forming
the steps of a rope ladder. [Written also ratlings, and rattlings.]
Totten.
