WHEW
Whew (hwu), n. & interj.

Defn: A sound like a half-formed whistle, expressing astonishment,
scorn, or dislike. Whew duck, the European widgeon. [Prov. Eng.]
WHEW

=====
Whew, v. i.

Defn: To whistle with a shrill pipe, like a plover. [Prov. Eng. &
Scot.]
