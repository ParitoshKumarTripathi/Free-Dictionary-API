UNBOLT
Un*bolt", v. t. Etym: [1st pref. un- + bolt.]

Defn: To remove a bolt from; to unfasten; to unbar; to open. "He
shall unbolt the gates." Shak.
UNBOLT

=====
Un*bolt", v. i.

Defn: To explain or unfold a matter; to make a revelation. [Obs.] "I
will unbolt to you." Shak.
