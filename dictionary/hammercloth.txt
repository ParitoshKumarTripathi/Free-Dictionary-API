HAMMERCLOTH
Ham"mer*cloth`, n. Etym: [Prob. fr. D. hemel heaven, canopy, tester
(akin to G. himmel, and perh. also to E. heaven) + E. cloth; or perh.
a corruption of hamper cloth.]

Defn: The cloth which covers a coach box.
