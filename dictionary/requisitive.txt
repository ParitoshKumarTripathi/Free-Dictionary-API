REQUISITIVE
Re*quis"i*tive (r-kwz"-tv), a.

Defn: Expressing or implying demand. [R.] Harris.
REQUISITIVE

=====
Re*quis"i*tive, n.

Defn: One who, or that which, makes requisition; a requisitionist.
[R.]
