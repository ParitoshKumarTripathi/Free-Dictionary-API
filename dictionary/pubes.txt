PUBES
Pu"bes, n. Etym: [L., the hair which appears on the body at puberty,
from pubes adult.]

1. (Anat.)
(a) The hair which appears upon the lower part of the hypogastric
region at the age of puberty.
(b) Hence (as more commonly used), the lower part of the hypogastric
region; the pubic region.

2. (Bot.)

Defn: The down of plants; a downy or villous substance which grows on
plants; pubescence.
