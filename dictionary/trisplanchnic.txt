TRISPLANCHNIC
Tri*splanch"nic, a. Etym: [Tri- + splanchnic.] (Anat.)

Defn: Of or pertaining to the three great splanchnic cavities,
namely, that of the head, the chest, and the abdomen; -- applied to
the sympathetic nervous system.
