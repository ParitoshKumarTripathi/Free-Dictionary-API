POLYHALITE
Pol`y*ha"lite, n. Etym: [Poly- + Gr. (Min.)

Defn: A mineral usually occurring in fibrous masses, of a brick-red
color, being tinged with iron, and consisting chiefly of the
sulphates of lime, magnesia, and soda.
