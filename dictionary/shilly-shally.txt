SHILLY-SHALLY
Shil"ly-shal`ly, v. i.

Defn: To hesitate; to act in an irresolute manner; hence, to occupy
one's self with trifles.
SHILLY-SHALLY

=====
Shil"ly-shal`ly, n.

Defn: Irresolution; hesitation; also, occupation with trifles.
She lost not one of her forty-five minutes in picking and choosing, -
- no shilly-shally in Kate. De Quincey.
