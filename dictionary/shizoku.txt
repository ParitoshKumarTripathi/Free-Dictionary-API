SHIZOKU
Shi*zo"ku, n. sing. & pl. [Jap. shi-zoku, fr. Chin. ch' (chi) branch,
posterity + tsu kindered, class.]

Defn: The Japanese warrior gentry or middle class, formerly called
samurai; also, any member of this class.
