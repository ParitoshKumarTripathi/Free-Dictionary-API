TRIVIUM
Triv"i*um, n. Etym: [LL. See Trivial.]

1. The three " liberal" arts, grammar, logic, and rhetoric; -- being
a triple way, as it were, to eloquence.

Note: The trivium and quadrivium together made up the seven liberal
arts. See Quadrivium.
