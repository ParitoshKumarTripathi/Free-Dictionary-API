TOLSTOYAN; TOLSTOIAN
Tol*stoy"an, Tol*stoi"an, a.

Defn: Of or pertaining to Tolstoy (1828-1910). -- n.

Defn: A follower of Tolstoy, who advocates and practices manual
labor, simplicity of living, nonresistance, etc., holds that
possession of wealth and ownership of property are sinful, and in
religion rejects all teachings not coming from Christ himself.
