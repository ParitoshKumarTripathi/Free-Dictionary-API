THOMSONIANISM
Thom*so"ni*an*ism, n. (Med.)

Defn: An empirical system which assumes that the human body is
composed of four elements, earth, air, fire, and water, and that
vegetable medicines alone should be used; -- from the founder, Dr.
Samuel Thomson, of Massachusetts.
