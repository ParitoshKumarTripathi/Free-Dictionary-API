HURONIAN
Hu*ro"ni*an, a. Etym: [Named from Lake Huron.] (Geol.)

Defn: Of or pertaining to certain non-fossiliferous rocks on the
borders of Lake Huron, which are supposed to correspond in time to
the latter part of the Archæan age.
