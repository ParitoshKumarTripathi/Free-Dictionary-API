RUTILANT
Ru"ti*lant (, a. Etym: [L. rutilans, p. pr. of rutilare to have a
reddish glow, fr. rutilus red: cf. F. rutilant.]

Defn: Having a reddish glow; shining.
Parchments . . . colored with this rutilant mixture. Evelin.
