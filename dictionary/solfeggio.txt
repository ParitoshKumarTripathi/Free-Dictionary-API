SOLFEGGIO
Sol*feg"gio, n.Etym: [It., fr. solfa the gamut.] (Mus.)

Defn: The system of arranging the scale by the names do, re, mi, fa,
sol, la, si, by which singing is taught; a singing exercise upon
these syllables.
