MURK
Murk, a. Etym: [See Murky.]

Defn: Dark; murky.
He can not see through the mantle murk. J. R. Drake.
MURK

=====
Murk, n.

Defn: Darkness; mirk. [Archaic] Shak.
MURK

=====
Murk, n.

Defn: The refuse of fruit, after the juice has been expressed; marc.
