MATELASSE
Mate`las`sé", a. [F., p.p. of matelasser to cushion, to cover as with
a mattress, fr. matelas mattress. See Mattress.]

Defn: Ornamented by means of an imitation or suggestion of quilting,
the surface being marked by depressed lines which form squares or
lozenges in relief; as, matelassé silks.
MATELASSE

=====
Mate`las`sé", n.

Defn: A quilted ornamented dress fabric of silk or silk and wool.
