NOONDAY
Noon"day`, n.

Defn: Midday; twelve o'clock in the day; noon.
NOONDAY

=====
Noon"day`, a.

Defn: Of or pertaining to midday; meridional; as, the noonday heat.
"Noonday walks." Addison.
