TIPTOP
Tip"top`, n. Etym: [Tip end + top.]

Defn: The highest or utmost degree; the best of anything. [Colloq.]
TIPTOP

=====
Tip"top`, a.

Defn: Very excellent; most excellent; perfect. [Colloq.] "Four tiptop
voices." Gray. "Sung in a tiptop manner." Goldsmith.
