VERGER
Ver"ger, n. Etym: [F. verger, from verge a rod. See 1st Verge.]

Defn: One who carries a verge, or emblem of office. Specifically: --
(a) An attendant upon a dignitary, as on a bishop, a dean, a justice,
etc. [Eng.] Strype.
(b) The official who takes care of the interior of a church building.
