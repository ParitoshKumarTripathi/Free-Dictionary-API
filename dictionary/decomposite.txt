DECOMPOSITE
De`com*pos"ite, a. Etym: [Pref. de- (intens.) + composite.]

1. Compounded more than once; compounded with things already
composite.

2. (Bot.)

Defn: See Decompound, a., 2.
DECOMPOSITE

=====
De`com*pos"ite, n.

Defn: Anything decompounded.
Decomposites of three metals or more. Bacon.
