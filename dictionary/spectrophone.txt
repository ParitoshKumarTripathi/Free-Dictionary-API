SPECTROPHONE
Spec"tro*phone, n.  [Spectrum + Gr.  sound.]

Defn: An instrument constructed on the principle of the photophone
and used in spectrum analysis as an adjunct to the spectroscope. --
Spec`tro*phon"ic (#), a.
