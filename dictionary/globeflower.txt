GLOBEFLOWER
Globe"flow`er, n. (Bot.)
(a) A plant of the genus Trollius (T. Europæus), found in the
mountainous parts of Europe, and producing handsome globe-shaped
flowers.
(b) The American plant Trollius laxus. Japan globeflower. See
Corchorus.
