POSTFRONTAL
Post*fron"tal, a. Etym: [Pref. post- + frontal.] (Anat.)

Defn: Situated behind the frontal bone or the frontal region of the
skull; -- applied especially to a bone back of and below the frontal
in many animals.
 -- n.

Defn: A postfrontal bone.
