TRIFLORAL; TRIFLOROUS
Tri*flo"ral, Tri*flo"rous, a. Etym: [Pref. tri- + L. flos, floris,
flower.] (Bot.)

Defn: Three-flowered; having or bearing three flowers; as, a
triflorous peduncle.
