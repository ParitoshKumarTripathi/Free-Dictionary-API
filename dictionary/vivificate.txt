VIVIFICATE
Vi*vif"i*cate, v. t. Etym: [L. vivificatus, p. p. vivificare. See
Vivify.]

1. To give life to; to animate; to revive; to vivify. [R.]
God vivificates and actuates the whole world. Dr. H. More.

2. (Chem.)

Defn: To bring back a metal to the metallic form, as from an oxide or
solution; to reduce. [Obs.]
