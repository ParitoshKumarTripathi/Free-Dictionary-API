TERREPLEIN
Terre"plein`, n. Etym: [F., fr. L. terra earth + planus even, level,
plain.] (Fort.)

Defn: The top, platform, or horizontal surface, of a rampart, on
which the cannon are placed. See Illust. of Casemate.
