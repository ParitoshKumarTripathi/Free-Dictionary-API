DENTICETE
Den`ti*ce"te, n. pl. Etym: [NL., fr. L. dens, dentis, tooth + cetus,
pl. cete, whale, Gr. (Zoöl.)

Defn: The division of Cetacea in which the teeth are developed,
including the sperm whale, dolphins, etc.
