TRANSFUND
Trans*fund", v. t. Etym: [L. transfundere; trans over, across +
fundere to pour, pour out. See Found to cast, and cf. Transfuse.]

Defn: To pour from one vessel into another; to transfuse. [Obs.]
Barrow.
