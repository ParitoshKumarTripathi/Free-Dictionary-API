RES
Res (rz), n.; pl. Res. Etym: [L.]

Defn: A thing; the particular thing; a matter; a point. Res gestæ
Etym: [L., things done] (Law), the facts which form the environment
of a litigated issue. Wharton.
 -- Res judicata [L.] (Law), a thing adjudicated; a matter no longer
open to controversy.
