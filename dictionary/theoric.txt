THEORIC
The*or"ic, a. Etym: [Cf. F. théorique. See Theory.]

1. Of or pertaining to the theorica.

2. (pron.

Defn: Relating to, or skilled in, theory; theoretically skilled.
[Obs.]
A man but young, Yet old in judgment, theoric and practic In all
humanity. Massinger.
THEORIC

=====
The"o*ric, n. Etym: [OF. theorique; cf. L. theorice.]

Defn: Speculation; theory. [Obs.] Shak.
