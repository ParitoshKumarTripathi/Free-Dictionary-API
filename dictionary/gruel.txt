GRUEL
Gru"el, n. Etym: [OF. gruel, F. gruau; of German origin; cf. OHG.
gruzzi groats, G. grütze, As. grut. See Grout.]

Defn: A light, liquid food, made by boiling meal of maize, oatmeal,
or fiour in water or milk; thin porridge.
