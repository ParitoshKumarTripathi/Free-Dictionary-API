OVISM
O"vism, n.  [Ovum + -ism.] (Zoöl.)

Defn: The old theory that the egg contains the whole embryo of the
future organism and the germs of all subsequent offsprings and is
merely awakened to activity by the spermatozoön; -- opposed to
spermism or animalculism.
