VETIVER
Vet"i*ver, n. (Bot.)

Defn: An East Indian grass (Andropogon muricatus); also, its fragrant
roots which are much used for making mats and screens. Also called
kuskus, and khuskhus. [Sometimes written vetivert, and vitivert.]
