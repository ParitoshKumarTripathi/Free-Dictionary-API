SPENDTHRIFT
Spend"thrift`, n.

Defn: One who spends money profusely or improvidently; a prodigal;
one who lavishes or wastes his estate. Also used figuratively.
A woman who was a generous spendthrift of life. Mrs. R. H. Davis.
SPENDTHRIFT

=====
Spend"thrift, a.

Defn: Prodigal; extravagant; wasteful.
