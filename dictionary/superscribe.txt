SUPERSCRIBE
Su`per*scribe", v. t. [imp. & p. p. Superscribed; p. pr. & vb. n.
Superscribing.] Etym: [L. superscribere, superscriptum; super over +
scribere to write. See Super-, and Scribe.]

Defn: To write or engrave (a name, address, inscription, or the like)
on the top or surface; to write a name, address, or the like, on the
outside or cover of (anything); as, to superscribe a letter.
