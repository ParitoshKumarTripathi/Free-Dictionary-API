PHONOLITE
Pho"no*lite, n. Etym: [Phono- + -lite: cf. F. phonolithe.] (Min.)

Defn: A compact, feldspathic, igneous rock containing nephelite,
haüynite, etc. Thin slabs give a ringing sound when struck; -- called
also clinkstone.
