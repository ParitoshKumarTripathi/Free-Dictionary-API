HEAVENLYMINDED; HEAVENLY-MINDED
Heav"en*ly*mind`ed, Heav"en*ly-mind`ed, a.

Defn: Having the thoughts and affections placed on, or suitable for,
heaven and heavenly objects; devout; godly; pious. Milner.
 -- Heav"en*ly*mind`ed*ness, n.
