TITLARK
Tit"lark`, n. Etym: [Tit a small bird + lark.] (Zoöl.)

Defn: Any one of numerous small spring birds belonging to Anthus,
Corydalla, and allied genera, which resemble the true larks in color
and in having a very long hind claw; especially, the European meadow
pipit (Anthus pratensis).
