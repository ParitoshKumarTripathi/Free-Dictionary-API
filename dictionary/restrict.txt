RESTRICT
Re*strict", a. Etym: [L. restrictus, p. p. of restringere. See
Restrain.]

Defn: Restricted. [Obs.]
RESTRICT

=====
Re*strict", v. t. [imp. & p. p. Restricted; p. pr. & vb. n.
Restricting.]

Defn: To restrain within bounds; to limit; to confine; as, to
restrict worlds to a particular meaning; to restrict a patient to a
certain diet.

Syn.
 -- To limit; bound; circumscribe; restrain; repress; curb; coerce.
