BONNYCLABBER
Bon"ny*clab`ber, n. Etym: [Ir. bainne, baine, milk + clabar mud,
mire.]

Defn: Coagulated sour milk; loppered milk; curdled milk; -- sometimes
called simply clabber. B. Jonson.
