USBEGS; USBEKS
Us"begs, Us"beks, n. pl. (Ethnol.)

Defn: A Turkish tribe which about the close of the 15th century
conquered, and settled in, that part of Asia now called Turkestan.
[Written also Uzbecks, and Uzbeks.]
