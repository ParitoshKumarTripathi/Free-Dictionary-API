NATALITIAL; NATALITIOUS
Na`ta*li"tial, Na`ta*li"tious, a. Etym: [L. natalitius, from natalis.
See Natal.]

Defn: Of or pertaining to one's birth or birthday, or one's nativity.
[Obs.] "Natalitial poplar." Evelyn. "Natalitious fire." W.
Cartwright.
