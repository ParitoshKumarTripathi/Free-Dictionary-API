UNGOVERNABLE
Un*gov"ern*a*ble, a.

Defn: Not governable; not capable of being governed, ruled, or
restrained; licentious; wild; unbridled; as, ungovernable passions.
 -- Un*gov"ern*a*bly, adv. Goldsmith.
