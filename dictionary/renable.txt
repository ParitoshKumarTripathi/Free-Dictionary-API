RENABLE
Ren"a*ble (rn"-b'l), a. Etym: [OF. resnable.]

Defn: Reasonable; also, loquacious. [Obs.] "Most renable of tongue."
Piers Plowman.
 -- Ren"a*bly, adv. [Obs.] Chaucer.
