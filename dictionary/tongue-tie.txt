TONGUE-TIE
Tongue"-tie`, n. (Med.)

Defn: Impeded motion of the tongue because of the shortness of the
frænum, or of the adhesion of its margins to the gums. Dunglison.
TONGUE-TIE

=====
Tongue"-tie`, v. t.

Defn: To deprive of speech or the power of speech, or of distinct
articulation.
