LAUDATIVE
Laud"a*tive, a. Etym: [L. laudativus laudatory: cf. F. laudatif.]

Defn: Laudatory.
LAUDATIVE

=====
Laud"a*tive, n.

Defn: A panegyric; a eulogy. [Obs.] Bacon.
