QUAQUAVERSAL
Qua`qua*ver"sal, a. Etym: [L. quaqua wheresoever, whithersoever +
versus, p. p. of vertere to turn.]

1. Turning or dipping in any or every direction.

2. (Geol.)

Defn: Dipping toward all points of the compass round a center, as
beds of lava round a crater.
