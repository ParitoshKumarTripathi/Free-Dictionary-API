MISORDER
Mis*or"der, v. t.

Defn: To order ill; to manage erroneously; to conduct badly. [Obs.]
Shak.
MISORDER

=====
Mis*or"der, n.

Defn: Irregularity; disorder. [Obs.] Camden.
