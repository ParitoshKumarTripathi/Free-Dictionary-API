JAGGED
Jag"ged, a.

Defn: Having jags; having rough, sharp notches, protuberances, or
teeth; cleft; laciniate; divided; as, jagged rocks. " Jagged vine
leaves' shade." Trench.
 -- Jag"ged*ly, adv.
 -- Jag"ged*ness, n.
