PALIFICATION
Pal`i*fi*ca"tion, n. Etym: [L. palus a stake + -ficare (in comp.) to
make: cf. F. palification. See -fy.]

Defn: The act or practice of driving piles or posts into the ground
to make it firm. [R.] Sir H. Wotton.
