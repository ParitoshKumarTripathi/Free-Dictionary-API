PARADIGMATIZE
Par`a*dig"ma*tize, v. t. [imp. & p. p. Paradigmatized; p. pr. & vb.
n. Paradigmatizing.] Etym: [Gr. Paradigm.]

Defn: To set forth as a model or example. [Obs.] Hammond.
