TENERAL
Ten"er*al, a. Etym: [L. tener, -eris, tender, delicate.] (Zoöl.)

Defn: Of, pertaining to, or designating, a condition assumed by the
imago of certain Neuroptera, after exclusion from the pupa. In this
state the insect is soft, and has not fully attained its mature
coloring.
