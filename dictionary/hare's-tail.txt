HARE'S-TAIL
Hare's"-tail` (-tal`), n. (Bot.)

Defn: A kind of grass (Eriophorum vaginatum). See Cotton grass, under
Cotton. Hare's-tail grass (Bot.), a species of grass (Lagurus ovatus)
whose head resembles a hare's tail.
