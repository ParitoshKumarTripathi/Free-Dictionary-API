RACEMOSE
Rac"e*mose`, a. Etym: [L. racemosus full of clusters.]

Defn: Resembling a raceme; growing in the form of a raceme; as,
(Bot.) racemose berries or flowers; (Anat.) the racemose glands, in
which the ducts are branched and clustered like a raceme. Gray.
