EXIGUOUS
Ex*ig"u*ous, a. Etym: [L. exiguus.]

Defn: Scanty; small; slender; diminutive. [R.] "Exiguous resources."
Carlyle.
 -- Ex*ig"uous*ness, n. [R.]
