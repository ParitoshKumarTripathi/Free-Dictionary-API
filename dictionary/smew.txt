SMEW
Smew, n. Etym: [Perhaps for ice-mew.] (Zoöl.)
(a) small European merganser (Mergus albellus) which has a white
crest; -- called also smee, smee duck, white merganser, and white
nun.
(b) The hooded merganser. [Local, U.S.]
