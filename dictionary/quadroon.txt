QUADROON
Quad*roon", n. Etym: [F. quarteron, or Sp. cuarteron. See Quarter a
fourth part, and cf. Quarteron.]

Defn: The offspring of a mulatto and a white person; a person
quarter-blooded. [Written also quarteron, quarteroon, and quateron.]
