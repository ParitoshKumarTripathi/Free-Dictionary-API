TINTO
Tin"to, n. Etym: [Pg., tinged, fr. L. tinctus, p.p. of tingere to
tinge. See Tint, n.]

Defn: A red Madeira wine, wanting the high aroma of the white sorts,
and, when old, resembling tawny port.
