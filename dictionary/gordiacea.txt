GORDIACEA
Gor`di*a"ce*a, n. pl. Etym: [NL. See Gordian, 1.] (Zoöl.)

Defn: A division of nematoid worms, including the hairworms or hair
eels (Gordius and Mermis). See Gordius, and Illustration in Appendix.
