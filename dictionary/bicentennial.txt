BICENTENNIAL
Bi`cen*ten"ni*al, a. Etym: [Pref. bi- + centennial.]

1. Consisting of two hundred years.

2. Occurring every two hundred years.
BICENTENNIAL

=====
Bi`cen*ten"ni*al, n.

Defn: The two hundredth year or anniversary, or its celebration.
