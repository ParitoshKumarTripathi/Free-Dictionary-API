FUN
Fun, n. Etym: [Perh. of Celtic origin; cf. Ir. & Gael. fonn
pleasure.]

Defn: Sport; merriment; frolicsome amusement. "Oddity, frolic, and
fun." Goldsmith. To make fan of, to hold up to, or turn into,
ridicule.
