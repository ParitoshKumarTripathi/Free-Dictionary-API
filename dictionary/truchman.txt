TRUCHMAN
Truch"man, n. Etym: [Cf. F. trucheman. See Dragoman.]

Defn: An interpreter. See Dragoman. [Obs.]
And after, by the tongue, Her truchman, she reports the mind's each
throw. B. Jonson.
