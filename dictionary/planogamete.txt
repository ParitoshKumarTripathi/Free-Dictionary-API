PLANOGAMETE
Plan"o*ga*mete`, n. [Gr.  wandering + E. gamete.] (Bot.)

Defn: One of the motile ciliated gametes, or zoögametes, found in
isogamous plants, as many green algæ (Chlorophyceæ).
