VERSICOLOR; VERSICOLORED
Ver"si*col`or, Ver"si*col`ored, a. Etym: [L. versicolor; versare to
change + color color.]

Defn: Having various colors; changeable in color. "Versicolor, sweet-
smelling flowers." Burton.
