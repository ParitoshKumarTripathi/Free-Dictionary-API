PALAETIOLOGY
Pa*læ`ti*ol"o*gy, n. Etym: [Palæo- + ætiology.]

Defn: The science which explains, by the law of causation, the past
condition and changes of the earth.
 -- Pa*læ`ti*o*log"ic*al, a.
