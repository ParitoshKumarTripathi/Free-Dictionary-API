ROMEINE; ROMEITE
Ro"me*ine, Ro"me*ite, n. Etym: [F. roméine. So calledafter the French
mineralogist Romé L'Isle.] (Min.)

Defn: A mineral of a hyacinth or honey-yellow color, occuring in
square octahedrons. It is an antimonate of calcium.
