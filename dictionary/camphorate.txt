CAMPHORATE
Cam"phor*ate, v. t.

Defn: To impregnate or treat with camphor.
CAMPHORATE

=====
Cam"phor*ate, n. Etym: [Cf. F. camphorate.] (Chem.)

Defn: A salt of camphoric acid.
