NOVACULITE
No*vac"u*lite, n. Etym: [L. novacula a sharp knife, razor: cf. F.
novaculite.] (Min.)

Defn: A variety of siliceous slate, of which hones are made; razor
stone; Turkey stone; hone stone; whet slate.
