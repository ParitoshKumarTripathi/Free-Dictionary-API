PLUVIOGRAPHY
Plu`vi*og"ra*phy, n. [L. pluvia rain + -graphy.]

Defn: The branch of meteorology treating of the automatic
registration of the precipitation of rain, snow, etc.; also, the
graphic presentation of precipitation data.
