INWALL
In*wall", v. t. [imp. & p. p. Inwalled; p. pr. & vb. n. Inwalling.]

Defn: To inclose or fortify as with a wall. Spenser.
INWALL

=====
In"wall`, n.

Defn: An inner wall; specifically (Metal.), the inner wall, or
lining, of a blast furnace.
