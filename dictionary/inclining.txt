INCLINING
In*clin"ing, a. (Bot.)

Defn: Same as Inclined, 3.
INCLINING

=====
In*clin"ing, n.

1. Inclination; disposition.
On the first inclining towards sleep. Burke.

2. Party or side chosen; a following.
Both you of my inclining, and the rest. Shak.
