HIDROSIS
Hi*dro"sis, n. [Written also, but incorrectly, idrosis.] [NL., fr.
Gr.  to sweat,  sweat.]

1. (Physiol.)

Defn: Excretion of sweat; perspiration.

2.  (Med.) Excessive perspiration; also, any skin disease
characterized by abnormal perspiration.
