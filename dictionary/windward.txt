WINDWARD
Wind"ward, n.

Defn: The point or side from which the wind blows; as, to ply to the
windward; -- opposed to Ant: leeward. To lay an anchor to the
windward, a figurative expression, signifying to adopt precautionary
or anticipatory measures for success or security.
WINDWARD

=====
Wind"ward, a.

Defn: Situated toward the point from which the wind blows; as, the
Windward Islands.
WINDWARD

=====
Wind"ward, adv.

Defn: Toward the wind; in the direction from which the wind blows.
