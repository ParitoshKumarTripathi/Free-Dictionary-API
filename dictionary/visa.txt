VISA
Vi"sa, n. Etym: [F.]

Defn: See Vis.
VISA

=====
Vi"sa, v. t. [imp. & p. p. Visaed; p. pr. & vb. n. Visaing.]

Defn: To indorse, after examination, with the word visé, as a
passport; to visé.
