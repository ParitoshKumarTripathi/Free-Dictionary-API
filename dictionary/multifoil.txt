MULTIFOIL
Mul"ti*foil, n. Etym: [Multi- + foil.] (Arch.)

Defn: An ornamental foliation consisting of more than five divisions
or foils. [R.] See Foil.
MULTIFOIL

=====
Mul"ti*foil, a.

Defn: Having more than five divisions or foils.
