UROCHROME
U"ro*chrome, n. Etym: [1st uro- + Gr. (Physiol. Chem.)

Defn: A yellow urinary pigment, considered by Thudichum as the only
pigment present in normal urine. It is regarded by Maly as identical
with urobilin.
