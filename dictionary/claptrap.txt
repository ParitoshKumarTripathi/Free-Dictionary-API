CLAPTRAP
Clap"trap`, n.

1. A contrivance for clapping in theaters. [Obs.]

2. A trick or device to gain applause; humbug.
CLAPTRAP

=====
Clap"trap`, a.

Defn: Contrived for the purpose of making a show, or gaining
applause; deceptive; unreal.
