SPOOR
Spoor, n. Etym: [D. spoor; akin to AS. spor, G. spur, and from the
root of E. spur. sq. root171. See Spur.]

Defn: The track or trail of any wild animal; as, the spoor of an
elephant; -- used originally by travelers in South Africa.
SPOOR

=====
Spoor, v. i.

Defn: To follow a spoor or trail. [R.]
