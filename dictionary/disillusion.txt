DISILLUSION
Dis`il*lu"sion, n.

Defn: The act or process of freeing from an illusion, or the state of
being freed therefrom. Lowell.
DISILLUSION

=====
Dis`il*lu"sion, v. t.

Defn: To free from an illusion; to disillusionize.
