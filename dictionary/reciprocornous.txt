RECIPROCORNOUS
Re*cip`ro*cor"nous, a. Etym: [L. reciprocus returning, reciprocal +
cornu horn.] (Zoöl.)

Defn: Having horns turning backward and then forward, like those of a
ram. [R.] Ash.
