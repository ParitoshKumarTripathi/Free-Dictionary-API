RED-TAILED
Red"-tailed` (-tld`), a.

Defn: Having a red tail. Red-tailed hawk (Zoöl.), a large North
American hawk (Buteo borealis). When adult its tail is chestnut red.
Called also hen hawck, and red-tailed buzzard.
