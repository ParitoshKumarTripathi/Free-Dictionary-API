SQUAMELLA
Squa*mel"la, n.; pl. Squamellæ. Etym: [NL., dim. fr. L. squama a
scale.] (Bot.)

Defn: A diminutive scale or bractlet, such as those found on the
receptacle in many composite plants; a palea.
