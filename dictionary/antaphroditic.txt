ANTAPHRODITIC
Ant`aph*ro*dit"ic, a. Etym: [Pref. anti- + Gr. antaphroditique.]
(Med.)

1. Antaphrodisiac.

2. Antisyphilitic. [R.]
ANTAPHRODITIC

=====
Ant`aph*ro*dit"ic, n.

Defn: An antaphroditic medicine.
