QUAD; QUADE
Quad, Quade, a. Etym: [Akin to AS. cwæd, cwead, dung, evil, G. kot,
dung, OHG. quat.]

Defn: Evil; bad; baffling; as, a quade wind. [Obs.]
Sooth play, quad play, as the Fleming saith. Chaucer.
