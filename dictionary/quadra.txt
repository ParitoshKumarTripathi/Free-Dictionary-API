QUADRA
Quad"ra, n.; pl. Quadræ. Etym: [L., a square, the socle, a platband,
a fillet.] (Arch.)
(a) The plinth, or lowest member, of any pedestal, podium, water
table, or the like.
(b) A fillet, or listel.
