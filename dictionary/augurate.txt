AUGURATE
Au"gu*rate, v. t. & i. Etym: [L. auguratus, p. p. of augurari to
augur.]

Defn: To make or take auguries; to augur; to predict. [Obs.] C.
Middleton.
AUGURATE

=====
Au"gu*rate, n.

Defn: The office of an augur. Merivale.
