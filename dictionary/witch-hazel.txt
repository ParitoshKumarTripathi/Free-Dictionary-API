WITCH-HAZEL
Witch"-ha`zel, n. Etym: [See Wych-elm, and Hazel.] (Bot.)

Defn: The wych-elm.
(b) An American shrub or small tree (Hamamelis Virginica), which
blossoms late in autumn.
