FERIAL
Fe"ri*al, n.

Defn: Same as Feria.
FERIAL

=====
Fe"ri*al, a. Etym: [LL. ferialis, fr. L. ferie holidays: cf. F.
férial. See 5th Fair.]

1. Of or pertaining to holidays. [Obs.] J. Gregory.

2. Belonging to any week day, esp. to a day that is neither a
festival nor a fast.
