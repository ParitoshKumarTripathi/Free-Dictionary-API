PUNTER
Punt"er, n.Etym: [Cf. F. ponte. See Punt, v. t.]

Defn: One who punts; specifically, one who plays against the banker
or dealer, as in baccara and faro. Hoyle.
PUNTER

=====
Punt"er, n.

Defn: One who punts a football; also, one who propels a punt.
