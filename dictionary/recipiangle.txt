RECIPIANGLE
Re*cip"i*an`gle, n. Etym: [L. recipere to take + angulus angle.]

Defn: An instrument with two arms that are pivoted together at one
end, and a graduated arc, -- used by military engineers for measuring
and laying off angles of fortifications.
