REDOUBTABLE
Re*doubt"a*ble (-*b'l), a. Etym: [F. redoutable, formerly also spelt
redoubtable.]

Defn: Formidable; dread; terrible to foes; as, a redoubtable hero;
hence, valiant; -- often in contempt or burlesque. [Written also
redoutable.]
