SCATTERED
Scat"tered, a.

1. Dispersed; dissipated; sprinkled, or loosely spread.

2. (Bot.)

Defn: Irregular in position; having no regular order; as, scattered
leaves.
 -- Scat"tered*ly, adv.
 -- Scat"tered*ness, n.
