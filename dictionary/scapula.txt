SCAPULA
Scap"u*la, n.; pl. L. Scapulæ, E. Scapuolas. Etym: [L.]

1. (Anat.)

Defn: The principal bone of the shoulder girdle in mammals; the
shoulder blade.

2. (Zoöl.)

Defn: One of the plates from which the arms of a crinoid arise.
