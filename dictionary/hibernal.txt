HIBERNAL
Hi*ber"nal, a. Etym: [L. hibernalis, from the root of hiems winter;
akin to Gr. hima cold, winter, snow: cf. F. hibernal.]

Defn: Belonging or relating to winter; wintry; winterish. Sir T.
Browne.
