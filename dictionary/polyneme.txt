POLYNEME
Pol"y*neme, n. Etym: [Poly- + Gr. (Zoöl.)

Defn: Any one of numerous species of tropical food fishes of the
family Polynemidæ. They have several slender filaments, often very
long, below the pectoral fin. Some of them yield isinglass of good
quality. Called also threadfish.
