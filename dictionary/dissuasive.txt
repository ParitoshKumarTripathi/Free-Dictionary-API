DISSUASIVE
Dis*sua"sive, a.

Defn: Tending to dissuade or divert from a measure or purpose;
dehortatory; as, dissuasive advice.
 -- n.

Defn: A dissuasive argument or counsel; dissuasion; dehortation.
Prynne.
 -- Dis*sua"sive*ly, adv.
