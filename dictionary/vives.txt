VIVES
Vives, n. Etym: [OF. vives, F. avives (cf. Sp. abivas, adiva) fr. Ar.
ad-dhiba. Cf. Fives vives.] (Far.)

Defn: A disease of brute animals, especially of horses, seated in the
glands under the ear, where a tumor is formed which sometimes ends in
suppuration.
