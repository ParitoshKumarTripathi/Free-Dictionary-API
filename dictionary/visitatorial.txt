VISITATORIAL
Vis`it*a*to"ri*al, a. Etym: [Cf. LL. visitator a bishop temporarily
put in place of another.]

Defn: Of or pertaining to visitation, or a judicial visitor or
superintendent; visitorial.
An archdeacon has visitatorial power. Ayliffe.
The queen, however, still had over the church a visitatorial power of
vast and undefined extent. Macaulay.
