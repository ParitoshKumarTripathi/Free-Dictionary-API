GERMANIC
Ger*man"ic, a. (Chem.)

Defn: Pertaining to, or containing, germanium.
GERMANIC

=====
Ger*man"ic, a. Etym: [L. Germanicus: cf. F. germanique. See German,
n.]

1. Of or pertaining to Germany; as, the Germanic confederacy.

2. Teutonic. [A loose sense]
