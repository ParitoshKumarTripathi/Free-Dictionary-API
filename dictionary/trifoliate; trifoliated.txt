TRIFOLIATE; TRIFOLIATED
Tri*fo"li*ate, Tri*fo"li*a`ted, a. Etym: [Tri- + foliate. Cf.
Trefoil.] (Bot.)

Defn: Having three leaves or leaflets, as clover. See Illust. of
Shamrock.
