SERRANOID
Ser*ra"noid, n. Etym: [NL. Serranus, a typical genus (fr. L. serra a
saw) + -oid.] (Zoöl.)

Defn: Any fish of the family Serranidæ, which includes the striped
bass, the black sea bass, and many other food fishes.
 -- a. (Zoöl.)

Defn: Of or pertaining to the Serranidæ.
