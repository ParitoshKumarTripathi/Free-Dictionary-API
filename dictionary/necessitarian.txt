NECESSITARIAN
Ne*ces`si*ta"ri*an, a.

Defn: Of or pertaining to the doctrine of philosophical necessity in
regard to the origin and existence of things, especially as applied
to the actings or choices of the will; -- opposed to libertarian.
NECESSITARIAN

=====
Ne*ces`si*ta"ri*an, n.

Defn: One who holds to the doctrine of necessitarianism.
