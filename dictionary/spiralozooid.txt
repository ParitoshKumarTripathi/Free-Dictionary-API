SPIRALOZOOID
Spi*ra`lo*zo"oid, n. Etym: [Spiral + zooid. So called because they
often have a spiral form when contracted.] (Zoöl.)

Defn: One of the special defensive zooids of certain hydroids. They
have the form of long, slender tentacles, and bear lasso cells.
