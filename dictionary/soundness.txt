SOUNDNESS
Sound"ness, n.

Defn: The quality or state of being sound; as, the soundness of
timber, of fruit, of the teeth, etc.; the soundness of reasoning or
argument; soundness of faith.

Syn.
 -- Firmness; strength; solidity; healthiness; truth; rectitude.
