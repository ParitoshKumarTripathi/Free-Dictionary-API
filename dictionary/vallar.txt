VALLAR
Val"lar, a. Etym: [L.vallaris.]

Defn: Of or pertaining to a rampart. Vallar crown (Rom. Antiq.), a
circular gold crown with palisades, bestowed upon the soldier who
first surmounted the rampart and broke into the enemy's camp.
VALLAR

=====
Val"lar, n.

Defn: A vallar crown.
