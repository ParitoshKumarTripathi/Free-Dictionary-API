REGENERATIVE
Re*gen"er*a*tive (r*jn"r**tv), a.

Defn: Of or pertaining to regeneration; tending to regenerate; as,
regenerative influences. H. Bushnell. Regenerative furnace (Metal.),
a furnace having a regenerator in which gas used for fuel, and air
for supporting combustion, are heated; a Siemens furnace.
