SPINA BIFIDA
Spi"na bif"i*da. (Med.) Etym: [L., cleft spine.]

Defn: A congenital malformation in which the spinal column is cleft
at its lower portion, and the membranes of the spinal cord project as
an elastic swelling from the gap thus formed.
