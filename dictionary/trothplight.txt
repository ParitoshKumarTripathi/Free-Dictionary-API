TROTHPLIGHT
Troth"plight`, v. t.

Defn: To betroth. [Obs.]
TROTHPLIGHT

=====
Troth"plight`, a.

Defn: Betrothed; espoused; affianced. [Obs.] Shak.
TROTHPLIGHT

=====
Troth"plight`, n.

Defn: The act of betrothing, or plighting faith; betrothing. [Obs.]
Shak.
