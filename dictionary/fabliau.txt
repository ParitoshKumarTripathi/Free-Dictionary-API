FABLIAU
Fa`bli`au", n.; pl. Fabliaux . Etym: [F., fr. OF.fablel, dim. of
fable a fable.] (Fr. Lit.)

Defn: One of the metrical tales of the Trouvères, or early poets of
the north of France.
