PONDFISH
Pond"fish`, n. (Zoöl.)

Defn: Any one of numerous species of American fresh-water fishes
belonging to the family Centrarchidæ; -- called also pond perch, and
sunfish.

Note: The common pondfish of New England (Lepomis gibbosus) is called
also bream, pumpkin seed, and sunny. See Sunfish. The long-eared
pondfish (Lepomis auritus) of the Eastern United States is
distinguished by its very long opercular flap.
