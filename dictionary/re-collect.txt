RE-COLLECT
Re`-col*lect", v. t. Etym: [Pref. re- + collect.]

Defn: To collect again; to gather what has been scattered; as, to re-
collect routed troops.
God will one day raise the dead, re-collecting our scattered dust.
Barrow.
