BOSSY
Boss"y, a.

Defn: Ornamented with bosses; studded.
BOSSY

=====
Bos"sy, n. Etym: [Dim. fr. Prov. E. boss in boss-calf, buss-calf, for
boose-calf, prop., a calf kept in the stall. See 1st Boose.]

Defn: A cow or calf; -- familiarly so called. [U. S.]
