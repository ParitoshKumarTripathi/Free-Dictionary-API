NAIVE
Na"ïve`, a. Etym: [F. naïf, fem. naïve, fr. L. nativus innate,
natural, native. See Native, and cf. Naïf.]

Defn: Having native or unaffected simplicity; ingenuous; artless;
frank; as, naïve manners; a naïve person; naïve and unsophisticated
remarks.
