SOLENOGASTRA
So*le`no*gas"tra, n. pl. Etym: [NL., from Gr. (Zoöl.)

Defn: An order of lowly organized Mollusca belonging to the
Isopleura. A narrow groove takes the place of the foot of other
gastropods.
