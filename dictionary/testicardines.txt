TESTICARDINES
Tes`ti*car"di*nes, n. pl. Etym: [NL. See Test a shell, and Cardo.]
(Zoöl.)

Defn: A division of brachiopods including those which have a
calcareous shell furnished with a hinge and hinge teeth. Terebratula
and Spirifer are examples.
