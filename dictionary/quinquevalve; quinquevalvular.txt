QUINQUEVALVE; QUINQUEVALVULAR
Quin"que*valve, Quin`que*val"vu*lar, a. Etym: [Quinque- + valve,
valvular: cf. F. quinquévalve.] (Bot.)

Defn: Having five valves, as a pericarp.
