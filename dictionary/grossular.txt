GROSSULAR
Gros"su*lar, a. Etym: [NL. grossularius, from Grossularia a subgenus
of Ribes, including the gooseberry, fr. F. groseille. See
Gooseberry.]

Defn: Pertaining too, or resembling, a gooseberry; as, grossular
garnet.
GROSSULAR

=====
Gros"su*lar, n. Etym: [See Grossular, a.] (Min.)

Defn: A translucent garnet of a pale green color like that of the
gooseberry; -- called also grossularite.
