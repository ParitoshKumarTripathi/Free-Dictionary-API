LEAPER
Leap"er, n. Etym: [AS. hleápere.]

Defn: One who, or that which, leaps.
LEAPER

=====
Leap"er, n. Etym: [See 1st Leap.]

Defn: A kind of hooked instrument for untwisting old cordage.
