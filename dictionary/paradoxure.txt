PARADOXURE
Par`a*dox"ure, n. Etym: [Gr. (Zoöl.)

Defn: Any species of Paradoxurus, a genus of Asiatic viverrine
mammals allied to the civet, as the musang, and the luwack or palm
cat (Paradoxurus hermaphroditus). See Musang.
