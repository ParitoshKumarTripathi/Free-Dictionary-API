TRACHEARIA
Tra`che*a"ri*a, n.pl. Etym: [NL.] (Zoöl.)

Defn: A division of Arachnida including those that breathe only by
means of tracheæ. It includes the mites, ticks, false scorpions, and
harvestmen.
