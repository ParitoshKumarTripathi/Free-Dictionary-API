PROTEIN
Pro"te*in, n. Etym: [Gr. prw	os first: cf. prwtei^on the first
place.] (Physiol. Chem.)

Defn: A body now known as alkali albumin, but originally considered
to be the basis of all albuminous substances, whence its name.
Protein crystal. (Bot.) See Crystalloid, n., 2.
