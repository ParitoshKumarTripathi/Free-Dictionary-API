HYPO
Hy"po, n.

Defn: Hypochondria. [Colloq.]
HYPO

=====
Hy"po, n. Etym: [Abbrev. from hyposulphite.] (Photog.)

Defn: Sodium hyposulphite, or thiosulphate, a solution of which is
used as a bath to wash out the unchanged silver salts in a picture.
[Colloq.]
