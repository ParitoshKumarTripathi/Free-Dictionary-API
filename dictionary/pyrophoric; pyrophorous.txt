PYROPHORIC; PYROPHOROUS
Pyr`o*phor"ic, Py*roph"o*rous, a. Etym: [Pyro- + Gr.

Defn: Light-producing; of or pertaining to pyrophorus. Pyrophoric
iron (Chem.), finely reduced iron, which ignites spontaneously on
contact with air.
