NECESSARIAN
Nec`es*sa"ri*an, n. Etym: [Cf. F. nécessarien. See Mecessary.]

Defn: An advocate of the doctrine of philosophical necessity; a
nacessitarian.
NECESSARIAN

=====
Nec`es*sa"ri*an, a.

Defn: Of or pertaining to necessarianism.
