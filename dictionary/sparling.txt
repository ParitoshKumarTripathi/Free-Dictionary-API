SPARLING
Spar"ling, n. Etym: [Akin to G. spierling, spiering, D. spiering: cf.
F. éperlan.] (Zoöl.)
(a) The European smelt (Osmerus eperlanus).
(b) A young salmon.
(c) A tern. [Scot. & Prov. Eng.]
