INGIRT
In*girt", v. t. Etym: [See Ingirt.]

Defn: To encircle to gird; to engirt.
The wreath is ivy that ingirts our beams. Drayton.
INGIRT

=====
In*girt", a.

Defn: Surrounded; encircled. Fenton.
