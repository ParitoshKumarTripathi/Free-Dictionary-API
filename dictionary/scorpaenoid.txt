SCORPAENOID
Scor*pæ"noid, a. Etym: [NL. Scorpaena, a typical genus (see Scorpene)
+ -oid.] (Zoöl.)

Defn: Of or pertaining to the family Scorpænidæ, which includes the
scorpene, the rosefish, the California rockfishes, and many other
food fishes. [Written also scorpænid.] See Illust. under Rockfish.
