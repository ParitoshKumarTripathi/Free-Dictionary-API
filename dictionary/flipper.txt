FLIPPER
Flip"per, n. Etym: [Cf. Flip, Flippant.]

1. (Zoöl.)

Defn: A broad flat limb used for swimming, as those of seals, sea
turtles, whales, etc.

2. (Naut.)

Defn: The hand. [Slang]
