UNSIGHTED
Un*sight"ed, a.

1. Not sighted, or seen. Suckling.

2. (Gun.)

Defn: Not aimed by means of a sight; also, not furnished with a
sight, or with a properly adjusted sight; as, to shoot and unsighted
rife or cannon.
