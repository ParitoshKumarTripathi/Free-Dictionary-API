SHAPELESS
Shape"less, a.

Defn: Destitute of shape or regular form; wanting symmetry of
dimensions; misshapen; -- opposed to Ant: shapely.
 -- Shape"less*ness, n.
The shapeless rock, or hanging precipice. Pope.
