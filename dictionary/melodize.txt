MELODIZE
Mel"o*dize, v. t. [imp. & p. p. Melodized; p. pr. & vb. n.
Melodizing.]

Defn: To make melodious; to form into, or set to, melody.
MELODIZE

=====
Mel"o*dize, v. i.

Defn: To make melody; to compose melodies; to harmonize.
