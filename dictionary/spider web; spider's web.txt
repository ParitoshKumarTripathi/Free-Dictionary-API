SPIDER WEB; SPIDER'S WEB
Spi"der web", or Spi"der's web". (Zoöl.)

Defn: The silken web which is formed by most kinds of spiders,
particularly the web spun to entrap their prey. See Geometric spider,
Triangle spider, under Geometric, and Triangle.
