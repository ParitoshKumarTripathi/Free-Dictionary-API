TRICHOGYNE
Trich"o*gyne, n. Etym: [F., fr. Gr. (Bot.)

Defn: The slender, hairlike cell which receives the fertilizing
particles, or antherozoids, in red seaweeds.
 -- Trich`o*gyn"ic, a.
