VITRIOLATED
Vit"ri*o*la`ted, a. (Old Chem.)

Defn: Changed into a vitriol or a sulphate, or subjected to the
action of sulphuric acid or of a sulphate; as, vitriolated potash, i.
e., potassium sulphate.
