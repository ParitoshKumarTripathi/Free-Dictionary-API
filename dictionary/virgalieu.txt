VIRGALIEU
Vir"ga*lieu, n. Etym: [Cf. Virgouleuse.] (Bot.)

Defn: A valuable kind of pear, of an obovate shape and with melting
flesh of delicious flavor; -- more properly called White Doyenné.
[Written also virgaloo, vergalieu, vergaloo, etc.]
