UNREMITTING
Un`re*mit"ting, a.

Defn: Not remitting; incessant; continued; persevering; as,
unremitting exertions. Cowper.
 -- Un`re*mit"ting*ly, adv.
 -- Un`re*mit"ting*ness, n.
