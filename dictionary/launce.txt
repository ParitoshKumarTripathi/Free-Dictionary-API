LAUNCE
Launce, n.

Defn: A lance. [Obs.]
LAUNCE

=====
Launce, n. Etym: [It. lance, L. lanx, lancis, plate, scale of a
balance. Cf. Balance.]

Defn: A balance. [Obs.]
Fortune all in equal launce doth sway. Spenser.
LAUNCE

=====
Launce, n. (Zoöl.)

Defn: See Lant, the fish.
