SPITTED
Spit"ted, a. Etym: [From Spit.]

1. Put upon a spit; pierced as if by a spit.

2. Shot out long; -- said of antlers. Bacon.
SPITTED

=====
Spit"ted,

Defn: p. p. of Spit, v. i., to eject, to spit. [Obs.]
