PARIETES
Pa*ri"e*tes, n. pl. Etym: [L. paries a wall.]

1. (Anat.)

Defn: The walls of a cavity or an organ; as, the abdominal parietes;
the parietes of the cranium.

2. (Bot.)

Defn: The sides of an ovary or of a capsule.
