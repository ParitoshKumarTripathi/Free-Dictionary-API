RESPIRATORY
Re*spir"a*to*ry (r*spr"*t*r or rs"p*r-), a. (Physiol.)

Defn: Of or pertaining to respiration; serving for respiration; as,
the respiratory organs; respiratory nerves; the respiratory function;
respiratory changes. Respiratory foods. (Physiol.) See 2d Note under
Food, n., 1.
 -- Respiratory tree (Zoöl.), the branched internal gill of certain
holothurians.
