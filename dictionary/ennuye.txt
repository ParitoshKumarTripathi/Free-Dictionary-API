ENNUYE
En`nuy`é", a. Etym: [F., p. p. of ennuyer. See Ennui.]

Defn: Affected with ennui; weary in spirits; emotionally exhausted.
ENNUYE

=====
En`nuy`é", n. Etym: [F.]

Defn: One who is affected with ennui.
