OVERWEENING
O`ver*ween"ing, a.

Defn: Unduly confident; arrogant; presumptuous; conceited.
 -- O`ver*ween"ingly, adv. Milton.
 -- O`ver*ween"ing*ness, n.
Here's an overweening rogue. Shak.
