REFRESHER
Re*fresh"er (-r), n.

1. One who, or that which, refreshes.

2. (Law)

Defn: An extra fee paid to counsel in a case that has been adjourned
from one term to another, or that is unusually protracted.
Ten guineas a day is the highest refresher which a counsel can
charge. London Truth.
