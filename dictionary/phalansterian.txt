PHALANSTERIAN
Phal`an*ste"ri*an, a. Etym: [F. phalanstérien, a. & n.]

Defn: Of or pertaining to phalansterianism.
PHALANSTERIAN

=====
Phal`an*ste"ri*an, n.

Defn: One who favors the system of phalansteries proposed by Fourier.
