QUADRIVIAL
Quad*riv"i*al, a. Etym: [L. quadrivium a place where four ways meet;
quattuor four + via way.]

Defn: Having four ways meeting in a point. B. Jonson.
QUADRIVIAL

=====
Quad*riv"i*al, n.

Defn: One of the four "liberal arts" making up the quadrivium.
