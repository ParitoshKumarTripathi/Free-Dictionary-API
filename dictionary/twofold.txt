TWOFOLD
Two"fold`, a. Etym: [Cf. Twifold.]

Defn: Double; duplicate; multiplied by two; as, a twofold nature; a
twofold sense; a twofold argument.
TWOFOLD

=====
Two"fold`, adv.

Defn: In a double degree; doubly.
