THEBAIC
The*ba"ic, a. Etym: [L. thebaicus, Gr.

Defn: Of or pertaining to Thebes in Egypt; specifically, designating
a version of the Bible preserved by the Copts, and esteemed of great
value by biblical scholars. This version is also called the Sahidic
version.
