THUNDEROUS
Thun"der*ous, a. Etym: [Written also thundrous.]

1. Producing thunder. [R.]
How he before the thunderous throne doth lie. Milton.

2. Making a noise like thunder; sounding loud and deep; sonorous.
 -- Thun"der*ous*ly, adv.
