POLY-MOUNTAIN
Po`ly-moun"tain, n. (Bot.)
(a) Same as Poly, n.
(b) The closely related Teucrium montanum, formerly called Polium
montanum, a plant of Southern Europe.
(c) The Bartsia alpina, a low purple-flowered herb of Europe.
