SALMONOID
Sal"mon*oid, a. Etym: [Salmon + -oid.] (Zoöl.)

Defn: Like, or pertaining to, the Salmonidæ, a family of fishes
including the trout and salmon.
 -- n.

Defn: Any fish of the family Salmonidæ.
