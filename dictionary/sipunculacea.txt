SIPUNCULACEA
Si*pun`cu*la"ce*a, n. pl. Etym: [NL., from Sipunculus, the typical
genus. See Siphuncle.] (Zoöl.)

Defn: A suborder of Gephyrea, including those which have the body
unarmed and the intestine opening anteriorly.
