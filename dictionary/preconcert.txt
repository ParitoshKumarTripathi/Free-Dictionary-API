PRECONCERT
Pre`con*cert", v. t. [imp. & p. p. Preconcerted; p. pr. & vb. n.
Preconcerting.]

Defn: To concert or arrange beforehand; to settle by previous
agreement.
PRECONCERT

=====
Pre*con"cert, n.

Defn: Something concerted or arranged beforehand; a previous
agreement.
