SAVORY; SAVOURY
Sa"vo*ry, n. Etym: [F. savorée; cf. It. santoreggia, satureja, L.
satureia,] (Bot.)

Defn: An aromatic labiate plant (Satireia hortensis), much used in
cooking; -- also called summer savory. [Written also savoury.]
