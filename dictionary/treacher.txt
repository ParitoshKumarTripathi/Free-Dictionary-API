TREACHER
Treach"er, n. Etym: [OE. trichour, trichur, OF. tricheor deceiver,
traitor, F. tricheur a cheat at play, a trickster. See Treachery.]

Defn: A traitor; a cheat. [Obs.]
Treacher and coward both. Beau. & Fl.
