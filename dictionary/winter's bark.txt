WINTER'S BARK
Win"ter's bark`. (Bot.)

Defn: The aromatic bark of tree (Drimys, or Drymis, Winteri) of the
Magnolia family, which is found in Southern Chili. It was first used
as a cure for scurvy by its discoverer, Captain John Winter, vice
admiral to sir Francis Drake, in 1577.
