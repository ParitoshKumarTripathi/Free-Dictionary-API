TUT
Tut.

Defn: Be still; hush; -- an exclamation used for checking or
rebuking.
TUT

=====
Tut, n. Etym: [Cf. Sw. tut a point, pipe, tube, Dan. tut a cornet.]

1. An imperial ensign consisting of a golden globe with a cross on
it.

2. A hassock. [Obs. or Prov. Eng.]
