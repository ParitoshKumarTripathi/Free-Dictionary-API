VIRGULE
Vir"gule, n. Etym: [F. virgule, fr. L. virgula, dim. of virga. See
Verge a rod.]

Defn: A comma. [R.]
In the MSS. of Chaucer, the line is always broken by a cæsura in the
middle, which is pointed by a virgule. Hallam.
