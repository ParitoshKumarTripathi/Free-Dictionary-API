STOCKFISH
Stock"fish`, n. Etym: [Cf. D. stokvisch.]

1. Salted and dried fish, especially codfish, hake, ling, and torsk;
also, codfish dried without being salted.

2. (Zoöl.)

Defn: Young fresh cod.
