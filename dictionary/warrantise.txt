WARRANTISE
War"rant*ise, n. Etym: [OF. warentise, warandise, garantise. See
Warrant, n.]

Defn: Authority; security; warranty. [Obs.] Shak.
WARRANTISE

=====
War"rant*ise, v. t.

Defn: To warrant. [Obs.] Hakluyt.
