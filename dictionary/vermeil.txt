VERMEIL
Ver"meil, n. Etym: [F., vermilion, fr. LL. vermiculus, fr. L.
vermiculus a little worm, the coccus Indicus, from vermis a worm. See
Worm, and cf. Vermicule.]

1. Vermilion; also, the color of vermilion, a bright, beautiful red.
[Poetic & R.]
In her cheeks the vermeil red did show Like roses in a bed of lilies
shed. Spenser.

2. Silver gilt or gilt bronze.

3. A liquid composition applied to a gilded surface to give luster to
the gold. Knight.
