SEA CLAM
Sea" clam`. (Zoöl.)

Defn: Any one of the large bivalve mollusks found on the open
seacoast, especially those of the family Mactridæ, as the common
American species. (Mactra, or Spisula, solidissima); -- called also
beach clam, and surf clam.
