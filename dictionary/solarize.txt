SOLARIZE
So"lar*ize, v. t. [imp. & p. p. Solarized; p. pr. & vb. n.
Solarizing.] (Photog.)

Defn: To injure by too long exposure to the light of the sun in the
camera; to burn.
SOLARIZE

=====
So"lar*ize, v. i. (Photog.)

Defn: To become injured by undue or too long exposure to the sun's
rays in the camera.
