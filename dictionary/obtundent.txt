OBTUNDENT
Ob*tund"ent, n. Etym: [L. obtundens, p.pr. of obtundere.] (Med.)

Defn: A substance which sheathes a part, or blunts irritation,
usually some bland, oily, or mucilaginous matter; -- nearly the same
as demulcent. Forsyth.
