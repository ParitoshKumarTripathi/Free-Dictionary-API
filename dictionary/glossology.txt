GLOSSOLOGY
Glos*sol"o*gy, n. Etym: [Gr. -logy: cf. F. glossologie. See 3d
Gloss.]

1. The definition and explanation of terms; a glossary.

2. The science of language; comparative philology; linguistics;
glottology.
