GLAUM
Glaum, v. i. Etym: [Etymol. uncertain.]

Defn: To grope with the hands, as in the dark. [Scot.] To glaum at,
to grasp or snatch at; to aspire to.
Wha glaum'd at kingdoms three. Burns.
