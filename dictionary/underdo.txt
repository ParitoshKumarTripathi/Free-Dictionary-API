UNDERDO
Un`der*do", v. i.

Defn: To do less than is requisite or proper; -- opposed to overdo.
Grew.
UNDERDO

=====
Un`der*do", v. t.

Defn: To do less thoroughly than is requisite; specifically, to cook
insufficiently; as, to underdo the meat; -- opposed to overdo.
