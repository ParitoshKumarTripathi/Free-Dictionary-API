SPARKLER
Spar"kler, n.

Defn: One who scatters; esp., one who scatters money; an improvident
person. [Obs.]
SPARKLER

=====
Spar"kler, n.

Defn: One who, or that which, sparkles.
SPARKLER

=====
Spar"kler, n. (Zoöl.)

Defn: A tiger beetle.
