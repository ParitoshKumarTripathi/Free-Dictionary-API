UNCIFORM
Un"ci*form, a. Etym: [L. uncus a hook + -form.]

Defn: Having the shape of a hook; being of a curved or hooked from;
hooklike. Unciform bone (Anat.), a bone of the carpus at the bases of
the fourth and fifth metacarpals; the hamatum.
UNCIFORM

=====
Un"ci*form, n. (Anat.)

Defn: The unciform bone. See Illust. of Perissodactyla.
