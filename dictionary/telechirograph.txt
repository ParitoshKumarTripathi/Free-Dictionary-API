TELECHIROGRAPH
Tel`e*chi"ro*graph, n. [Gr. th e far + chei`r, cheiro`s, hand + -
graph.]

Defn: An instrument for  telegraphically transmitting and receiving
handwritten messages, as photographically by a beam of light from a
mirror.
