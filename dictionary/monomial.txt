MONOMIAL
Mo*no"mi*al, n. Etym: [See Monome, Binomial.] (Alg.)

Defn: A single algebraic expression; that is, an expression
unconnected with any other by the sign of addition, substraction,
equality, or inequality.
MONOMIAL

=====
Mo*no"mi*al, a. (Alg.)

Defn: Consisting of but a single term or expression.
