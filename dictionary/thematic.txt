THEMATIC
The*mat"ic, a. Etym: [Gr. thématique.]

1. (Gram.)

Defn: Of or pertaining to the theme of a word. See Theme, n., 4.

2. (Mus.)

Defn: Of or pertaining to a theme, or subject. Thematic catalogue
(Mus.), a catalogue of musical works which, besides the title and
other particulars, gives in notes the theme, or first few measures,
of the whole work or of its several movements.
