RENNE
Ren"ne (rn"ne), v. t.

Defn: To plunder; -- only in the phrase "to rape and renne." See
under Rap, v. t., to snatch. [Obs.] Chaucer.
RENNE

=====
Ren"ne, v. i.

Defn: To run. [Obs.] Chaucer.
