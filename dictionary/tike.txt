TIKE
Tike, n. (Zoöl.)

Defn: A tick. See 2d Tick. [Obs.]
TIKE

=====
Tike, n. Etym: [Icel. tik a bitch; akin to Sw. tik.]

1. A dog; a cur. "Bobtail tike or trundle-tail." Shak.

2. A countryman or clown; a boorish person.
