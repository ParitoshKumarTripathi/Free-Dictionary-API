PORPHYRITIC
Por`phy*rit"ic, a. Etym: [Cf. F. porphyritique.] (Min.)

Defn: Relating to, or resembling, porphyry, that is, characterized by
the presence of distinct crystals, as of feldspar, quartz, or augite,
in a relatively fine-grained base, often aphanitic or
cryptocrystalline.
