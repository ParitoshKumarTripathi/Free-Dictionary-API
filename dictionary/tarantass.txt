TARANTASS
Tar`an*tass", n. Etym: [Russ. tarantas'.]

Defn: A low four-wheeled carriage used in Russia. The carriage box
rests on two long, springy poles which run from the fore to the hind
axletree. When snow falls, the wheels are taken off, and the body is
mounted on a sledge.
