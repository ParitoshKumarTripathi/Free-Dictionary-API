PTEROCLETES
Pter`o*cle"tes, n. pl. Etym: [NL., fr Pterocles, the typical genus,
fr. Gr. (Zoöl.)

Defn: A division of birds including the sand grouse. They are in some
respects intermediate between the pigeons and true grouse. Called
also Pteroclomorphæ.
