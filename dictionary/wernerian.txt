WERNERIAN
Wer*ne"ri*an, a.

Defn: Of or pertaining to A. G. Werner, The German mineralogist and
geologist, who classified minerals according to their external
characters, and advocated the theory that the strata of the earth's
crust were formed by depositions from water; designating, or
according to, Werner's system.
