PSEUDO-DIPTERAL
Pseu`do-dip"ter*al, a. Etym: [Pseudo- + dipteral: cf. F.
pseudodiptère.] (Arch.)

Defn: Falsely or imperfectly dipteral, as a temple with the inner
range of columns surrounding the cella omitted, so that the space
between the cella wall and the columns is very great, being equal to
two intercolumns and one column.
 -- n.

Defn: A pseudo-dipteral temple.
