REVULSIVE
Re*vul"sive, a. Etym: [Cf. F. révulsif.]

Defn: Causing, or tending to, revulsion.
REVULSIVE

=====
Re*vul"sive, n.

Defn: That which causes revulsion; specifically (Med.), a revulsive
remedy or agent.
