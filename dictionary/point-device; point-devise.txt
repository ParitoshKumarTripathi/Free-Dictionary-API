POINT-DEVICE; POINT-DEVISE
Point`-de*vice", Point`-de*vise", a. Etym: [OE. at point devis; at at
+ point point, condition + devis exact, careful, OF. devis fixed,
set. See Device.]

Defn: Uncommonly nice and exact; precise; particular.
You are rather point-devise in your accouterments. Shak.
Thus he grew up, in logic point-devise, Perfect in grammar, and in
rhetoric nice. Longfellow.

=====
Point`-de*vice", Point`-de*vise", adv.

Defn: Exactly. [Obs.] Shak.
