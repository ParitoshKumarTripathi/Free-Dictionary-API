REARMOUSE; REREMOUSE
Rear"mouse`, Rere"mouse`, n. Etym: [AS. hreremus; probably fr. hreran
to agitate, stir (akin to G. rühren, Icel. hræra) + mus mouse.]
(Zoöl.)

Defn: The leather-winged bat (Vespertilio murinus). [Written also
reermouse.]
