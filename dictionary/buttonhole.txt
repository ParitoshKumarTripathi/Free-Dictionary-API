BUTTONHOLE
But"ton*hole`, n.

Defn: The hole or loop in which a button is caught.
BUTTONHOLE

=====
But"ton*hole`, v. t.

Defn: To hold at the button or buttonhole; to detain in conversation
to weariness; to bore; as, he buttonholed me a quarter of an hour.
