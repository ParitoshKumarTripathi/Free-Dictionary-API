PSEUDORHABDITE
Pseu`do*rhab"dite, n. Etym: [Pseudo- + Gr. (Zoöl.)

Defn: One of the peculiar rodlike corpuscles found in the integument
of certain Turbellaria. They are filled with a soft granular
substance.
