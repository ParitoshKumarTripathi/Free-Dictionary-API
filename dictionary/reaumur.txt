REAUMUR
Ré`au`mur", a.

Defn: Of or pertaining to René Antoine Ferchault de Réaumur;
conformed to the scale adopted by Réaumur in graduating the
thermometer he invented.
 -- n.

Defn: A Réaumur thermometer or scale.

Note: The Réaumur thermometer is so graduated that 0º marks the
freezing point and 80º the boiling point of water. Frequently
indicated by R. Cf. Centigrade, and Fahrenheit. See Illust. of
Thermometer.
