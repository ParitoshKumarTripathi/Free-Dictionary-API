VAGINANT
Vag"i*nant, a. Etym: [Cf. F. vaginant. See Vagina.]

Defn: Serving to in invest, or sheathe; sheathing. Vaginant leaf
(Bot.), a leaf investing the stem or branch by its base, which has
the form of a tube.
