TRICENTENARY
Tri*cen"te*na*ry, a. Etym: [Pref. tri- + centenary.]

Defn: Including, or relating to, the interval of three hundred years;
tercentenary.
 -- n.

Defn: A period of three centuries, or three hundred years, also, the
three-hundredth anniversary of any event; a tercentenary.
