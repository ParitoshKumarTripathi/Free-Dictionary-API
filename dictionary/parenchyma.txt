PARENCHYMA
Pa*ren"chy*ma, n. Etym: [NL., from Gr. parenchyme.] (Biol.)

Defn: The soft celluar substance of the tissues of plants and
animals, like the pulp of leaves, to soft tissue of glands, and the
like.
