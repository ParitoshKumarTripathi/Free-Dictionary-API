DUMOSE; DUMOUS
Du`mose", Du"mous, a. Etym: [L. dumosus, fr. dumus a thornbush, a
bramble.]

1. Abounding with bushes and briers.

2. (Bot.)

Defn: Having a compact, bushy form.
