RHODOCHROSITE
Rho`do*chro"site, n. Etym: [Gr. "ro`don the rose + (Min.)

Defn: Manganese carbonate, a rose-red mineral sometimes occuring
crystallized, but generally massive with rhombohedral cleavage like
calcite; -- called also dialogite.
