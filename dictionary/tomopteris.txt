TOMOPTERIS
To*mop"te*ris, n. Etym: [NL., fr. Gr. (Zoöl.)

Defn: A genus of transparent marine annelids which swim actively at
the surface of the sea. They have deeply divided or forked finlike
organs (parapodia). This genus is the type of the order, or suborder,
Gymnocopa.
