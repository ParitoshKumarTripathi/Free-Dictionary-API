DESICCANT
De*sic"cant, a. Etym: [L. desiccans, p. pr. of desiccare. See
Desiccate.]

Defn: Drying; desiccative.
 -- n.

Defn: (Med.) A medicine or application for drying up a sore. Wiseman.
