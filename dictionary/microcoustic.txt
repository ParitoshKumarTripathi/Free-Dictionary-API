MICROCOUSTIC
Mi`cro*cous"tic, a. Etym: [Micro- + acoustic: cf. F. microcoustique,
micracoustique.]

Defn: Pertaining, or suited, to the audition of small sounds; fitted
to assist hearing.
MICROCOUSTIC

=====
Mi`cro*cous"tic, n.

Defn: An instrument for making faint sounds audible, as to a
partially deaf person.
