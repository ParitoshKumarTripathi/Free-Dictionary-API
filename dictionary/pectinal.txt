PECTINAL
Pec"ti*nal, a. Etym: [L. pecten comb. See Pectinate.]

Defn: Of or pertaining to a comb; resembling a comb.
PECTINAL

=====
Pec"ti*nal, n.

Defn: A fish whose bone Sir T. Browne.
