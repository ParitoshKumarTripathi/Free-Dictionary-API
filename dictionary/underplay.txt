UNDERPLAY
Un`der*play", v. i.

1. To play in a subordinate, or in an inferior manner; to underact a
part.

2. (Card Playing)

Defn: To play a low card when holding a high one, in the hope of a
future advantage.
UNDERPLAY

=====
Un"der*play`, n. (Card Playing)

Defn: The act of underplaying.
