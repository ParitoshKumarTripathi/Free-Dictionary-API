TRILATERAL
Tri*lat"er*al, a.Etym: [L. trilaterus; tri- (see Tri-) + latus,
lateris, side: cf. F. trilatéral. See Lateral.] (Geom.)

Defn: Having three sides; being three-sided; as, a trilateral
triangle.
 -- Tri*lat"er*al*ly, adv.
 -- Tri*lat"er*al*ness, n.
