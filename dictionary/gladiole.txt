GLADIOLE
Glad"i*ole, n. Etym: [L. gladiolus a small sword, the sword lily,
dim. of gladius sword. See Glaive.] (Bot.)

Defn: A lilylike plant, of the genus Gladiolus; -- called also corn
flag.
