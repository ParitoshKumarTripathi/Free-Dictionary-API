DISPROFIT
Dis*prof"it, n.

Defn: Loss; damage. Foxe.
DISPROFIT

=====
Dis*prof"it, v. i. & i.

Defn: To be, or to cause to be, without profit or benefit. [Obs. or
Archaic] Bale.
