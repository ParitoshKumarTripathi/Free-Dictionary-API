UNFREQUENT
Un*fre"quent (, a. Etym: [Pref. un- not + frequent.]

Defn: Infrequent. J. H. Newman.
 -- Un*fre"quent*ly adv.
UNFREQUENT

=====
Un`fre*quent", v. t. Etym: [1st pref. un- + frequent.]

Defn: To cease to frequent. [Obs.]
They quit their thefts and unfrequent the fields. J. Philips.
