NOVEMBER
No*vem"ber, n. Etym: [L. November, or Novembris (sc. mensis), the
ninth month of the old Roman year, which began with March, fr. novem
nine: cf. F. Novembre. See Nine.]

Defn: The eleventh month of the year, containing thirty days.
