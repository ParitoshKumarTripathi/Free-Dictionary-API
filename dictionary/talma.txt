TALMA
Tal"ma, n.; pl. Talmas. Etym: [Prob. so called from Talma, a French
actor.]
(a) A kind of large cape, or short, full cloak, forming part of the
dress of ladies.
(b) A similar garment worn formerly by gentlemen.
