MAGIAN
Ma"gi*an, a.

Defn: Of or pertaining to the Magi.
MAGIAN

=====
Ma"gi*an, n.

Defn: One of the Magi, or priests of the Zoroastrian religion in
Persia; an adherent of the Zoroastrian religion.
 -- Ma"gi*an*ism, n.
