NUB
Nub, v. t. Etym: [Cf. Knob.]

Defn: To push; to nudge; also, to beckon. [Prov. Eng.]
NUB

=====
Nub, n.

Defn: A jag, or snag; a knob; a protuberance; also, the point or
gist, as of a story. [Colloq.]
