RHAPSODIZE
Rhap"so*dize, v. t. [imp. & p. p. Rhapsodized; p. pr. & vb. n.
Rhapsodizing.]

Defn: To utter as a rhapsody, or in the manner of a rhapsody Sterne.
RHAPSODIZE

Rhap"so*dize, v. i.

Defn: To utter rhapsodies. Jefferson.
