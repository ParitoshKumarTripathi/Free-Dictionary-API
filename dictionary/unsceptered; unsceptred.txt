UNSCEPTERED; UNSCEPTRED
Un*scep"tered, Un*scep"tred, a.

1. Etym: [Pref. un- not + sceptered.]

Defn: Having no scepter.

2. Etym: [1st pref. un- + scepter.]

Defn: Deprived of a scepter.
