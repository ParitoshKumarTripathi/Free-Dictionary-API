TREFLE
Tre"fle, n. Etym: [F. tréfle. See Trefoil.] (Fort.)

Defn: A species of time; -- so called from its resemblance in form to
a trefoil.
TREFLE

=====
Tré`flé", a. Etym: [F. tréflé. See Trefoil.] (Her.)

Defn: Having a three-lobed extremity or extremities, as a cross;
also, more rarely, ornamented with trefoils projecting from the
edges, as a bearing.
