EGGSHELL
Egg"shell`, n.

1. The shell or exterior covering of an egg. Also used figuratively
for anything resembling an eggshell.

2. (Zoöl.)

Defn: A smooth, white, marine, gastropod shell of the genus Ovulum,
resembling an egg in form.
