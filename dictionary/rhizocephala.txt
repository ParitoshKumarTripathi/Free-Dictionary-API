RHIZOCEPHALA
Rhi`zo*ceph"a*la, n. pl. Etym: [NL., fr. Gr. (Zoöl.)

Defn: A division of Pectostraca including saclike parasites of
Crustacea. They adhere by rootlike extensions of the head. See
Illusration in Appendix.
