SQUAMOZYGOMATIC
Squa`mo*zyg`o*mat"ic, a. (Anat.)

Defn: Of or pertaining to both the squamosal and zygomatic bones; --
applied to a bone, or a center of ossification, in some fetal skulls.
 -- n.

Defn: A squamozygomatic bone.
