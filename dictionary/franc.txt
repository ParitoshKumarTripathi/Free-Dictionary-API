FRANC
Franc, n. Etym: [F., fr. franc a Franc. See Frank, a.]

Defn: A silver coin of France, and since 1795 the unit of the French
monetary system. It has been adopted by Belgium and Swizerland. It is
equivalent to about nineteen cents, or ten pence, and is divided into
100 centimes.
