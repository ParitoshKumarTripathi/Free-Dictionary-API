SLOGAN
Slo"gan, n. Etym: [Gael. sluagh-ghairm, i.e., an army cry; sluagh
army + gairm a call, calling.]

Defn: The war cry, or gathering word, of a Highland clan in Scotland;
hence, any rallying cry. Sir W. Scott.
