CLINQUANT
Clin"quant, a. Etym: [F.]

Defn: Glittering; dressed in, or overlaid with, tinsel finery. [Obs.]
Shak.
CLINQUANT

=====
Clin"quant, n.

Defn: Tinse;l; Dutch gold.
