PANEGYRIZE
Pan"e*gy*rize, v. t. [imp. & p. p. Panegyrized; p. pr. & vb. n.
Panegyrizing.] Etym: [Gr. Panegyrist.]

Defn: To praise highly; to extol in a public speech; to write or
deliver a panegyric upon; to eulogize.
PANEGYRIZE

=====
Pan"e*gy*rize, v. i.

Defn: To indulge in panegyrics. Mitford.
