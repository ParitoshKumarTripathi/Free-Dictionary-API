UNBOWEL
Un*bow"el, v. t. [imp. & p. p. Unboweled or Unbowelled; p. pr. & vb.
n. Unboweling or Unbowelling.] Etym: [1st pref. un- + bowel.]

Defn: To deprive of the entrails; to disembowel. Dr. H. More.
