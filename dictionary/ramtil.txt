RAMTIL
Ram"til, n. [Bengali ram-til.]

Defn: A tropical African asteraceous shrub (Guizotia abyssinica)
cultivated for its seeds (called ramtil, or niger, seeds) which yield
a valuable oil used for food and as an illuminant.
