SURVEILLANCE
Sur*veil"lance, n. Etym: [F., fr. surveiller to watch over; sur over
+ veiller to watch, L. vigilare. See Sur-, and Vigil.]

Defn: Oversight; watch; inspection; supervision.
That sort of surveillance of which . . . the young have accused the
old. Sir W. Scott.
