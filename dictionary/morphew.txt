MORPHEW
Mor"phew, n. Etym: [F. morpheé, LL. morphea; cf. It. morfea.]

Defn: A scurfy eruption. [Obs.] Drayton.
MORPHEW

=====
Mor"phew, v. t.

Defn: To cover with a morphew. [Obs.]
