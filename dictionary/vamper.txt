VAMPER
Vamp"er, n.

Defn: One who vamps; one who pieces an old thing with something new;
a cobbler.
VAMPER

=====
Vamp"er, v. i. Etym: [Cf. Vaunt.]

Defn: To swagger; to make an ostentatious show. [Prov. eng. & Scot.]
Jamieson.
