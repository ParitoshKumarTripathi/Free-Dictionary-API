PALMARY
Pal"ma*ry, a. (Anat.)

Defn: Palmar.
PALMARY

=====
Pal"ma*ry, a. Etym: [L. palmarius, palmaris, belonging to palms,
deserving the palm or prize, fr. palma a palm.]

Defn: Worthy of the palm; palmy; preëminent; superior; principal;
chief; as, palmary work. Br. Horne.
