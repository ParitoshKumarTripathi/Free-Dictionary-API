HOGBACK
Hog"back`, n.

1. (Arch.)

Defn: An upward curve or very obtuse angle in the upper surface of
any member, as of a timber laid horizontally; -- the opposite of
camber.

2. (Naut.)

Defn: See Hogframe.

3. (Geol.)

Defn: A ridge formed by tilted strata; hence, any ridge with a sharp
summit, and steeply sloping sides.
