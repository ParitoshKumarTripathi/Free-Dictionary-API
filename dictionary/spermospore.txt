SPERMOSPORE
Sper"mo*spore, n. Etym: [Spermo- + spore.] (Physiol.)

Defn: The male germinal or seminal cell, from the breaking up of
which the spermoblasts are formed and ultimately the spermatozoids; a
spermatospore. Balfour.
