SKUTTERUDITE
Skut"ter*ud*ite, n. Etym: [From Skuttertid, in Norway, whence it is
ibtained.] (Min.)

Defn: A mineral of a bright metallic luster and tin-white to pale
lead-gray color. It consist of arsenic and cobalt.
