OCTAMETER
Oc*tam"e*ter, n. Etym: [Cf.L. octameter in eight feet. See Octa-, and
meter.] (Pros.)

Defn: A verse containing eight feet; as, --
Deep'' in|to'' the | dark''ness | peer''ing, | long'' I | stood''
there | wond'''ring, | fear''ing. Poe.
