VARTABED
Var"ta*bed, n. Etym: [Armen., a doctor, master, preceptor.] (Eccl.)

Defn: A doctor or teacher in the Armenian church. Members of this
order of ecclesiastics frequently have charge of dioceses, with
episcopal functions.
