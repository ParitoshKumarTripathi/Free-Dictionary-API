VITALITY
Vi*tal"i*ty, n. Etym: [L. vitalitas: cf. F. vitalité.]

Defn: The quality or state of being vital; the principle of life;
vital force; animation; as, the vitality of eggs or vegetable seeds;
the vitality of an enterprise.
