POSTCOMMISSURE
Post*com"mis*sure, n. Etym: [Pref. post- + commisure.] (Anat.)

Defn: A transverse commisure in the posterior part of the roof of the
third ventricle of the brain; the posterior cerebral commisure. B. G.
Wilder.
