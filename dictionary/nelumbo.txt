NELUMBO
Ne*lum"bo, n. Etym: [Ceylonese word.] (Bot.)

Defn: A genus of great water lilies. The North American species is
Nelumbo lutea, the Asiatic is the sacred lotus, N. speciosa. [Written
also Nelumbium.]
