REGRESSION
Re*gres"sion (r*grsh"n), n. Etym: [L. regressio: cf. F. régression.]

Defn: The act of passing back or returning; retrogression;
retrogradation. Sir T. Browne. Edge of regression (of a surface)
(Geom.), the line along which a surface turns back upon itself; --
called also a cuspidal edge.
 -- Regression point (Geom.), a cusp.
