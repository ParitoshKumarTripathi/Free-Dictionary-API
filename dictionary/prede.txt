PREDE
Prede, v. i. Etym: [L. praedari. See Prey.]

Defn: To prey; to plunder. [Obs.] Holinshed.
PREDE

=====
Prede, n.

Defn: Prey; plunder; booty. [Obs.] Holinshed.
