HEMISTICH
Hem"i*stich, n. Etym: [L. hemistichium, Gr. "hmisti`chion; "hmi- half
+ sti`chos row, line, verse: cf. F. hémistiche.]

Defn: Half a poetic verse or line, or a verse or line not completed.
