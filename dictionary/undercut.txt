UNDERCUT
Un"der*cut`, n.

Defn: The lower or under side of a sirloin of beef; the fillet.
UNDERCUT

=====
Un`der*cut", v. t.

Defn: To cut away, as the side of an object, so as to leave an
overhanging portion.
