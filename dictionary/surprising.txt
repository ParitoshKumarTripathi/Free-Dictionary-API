SURPRISING
Sur*pris"ing, a.

Defn: Exciting surprise; extraordinary; of a nature to excite wonder
and astonishment; as, surprising bravery; a surprising escape from
danger.
 -- Sur*pris"ing*ly, adv.
 -- Sur*pris"ing*ness, n.

Syn.
 -- Wonderful; extraordinary; unexpected; astonishing; striking.
