CURRYCOMB
Cur"ry*comb` (kr"r-km`), n.

Defn: A kind of card or comb having rows of metallic teeth or
serrated ridges, used in curryng a horse.
CURRYCOMB

=====
Cur"ry*comb`, v. t.

Defn: To comb with a currycomb.
