VAPORIFIC
Vap`o*rif"ic, a. Etym: [L. vapor vapor + facere to make.] (Chem.)

Defn: Producing vapor; tending to pass, or to cause to pass, into
vapor; thus, volatile fluids are vaporific; heat is a vaporific
agent.
