DISHUMOR
Dis*hu"mor, n.

Defn: Ill humor. [Obs.]
DISHUMOR

=====
Dis*hu"mor, v. t.

Defn: To deprive of humor or desire; to put out of humor. [Obs.] B.
Jonson.
