RAVENING
Rav"en*ing, n.

Defn: Eagerness for plunder; rapacity; extortion. Luke xi. 39.
RAVENING

=====
Rav"en*ing, a.

Defn: Greedily devouring; rapacious; as, ravening wolves.
 -- Rav"en*ing*ly, adv.
