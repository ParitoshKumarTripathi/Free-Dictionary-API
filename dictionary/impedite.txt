IMPEDITE
Im"pe*dite, a. Etym: [L. impeditus, p. p. See Impede.]

Defn: Hindered; obstructed. [R.] Jer. Taylor.
IMPEDITE

=====
Im"pe*dite, v. t.

Defn: To impede. [Obs.] Boyle.
