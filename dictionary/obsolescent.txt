OBSOLESCENT
Ob`so*les"cent, a. Etym: [L. obsolescens, -entis, p.pr. of
obsolescere, to wear out gradually, to fall into disuse; ob (see Ob-)
+ solere to use, be wont.]

Defn: Going out of use; becoming obsolete; passing into desuetude.
