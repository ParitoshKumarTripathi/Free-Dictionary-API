UNCONFORMABLE
Un`con*form"a*ble, a.

1. Not conformable; not agreeable; not conforming.
Moral evil is an action unconformable to it [the rule of our duty].
I. Watts.

2. (Geol.)

Defn: Not conformable; not lying in a parallel position; as,
unconformable strata.
 -- Un`con*form"a*ble*ness, n.
 -- Un`con*form"a*bly, adv.
