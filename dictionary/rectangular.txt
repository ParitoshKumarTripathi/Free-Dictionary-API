RECTANGULAR
Rec*tan"gu*lar (rk*tn"g*lr), a. Etym: [CF. F. rectangulaire.]

Defn: Right-angled; having one or more angles of ninety degrees.
 -- Rec*tan"gu*lar*ly (r, adv.
 -- Rec*tan"gu*lar*ness, n.
