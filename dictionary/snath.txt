SNATH
Snath, n. Etym: [Cf. AS. sni to cut, to mow, sn a bite, bit snip.]

Defn: The handle of a scythe; a snead. [Variously written in England
snead, sneed, sneath, sneeth, snathe, etc.; in Scotland written
sned.]
