DEDICATORY
Ded"i*ca*to*ry, a. Etym: [Cf. F. dédicatoire.]

Defn: Constituting or serving as a dedication; complimental. "An
epistle dedicatory." Dryden.
DEDICATORY

Defn: Dedication. [R.] Milton.
