WELDER
Weld"er, n.

Defn: One who welds, or unites pieces of iron, etc., by welding.
WELDER

=====
Weld"er, n.

1. One who welds, or wields. [Obs.]

2. A manager; an actual occupant. [Ireland. Obs.] "The welder . . .
who . . . lives miserably." Swift.
