TING
Ting, n. Etym: [An imitative word. Cf. Tink.]

Defn: A sharp sound, as of a bell; a tinkling.
TING

=====
Ting, v. i.

Defn: To sound or ring, as a bell; to tinkle. [R.] Holland.
TING

=====
Ting, n.

Defn: The apartment in a Chinese temple where the idol is kept.
