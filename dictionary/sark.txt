SARK
Sark, n. Etym: [AS. serce, syrce, ashirt; akin to Icel. serkr, Sw.
särk.]

Defn: A shirt. [Scot.]
SARK

=====
Sark, v. t. (Carp.)

Defn: To cover with sarking, or thin boards.
