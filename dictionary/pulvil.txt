PULVIL
Pul"vil, n. Etym: [It. polviglio, fr. L. pulvis, pulveris, dust,
powder: cf. Sp. polvillo.]

Defn: A sweet-scented powder; pulvillio. [Written also pulville.]
[Obs.] Gay.
PULVIL

=====
Pul"vil, v. t.

Defn: To apply pulvil to. [Obs.] Congreve.
