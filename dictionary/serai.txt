SERAI
Se*ra"i, n. Etym: [Per. sera\'8b, or saraï, a palace, a king's court,
a seraglio, an inn. Cf. Caravansary.]

Defn: A palace; a seraglio; also, in the East, a place for the
accommodation of travelers; a caravansary, or rest house.
