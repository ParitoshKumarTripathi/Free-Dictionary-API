SCIOTHERIC
Sci`o*ther"ic, a. Etym: [Cf. L. sciothericon Sciatheric.]

Defn: Of or pertaining to a sundial. Sciotheric telescope (Dialing),
an instrument consisting of a horizontal dial, with a telescope
attached to it, used for determining the time, whether of day or
night.
