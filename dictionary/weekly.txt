WEEKLY
Week"ly, a.

1. Of or pertaining to a week, or week days; as, weekly labor.

2. Coming, happening, or done once a week; hebdomadary; as, a weekly
payment; a weekly gazette.
WEEKLY

=====
Week"ly, n.; pl. Weeklies (.

Defn: A publication issued once in seven days, or appearing once a
week.
WEEKLY

=====
Week"ly, adv.

Defn: Once a week; by hebdomadal periods; as, each performs service
weekly.
