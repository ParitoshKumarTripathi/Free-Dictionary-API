THORITE
Tho"rite, n. Etym: [So called by Berzelius from the Scandinavian god
Thor. See Thor.] (Min.)

Defn: A mineral of a brown to black color, or, as in the variety
orangite, orange-yellow. It is essentially a silicate of thorium.
