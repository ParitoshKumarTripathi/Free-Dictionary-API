VENERY
Ven"er*y, n. Etym: [L. Venus, Veneris, the goddess of love.]

Defn: Sexual love; sexual intercourse; coition.
Contentment, without the pleasure of lawful venery, is continence; of
unlawful, chastity. Grew.
