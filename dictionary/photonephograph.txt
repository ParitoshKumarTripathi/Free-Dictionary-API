PHOTONEPHOGRAPH
Pho`to*neph"o*graph (-nef"o*graf), n.  [Photo- + Gr. ne`fos a cloud +
-graph.] (Meteor.)

Defn: A nephoscope registering by photography, commonly consisting of
a pair of cameras used simultaneously.
