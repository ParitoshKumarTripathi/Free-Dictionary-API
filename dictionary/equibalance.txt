EQUIBALANCE
E`qui*bal"ance, n. Etym: [Equi- + balance.]

Defn: Equal weight; equiponderance.
EQUIBALANCE

=====
E`qui*bal"ance, v. t. [imp. & p. p. Equibalanced; p. pr. & vb. n.
Equibalancing.]

Defn: To make of equal weight; to balance equally; to counterbalance;
to equiponderate.
