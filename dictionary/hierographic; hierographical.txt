HIEROGRAPHIC; HIEROGRAPHICAL
Hi`er*o*graph"ic, Hi`er*o*graph"ic*al, a. Etym: [L. hierographicus,
Gr. hiérographique.]

Defn: Of or pertaining to sacred writing.
