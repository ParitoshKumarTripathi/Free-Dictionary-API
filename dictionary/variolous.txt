VARIOLOUS
Va*ri"o*lous, a. Etym: [LL. variolosus, fr. variola the smallpox: cf.
F. varioleux.] (Med.)

Defn: Of or pertaining to the smallpox; having pits, or sunken
impressions, like those of the smallpox; variolar; variolic.
