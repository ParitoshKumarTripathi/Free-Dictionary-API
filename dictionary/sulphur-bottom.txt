SULPHUR-BOTTOM
Sul`phur-bot"tom, n. (Zoöl.)

Defn: A very large whalebone whale of the genus Sibbaldius, having a
yellowish belly; especially, S. sulfureus of the North Pacific, and
S. borealis of the North Atlantic; -- called also sulphur whale.
