GRYPE
Grype, v. t.

Defn: To gripe. [Obs.] See Gripe. Spenser.
GRYPE

=====
Grype, n. Etym: [Gr. gry`f, grypo`s, griffin. See Griffin.] (Zoöl.)

Defn: A vulture; the griffin. [Written also gripe.] [Obs.]
