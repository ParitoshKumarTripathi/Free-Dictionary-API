BRABBLE
Brab"ble, v. i. Etym: [D. brabbelen to talk confusedly. Blab,
Babble.]

Defn: To clamor; to contest noisily. [R.]
BRABBLE

=====
Brab"ble, n.

Defn: A broil; a noisy contest; a wrangle.
This petty brabble will undo us all. Shak.
