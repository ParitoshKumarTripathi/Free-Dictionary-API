RUNG
Rung,

Defn: imp. & p. p. of Ring.
RUNG

=====
Rung, n. Etym: [OE. ronge, AS. hrung, a staff, rod, pole; akin to G.
runge a short, thick piece of iron or wood, OD. ronghe a prop,
support, Icel. röng a rib in a ship, Goth. Hrugga a staff.]

1. (Shipbuilding)

Defn: A floor timber in a ship.

2. One of the rounds of a ladder.

3. One of the stakes of a cart; a spar; a heavy staff.

4. (Mach.)

Defn: One of the radial handles projecting from the rim of a steering
wheel; also, one of the pins or trundles of a lantern wheel.
