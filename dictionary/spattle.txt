SPATTLE
Spat"tle, n.

Defn: Spawl; spittle. [Obs.] Bale.
SPATTLE

=====
Spat"tle, n.

1. A spatula.

2. (Pottery)

Defn: A tool or implement for mottling a molded article with coloring
matter Knoght.
