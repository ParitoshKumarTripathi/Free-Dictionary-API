SWALLOWWORT
Swal"low*wort`, n. (Bot.)
(a) See Celandine.
(b) A poisonous plant (Vincetoxicum officinale) of the Milkweed
family, at one time used in medicine; -- also called white
swallowwort. African swallowwort, a plant of the genus Stapelia.
