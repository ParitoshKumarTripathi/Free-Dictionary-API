HADROSAURUS
Had`ro*sau"rus, n. Etym: [NL., fr. Gr. "adro`s thick + say^ros
lizard.] (Paleon.)

Defn: An American herbivorous dinosaur of great size, allied to the
iguanodon. It is found in the Cretaceous formation.
