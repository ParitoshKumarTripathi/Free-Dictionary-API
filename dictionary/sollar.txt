SOLLAR
Sol"lar, n.

1. See Solar, n. [Obs.]

2. (Mining)

Defn: A platform in a shaft, especially one of those between the
series of ladders in a shaft.
SOLLAR

Sol"lar, v. t.

Defn: To cover, or provide with, a sollar.
