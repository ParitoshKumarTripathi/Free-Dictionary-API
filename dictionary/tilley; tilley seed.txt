TILLEY; TILLEY SEED
Til"ley, n., or Til"ley seed`. (Bot.)

Defn: The seeds of a small tree (Croton Pavana) common in the Malay
Archipelago. These seeds furnish croton oil, like those of Croton
Tiglium. [Written also tilly.]
