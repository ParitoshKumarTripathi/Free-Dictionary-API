EXTRATERRITORIAL
Ex`tra*ter`ri*to"ri*al, a.

Defn: Beyond the limits of a territory or particular jurisdiction;
exterritorial.
 -- Ex`tra*ter`ri*to"ri*al*ly(#), adv.
