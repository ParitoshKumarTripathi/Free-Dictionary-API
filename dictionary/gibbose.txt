GIBBOSE
Gib*bose", a. Etym: [L. gibbosus, fr. gibbus, gibba, hunch, hump. Cf.
Gibbous.]

Defn: Humped; protuberant; -- said of a surface which presents one or
more large elevations. Brande & C.
