PAXILLUS
Pax*il"lus, n.; pl. Paxilli. Etym: [L., a peg.] (Zoöl.)

Defn: One of a peculiar kind of spines covering the surface of
certain starfishes. They are pillarlike, with a flattened summit
which is covered with minute spinules or granules. See Illustration
in Appendix.
