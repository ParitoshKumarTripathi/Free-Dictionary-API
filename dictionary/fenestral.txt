FENESTRAL
Fe*nes"tral, a. Etym: [L. fenestra a window.]

1. (Arch.)

Defn: Pertaining to a window or to windows.

2. (Anat.)

Defn: Of or pertaining to a fenestra.
FENESTRAL

=====
Fe*nes"tral, n. (Arch.)

Defn: A casement or window sash, closed with cloth or paper instead
of glass. Weale.
