NECROSIS
Ne*cro"sis, n. Etym: [NL., fr. gr.

1. (med.)

Defn: Mortification or gangrene of bone, or the death of a bone or
portion of a bone in mass, as opposed to its death by molecular
disintegration. See Caries.

2. (Bot.)

Defn: A disease of trees, in which the branches gradually dry up from
the bark to the center.
