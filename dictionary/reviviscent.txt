REVIVISCENT
Rev`i*vis"cent, a. Etym: [L. reviviscens, p. pr. ofreviviscere to
revive; pref. re- re- + viviscere, v. incho. fr. vivere to live.]

Defn: Able or disposed to revive; reviving. E. Darwin.
