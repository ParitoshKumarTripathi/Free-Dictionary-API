NATHLESS
Nath"less, adv. Etym: [OE. natheles, na the les, not the less, AS. na
never. See Na, The, conj., and cf. Nevertheless.]

Defn: Nevertheless. [Archaic] Chaucer. Milton. E. Arnold.
