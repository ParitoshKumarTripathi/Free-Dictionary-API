PSEUDOPOD
Pseu"do*pod, n. Etym: [Pseudo- + -pod.]

1. (Biol.)

Defn: Any protoplasmic filament or irregular process projecting from
any unicellular organism, or from any animal or plant call.

2. (Zoöl.)

Defn: A rhizopod.
