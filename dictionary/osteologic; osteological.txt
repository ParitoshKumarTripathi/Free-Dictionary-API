OSTEOLOGIC; OSTEOLOGICAL
Os`te*o*log"ic, Os`te*o*log"ic*al, a. Etym: [Cf. F. ostéologique.]

Defn: Of or pertaining to osteology.
 -- Os`te*o*log"ic*al*ly, adv.
