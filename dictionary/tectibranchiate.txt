TECTIBRANCHIATE
Tec`ti*bran"chi*ate, a. Etym: [L. tectus (p.p. of tegere to cover) +
E. branchiate.] (Zoöl.)

Defn: Having the gills covered by the mantle; of or pertaining to the
Tectibranchiata.
 -- n.

Defn: A tectibranchiate mollusk.
