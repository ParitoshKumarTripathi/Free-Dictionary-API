PRIMIPILAR
Pri*mip"i*lar, a. Etym: [L. primipilaris, fr. primipilus the
centurion of the first cohort of a Roman legion, fr. primus pilus the
division made up of the triarii in the Roman army.]

Defn: Of or pertaining to the captain of the vanguard of a Roman
army. Barrow.
