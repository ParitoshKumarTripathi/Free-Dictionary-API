SMACKING
Smack"ing, n.

Defn: A sharp, quick noise; a smack.
Like the faint smacking of an after kiss. Dryden.
SMACKING

=====
Smack"ing, a.

Defn: Making a sharp, brisk sound; hence, brisk; as, a smacking
breeze.
