HYDROCELE
Hy`dro*cele, n. Etym: [L., fr. Gr. "y`dwr water + (Med.)

Defn: A collection of serous fluid in the areolar texture of the
scrotum or in the coverings, especially in the serous sac, investing
the testicle or the spermatic cord; dropsy of the testicle.
