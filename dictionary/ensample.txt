ENSAMPLE
En*sam"ple, n. Etym: [OF. ensample, essample, F. exemple. See
Example.]

Defn: An example; a pattern or model for imitation. [Obs.] Tyndale.
Being ensamples to the flock.
ENSAMPLE

=====
En*sam"ple, v. t.

Defn: To exemplify, to show by example. [Obs.] Spenser.
