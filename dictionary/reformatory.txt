Re*form"a*to*ry (-t*r), a.

REFORMATORY

=====
Re*form"a*to*ry, n.; pl. -ries (-r.

Defn: An institution for promoting the reformation of offenders.
Magistrates may send juvenile offenders to reformatories instead of
to prisons. Eng. Cyc.
