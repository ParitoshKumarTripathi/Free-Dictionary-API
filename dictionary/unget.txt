UNGET
Un*get", v. t. Etym: [1st pref. un- + get.]

Defn: To cause to be unbegotten or unborn, or as if unbegotten or
unborn. [R.]
I 'll disown you, I 'll disinherit you, I 'll unget you. Sheridan.
