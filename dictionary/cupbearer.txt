CUPBEARER
Cup"bear`er (-br`r), n.

1. One whose office it is to fill and hand the cups at an
enterainment.

2. (Antiq.)

Defn: One of the attendants of a prince or noble, permanently charged
with the performance of this office for his master. "I was the king's
cupbearer." Neh. i. 11.
