MANNERLY
Man"ner*ly, a.

Defn: Showing good manners; civil; respectful; complaisant.
What thou thinkest meet, and is most mannerly. Shak.
MANNERLY

=====
Man"ner*ly, adv.

Defn: With good manners. Shak.
