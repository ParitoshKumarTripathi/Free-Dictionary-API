PASQUINADE
Pas`quin*ade", n. Etym: [F. pasquinade, It. pasquinata.]

Defn: A lampoon or satirical writing. Macaulay.
PASQUINADE

=====
Pas`quin*ade", v. t.

Defn: To lampoon, to satirize.
