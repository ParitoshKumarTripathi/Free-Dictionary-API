RECOMMENDABLE
Rec`om*mend"a*ble (-*b'l), a. Etym: [Cf. F. recommandable.]

Defn: Suitable to be recommended; worthy of praise; commendable.
Glanvill.
 -- Rec`om*mend"a*ble*ness, n.
 -- Rec`om*mend"a*bly, adv.
