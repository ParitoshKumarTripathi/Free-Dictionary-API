SURVIEW
Sur*view", v. t. Etym: [Pref. sur- + view. Cf. Survey.]

Defn: To survey; to make a survey of. [Obs.] "To surview his ground."
Spenser.
SURVIEW

=====
Sur*view", n.

Defn: A survey. [Obs.] Bp. Sanderson.
