PERRY
Per"ry, n. Etym: [OF. peré, F. poiré, fr. poire a pear, L. pirum. See
Pear the fruit.]

Defn: A fermented liquor made from pears; pear cider. Mortimer.
PERRY

=====
Per"ry, n.

Defn: A suddent squall. See Pirry. [Obs.]
