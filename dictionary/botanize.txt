BOTANIZE
Bot"a*nize, v. i. [imp. & p. p. Botanized; p. pr. & vb. n.
Botanizing.] Etym: [Cf. F. botaniser.]

Defn: To seek after plants for botanical investigation; to study
plants.
BOTANIZE

=====
Bot"a*nize, v. t.

Defn: To explore for botanical purposes.
