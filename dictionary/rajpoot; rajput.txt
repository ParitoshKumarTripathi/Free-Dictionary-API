RAJPOOT; RAJPUT
Raj`poot", Raj`put", n. Etym: [Hind. raj-put, Skr. raja-putra king's
son.]

Defn: A Hindoo of the second, or royal and military, caste; a
Kshatriya; especially, an inhabitant of the country of Rajpootana, in
northern central India.
