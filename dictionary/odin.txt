ODIN
O"din, n. Etym: [Icel. wood, a. See Wednesday.] (Northern Myth.)

Defn: The supreme deity of the Scandinavians; -- the same as Woden,
of the German tribes.
There in the Temple, carved in wood, The image of great Odin stood.
Longfellow.
