BUFFLE
Buf"fle, n. Etym: [OE., from F. buffle. See Buffalo.]

Defn: The buffalo. [Obs.] Sir T. Herbert.
BUFFLE

=====
Buf"fle, v. i.

Defn: To puzzle; to be at a loss. [Obs.] Swift.
