CHIPPY
Chip"py, a.

Defn: Abounding in, or resembling, chips; dry and tasteless.
CHIPPY

=====
Chip"py, n. (Zoöl.)

Defn: A small American sparrow (Spizella socialis), very common near
dwelling; -- also called chipping bird and chipping sparrow, from its
simple note.
