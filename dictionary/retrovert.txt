RETROVERT
Re"tro*vert, v. t. [imp. & p. p. Retroverted; p. pr. & vb. n.
Retroverting.] Etym: [Pref. retro- + L. vertere, versum, to turn. Cf.
Retrorse.]

Defn: To turn back.
