TORQUED
Torqued, a. Etym: [L. torquere to twist, to turn, to wind.]

1. Wreathed; twisted. [R.]

2. (Her.)

Defn: Twisted; bent; -- said of a dolphin haurient, which forms a
figure like the letter S.
