PECTOSE
Pec"tose`, n. Etym: [Pectic + cellulose.] (Chem.)

Defn: An amorphous carbohydrate found in the vegetable kingdom, esp.
in unripe fruits. It is associated with cellulose, and is converted
into substances of the pectin group.
