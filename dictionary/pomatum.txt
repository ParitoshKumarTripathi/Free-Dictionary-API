POMATUM
Po*ma"tum, n. Etym: [See Pomade.]

Defn: A perfumed unguent or composition, chiefly used in dressing the
hair; pomade. Wiseman.
POMATUM

=====
Po*ma"tum, v. t.

Defn: To dress with pomatum.
