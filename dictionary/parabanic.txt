PARABANIC
Par`a*ban"ic, a. Etym: [Gr. (Chem.)

Defn: Pertaining to, or designating, a nitrogenous acid which is
obtained by the oxidation of uric acid, as a white crystalline
substance (C3N2H2O3); -- also called oxalyl urea.
