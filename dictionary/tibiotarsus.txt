TIBIOTARSUS
Tib`i*o*tar"sus, n.; pl. Tibiotarsi (. (Anat.)

Defn: The large bone between the femur and tarsometatarsus in the leg
of a bird. It is formed by the union of the proximal part of the
tarsus with the tibia.
