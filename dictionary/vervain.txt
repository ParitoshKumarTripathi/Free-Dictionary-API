VERVAIN
Ver"vain, n. Etym: [OE. verveine, F. verveine, fr. L. verbena, pl.
verbenae sacred boughs of laurel, olive, or myrtle, a class of
plants; cf. verbenaca vervain. Cf. Verbena.] (Bot.)

Defn: Any plant of the genus Verbena. Vervain mallow (Bot.), a
species of mallow (Malva Alcea) with rose-colored flowers.
