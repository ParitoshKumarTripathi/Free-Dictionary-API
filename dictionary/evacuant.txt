EVACUANT
E*vac"u*ant, a. Etym: [L.evacuans, -antis, p. pr. of evacuare: cf. F.
évacuant.]

Defn: Emptying; evacuative; purgative; cathartic.
 -- n. (Med.)

Defn: A purgative or cathartic.
