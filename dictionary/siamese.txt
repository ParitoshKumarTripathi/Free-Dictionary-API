SIAMESE
Si`a*mese", a.

Defn: Of or pertaining to Siam, its native people, or their language.
SIAMESE

=====
Si`a*mese`, n. sing. & pl.

1. A native or inhabitant of Siam; pl., the people of Siam.

2. sing.

Defn: The language of the Siamese.
