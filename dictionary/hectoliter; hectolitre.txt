HECTOLITER; HECTOLITRE
Hec"to*li`ter, Hec"to*li`tre, n. Etym: [F. hectolitre, fr. Gr. litre
a liter.]

Defn: A measure of liquids, containing a hundred liters; equal to a
tenth of a cubic meter, nearly 26
