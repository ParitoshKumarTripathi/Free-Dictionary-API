THOMSEN'S DISEASE
Thom"sen's dis*ease". Etym: [From Thomsen, a physician of Sleswick.]
(Med.)

Defn: An affection apparently congenital, consisting in tonic
contraction and stiffness of the voluntary muscles occurring after a
period of muscular inaction.
