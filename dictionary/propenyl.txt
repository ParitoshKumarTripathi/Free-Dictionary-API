PROPENYL
Pro"pe*nyl, n. Etym: [Propene + -yl.] (Chem.)

Defn: A hypothetical hydrocarbon radical, C3H5, isomeric with allyl
and glyceryl, and regarded as the essential residue of glycerin. Cf.
Allyl, and Glyceryl.
