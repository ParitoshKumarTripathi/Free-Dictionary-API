GLUTINATE
Glu"ti*nate, v. t. [imp. & p. p. Glutinated; p. pr. & vb. n.
Glutinating.] Etym: [L. glutinatus, p. p. of glutinare to glue, fr.
gluten glue.]

Defn: To unite with glue; to cement; to stick together. Bailey.
