WARRIOR
War"rior, n. Etym: [OE. werreour, OF. werreour, guerreor, from
guerre, werre, war. See War, and Warray.]

Defn: A man engaged or experienced in war, or in the military life; a
soldier; a champion.
Warriors old with ordered spear and shield. Milton.
Warrior ant (Zoöl.), a reddish ant (Formica sanguinea) native of
Europe and America. It is one of the species which move in armies to
capture and enslave other ants.
