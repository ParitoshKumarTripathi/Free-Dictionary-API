NOY
Noy, v. t. Etym: [See Annoy.]

Defn: To annoy; to vex. [Obs. or Prov. Eng.] Piers Plowman.
All that noyed his heavy spright. Spenser.
NOY

=====
Noy, n.

Defn: That which annoys. [Obs.] Piers Plowman.
