MINGLE-MANGLE
Min"gle-man`gle, v. t. Etym: [Reduplicated fr. mingle.]

Defn: To mix in a disorderly way; to make a mess of. [Obs.] Udall.
MINGLE-MANGLE

=====
Min"gle-man`gle, n.

Defn: A hotchpotch. [Obs.] Latimer.
