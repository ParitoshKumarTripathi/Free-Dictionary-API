SEPTILLION
Sep*til"lion, n. Etym: [F. septilion, formed fr. L. septem seven, in
imitation of million.]

Defn: According to the French method of numeration (which is followed
also in the United States), the number expressed by a unit with
twenty-four ciphers annexed. According to the English method, the
number expressed by a unit with forty-two ciphers annexed. See
Numeration.
