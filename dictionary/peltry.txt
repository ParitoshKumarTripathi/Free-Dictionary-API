PELTRY
Pelt"ry, n. Etym: [F. pelleterie peltry, furriery, fr. pelletier a
furrier, fr. OF. pel skin, F. peau, L. pelis. See Pelt a skin, Pell,
n., Fell a skin.]

Defn: Pelts or skins, collectively; skins with the fur on them; furs.
