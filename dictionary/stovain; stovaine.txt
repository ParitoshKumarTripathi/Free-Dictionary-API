STOVAIN; STOVAINE
Sto"va*in, n. Also -ine. [Stove (a translation of the name of the
discoverer, Fourneau + -in, -ine.] (Pharm.)

Defn: A substance, C14H22O2NCl, the hydrochloride of an amino
compound containing benzol, used, in solution with strychnine, as a
local anæsthetic, esp. by injection into the sheath of the spinal
cord, producing anæsthesia below the point of introduction.
