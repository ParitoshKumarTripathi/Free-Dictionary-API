WENTLETRAP
Wen"tle*trap`, n. Etym: [D. wenteltrap a winding staircase; cf. G.
wendeltreppe.] [Obs.]

Defn: Any one of numerous species of elegant, usually white, marine
shells of the genus Scalaria, especially Scalaria pretiosa, which was
formerly highly valued; -- called also staircase shell. See Scalaria.
