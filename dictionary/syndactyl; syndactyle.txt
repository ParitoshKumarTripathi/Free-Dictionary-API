SYNDACTYL; SYNDACTYLE
Syn*dac"tyl, Syn*dac"tyle, a.  [Syn- + Gr.  finger, toe.] (Zoöl. &
Med.)

Defn: Having two or more digits wholly or partly united. See
Syndactylism.
