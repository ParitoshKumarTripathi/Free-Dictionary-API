TODDLE
Tod"dle, v. i. [imp. & p. p. Toddled; p. pr. & vb. n. Toddling.]
Etym: [Akin to tottle, totter.]

Defn: To walk with short, tottering steps, as a child.
TODDLE

=====
Tod"dle, n.

Defn: A toddling walk. Trollope.
