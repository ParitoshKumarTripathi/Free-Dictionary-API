TESTING
Test"ing, n.

1. The act of testing or proving; trial; proof.

2. (Metal.)

Defn: The operation of refining gold or silver in a test, or cupel;
cupellation. Testing machine (Engin.), a machine used in the
determination of the strength of materials, as iron, stone, etc., and
their behavior under strains of various kinds, as elongation,
bending, crushing, etc.
