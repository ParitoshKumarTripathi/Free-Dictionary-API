REASTY
Reas"ty, a. Etym: [Etymol. uncertain.]

Defn: Rusty and rancid; -- applied to salt meat. [Obs. or Prov. Eng.]
Tusser.
 -- Reas"ti*ness, n. [Obs. or Prov. Eng.]
