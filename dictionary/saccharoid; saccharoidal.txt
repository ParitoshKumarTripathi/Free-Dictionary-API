SACCHAROID; SACCHAROIDAL
Sac"cha*roid, Sac`cha*roid"al, a. Etym: [L. saccharon sugar + -oid:
cf. F. saccharoïde.]

Defn: resembling sugar, as in taste, appearance, consistency, or
composition; as, saccharoidal limestone.
