RECALCITRATE
Re*cal"ci*trate, v. t.

Defn: To kick against; to show repugnance to; to rebuff.
The more heartily did one disdain his disdain, and recalcitrate his
tricks. De Quincey.
RECALCITRATE

=====
Re*cal"ci*trate, v. i.

Defn: To kick back; to kick against anything; hence, to express
repugnance or opposition.
