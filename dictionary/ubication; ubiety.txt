UBICATION; UBIETY
U`bi*ca"tion, U*bi"e*ty, n. Etym: [NL. ubicatio, ubietas, fr. L. ubi
where.]

Defn: The quality or state of being in a place; local relation;
position or location; whereness. [R.] Glanvill.
