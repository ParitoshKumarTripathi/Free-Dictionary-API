SOUTHEAST
South`east" (; by sailors sou"-), n.

Defn: The point of the compass equally distant from the south and the
east; the southeast part or region.
SOUTHEAST

=====
South`east (; by sailors sou"-), a.

Defn: Of or pertaining to the southeast; proceeding toward, or coming
from, the southeast; as, a southeast course; a southeast wind.
