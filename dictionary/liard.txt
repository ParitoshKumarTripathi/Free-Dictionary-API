LIARD
Li"ard, a. Etym: [OF. liart, LL. liardus gray, dappie.]

Defn: Gray. [Obs.] Chaucer.

Note: Used by Chaucer as an epithet of a gray or dapple gray horse.
Also used as a name for such a horse.
LIARD

=====
Liard, n. Etym: [F.]

Defn: A French copper coin of one fourth the value of a sou.
