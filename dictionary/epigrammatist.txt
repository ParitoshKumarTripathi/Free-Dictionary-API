EPIGRAMMATIST
Ep`i*gram"ma*tist, n. Etym: [L. epigrammatista: cf. F.
épigrammatiste.]

Defn: One who composes epigrams, or makes use of them.
The brisk epigrammatist showing off his own cleverness. Holmes.
