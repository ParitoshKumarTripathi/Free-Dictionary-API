URINOSE; URINOUS
U"ri*nose, U"ri*nous, a. Etym: [Cf. F. urineux. See Urine.]

Defn: Of or pertaining to urine, or partaking of its qualities;
having the character or odor of urine; similar to urine. Arbuthnot.
