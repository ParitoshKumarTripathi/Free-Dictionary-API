PRIMULACEOUS
Prim`u*la"ceous, a. (Bot.)

Defn: Of or pertaining to an order of herbaceous plants (Primulaceæ),
of which the primrose is the type, and the pimpernel, the cyclamen,
and the water violet are other examples.
