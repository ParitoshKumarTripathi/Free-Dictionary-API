TAEL
Tael, n. Etym: [Malay ta, a certain weight, probably fr. Hind. tola,
Skr. tula a balance, weight, tul to weigh.]

Defn: A denomination of money, in China, worth nearly six shillings
sterling, or about a dollar and forty cents; also, a weight of one
ounce and a third. [Written also tale.]
