SFORZANDO; SFORZATO
Sfor*zan"do, Sfor*za"to, a. Etym: [It. sforzando, p. pr., and
sforzato, p. p. of sforzare to force.] (Mus.)

Defn: Forcing or forced; -- a direction placed over a note, to
signify that it must be executed with peculiar emphasis and force; --
marked fz (an abbreviation of forzando), sf, sfz, or
