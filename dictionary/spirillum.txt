SPIRILLUM
Spi*ril"lum, n. Etym: [NL., dim. of L. spira a coil.] (Biol.)

Defn: A genus of common motile microörganisms (Spirobacteria) having
the form of spiral-shaped filaments. One species is said to be the
cause of relapsing fever.
