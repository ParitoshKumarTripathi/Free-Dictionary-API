YESTERNIGHT
Yes"ter*night`, n.

Defn: The last night; the night last past.
YESTERNIGHT

=====
Yes"ter*night`, adv. Etym: [AS. gystran niht. See Yesterday.]

Defn: On the last night. B. Jonson.
