RESTORATIVE
Re*stor"a*tive (r*str"*tv), a. Etym: [Cf. F. restoratif.]

Defn: Of or pertaining to restoration; having power to restore.
Destroys life's enemy, Hunger, with sweet restorative delight.
Milton.
RESTORATIVE

=====
Re*stor"a*tive, n.

Defn: Something which serves to restore; especially, a restorative
medicine. Arbuthnot.
