STASIMON
Stas"i*mon, n.; pl. Stasmia. Etym: [NL., from Gr. sta`simon, neut. of
sta`simos stationary, steadfast.]

Defn: In the Greek tragedy, a song of the chorus, continued without
the interruption of dialogue or anapæstics. Liddell & Scott.
