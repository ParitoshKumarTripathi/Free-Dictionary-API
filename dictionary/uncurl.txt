UNCURL
Un*curl", v. t. Etym: [1st pref. un- + curl.]

Defn: To loose from curls, or ringlets; to straighten out, as
anything curled or curly.
He sheaths his paw, uncurls his angry mane. Dryden.
UNCURL

=====
Un*curl", v. i.

Defn: To become uncurled, or straight.
