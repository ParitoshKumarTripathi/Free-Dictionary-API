PARACENTESIS
Par`a*cen*te"sis, n. Etym: [L., fr. Gr. (Med.)

Defn: The perforation of a cavity of the body with a trocar,
aspirator, or other suitable instrument, for the evacuation of
effused fluid, pus, or gas; tapping.
