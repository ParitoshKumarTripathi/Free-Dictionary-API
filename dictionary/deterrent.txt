DETERRENT
De*ter"rent, a. Etym: [L. deterrens, p. pr. of deterrere. See Deter.]

Defn: Serving to deter. "The deterrent principle." E. Davis.
DETERRENT

=====
De*ter"rent, n.

Defn: That which deters or prevents.
