PYTHAGORISM
Py*thag"o*rism, n. Etym: [Gr.

Defn: The doctrines taught by Pythagoras.

Note: Pythagoras made numbers the basis of his philosophical system,
as well physical as metaphysical. The doctrine of the transmigration
of souls (metempsychosis) is associated closely with name of
Pythagoras.
