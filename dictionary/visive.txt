VISIVE
Vi"sive, a. Etym: [Cf. F. visif, LL. visivus. See Vision.]

Defn: Of or pertaining to the sight; visual. [Obs.]
I can not satisfy myself how men should be so little surprised about
this visive faculty. Berkeley.
