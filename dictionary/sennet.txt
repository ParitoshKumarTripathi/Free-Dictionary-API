SENNET
Sen"net, n. Etym: [Properly, a sign given for the entrance or exit of
actors, from OF. sinet, signet, dim. of signe. See Signet.]

Defn: A signal call on a trumpet or cornet for entrance or exit on
the stage. [Obs.]
SENNET

=====
Sen"net, n. (Zoöl.)

Defn: The barracuda.
