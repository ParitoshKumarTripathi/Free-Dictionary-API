SUPPORTABLE
Sup*port"a*ble, a. Etym: [Cf. F. supportable.]

Defn: Capable of being supported, maintained, or endured; endurable.
 -- Sup*port"a*ble*ness, n.
 -- Sup*port"a*bly, adv.
