HAIN
Hain, v. t. Etym: [Cf. Sw. h채gn hedge, inclosure, Dan. hegn hedge,
fence. See Hedge.]

Defn: To inclose for mowing; to set aside for grass. "A ground . . .
hained in." Holland.
