MODER
Mo"der, n. Etym: [OE. See Mother female parent.]

1. A mother. [Obs.] Chaucer.

2. The principal piece of an astrolabe, into which the others are
fixed. [Obs.]
MODER

=====
Mod"er, v. t.

Defn: To moderate. [Obs.]
