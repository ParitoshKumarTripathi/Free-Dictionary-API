PORRINGER
Por"rin*ger, n. Etym: [OE. pottanger, for pottager; cf. F. potager a
soup basin. See Porridge.]

Defn: A porridge dish; esp., a bowl or cup from which children eat or
are fed; as, a silver porringer. Wordsworth.
