ERUBESCENCE; ERUBESCENCY
Er`u*bes"cence, Er`u*bes"cen*cy, n. Etym: [L. erubescentia: cf. F.
érubescence.]

Defn: The act of becoming red; redness of the skin or surface of
anything; a blushing.
