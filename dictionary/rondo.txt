RONDO

1. (Mus.)

Defn: A composition, vocal or instrumental, commonly of a lively,
cheerful character, in which the first strain recurs after each of
the other strains. "The Rondo-form was the earliest and most frequent
definite mold for musical construction." Grove.

2. (Poetry)

Defn: See Rondeau, 1.
