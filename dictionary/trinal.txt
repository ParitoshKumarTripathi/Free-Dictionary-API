TRINAL
Tri"nal, a. Etym: [L. trinus triple, trini three each, fr. tres,
tria, three. See Three, and cf. Trine, a. & n., Tern, a.]

Defn: Threefold. "Trinal unity." Milton.
In their trinal triplicities on high. Spenser.
