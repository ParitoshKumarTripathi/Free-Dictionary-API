RECAPITULATION
Re`ca*pit`u*la"tion, n. Etym: [LL. recapitulatio: cf. F.
recapitulation.]

Defn: The act of recapitulating; a summary, or concise statement or
enumeration, of the principal points, facts, or statements, in a
preceding discourse, argument, or essay.
