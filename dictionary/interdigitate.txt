INTERDIGITATE
In`ter*dig"i*tate, v. t.

Defn: To interweave. [R.]
INTERDIGITATE

=====
In`ter*dig"i*tate, v. i. Etym: [Pref. inter- + L. digitus finger.]

Defn: To interlock, as the fingers of two hands that are joined; to
be interwoven; to commingle. Owen.
