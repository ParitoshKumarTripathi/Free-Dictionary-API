PEDIPALPI
Ped`i*pal"pi, n pl. Etym: [NL. See Pedipalpus.] (Zoöl.)

Defn: A division of Arachnida, including the whip scorpions
(Thelyphonus) and allied forms. Sometimes used in a wider sense to
include also the true scorpions.
