NOVERCAL
No*ver"cal, a. Etym: [L. novennis of nine years; novem nine + annus
year.]

Defn: Done or recurring every ninth year.
NOVERCAL

=====
No*ver"cal, a. Etym: [L. novercalis, from noverca a stepmother.]

Defn: Of or pertaining to a stepmother; suitable to, or in the manner
of, a stepmother. Derham.
