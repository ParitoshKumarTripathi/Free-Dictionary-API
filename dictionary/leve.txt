LEVE
Leve, a.

Defn: Dear. See Lief. [Obs.] Chaucer.
LEVE

=====
Leve, n. & v.

Defn: Same as 3d & 4th Leave. [Obs.]
LEVE

=====
Leve, v. i.

Defn: To live. [Obs.] Chaucer.
LEVE

=====
Leve, v. t. Etym: [OE., fr. AS. l, abbrev. fr. gel. See Believe.]

Defn: To believe. [Obs.] Chaucer.
LEVE

=====
Leve, v. t. Etym: [OE. leven, AS. l, l. See Leave permission.]

Defn: To grant; -- used esp. in exclamations or prayers followed by a
dependent clause. [Obs.]
God leve all be well. Chaucer.
