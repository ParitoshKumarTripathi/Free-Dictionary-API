PNEUMONOPHORA
Pneu`mo*noph"o*ra, n. pl. Etym: [NL., fr. Gr. (Zoöl.)

Defn: The division of Siphonophora which includes the Physalia and
allied genera; -- called also Pneumatophoræ.
