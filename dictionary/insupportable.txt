INSUPPORTABLE
In`sup*port"a*ble, a. Etym: [L. insupportabilis: cf. F.
insupportable. See In- not, and Support.]

Defn: Incapable of being supported or borne; unendurable;
insufferable; intolerable; as, insupportable burdens; insupportable
pain.
 -- In`sup*port"a*ble*ness, n.
 -- In`sup*port"a*bly, adv.
