TRECENTO
Tre*cen"to, n. & a. [It., three hundred, abbr. for thirteen hundred.]

Defn: The fourteenth century, when applied to Italian art,
literature, etc. It marks the period of Dante, Petrarch, and
boccaccio in literature, and of Giotto in painting.
