VIVE
Vive (vev). Etym: [F., imperative sing. pres. fr. vivre to live, L.
vivere.]

Defn: Long live, that is, success to; as, vive le roi, long live the
king; vive la bagatelle, success to trifles or sport.
VIVE

=====
Vive (viv), a. Etym: [L. vivus: cf. F. vif. See Vivid.]

Defn: Lively; animated; forcible. [Obs.] Bacon.
