EXACUATE
Ex*ac"u*ate, v. t. Etym: [L. exacure; ex out (intens.) + acuere to
make sharp.]

Defn: To whet or sharpen. [Obs.] B. Jonson.
 -- Ex*ac`u*a"tion, n. [Obs.]
