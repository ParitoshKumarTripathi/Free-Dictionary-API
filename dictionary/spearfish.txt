SPEARFISH
Spear"fish`, n. (Zoöl.)
(a) A large and powerful fish (Tetrapturus albidus) related to the
swordfish, but having scales and ventral fins. It is found on the
American coast and the Mediterranean.
(b) The carp sucker.
