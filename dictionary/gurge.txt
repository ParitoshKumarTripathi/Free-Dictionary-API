GURGE
Gurge, n. Etym: [L. gurges.]

Defn: A whirlpool. [Obs.]
The plain, wherein a black bituminous gurge Boils out from under
ground. Milton.
GURGE

=====
Gurge, v. t. Etym: [See Gorge.]
