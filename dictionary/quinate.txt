QUINATE
Qui"nate, a. Etym: [L. quini five each.] (Bot.)

Defn: Growing in sets of five; -- said especially of leaves composed
of five leaflets set at the end of a common petiole.
QUINATE

=====
Qui"nate, n. (Chem.)

Defn: A salt of quinic acid. [Written also kinate.]
