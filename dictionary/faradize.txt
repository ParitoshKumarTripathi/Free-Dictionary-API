FARADIZE
Far"a*dize, v. t.  [imp. & p. p. Faradized; p. pr. & vb. n.
Faradizing.] (Med.)

Defn: To stimulate with, or subject to, faradic, or inducted,
electric currents. --Far"a*diz`er (#), n.
