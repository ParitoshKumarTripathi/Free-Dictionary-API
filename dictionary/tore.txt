TORE

=====
Tore, n. Etym: [See Torus.]

1. (Arch.)

Defn: Same as Torus.

2. (Geom.)
(a) The surface described by the circumference of a circle revolving
about a straight line in its own plane.
(b) The solid inclosed by such a surface; -- sometimes called an
anchor ring.
