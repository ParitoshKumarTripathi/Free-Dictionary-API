PEDOMETER
Pe*dom"e*ter, n. Etym: [Pedi-, pedo- + -meter: cf. F. pédomètre.]
(Mech.)

Defn: An instrument for including the number of steps in walking, and
so ascertaining the distance passed over. It is usually in the form
of a watch; an oscillating weight by the motion of the body causes
the index to advance a certain distance at each step.
