TUSSLE
Tus"sle, v. i. & t. Etym: [See Tousle.]

Defn: To struggle, as in sport; to scuffle; to struggle with.
[Colloq.]
TUSSLE

=====
Tus"sle, n.

Defn: A struggle; a scuffle. [Colloq.]
