TECHNICALITY
Tech`ni*cal"i*ty, n.; pl. Technicalities (.

1. The quality or state of being technical; technicalness.

2. That which is technical, or peculiar to any trade, profession,
sect, or the like.
The technicalities of the sect. Palfrey.
