Nil. Etym: [See Nill, v. t.]

Defn: Will not. [Obs.] Chaucer.
NIL

=====
Nil, n. & a. Etym: [L., a contr. of nihil.]

Defn: Nothing; of no account; worthless; -- a term often used for
canceling, in accounts or bookkeeping. A. J. Ellis.
