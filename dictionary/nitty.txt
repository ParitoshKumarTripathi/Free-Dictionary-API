NITTY
Nit"ty, a.

Defn: Full of nits. B. Jonson.
NITTY

=====
Nit"ty, a. Etym: [L. nitidus. See Nitid.]

Defn: Shining; elegant; spruce. [Obs.] "O sweet, nitty youth."
Marston.
