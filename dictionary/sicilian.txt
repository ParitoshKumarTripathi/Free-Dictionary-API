SICILIAN
Si*cil"i*an, a.

Defn: Of or pertaining to Sicily or its inhabitants. Sicilian
vespers, the great massacre of the French in Sicily, in the year
1282, on the evening of Easter Monday, at the hour of vespers.
SICILIAN

=====
Si*cil"i*an, n.

Defn: A native or inhabitant of Sicily.
