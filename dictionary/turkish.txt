TURKISH
Turk"ish, a.

Defn: Of or pertaining to Turkey or the Turks.
 -- n.

Defn: The language spoken by Turks, esp. that of the people of
Turkey.
 -- Turk"ish*ly, adv.
 -- Turk"ish*ness, n.
