SPELLING
Spell"ing, n.

Defn: The act of one who spells; formation of words by letters;
orthography.
SPELLING

=====
Spell"ing, a.

Defn: Of or pertaining to spelling. Spelling bee, a spelling match.
[U.S.] -- Spelling book, a book with exercises for teaching children
to spell; a speller.
 -- Spelling match, a contest of skill in spelling words, between two
or more persons.
