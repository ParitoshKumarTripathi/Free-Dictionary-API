NULLIFIDIAN
Nul`li*fid"i*an, a. Etym: [L. nullus none + fide faith.]

Defn: Of no faith; also, not trusting to faith for salvation; --
opposed to Ant: solifidian. Feltham.
NULLIFIDIAN

=====
Nul`li*fid"i*an, n.

Defn: An unbeliever. B. Jonson.
