WALLOONS
Wal*loons", n. pl.; sing. Walloon (. Etym: [Cf. F. wallon.]

Defn: A Romanic people inhabiting that part of Belgium which
comprises the provinces of Hainaut, Namur, Liége, and Luxembourg, and
about one third of Brabant; also, the language spoken by this people.
Used also adjectively. [Written also Wallons.] "A base Walloon . . .
thrust Talbot with a spear." Shak. Walloon guard, the bodyguard of
the Spanish monarch; -- so called because formerly consisting of
Walloons.
