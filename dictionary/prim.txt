PRIM
Prim, n. Etym: [See Privet.] (Bot)

Defn: The privet.
PRIM

=====
Prim, a. Etym: [OF. prim, prin, prime, first, principal. sharp, thin,
piercing, fr. L. primus first. See Prime, a.]

Defn: Formal; precise; affectedly neat or nice; as, prim regularity;
a prim person. Swift.
PRIM

=====
Prim, v. t. [imp. & p. p. Primmed; p. pr. & vb. n. Primming.]

Defn: To deck with great nicety; to arrange with affected
preciseness; to prink.
PRIM

=====
Prim, v. i.

Defn: To dress or act smartly. [R.]
