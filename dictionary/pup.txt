PUP
Pup, n. Etym: [See Puppy.] (Zoöl.)
(a) A young dog; a puppy.
(b) a young seal.
PUP

=====
Pup, v. i. [imp. & p. p. Pupped; p. pr. & vb. n. Pupping.]

Defn: To bring forth whelps or young, as the female of the canine
species.
