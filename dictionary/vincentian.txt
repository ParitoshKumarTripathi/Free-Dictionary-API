VINCENTIAN
Vin*cen"tian, a.

Defn: Of or pertaining to Saint Vincent de Paul, or founded by him.
[R.]
VINCENTIAN

=====
Vin*cen"tian, n. (R. C. Ch.)
(a) Same as Lazarist.
(b) A member of certain charitable sisterhoods.
