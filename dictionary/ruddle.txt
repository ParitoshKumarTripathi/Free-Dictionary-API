RUDDLE
Rud"dle, v. t.

Defn: To raddle or twist. [Obs.]
RUDDLE

=====
Rud"dle, n.

Defn: A riddle or sieve. [Obs.] Holland.
RUDDLE

=====
Rud"dle, n. Etym: [See Rud; cf. Reddle.] (Min.)

Defn: A species of red earth colored by iron sesquioxide; red ocher.
RUDDLE

=====
Rud"dle, v. t.

Defn: To mark with ruddle; to raddle; to rouge. "Their ruddled
cheeks." Thackeray.
A fair sheep newly ruddled. Lady M. W. Montagu.
