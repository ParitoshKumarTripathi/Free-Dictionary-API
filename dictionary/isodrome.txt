ISODROME
I"so*drome, n.  [Iso- + Gr.  course.] (Nav.)

Defn: A method of moving a fleet from one formation to another, the
direction usually being changed eight points (90ยบ), by means of paths
of equal length for each ship. It is prohibited in the United States
navy.
