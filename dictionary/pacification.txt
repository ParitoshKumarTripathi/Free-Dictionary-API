PACIFICATION
Pa*cif`i*ca"tion, n. [L. pacificatio: cf. F. pacification. See
Pacify.]

Defn: The act or process of pacifying, or of making peace between
parties at variance; reconciliation. "An embassy of pacification."
Bacon.
