WINDOWPANE
Win"dow*pane`, n.

1. (Arch.)

Defn: See Pane, n., (3) b. [In this sense, written also window pane.]

2. (Zoöl.)

Defn: A thin, spotted American turbot (Pleuronectes maculatus)
remarkable for its translucency. It is not valued as a food fish.
Called also spotted turbot, daylight, spotted sand flounder, and
water flounder.
