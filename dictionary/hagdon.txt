HAGDON
Hag"don, n. (Zoöl.)

Defn: One of several species of sea birds of the genus Puffinus;
esp., P. major, the greater shearwarter, and P. Stricklandi, the
black hagdon or sooty shearwater; -- called also hagdown, haglin, and
hag. See Shearwater.
