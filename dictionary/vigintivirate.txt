VIGINTIVIRATE
Vi`gin*tiv"i*rate, n. Etym: [L. vigintiviratus, fr. vigintiviri;
viginti twenty + vir a man.]

Defn: The office of the vigintiviri, a body of officers of government
consisting of twenty men; also, the vigintiviri. [R.]
