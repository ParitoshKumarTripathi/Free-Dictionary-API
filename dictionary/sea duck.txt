SEA DUCK
Sea" duck`. (Zoöl.)

Defn: Any one of numerous species of ducks which frequent the
seacoasts and feed mainly on fishes and mollusks. The scoters,
eiders, old squaw, and ruddy duck are examples. They may be
distinguished by the lobate hind toe.
