DISSIMULATE
Dis*sim"u*late, a. Etym: [L. dissimulatus, p. p. of dissimulare. See
Dissemble.]

Defn: Feigning; simulating; pretending. [Obs.] Henryson.
DISSIMULATE

=====
Dis*sim"u*late, v. i.

Defn: To dissemble; to feign; to pretend.
