ACETIFY
A*cet"i*fy, v. t. [imp. & p. p. Acetified; p. pr. & vb. n.
Acetifying.] Etym: [L. acetum vinegar + -fly.]

Defn: To convert into acid or vinegar.
ACETIFY

=====
A*cet"i*fy, v. i.

Defn: To turn acid. Encyc. Dom. Econ.
