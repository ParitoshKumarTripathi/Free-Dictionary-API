PARACELSIAN
Par`a*cel"si*an, a.

Defn: Of, pertaining to, or in conformity with, the practice of
Paracelsus, a Swiss physician of the 15th century. Ferrand.
PARACELSIAN

=====
Par`a*cel"si*an, n.

Defn: A follower of Paracelsus or his practice or teachings.
Hakewill.
