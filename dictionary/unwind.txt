UNWIND
Un*wind", v. t. Etym: [AS. unwindan. See 1st Un-, and Wind to coil.]

1. To wind off; to loose or separate, as what or convolved; to
untwist; to untwine; as, to unwind thread; to unwind a ball of yarn.

2. To disentangle. [Obs.] Hooker.
UNWIND

=====
Un*wind", v. i.

Defn: To be or become unwound; to be capable of being unwound or
untwisted.
