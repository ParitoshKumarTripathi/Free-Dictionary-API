PEASE
Pease, n.; obs.pl. Peases, Peasen. Etym: [See Pea.]

1. A pea. [Obs.] "A peose." "Bread . . . of beans and of peses."
Piers Plowman.

2. A plural form of Pea. See the Note under Pea.
