TWELFTH
Twelfth, a. Etym: [For twelft, OE. twelfte, AS. twelfta. See Twelve.]

1. Next in order after the eleventh; coming after eleven others; --
the ordinal of twelve.

2. Consisting, or being one of, twelve equal parts into which
anything is divided.
TWELFTH

=====
Twelfth, n.

1. The quotient of a unit divided by twelve; one of twelve equal
parts of one whole.

2. The next in order after the eleventh.

3. (Mus.)

Defn: An interval comprising an octave and a fifth.
