FREYA
Frey"a, n. Etym: [Icel. Freyja.] (Scand. Myth.)

Defn: The daughter of Njörd, aud goddess of love and beauty; the
Scandinavian Venus; -- in Teutonic myths confounded with Frigga, but
in Scandinavian, distinct. [Written also Frea, Fraying, and Ereyja.]
