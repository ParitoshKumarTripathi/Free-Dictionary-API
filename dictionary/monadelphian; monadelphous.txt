MONADELPHIAN; MONADELPHOUS
Mon`a*del"phi*an, Mon`a*del"phous, a. Etym: [Cf. F. monadelphie.]
(Bot.)

Defn: Of or pertaining to the Monadelphia; having the stamens united
in one body by the filaments.
