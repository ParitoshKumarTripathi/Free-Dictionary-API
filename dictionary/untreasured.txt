UNTREASURED
Un*treas"ured, a.

1. Etym: [Properly p. p. of untreasure.]

Defn: Deprived of treasure. [Obs.] Shak.

2. Etym: [Pref. un- not + treasured.]

Defn: Not treasured; not kept as treasure.
