REPOUSSAGE
Re*pous`sage", n. [F. See Repoussé.] (Art)

Defn: Art or process of hammering out or pressing thin metal from the
reverse side: (1) in producing repoussé work; (2) in leveling up any
part of an etched plate that has been worked so as to cause a
depression.
