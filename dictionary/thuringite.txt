THURINGITE
Thu*rin"gite, n. Etym: [From Thuringia, where it is found.] (Min.)

Defn: A mineral occurring as an aggregation of minute scales having
an olive-green color and pearly luster. It is a hydrous silicate of
aluminia and iron.
