PYRARGYRITE
Py*rar"gy*rite, n. Etym: [Gr. (Min.)

Defn: Ruby silver; dark red silver ore. It is a sulphide of antimony
and silver, occurring in rhombohedral crystals or massive, and is of
a dark red or black color with a metallic adamantine luster.
