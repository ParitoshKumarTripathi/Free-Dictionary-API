TRIPLICATION
Trip"li*ca"tion, n. Etym: [L. triplicatio: cf. F. triplication.]

1. The act of tripling, or making threefold, or adding three
together. Glanvill.

2. (Civil Law)

Defn: Same as Surrejoinder.
