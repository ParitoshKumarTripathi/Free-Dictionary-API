ENCYCLOPEDIC; ENCYCLOPEDICAL
En*cy`clo*ped"ic, En*cy`clo*ped"ic*al, a. Etym: [Cf. F.
encyclopédique.]

Defn: Pertaining to, or of the nature of, an encyclopedia; embracing
a wide range of subjects.
