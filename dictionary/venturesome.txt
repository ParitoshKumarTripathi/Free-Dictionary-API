VENTURESOME
Ven"ture*some, a.

Defn: Inclined to venture; not loth to run risk or danger; venturous;
bold; daring; adventurous; as, a venturesome boy or act.
 -- Ven"ture*some*ly, adv.
 -- Ven"ture*some*ness, n.
