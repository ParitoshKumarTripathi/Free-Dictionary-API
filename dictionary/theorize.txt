THEORIZE
The"o*rize, v. i. [imp. & p. p. Theorized; p. pr. & vb. n.
Theorizing.] Etym: [Cf. F. théoriser.]

Defn: To form a theory or theories; to form opinions solely by
theory; to speculate.
