HAEMATOXYLIN
Hæm`a*tox"y*lin, n. Etym: [See Hæmatoxylon.] (Chem.)

Defn: The coloring principle of logwood. It is obtained as a yellow
crystalline substance, C16H14O6, with a sweetish taste. Formerly
called also hematin.
