TAMIL
Ta"mil, a.

Defn: Of or pertaining to the Tamils, or to their language. [Written
also Tamul.]
TAMIL

=====
Ta"mil, n. Etym: [Written also Tamul.]

1. (Ethnol.)

Defn: One of a Dravidian race of men native of Northern Ceylon and
Southern India.

2. The Tamil language, the most important of the Dravidian languages.
See Dravidian, a.
