TWENTY
Twen"ty, a. Etym: [OE. twenty, AS. tw, twentig; akin to OFris.
twintich, OS. tw, D. & LG. twintig, OHG. zweinzug, G. zwanzig, Goth.
twai tigjis; that is, two tens. See Twain, Two, and Ten.]

1. One more that nineteen; twice; as, twenty men.

2. An indefinite number more or less that twenty. Shak.
Maximilian, upon twenty respects, could not have been the man. Bacon.
TWENTY

=====
Twen"ty, n.; pl. Twenties (.

1. The number next following nineteen; the sum of twelve and eight,
or twice ten; twenty units or objects; a score.

2. A symbol representing twenty units, as 20, or xx.
