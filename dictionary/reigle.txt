REIGLE
Rei"gle (r"g'l), n. Etym: [F. règle a rule, fr. L. regula. See Rule.]

Defn: A hollow cut or channel for quiding anything; as, the reigle of
a side post for a flood gate. Carew.
REIGLE

=====
Rei"gle, v. t.

Defn: To regulate; to govern. [Obs.]
