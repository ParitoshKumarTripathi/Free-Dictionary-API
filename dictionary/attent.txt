ATTENT
At*tent", a. Etym: [L. attentus, p. p. of attendere. See Attend, v.
t.]

Defn: Attentive; heedful. [Archaic]
Let thine ears be attent unto the prayer. 2 Chron. vi. 40.
ATTENT

=====
At*tent", n.

Defn: Attention; heed. [Obs.] Spenser.
