UNWIT
Un*wit", v. t. Etym: [1st pref. un- + wit.]

Defn: To deprive of wit. [Obs.] Shak.
UNWIT

=====
Un*wit", n. Etym: [Pref. un- not + wit.]

Defn: Want of wit or understanding; ignorance. [Obs.] Chaucer.
