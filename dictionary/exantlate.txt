EXANTLATE
Ex*ant"late, v. t. Etym: [L. exantlatus, p. p. of exantlare,
exanclare, to endure.]

Defn: To exhaust or wear out. [Obs.] "Seeds . . . wearied or
exantlated." Boyle.
