SUPERCELESTIAL
Su`per*ce*les"tial, a. Etym: [Pref. super- + celestial: cf. L.
supercaelestis.]

1. Situated above the firmament, or great vault of heaven. Waterland.

2. Higher than celestial; superangelic.
