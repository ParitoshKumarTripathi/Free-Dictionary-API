WARRAY
War"ray, v. t. Etym: [OF. werreier, werrier, guerroier, F. guerroyer,
from OF. werre war, F. guerre; of German origin. See War.]

Defn: To make war upon. [Obs.] Fairfax. "When a man warrayeth truth."
Chaucer.
