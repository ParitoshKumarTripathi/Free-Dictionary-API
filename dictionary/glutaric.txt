GLUTARIC
Glu*tar"ic, a. Etym: [Glutamic + tartaric.] (Chem.)

Defn: Of, pertaining to, or designating, an acid so called; as,
glutaric ethers. Glutaric acid, an organic acid obtained as a white
crystalline substance, isomeric with pyrotartaric acid; -- called
also normal pyrotartaric acid.
