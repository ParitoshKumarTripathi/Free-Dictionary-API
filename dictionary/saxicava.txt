SAXICAVA
Sax`i*ca"va, n.; pl. E. saxicavas, L. Saxicavæ. Etym: [NL. See
Saxicavous.] (Zoöl.)

Defn: Any species of marine bivalve shells of the genus Saxicava.
Some of the species are noted for their power of boring holes in
limestone and similar rocks.
