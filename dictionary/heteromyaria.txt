HETEROMYARIA
Het`e*ro*my*a"ri*a, n. pl. Etym: [NL., fr. Gr. (Zoöl.)

Defn: A division of bivalve shells, including the marine mussels, in
which the two adductor muscles are very unequal. See Dreissena, and
Illust. under Byssus.
