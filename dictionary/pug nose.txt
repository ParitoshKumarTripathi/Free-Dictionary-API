PUG NOSE
Pug" nose`.

Defn: A short, thick nose; a snubnose.
 -- Pug"-nosed`, a. Pug-nose eel (Zoöl.), a deep-water marine eel
(Simenchelys parasiticus) which sometimes burrows into the flesh of
the halibut.
