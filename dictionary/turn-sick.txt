TURN-SICK
Turn"-sick`, a.

Defn: Giddy. [Obs.] Bacon.
TURN-SICK

=====
Turn"-sick`, n. (For.)

Defn: A disease with which sheep are sometimes affected; gid; sturdy.
See Gid.
