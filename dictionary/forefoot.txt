FOREFOOT
Fore"foot`, n.

1. One of the anterior feet of a quardruped or multiped; -- usually
written fore foot.

2. (Shipbuilding)

Defn: A piece of timber which terminates the keel at the fore end,
connecting it with the lower end of the stem.
