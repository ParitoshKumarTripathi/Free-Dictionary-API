TABLOID
Tab"loid, n. [A table-mark.]

Defn: A compressed portion of one or more drugs or chemicals, or of
food, etc.
TABLOID

=====
Tab"loid, a.

Defn: Compressed or condensed, as into a tabloid; administrated in or
as in tabloids, or small condensed bits; as, a tabloid form of
imparting information.
