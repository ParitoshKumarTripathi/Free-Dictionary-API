ROMPU
Rom"pu, a. Etym: [F. rompu, p. p. of rompre to breeak, L. rumpere.
See Rupture.] (Her.)

Defn: Broken, as an ordinary; cut off, or broken at the top, as a
chevron, a bend, or the like.
