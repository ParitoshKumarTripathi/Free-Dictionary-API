UMBELLULARIA
Um*bel`lu*la"ri*a, n. Etym: [NL. Umbellule.] (Zoöl.)

Defn: A genus of deep-sea alcyonaria consisting of a cluster of large
flowerlike polyps situated at the summit of a long, slender stem
which stands upright in the mud, supported by a bulbous base.
