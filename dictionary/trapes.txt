TRAPES
Trapes, n. Etym: [See Trape.]

Defn: A slattern; an idle, sluttish, or untidy woman. [Obs. or
Colloq.]
TRAPES

=====
Trapes, v. i.

Defn: To go about in an idle or slatternly fashion; to trape; to
traipse. [Colloq.] Thackeray.
