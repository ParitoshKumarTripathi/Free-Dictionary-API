SPURLING-LINE
Spur"ling-line`, n. Etym: [Cf. Prov. E. spurling the rut of a wheel,
a cart rut, AS. spor a track, trace, E. spoor. Scot. spurl to
sprawl.] (Naut.)

Defn: The line which forms the communication between the steering
wheel and the telltale.
