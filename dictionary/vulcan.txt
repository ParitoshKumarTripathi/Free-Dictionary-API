VULCAN
Vul"can, n. Etym: [L. Vulcanus, Volcanus: cf. Skr. ulka a firebrand,
meteor. Cf. Volcano.] (Rom. Myth.)

Defn: The god of fire, who presided over the working of metals; --
answering to the Greek Hephæstus.
