UNBESEEMING
Un`be*seem"ing, a. Etym: [Pref. un- not + beseeming.]

Defn: Unbecoming; not befitting.
 -- Un`be*seem"ing*ly, adv.
 -- Un`be*seem"ing*ness, n.
