TRANSMOGRIFICATION
Trans*mog`ri*fi*ca"tion, n.

Defn: The act of transmogrifying, or the state of being
transmogrified; transformation. [Colloq.]
Clive, who wrote me about the transmogrification of our schoolfellow,
an attorney's son. Thackeray.
