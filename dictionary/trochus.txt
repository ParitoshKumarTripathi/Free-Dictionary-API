TROCHUS
Tro"chus, n.; pl. Trochi. Etym: [NL., fr. Gr. (Zoöl.)

Defn: Any one of numerous species of marine univalve shells belonging
to Trochus and many allied genera of the family Trochidæ. Some of the
species are called also topshells.
