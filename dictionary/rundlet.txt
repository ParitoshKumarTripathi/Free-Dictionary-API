RUNDLET
Rund"let, n. Etym: [Dim. of OF. rondele a little tun, fr. rond round.
See Round, and cf. Roundlet, Runlet.]

Defn: A small barrel of no certain dimensions. It may contain from 3
to 20 gallons, but it usually holds about 14 [Written also runlet.]
