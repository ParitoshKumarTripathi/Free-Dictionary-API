QUEENSLAND NUT
Queens"land nut`. (Bot.)

Defn: The nut of an Australian tree (Macadamia ternifolia). It is
about an inch in diameter, and contains a single round edible seed,
or sometimes two hemispherical seeds. So called from Queensland in
Australia.
