MIDDAY
Mid"day`, n. Etym: [AS. middæg. See Mid, a., and Day.]

Defn: The middle part of the day; noon.
MIDDAY

=====
Mid"day`, a.

Defn: Of or pertaining to noon; meridional; as, the midday sun.
