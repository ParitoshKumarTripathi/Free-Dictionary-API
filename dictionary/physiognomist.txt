PHYSIOGNOMIST
Phys`i*og*nom"ist, n.

Defn: Same as Physiognomy, 1.
PHYSIOGNOMIST

=====
Phys`i*og"no*mist, n. Etym: [Cf. F. physiognomiste.]

1. One skilled in physiognomy. Dryden.

2. One who tells fortunes by physiognomy. Holland.
