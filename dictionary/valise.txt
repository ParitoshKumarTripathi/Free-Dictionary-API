VALISE
Va*lise", n. Etym: [F. valise; cf. It. valigia, Sp. balija, LL.
valisia, valesia; of uncertain origin, perhaps through (assumed) LL.
vidulitia, from L. vidulus a leathern trunk; a knapsack.]

Defn: A small sack or case, usually of leather, but sometimes of
other material, for containing the clothes, toilet articles, etc., of
a traveler; a traveling bag; a portmanteau.
