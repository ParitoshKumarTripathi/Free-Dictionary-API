VENTIDUCT
Ven"ti*duct, n. Etym: [L. ventus wind + ductus a leading, conduit,
fr. ducere, ductum, to lead.]

Defn: A passage for wind or air; a passage or pipe for ventilating
apartments. Gwilt.
