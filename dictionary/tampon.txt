TAMPON
Tam"pon, n. Etym: [F. See Tampion.] (Surg.)

Defn: A plug introduced into a natural or artificial cavity of the
body in order to arrest hemorrhage, or for the application of
medicine.
TAMPON

=====
Tam"pon, v. t. (Surg.)

Defn: To plug with a tampon.
