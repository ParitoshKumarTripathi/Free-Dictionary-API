PROFACE
Pro"face, interj. Etym: [OF. prou face, prou fasse; prou profit +
faire to make, do.]

Defn: Much good may it do you! -- a familiar salutation or welcome.
[Obs.]
Master page, good master page, sit. Proface! Shak.
