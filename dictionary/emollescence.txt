EMOLLESCENCE
Em`ol*les"cence, n. Etym: [L. e out + mollescere, incho. fr. mollere
to be soft, mollis soft.]

Defn: That degree of softness in a body beginning to melt which
alters its shape; the first or lowest degree of fusibility.
