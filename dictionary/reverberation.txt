REVERBERATION
Re*ver`ber*a"tion, n. Etym: [CF. F. réverbération.]

Defn: The act of reverberating; especially, the act of reflecting
light or heat, or reëchoing sound; as, the reverberation of rays from
a mirror; the reverberation of rays from a mirror; the reverberation
of voices; the reverberation of heat or flame in a furnace.
