UNSEARCHABLE
Un*search"a*ble, a.

Defn: Not searchable; inscrutable; hidden; mysterious.
The counsels of God are to us unsearchable. Rogers.
-- Un*search"a*ble*ness, n.
 -- Un*search"a*bly, adv.
