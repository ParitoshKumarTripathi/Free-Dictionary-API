TURACOU
Tu*ra"cou, n. Etym: [Cf. F. touraco.] (Zoöl.)

Defn: Any one of several species of plantain eaters of the genus
Turacus, native of Africa. They are remarkable for the peculiar green
and red pigments found in their feathers. [Written also touraco, and
touracou.]
