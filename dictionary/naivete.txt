NAIVETE
Na`ïve`té", n. Etym: [F. See Naïve, and cf. Nativity.]

Defn: Native simplicity; unaffected plainness or ingenuousness;
artlessness.
A story which pleases me by its naïveté -- that is, by its
unconscious ingenuousness. De Quincey.
