VARANUS
Va*ra"nus, n. Etym: [NL., fr. Ar. waran, waral; cf. F. varan, from
the Arabic.] (Zoöl.)

Defn: A genus of very large lizards native of Asia and Africa. It
includes the monitors. See Monitor, 3.
