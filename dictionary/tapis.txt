TAPIS
Ta"pis, n. Etym: [F. See Tapestry.]

Defn: Tapestry; formerly, the cover of a council table. On, or Upon,
the tapis, on the table, or under consideration; as, to lay a motion
in Parliament on the tapis.
TAPIS

=====
Tap"is, v. t.

Defn: To cover or work with figures like tapestry. [R.] Holland.
