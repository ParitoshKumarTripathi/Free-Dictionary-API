SOUNDER
Sound"er, n.

Defn: One who, or that which; sounds; specifically, an instrument
used in telegraphy in place of a register, the communications being
read by sound.
SOUNDER

=====
Sound"er, n. (Zoöl.)

Defn: A herd of wild hogs.
