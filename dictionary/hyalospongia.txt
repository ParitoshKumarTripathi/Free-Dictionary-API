HYALOSPONGIA
Hy`a*lo*spon"gi*a, n. pl. Etym: [NL., fr. Gr. (Zoöl.)

Defn: An order of vitreous sponges, having glassy six-rayed,
siliceous spicules; -- called also Hexactinellinæ.
