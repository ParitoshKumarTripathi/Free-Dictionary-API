VARLETRY
Var"let*ry, n. Etym: [Cf. OF. valeterie the young unmarried nobles.]

Defn: The rabble; the crowd; the mob.
Shall they hoist me up, And show me to the shouting varletry Of
censuring Rome. Shak.
