HYMENEAL; HYMENEAN
Hy`me*ne"al, Hy`me*ne"an, a. Etym: [L. hymeneius, a., also Hymenaeus,
n., Hymen, Gr. hyménéal, hyménéen.]

Defn: Of or pertaining to marriage; as, hymeneal rites. Pope.
HYMENEAL; HYMENEAN

=====
Hy`me*ne"al, Hy`me*ne"an, n.

Defn: A marriage song. Milton.
