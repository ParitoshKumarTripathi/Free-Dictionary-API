DAUBER
Daub"er, n.

1. One who, or that which, daubs; especially, a coarse, unskillful
painter.

2. (Copperplate Print.)

Defn: A pad or ball of rags, covered over with canvas, for inking
plates; a dabber.

3. A low and gross flattere.

4. (Zoöl.)

Defn: The mud wasp; the mud dauber.
