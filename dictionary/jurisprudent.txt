JURISPRUDENT
Ju`ris*pru"dent, a.Etym: [See Jurisprudence.]

Defn: Understanding law; skilled in jurisprudence. G. West.

Ju`ris*pru"dent, n. Etym: [Cf. F. jurisprudent.]
