MISHAP
Mis*hap", n.

Defn: Evil accident; ill luck; misfortune; mischance. Chaucer.
Secure from worldly chances and mishaps. Shak.
MISHAP

=====
Mis*hap", v. i.

Defn: To happen unluckily; -- used impersonally. [Obs.] "If that me
mishap." Chaucer.
