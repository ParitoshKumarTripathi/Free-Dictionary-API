UPROARIOUS
Up*roar"i*ous, a.

Defn: Making, or accompanied by, uproar, or noise and tumult; as,
uproarious merriment.
 -- Up*roar"i*ous*ly, adv.
 -- Up*roar"i*ous*ness, n.
