KANTIAN
Kant"i*an, a.

Defn: Of or pertaining to Immanuel Kant, the German philosopher;
conformed or relating to any or all of the philosophical doctrines of
Immanuel Kant.
KANTIAN

=====
Kant"i*an, n.

Defn: A follower of Kant; a Kantist.
