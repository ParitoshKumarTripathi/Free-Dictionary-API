RUSSOPHILE; RUSSOPHILIST
Rus"so*phile, Rus"soph"i*list, n. Etym: [Russia + Gr. to love: cf. F.
russophile.]

Defn: One who, not being a Russian, favors Russian policy and
aggrandizement.
 -- Rus*soph"ilism, n. [Chiefly newspaper words.]
