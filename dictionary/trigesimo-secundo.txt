TRIGESIMO-SECUNDO
Tri*ges"i*mo-se*cun"do, a. Etym: [L. in trigesimo-secundo in the
thirty-second.]

Defn: Having thirty-two leaves to a sheet; as, a trigesimo-secundo
form, book, leaf, size, etc.
TRIGESIMO-SECUNDO

=====
Tri*ges"i*mo-se*cun"do, n.

Defn: A book composed of sheets so folded that each one makes thirty-
two leaves; hence, indicating, more or less definitely, a size of
book; -- usually written 32mo, or 32ยบ, and called thirty-twomo.
