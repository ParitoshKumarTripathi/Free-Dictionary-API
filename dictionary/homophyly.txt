HOMOPHYLY
Ho*moph"y*ly, n. Etym: [Homo- + Gr. (Biol.)

Defn: That form of homology due to common ancestry (phylogenetic
homology), in opposition to homomorphy, to which genealogic basis is
wanting. Haeckel.
