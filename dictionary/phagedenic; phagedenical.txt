PHAGEDENIC; PHAGEDENICAL
Phag`e*den"ic, Phag`e*den"ic*AL, a. Etym: [L. phagedaenicus, Gr.
phagédénique.] (Med.)

Defn: Of, like, or pertaining to, phagedena; used in the treatment of
phagedena; as, a phagedenic ulcer or medicine.
 -- n.

Defn: A phagedenic medicine.
