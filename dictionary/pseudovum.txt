PSEUDOVUM
Pseu*do"vum, n.; pl. Pseudova. Etym: [NL. See Pseudo-, and Ovum.]
(Zoöl.)

Defn: An egglike germ produced by the agamic females of some insects
and other animals, and by the larvæ of certain insects. It is capable
of development without fertilization. See Illust. of Pædogenesis.
