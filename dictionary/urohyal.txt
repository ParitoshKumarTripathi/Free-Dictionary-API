UROHYAL
U`ro*hy"al, a. Etym: [2d uro- + the Gr. letter (Anat.)

Defn: Of or pertaining to one or more median and posterior elements
in the hyoidean arch of fishes.
 -- n.

Defn: A urohyal bone or cartilage.
