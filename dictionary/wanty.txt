WANTY
Wan"ty, n. Etym: [For womb tie, that is, bellyWomb, and Tie.]

Defn: A surcingle, or strap of leather, used for binding a load upon
the back of a beast; also, a leather tie; a short wagon rope. [Prov.
Eng.]
