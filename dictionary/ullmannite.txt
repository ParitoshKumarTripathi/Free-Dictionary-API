ULLMANNITE
Ull"mann*ite, n. Etym: [So named after J. C. Ullman, a German
chemist.] (Min.)

Defn: A brittle mineral of a steel-gray color and metallic luster,
containing antimony, arsenic, sulphur, and nickel.
