URTICACEOUS
Ur`ti*ca"ceous, a. (Bot.)

Defn: Of or pertaining to a natural order (Urticaceæ) of plants, of
which the nettle is the type. The order includes also the hop, the
elm, the mulberry, the fig, and many other plants.
