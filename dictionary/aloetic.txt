ALOETIC
Al`o*et"ic, a. Etym: [Cf. F. aloétique.]

Defn: Consisting chiefly of aloes; of the nature of aloes.
ALOETIC

=====
Al`o*et"ic, n.

Defn: A medicine containing chiefly aloes.
