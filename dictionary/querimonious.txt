QUERIMONIOUS
Quer`i*mo"ni*ous, a. Etym: [L. querimonia a complaint, fr. queri to
complain. See Querulous.]

Defn: Complaining; querulous; apt to complain.
 -- Quer`i*mo"ni*ous*ly, adv.
 -- Quer`i*mo"ni*ous*ness, n.
