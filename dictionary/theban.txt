THEBAN
The"ban, a. Etym: [L. Thebanus.]

Defn: Of or pertaining to Thebes. Theban year (Anc. Chron.), the
Egyptian year of 365 days and 6 hours. J. Bryant.
THEBAN

=====
The"ban, n.

Defn: A native or inhabitant of Thebes; also, a wise man.
I'll talk a word with this same learned Theban. Shak.
