SIX

=====
Six, n.

1. The number greater by a unit than five; the sum of three and
three; six units or objects.

2. A symbol representing six units, as 6, vi., or VI. To be at six
and seven or at sixes and sevens, to be in disorder. Bacon. Shak.
Swift.
