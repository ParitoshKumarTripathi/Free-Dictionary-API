VESPERS
Ves"pers, n.; pl. Etym: [OF. vespres, F. vêpres, LL. vesperae, fr. L.
vespera evening. See Vesper, n.] (R. C. Ch.)
(a) One of the little hours of the Breviary.
(b) The evening song or service. Sicilian vespers. See under
Sicilian, a.
