ENDING
End"ing, n.

1. Termination; concluding part; result; conclusion; destruction;
death.

2. (Gram.)

Defn: The final syllable or letter of a word; the part joined to the
stem. See 3d Case, 5. Ending day, day of death. Chaucer.
