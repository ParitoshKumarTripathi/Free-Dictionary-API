VULPINE
Vul"pine, a. Etym: [L. vulpinus, from vulpes a fox.]

Defn: Of or pertaining to the fox; resembling the fox; foxy; cunning;
crafty; artful. Vulpine phalangist (Zoöl.), an Australian carnivorous
marsupial (Phalangista, or Trichosurus, vulpina); -- called also
vulpine phalanger, and vulpine opossum.
