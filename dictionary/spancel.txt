SPANCEL
Span"cel, n. Etym: [Perhaps span + AS. sal a rope.]

Defn: A rope used for tying or hobbling the legs of a horse or cow.
[Prov. Eng. & Local, U.S.] Grose.
SPANCEL

=====
Span"cel, v. t. [imp. & p. p. Spanceled or Spancelled; p. pr. & vb.
n. Spanceling or Spancelling.]

Defn: To tie or hobble with a spancel. [Prov. Eng. & Local, U.S.]
Malone.
