ARABIAN
A*ra"bi*an, a.

Defn: Of or pertaining to Arabia or its inhabitants. Arabian bird,
the phenix. Shak.
ARABIAN

=====
A*ra"bi*an, n.

Defn: A native of Arabia; an Arab.
