PHARYNGOPNEUSTA
Pha*ryn`gop*neus"ta, n. pl. Etym: [NL., fr. Gr. (Zoöl.)

Defn: A group of invertebrates including the Tunicata and
Enteropneusta.
 -- Pha*ryn`gop*neus"tal, a.
