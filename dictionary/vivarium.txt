VIVARIUM
Vi*va"ri*um, n.; pl. E. Vivariums, L. Vivaria. Etym: [L., fr.
vivarius belonging to living creatures, fr. vivus alive, living. See
Vivid.]

Defn: A place artificially arranged for keeping or raising living
animals, as a park, a pond, an aquarium, a warren, etc.
