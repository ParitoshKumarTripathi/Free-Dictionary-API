VERISIMILAR
Ver`i*sim"i*lar, a. Etym: [L. verisimilis; verus true + similis like,
similar. See Very, and Similar.]

Defn: Having the appearance of truth; probable; likely. "How
verisimilar it looks." Carlyle.
