SCRIBBLING
Scrib"bling, n. Etym: [See 1st Scribble.]

Defn: The act or process of carding coarsely. Scribbing machine, the
machine used for the first carding of wool or other fiber; -- called
also scribbler.
SCRIBBLING

=====
Scrib"bling, a.

Defn: Writing hastily or poorly.
Ye newspaper witlings! ye pert scribbling folks! Goldsmith.
SCRIBBLING

=====
Scrib"bling, n.

Defn: The act of writing hastily or idly.
