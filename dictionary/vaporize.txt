VAPORIZE
Vap"o*rize, v. t. [imp. & p. p. Vaporized; p. pr. & vb. n.
Vaporizing.] Etym: [Cf. F. vaporiser.]

Defn: To convert into vapor, as by the application of heat, whether
naturally or artificially. Vaporizing surface. (Steam Boilers) See
Evaporating surface, under Evaporate, v. t.
VAPORIZE

=====
Vap"o*rize, v. i.

Defn: To pass off in vapor.
