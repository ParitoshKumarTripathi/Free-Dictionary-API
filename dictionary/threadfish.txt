THREADFISH
Thread"fish`, n. (Zoöl.)
(a) The cutlass fish.
(b) A carangoid fish (Caranx gallus, or C. crinitus) having the
anterior rays of the soft dorsal and anal fins prolonged in the form
of long threads.
