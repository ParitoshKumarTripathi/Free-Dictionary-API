CAUTEL
Cau"tel, n. Etym: [F. cautèle, L. cautela, fr. cavere to be on one's
guard, to take care.]

1. Caution; prudence; wariness. [Obs.] Fulke.

2. Craft; deceit; falseness. [Obs.] Shak.
