RADIO-ACTIVE
Ra`di*o-ac"tive, a.  [Radio-+ active.] (Physics)

Defn: Capable of luminescence under the action of cathode rays, X
rays, or any of the allied forms of radiation. -- Ra`di*o-
ac*tiv"i*ty, n.
