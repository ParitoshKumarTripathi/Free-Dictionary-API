RETEPORE
Re`te*pore, n. Etym: [L. rete a net + porus pore.] (Zoöl.)

Defn: Any one of several species of bryozoans of the genus Retepora.
They form delicate calcareous corals, usually composed of thin
fenestrated fronds.
