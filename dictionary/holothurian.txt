HOLOTHURIAN
Hol`o*thu"ri*an, a.

Defn: ( -- n.

Defn: One of the Holothurioidea.

Note: Some of the species of Holothurians are called sea cucumbers,
sea slugs, trepang, and bêche de mèr. Many are used as food, esp. by
the Chinese. See Trepang.
