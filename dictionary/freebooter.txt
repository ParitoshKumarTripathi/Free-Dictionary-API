FREEBOOTER
Free"boot`er, n. Etym: [D. vrijbuiter, fr. vrijbuiten to plunder;
vrij free + buit booty, akin to E. booty. See Free, and Booty, and
cf. Filibuster.]

Defn: One who plunders or pillages without the authority of national
warfare; a member of a predatory band; a pillager; a buccaneer; a sea
robber. Bacon.
