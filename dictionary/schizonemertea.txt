SCHIZONEMERTEA
Schiz`o*ne*mer"te*a, n. pl. Etym: [NL. See Schizo-, and Nemertes.]
(Zoöl.)

Defn: A group of nemerteans comprising those having a deep slit along
each side of the head. See Illust. in Appendix.
