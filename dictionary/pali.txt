PALI
Pa"li, n.,

Defn: pl. of Palus.
PALI

=====
Pa"li, n. Etym: [Ceylonese, fr. Skr. pali row, line, series, applied
to the series of Buddhist sacred texts.]

Defn: A dialect descended from Sanskrit, and like that, a dead
language, except when used as the sacred language of the Buddhist
religion in Farther India, etc.
