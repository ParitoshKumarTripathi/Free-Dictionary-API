DELETERIOUS
Del`e*te"ri*ous, a. Etym: [LL. deleterius noxious, Gr. delere to
destroy.]

Defn: Hurtful; noxious; destructive; pernicious; as, a deleterious
plant or quality; a deleterious example.
 -- Del`e*te"ri*ous*ly, adv.
 -- Del`e*te"ri*ous*ness, n.
