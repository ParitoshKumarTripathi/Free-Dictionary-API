SULPHOTUNGSTIC
Sul`pho*tung"stic, a. (Chem.)

Defn: Of, pertaining to, or designating, hypothetical sulphacid of
tungsten (called also sulphowolframic acid), analogous to sulphuric
acid, and known in its salts.
