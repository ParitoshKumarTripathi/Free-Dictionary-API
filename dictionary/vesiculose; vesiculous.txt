VESICULOSE; VESICULOUS
Ve*sic"u*lose`, Ve*sic"u*lous, a. Etym: [L. vesiculosus: cf. F.
vésiculeux.]

Defn: Bladdery; vesicular; vesiculate; composed of vesicles; covered
with vesicles; as, a vesiculose shell.
