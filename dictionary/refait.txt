REFAIT
Re*fait", n. [F.] (Card Playing)

Defn: A drawn game; specif. (Trente et quarante),

Defn: a state of the game in which the aggregate pip value of cards
dealt to red equals that of those dealt to black. All bets are then
off; unless the value is 31, in which case the banker wins half the
stakes.
