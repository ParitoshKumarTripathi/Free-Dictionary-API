UPCOUNTRY
Up"coun`try, adv.

Defn: In an upcountry direction; as, to live upcountry. [Colloq.]
UPCOUNTRY

=====
Up"coun`try, a.

Defn: Living or situated remote from the seacoast; as, an upcountry
residence. [Colloq.] -- n.

Defn: The interior of the country. [Colloq.]
