REVOCABLE
Rev"o*ca*ble, a. Etym: [L. revocabilis: cf. F. révocable. See
Revoke.]

Defn: Capable of being revoked; as, a revocable edict or grant; a
revocable covenant.
 -- Rev"o*ca*ble*ness, n.
 -- Rev"o*ca*bly, adv.
