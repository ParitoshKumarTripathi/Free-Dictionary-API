WELSHMAN
Welsh"man, n.; pl. Welshmen (.

1. A native or inhabitant of Wales; one of the Welsh.

2. (Zoöl.)
(a) A squirrel fish.
(b) The large-mouthed black bass. See Black bass. [Southern U. S.]
