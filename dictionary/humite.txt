HUMITE
Hum"ite, n. Etym: [Named after Sir A.Hume.] (Min.)

Defn: A mineral of a transparent vitreous brown color, found in the
ejected masses of Vesuvius. It is a silicate of iron and magnesia,
containing fluorine.
