SEMPITERNAL
Sem`pi*ter"nal, a. Etym: [L. sempiternus, fr. semper always: cf. F.
sempiternel.]

1. Of neverending duration; everlasting; endless; having beginning,
but no end. Sir M. Hale.

2. Without beginning or end; eternal. Blackmore.
