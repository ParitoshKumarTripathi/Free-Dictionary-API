PHLEGMASIA
Phleg*ma"si*a, n. Etym: [NL., from Gr. Phlegm.] (Med.)

Defn: An inflammation; more particularly, an inflammation of the
internal organs. Phlegmasia dolens ( Etym: [NL.], milk leg.
