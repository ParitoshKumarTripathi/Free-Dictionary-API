PEWIT
Pe"wit, n. Etym: [Prob. of imitative origin; cf. OD. piewit, D.
kievit, G. kibitz.] (Zoöl.)
(a) The lapwing.
(b) The European black-headed, or laughing, gull (Xema ridibundus).
See under Laughing.
(c) The pewee. [Written also peevit, peewit, pewet.]
