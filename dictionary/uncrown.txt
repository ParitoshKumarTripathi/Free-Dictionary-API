UNCROWN
Un*crown", v. t. Etym: [1st pref. un- + crown.]

Defn: To deprive of a crown; to take the crown from; hence, to
discrown; to dethrone.
He hath done me wrong, And therefore I'll uncrown him ere't be long.
Shak.
