RECIPE
Rec"i*pe, n.; pl. Recipes. Etym: [L., imperative of recipere to take
back, take in, receive. See Receive.]

Defn: A formulary or prescription for making some combination,
mixture, or preparation of materials; a receipt; especially, a
prescription for medicine.
