ZOLAISM
Zo"la*ism, n.

Defn: The literary theories and practices of the French novelist
Emile Zola (1840-1902); naturalism, esp. in a derogatory sense. --
Zo"la*ist, n. -- Zo`la*is"tic (#), a. -- Zo"la*ize (#), v.
