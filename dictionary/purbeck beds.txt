PURBECK BEDS
Pur"beck beds`. Etym: [So called from the Isle of Purbeck in
England.] (Geol.)

Defn: The strata of the Purbeck stone, or Purbeck limestone,
belonging to the Oölitic group. See the Chart of Geology.
