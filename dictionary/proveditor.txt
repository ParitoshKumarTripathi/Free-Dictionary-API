PROVEDITOR
Pro*ved"i*tor, n. Etym: [It. proveditore, provveditore, fr.
provedere, L. providere. See Provide, and cf. Purveyor, Provedore.]

Defn: One employed to procure supplies, as for an army, a steamer,
etc.; a purveyor; one who provides for another. Jer. Taylor.
