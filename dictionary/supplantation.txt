SUPPLANTATION
Sup`plan*ta"tion, n. Etym: [Cf. F. supplantation, L. supplantatio
hypocritical deceit.]

Defn: The act of supplanting or displacing.
Habitual supplantation of immediate selfishness. Cloeridge.
