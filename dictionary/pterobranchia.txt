PTEROBRANCHIA
Pter`o*bran"chi*a, n. pl. Etym: [NL., fr. Gr. (Zoöl.)

Defn: An order of marine Bryozoa, having a bilobed lophophore and an
axial cord. The genus Rhabdopleura is the type. Called also
Podostomata. See Rhabdopleura.
