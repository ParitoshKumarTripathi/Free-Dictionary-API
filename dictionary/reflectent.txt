REFLECTENT
Re*flect"ent (r*flkt"ent), a. Etym: [L. reflectens, p. pr. of
reflectere. See Reflect.]

1. Bending or flying back; reflected. "The ray descendent, and the
ray reflectent flying with so great a speed." Sir K. Digby.

2. Reflecting; as, a reflectent body. Sir K. Digby.
