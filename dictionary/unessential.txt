UNESSENTIAL
Un`es*sen"tial, a.

1. Not essential; not of prime importance; not indispensable;
unimportant. Addison.

2. Void of essence, or real being. [R.] Milton.
UNESSENTIAL

=====
Un`es*sen"tial, n.

Defn: Something not constituting essence, or something which is not
of absolute necessity; as, forms are among the unessentials of
religion.
