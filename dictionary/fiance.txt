FIANCE
Fi"ance, v. t. Etym: [F. fiancer. See Affiance.]

Defn: To betroth; to affiance. [Obs.] Harmar.
FIANCE

=====
Fi`an`cé", n. Etym: [F.]

Defn: A betrothed man.
