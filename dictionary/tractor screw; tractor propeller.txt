TRACTOR SCREW; TRACTOR PROPELLER
Tractor screw or propeller. (Aviation)

Defn: A propeller screw placed in front of the supporting planes of
an aëroplane instead of behind them, so that it exerts a pull instead
of a push. Hence, Tractor monoplane, Tractor biplane, etc.
