SCIRRHOUS
Scir"rhous, a. Etym: [NL. scirrhosus.] (Med.)

Defn: Proceeding from scirrhus; of the nature of scirrhus; indurated;
knotty; as, scirrhous affections; scirrhous disease. [Written also
skirrhous.]
