EPIPTERYGOID
Ep`ip*ter"y*goid, a. Etym: [Pref. epi- + pterygoid.] (Anat.)

Defn: Situated upon or above the pterygoid bone.
 -- n.

Defn: An epipterygoid bone or cartilage; the columella in the skulls
of many lizards.
