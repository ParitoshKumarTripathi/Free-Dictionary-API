HOLCAD
Hol"cad, n. Etym: [Gr. 'olka`s, -a`dos, a ship which is towed, a ship
of burden, fr. 'e`lkein to draw. Gf. Hulk.]

Defn: A large ship of burden, in ancient Greece. Mitford.
