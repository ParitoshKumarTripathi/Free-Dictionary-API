RAPIDITY
Ra*pid"i*ty, n. Etym: [L. rapiditas: cf. F. rapidité.]

Defn: The quality or state of being rapid; swiftness; celerity;
velocity; as, the rapidity of growth or improvement.

Syn.
 -- -- Rapidness; haste; speed; celerity; velocity; swiftness;
fleetness; quickness; agility.
