COACERVATE
Co`a*cer"vate, a. Etym: [L. coacervatus, p. p. of coacervare to heap
up; co- + acervare. See Acervate.]

Defn: Raised into a pile; collected into a crowd; heaped. [R.] Bacon.
COACERVATE

=====
Co`a*cer"vate, v. t.

Defn: To heap up; to pile. [R.]
