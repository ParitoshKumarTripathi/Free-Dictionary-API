PLUMBAGIN
Plum*ba"gin, n. Etym: [L. plumbago leadwort, fr. plumbum lead; cf. F.
plombagin.] (Chem.)

Defn: A crystalline substance said to be found in the root of a
certain plant of the Leadwort (Plumbago) family.
