ULTRA
Ul"tra, a. Etym: [See Ultra-.]

Defn: Going beyond others, or beyond due limit; extreme; fanatical;
uncompromising; as, an ultra reformer; ultra measures.
ULTRA

=====
Ul"tra, n.

Defn: One who advocates extreme measures; an ultraist; an extremist;
a radical. Brougham.
