UTOPIAN
U*to"pi*an, a.

Defn: Of or pertaining to Utopia; resembling Utopia; hence, ideal;
chimerical; fanciful; founded upon, or involving, imaginary
perfections; as, Utopian projects; Utopian happiness.
UTOPIAN

=====
U*to"pi*an, n.

Defn: An inhabitant of Utopia; hence, one who believes in the
perfectibility of human society; a visionary; an idealist; an
optimist. Hooker.
