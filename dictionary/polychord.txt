POLYCHORD
Pol"y*chord, a. Etym: [Gr.

Defn: Having many strings.
POLYCHORD

=====
Pol"y*chord, n. (Mus.)
(a) A musical instrument of ten strings.
(b) An apparatus for coupling two octave notes, capable of being
attached to a keyed instrument.
