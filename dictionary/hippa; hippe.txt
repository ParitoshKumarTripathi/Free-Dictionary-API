HIPPA; HIPPE
Hip"pa, Hip"pe, n. (Zoöl.)

Defn: A genus of marine decapod crustaceans, which burrow rapidly in
the sand by pushing themselves backward; -- called also bait bug. See
Illust. under Anomura.
