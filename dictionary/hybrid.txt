HYBRID
Hy"brid, n. Etym: [L. hybrida, hibrida, prob. allied to Gr. over: cf.
F. hybride.] (Biol.)

Defn: The offspring of the union of two distinct species; an animal
or plant produced from the mixture of two species. See Mongrel.
HYBRID

=====
Hy"brid, a.

Defn: Produced from the mixture of two species; as, plants of hybrid
nature.
