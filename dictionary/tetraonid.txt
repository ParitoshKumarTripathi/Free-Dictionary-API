TETRAONID
Te*tra"o*nid, n. Etym: [L. tetrao a heath cock, grouse, Gr.
tétraonide.] (Zoöl.)

Defn: A bird belonging to the tribe of which the genus Tetrao is the
type, as the grouse, partridge, quail, and the like. Used also
adjectively.
