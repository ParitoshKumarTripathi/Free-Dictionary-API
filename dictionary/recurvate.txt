RECURVATE
Re*cur"vate (r*kr"vt), a. Etym: [L. recurvatus, p. p. of recurvare.
See Re-, and Curvate.] (Bot.)

Defn: Recurved.
RECURVATE

=====
Re*cur"vate (-vt), v. t.

Defn: To bend or curve back; to recurve. Pennant.
