PRIVATEER
Pri`va*teer" n. Etym: [From Private.]

1. An armed private vessel which bears the commission of the
sovereign power to cruise against the enemy. See Letters of marque,
under Marque.

2. The commander of a privateer.
Kidd soon threw off the character of a privateer and became a pirate.
Macaulay.
PRIVATEER

=====
Pri`va*teer", v. i. [imp. & p. p. Privateered; p. pr. & vb. n.
Privateering.]

Defn: To cruise in a privateer.
