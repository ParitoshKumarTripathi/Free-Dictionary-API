CONFESSIONARY
Con*fes"sion*a*ry, n. Etym: [LL. confessionarium.]

Defn: A confessional. [Obs.] Johnson.
CONFESSIONARY

=====
Con*fes"sion*a*ry, a.

Defn: Pertaining to auricular confession; as, a confessionary litany.
