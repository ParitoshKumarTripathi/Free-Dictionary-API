MOESOGOTHIC
Moe`so*goth"ic, a.

Defn: Belonging to the Moesogoths, a branch of the Goths who settled
in Moesia.
MOESOGOTHIC

=====
Moe`so*goth"ic, n.

Defn: The language of the Moesogoths; -- also called Gothic.
