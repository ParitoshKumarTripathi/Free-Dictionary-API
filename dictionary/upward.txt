UPWARD
Up"ward, a. Etym: [AS. upweard. See Up, and -ward.]

Defn: Directed toward a higher place; as, with upward eye; with
upward course.
UPWARD

=====
Up"ward, n.

Defn: The upper part; the top. [Obs.]
From the extremest upward of thy head. Shak.
