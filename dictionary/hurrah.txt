HURRAH
Hur*rah", n.

Defn: A cheer; a shout of joy, etc. Hurrah's nest, state of utmost
confusion. [Colloq. U.S.]
A perfect hurrah's nest in our kitchen. Mrs. Stowe.
HURRAH

=====
Hur*rah", v. i.

Defn: To utter hurrahs; to huzza.
HURRAH

=====
Hur*rah", v. t.

Defn: To salute, or applaud, with hurrahs.
