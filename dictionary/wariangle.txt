WARIANGLE
War`i*an"gle, n. Etym: [OE. wariangel, weryangle; cf. AS. wearg
outlaw, criminal, OHG, warg, warch, Goth. wargs (in comp.), G.
würgengel, i. e., destroying angel, destroyer, killer, and E. worry.]
(Zoöl.)

Defn: The red-backed shrike (Lanius collurio); -- called also würger,
worrier, and throttler. [Written also warriangle, weirangle, etc.]
[Obs. or Prov. Eng.]
