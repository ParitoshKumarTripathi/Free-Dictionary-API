VILLANIZE
Vil"lan*ize, v. t. [imp. & p. p. Villanized; p. pr. & vb. n.
Villanizing.]

Defn: To make vile; to debase; to degrade; to revile. [R.]
Were virtue by descent, a noble name Could never villanize his
father's fame. Dryden.
