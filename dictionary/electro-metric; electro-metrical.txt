ELECTRO-METRIC; ELECTRO-METRICAL
E*lec`tro-met"ric, E*lec`tro-met"ric*al, a. Etym: [Cf. F.
électrométrique.]

Defn: Pertaining to electrometry; made by means of electrometer; as,
an electrometrical experiment.
