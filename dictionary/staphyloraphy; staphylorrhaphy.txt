STAPHYLORAPHY; STAPHYLORRHAPHY
Staph`y*lor"a*phy, Staph`y*lor"rha*phy, n. Etym: [Gr.
staphylorraphie.]

Defn: The operation of uniting a cleft palate, consisting in paring
and bringing together the edges of the cleft.
 -- Staph`y*lo*raph"ic, Staph`y*lor*rhaph"ic, a.
