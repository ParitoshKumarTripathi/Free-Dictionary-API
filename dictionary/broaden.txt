BROADEN
Broad"en, v. t. [p. & p. p. Broadened (; p. pr. & vb.n. Broadening
(.] Etym: [From Broad, a.]

Defn: To grow broad; to become broader or wider.
The broadening sun appears. Wordsworth.
BROADEN

=====
Broad"en, v. t.

Defn: To make broad or broader; to render more broad or
comprehensive.
