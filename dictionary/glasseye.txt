GLASSEYE
Glass"eye`, n.

1. (Zoöl.)

Defn: A fish of the great lakes; the wall-eyed pike.

2. (Far.)

Defn: A species of blindness in horses in which the eye is bright and
the pupil dilated; a sort of amaurosis. Youatt.
