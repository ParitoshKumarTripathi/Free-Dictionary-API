SEA LION
Sea" li"on. (Zoöl.)

Defn: Any one of several large species of seals of the family
Otariidæ native of the Pacific Ocean, especially the southern sea
lion (Otaria jubata) of the South American coast; the northern sea
lion (Eumetopias Stelleri) found from California to Japan; and the
black, or California, sea lion (Zalophus Californianus), which is
common on the rocks near San Francisco.
