HETERODONT
Het"er*o*dont, a. Etym: [Hetero- + Gr. (Anat.)

Defn: Having the teeth differentiated into incisors, canines, and
molars, as in man; -- opposed to homodont.
HETERODONT

=====
Het"er*o*dont, n. (Zoöl.)

Defn: Any animal with heterodont dentition.
