RUMINANT
Ru"mi*nant, a. Etym: [L. ruminans, -antis, p.pr.: cf. F. ruminant.
See Ruminate.] (Zoöl.)

Defn: Chewing the cud; characterized by chewing again what has been
swallowed; of or pertaining to the Ruminantia.
RUMINANT

=====
Ru"mi*nant, n. (Zoöl.)

Defn: A ruminant animal; one of the Ruminantia.
