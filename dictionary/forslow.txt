FORSLOW
For*slow", v. t. Etym: [Pref. for- + slow.]

Defn: To delay; to hinder; to neglect; to put off. [Obs.] Bacon.
FORSLOW

=====
For*slow", v. i.

Defn: To loiter. [Obs.] Shak.
