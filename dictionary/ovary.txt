OVARY
O"va*ry, n.; pl. Ovaries. Etym: [NL. ovarium, fr. L. ovum egg: cf. F.
ovaire. See Oval.]

1. (Bot.)

Defn: That part of the pistil which contains the seed, and in most
flowering plants develops into the fruit. See Illust. of Flower.

2. (Zoöl. & Anat.)

Defn: The essential female reproductive organ in which the ova are
produced. See Illust. of Discophora.
