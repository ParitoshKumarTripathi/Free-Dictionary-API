UNDAUNTED
Un*daunt"ed, a.

Defn: Not daunted; not subdued or depressed by fear. Shak.

Syn.
 -- Bold; fearless; brave; courageous; intrepid.
 -- Un*daunt"ed*ly, adv.
 -- Un*daunt"ed*ness, n.
