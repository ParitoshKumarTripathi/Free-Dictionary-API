SCALL
Scall, n. Etym: [Icel. skalli a bald head. Cf. Scald, a.]

Defn: A scurf or scabby disease, especially of the scalp.
It is a dry scall, even a leprosy upon the head. Lev. xiii. 30.
SCALL

=====
Scall, a.

Defn: Scabby; scurfy. [Obs.] Shak.
