WARRIN
War"rin, n. Etym: [From a native name.] (Zoöl.)

Defn: An Australian lorikeet (Trichoglossus multicolor) remarkable
for the variety and brilliancy of its colors; -- called also blue-
bellied lorikeet, and blue-bellied parrot.
