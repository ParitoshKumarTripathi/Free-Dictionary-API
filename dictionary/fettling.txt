FETTLING
Fet"tling, n.

1. (Metal.)

Defn: A mixture of ore, cinders, etc., used to line the hearth of a
puddling furnace. [Eng.] [It is commonly called fix in the United
States.]

2. (Pottery)

Defn: The operation of shaving or smoothing the surface of undried
clay ware.
