SUSPENSORY
Sus*pen"so*ry, a.

1. Suspended; hanging; depending.

2. Fitted or serving to suspend; suspending; as, a suspensory muscle.
Ray.

3. (Anat.)

Defn: Of or pertaining to a suspensorium.
SUSPENSORY

=====
Sus*pen"so*ry, n. Etym: [Cf. F. suspensoir, suspensoire.]

Defn: That which suspends, or holds up, as a truss; specifically
(Med.),

Defn: a bandage or bag for supporting the scrotum.
