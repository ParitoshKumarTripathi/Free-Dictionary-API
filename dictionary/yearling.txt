YEARLING
Year"ling, n. Etym: [Year + -ling.]

Defn: An animal one year old, or in the second year of its age; --
applied chiefly to cattle, sheep, and horses.
YEARLING

=====
Year"ling, a.

Defn: Being a year old. "A yearling bullock to thy name small smoke."
Pope.
