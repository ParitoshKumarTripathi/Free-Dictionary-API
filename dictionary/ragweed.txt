RAGWEED
Rag"weed, n. (Bot.)

Defn: A common American composite weed (Ambrosia artemisiæfolia) with
finely divided leaves; hogweed. Great ragweed, a coarse American herb
(Ambrosia trifida), with rough three-lobed opposite leaves.
