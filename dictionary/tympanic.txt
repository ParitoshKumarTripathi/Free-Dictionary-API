TYMPANIC
Tym*pan"ic, a. Etym: [See Tympanum.]

1. Like a tympanum or drum; acting like a drumhead; as, a tympanic
membrane.

2. (Anat.)

Defn: Of or pertaining to the tympanum. Tympanic bone (Anat.), a bone
of the skull which incloses a part of the tympanum and supports the
tympanic membrane.
 -- Tympanic membrane. (Anat.) See the Note under Ear.
TYMPANIC

=====
Tym*pan"ic, n. (Anat.)

Defn: The tympanic bone.
