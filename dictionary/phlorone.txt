PHLORONE
Phlo"rone, n. Etym: [Phlorol + quinone.] (Chem.)

Defn: A yellow crystalline substance having a peculiar unpleasant
odor, resembling the quinones, and obtained from beechwood tar and
coal tar, as also by the oxidation of xylidine; -- called also
xyloquinone.
