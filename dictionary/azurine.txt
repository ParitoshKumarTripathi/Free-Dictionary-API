AZURINE
Az"u*rine, a. Etym: [Cf. Azurn.]

Defn: Azure.
AZURINE

=====
Az"u*rine, n. (Zoöl.)

Defn: The blue roach of Europe (Leuciscus cæruleus); -- so called
from its color.
