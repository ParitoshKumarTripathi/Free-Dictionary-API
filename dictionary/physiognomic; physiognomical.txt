PHYSIOGNOMIC; PHYSIOGNOMICAL
Phys`i*og*nom"ic, Phys`i*og*nom"ic*al, a. Etym: [Gr.
physiognomonique.]

Defn: Of or pertaining to physiognomy; according with the principles
of physiognomy.
 -- Phys`i*og*nom"ic*al*ly, adv.
