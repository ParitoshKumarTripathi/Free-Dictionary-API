GUILLOCHE
Guil"loche`, n. Etym: [F. guillochis; -- said to be fr. Guillot, the
inventor of a machine for carving it.] (Arch.)

Defn: An ornament in the form of two or more bands or strings twisted
over each other in a continued series, leaving circular openings
which are filled with round ornaments.
