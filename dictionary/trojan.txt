TROJAN
Tro"jan, a. Etym: [L. Trojanus, fr. Troja, Troia, Troy, from Tros,
Gr. Trw`s, Trwo`s, Tros, the mythical founder of Troy.]

Defn: Of or pertaining to ancient Troy or its inhabitants.
 -- n.

Defn: A native or inhabitant of Troy. Tim jumped like a Trojan from
the bed. Finnegan's Wake (Irish song)
