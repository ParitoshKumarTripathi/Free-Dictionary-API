URBAN
Ur"ban, a. Etym: [L. urbanus belonging to the urbs, urbis, a city:
cf. F. urbain. Cf. Urbane.]

1. Of or belonging to a city or town; as, an urban population.

2. Belonging to, or suiting, those living in a city; cultivated;
polite; urbane; as, urban manners. Urban servitude. See Predial
servitude, under Servitude.
