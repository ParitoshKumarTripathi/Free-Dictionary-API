HALF TONE; HALF-TONE
Half tone, or Half"-tone`, n.

1. (Fine Arts)
 (a) An intermediate or middle tone in a painting, engraving,
photograph, etc.; a middle tint, neither very dark nor very light.
 (b) A half-tone photo-engraving.

2.  (Music) A half step.
