RECENCY
Re"cen*cy, n. Etym: [LL. recentia, fr. L. recens. See Recent.]

Defn: The state or quality of being recent; newness; new state; late
origin; lateness in time; freshness; as, the recency of a
transaction, of a wound, etc.
