DISIMPROVE
Dis`im*prove", v. t.

Defn: To make worse; -- the opposite of improve. [R.] Jer. Taylor.
DISIMPROVE

=====
Dis`im*prove", v. i.

Defn: To grow worse; to deteriorate.
