MANGLER
Man"gler, n. Etym: [See 1st Mangle.]

Defn: One who mangles or tears in cutting; one who mutilates any work
in doing it.
MANGLER

=====
Man"gler, n. Etym: [See 3d Mangle.]

Defn: One who smooths with a mangle.
