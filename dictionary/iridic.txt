IRIDIC
I*rid"ic, a.

Defn: Of or pertaining to the iris of the eye.
IRIDIC

=====
I*rid"ic, a. (Chem.)

Defn: Of or pertaining to iridium; -- said specifically of those
compounds in which iridium has a relatively high valence.
