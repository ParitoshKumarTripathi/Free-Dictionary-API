ARCHY
Arch"y, a.

Defn: Arched; as, archy brows.
ARCHY

=====
*ar"chy. Etym: [Gr. Arch-, pref.]

Defn: A suffix properly meaning a rule, ruling, as in monarchy, the
rule of one only. Cf. -arch.
