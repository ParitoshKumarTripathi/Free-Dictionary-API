OVIPOSIT
O`vi*pos"it, v. i. [imp. & p. p. Oviposited; p. pr. & vb. n.
Ovipositing.] Etym: [See Ovum, and Posit.]

Defn: To lay or deposit eggs; -- said esp. of insects.
OVIPOSIT

=====
O`vi*pos"it, v. t.

Defn: To deposit or lay (an egg).
