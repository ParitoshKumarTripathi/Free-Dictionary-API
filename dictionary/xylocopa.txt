XYLOCOPA
Xy*loc"o*pa, n. Etym: [NL., fr. Gr. xy`lon wood + (Zoöl.)

Defn: A genus of hymenopterous insects including the carpenter. See
Carpenter bee, under Carpenter.
 -- Xy*loc"o*pine, a.
