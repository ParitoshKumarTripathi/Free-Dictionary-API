VOCIFERATE
Vo*cif"er*ate, v. i. Etym: [L. vociferatus, p. p. vociferari to
vociferate; vox, vocis, voice + ferre to bear. See Voice, and Bear to
carry.]

Defn: To cry out with vehemence; to exclaim; to bawl; to clamor.
Cowper.
VOCIFERATE

=====
Vo*cif"er*ate, v. t. [imp. & p. p. Vociferated; p. pr. & vb. n.
Vociferating.]

Defn: To utter with a loud voice; to shout out.
Though he may vociferate the word liberty. V. Knox.
