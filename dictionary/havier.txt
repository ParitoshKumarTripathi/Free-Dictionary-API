HAVIER
Hav"ier, n. [Formerly haver, prob. fr. Half; cf. L. semimas
emasculated, prop., half male.]

Defn: A castrated deer.

Haviers, or stags which have been gelded when young, have no horns.
Encyc. of Sport.
