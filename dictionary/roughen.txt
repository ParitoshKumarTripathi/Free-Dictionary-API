ROUGHEN
Rough"en, v. t. [imp. & p. p. Roughened; p. pr. & vb. n. Roughening.]
Etym: [From Rough.]

Defn: To make rough.
ROUGHEN

=====
Rough"en, v. i.

Defn: To grow or become rough.
