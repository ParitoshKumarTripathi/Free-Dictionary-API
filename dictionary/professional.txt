PROFESSIONAL
Pro*fes"sion*al, a.

1. Of or pertaining to a profession, or calling; conforming to the
rules or standards of a profession; following a profession; as,
professional knowledge; professional conduct. "Pride, not personal,
but professional." Macaulay. "A professional sneerer." De Quincey.

2. Engaged in by professionals; as, a professional race; -- opposed
to amateur.
PROFESSIONAL

=====
Pro*fes"sion*al, n.

Defn: A person who prosecutes anything professionally, or for a
livelihood, and not in the character of an amateur; a professional
worker.
