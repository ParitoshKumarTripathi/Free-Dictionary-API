TRACHELIPODA
Tra`che*lip"o*da, n.pl. Etym: [NL. See Trachelipod.] (Zoöl.)

Defn: An extensive artificial group of gastropods comprising all
those which have a spiral shell and the foot attached to the base of
the neck.
