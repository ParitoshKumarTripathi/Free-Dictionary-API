GYNOCRACY
Gy*noc"ra*cy, n. Etym: [See Gynecocracy.]

Defn: Female government; gynecocracy.
The aforesaid state has repeatedly changed from absolute despotism to
republicanism, not forgetting the intermediate stages of oligarchy,
limited monarchy, and even gynocracy; for I myself remember Alsatia
governed for nearly nine months by an old fishwoman. Sir H. Scott.
