OBSTRUENT
Ob"stru*ent, a. Etym: [L. obstruens, p.pr. of obstruere. See
Obstruct.]

Defn: Causing obstruction; blocking up; hindering; as, an obstruent
medicine. Johnson.
OBSTRUENT

=====
Ob"stru*ent, n.

Defn: Anything that obstructs or closes a passage; esp., that which
obstructs natural passages in the body; as, a medicine which acts as
an obstruent.
