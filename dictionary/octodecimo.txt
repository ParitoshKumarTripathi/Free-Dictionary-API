OCTODECIMO
Oc`todec"i*mo, a. Etym: [L. octodecim eighteen. See Octavo, Decimal,
and -mo.]

Defn: Having eighteen leaves to a sheet; as, an octodecimo form,
book, leaf, size, etc.
OCTODECIMO

=====
Oc`to*dec"i*mo, n.; pl. Octodecimos (.

Defn: A book composed of sheets each of which is folded into eighteen
leaves; hence; indicating more or less definitely a size of book,
whose sheets are so folded; -- usually written 18mo or 18ยบ, and
called eighteenmo.
