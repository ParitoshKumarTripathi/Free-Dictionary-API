HAIRSPLITTING
Hair"split`ting, a.

Defn: Making excessively nice or trivial distinctions in reasoning;
subtle.
 -- n.

Defn: The act or practice of making trivial distinctions.
The ancient hairsplitting technicalities of special pleading. Charles
Sumner.
