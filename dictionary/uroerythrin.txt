UROERYTHRIN
U`ro*e*ryth"rin, n. Etym: [See 1st Uro-, and Erythrin.] (Physiol.
Chem.)

Defn: A reddish urinary pigment, considered as the substance which
gives to the urine of rheumatism its characteristic color. It also
causes the red color often seen in deposits of urates.
