PATENA
Pat"e*na, n. Etym: [LL.] (Eccl.)

Defn: A paten.
PATENA

=====
Pa*te"na, n. Etym: [Cf. Pg. patena a paten.]

Defn: A grassy expanse in the hill region of Ceylon.
