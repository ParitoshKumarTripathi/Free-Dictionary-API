RHEIC
Rhe"ic, a. Etym: [NL. Rheum rhubarb, Gr. Rhubarb.] (Chem.)

Defn: Pertaining to, or designating, an acid (commonly called
chrysophanic acid) found in rhubarb (Rheum). [Obsoles.]
