PITTER-PATTER
Pit"ter-pat`ter, n.

Defn: A sound like that of alternating light beats. Also, a pattering
of words.
PITTER-PATTER

=====
Pit"ter-pat`ter, adv.

Defn: With, or with the sound of, alternating light beats; as, his
heart went pitter-patter.
