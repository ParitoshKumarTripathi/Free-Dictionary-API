PRESERVATORY
Pre*serv"a*to*ry, a.

Defn: Preservative. Bp. Hall.
PRESERVATORY

=====
Pre*serv"a*to*ry, n.; pl. Preservatories (.

1. A preservative. [Obs.] Whitlock.

2. A room, or apparatus, in which perishable things, as fruit,
vegetables, etc., can be preserved without decay.
