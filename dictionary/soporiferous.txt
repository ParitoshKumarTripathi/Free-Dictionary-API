SOPORIFEROUS
Sop`o*rif"er*ous, a. Etym: [L. soparifer; sopor a heavy sleep +
ferere to bring.]

Defn: Causing sleep; somniferous; soporific. "Soporiferous medicine."
Swift. --- Sop`o*rif"er*ous*ly, adv.
 -- Sop`o*rif"er*ous*ness, n.
