INTERMIX
In`ter*mix", v. t.

Defn: To mix together; to intermingle.
In yonder spring of roses, intermixed With myrtle, find what to
redress till noon. Milton.
INTERMIX

=====
In`ter*mix", v. i.

Defn: To be mixed together; to be intermingled.
