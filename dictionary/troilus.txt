TROILUS
Tro"i*lus, n.; pl. L. Troili, E. Troiluses. Etym: [NL., fr. L.
Troilus, Gr. (Zoöl.)

Defn: A large, handsome American butterfly (Euphoeades, or Papilio,
troilus). It is black, with yellow marginal spots on the front wings,
and blue spots on the rear wings.
