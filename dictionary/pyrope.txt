PYROPE
Pyr"ope, n. Etym: [L. pyropus a kind of red bronze, fr. Gr. pyrope.]
(Min.)

Defn: A variety of garnet, of a poppy or blood-red color, frequently
with a tinge of orange. It is used as a gem. See the Note under
Garnet.
