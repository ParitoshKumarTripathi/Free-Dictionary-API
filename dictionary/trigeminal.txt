TRIGEMINAL
Tri*gem"i*nal, a. Etym: [See Trigeminous.] (Anat.)

Defn: Of, pertaining to, or designating, the fifth pair of cranial
nerves, which divide on each side of the head into three main
branches distributed to the orbits, jaws, and parts of the mouth;
trifacial.
