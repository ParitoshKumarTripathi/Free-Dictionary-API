TRUAGE
Tru"age, n. Etym: [Cf. OF. truage a tax. See True.]

1. A pledge of truth or peace made on payment of a tax. [Obs.] Ld.
Berners.

2. A tax or impost; tribute. [Obs.] R. of Gloucester.
