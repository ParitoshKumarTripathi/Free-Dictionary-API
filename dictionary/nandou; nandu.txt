NANDOU; NANDU
Nan"dou, Nan"du, n. Etym: [Braz. nhandu or yandu.] (Zoöl.)

Defn: Any one of three species of South American ostriches of the
genera Rhea and Pterocnemia. See Rhea. [Written also nandow.]
