QUAMOCLIT
Quam"o*clit, n. Etym: [Gr. (Bot.)

Defn: Formerly, a genus of plants including the cypress vine
(Quamoclit vulgaris, now called Ipomoea Quamoclit). The genus is now
merged in Ipomoea.
