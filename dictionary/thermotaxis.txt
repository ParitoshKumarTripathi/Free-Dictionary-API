THERMOTAXIS
Ther`mo*tax"is, n. [NL.; thermo-+ Gr.  an arranging.] (Physiol.)
 (a) The property possessed by protoplasm of moving under the
influence of heat.
 (b) Determination of the direction of locomotion by heat.
