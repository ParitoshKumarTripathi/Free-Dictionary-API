IRRECONCILABLE
Ir*rec"on*ci`la*ble, a. Etym: [Pref. ir- not + reconcilable: cf. F.
irréconciliable.]

Defn: Not reconcilable; implacable; incompatible; inconsistent;
disagreeing; as, irreconcilable enemies, statements.
 -- Ir*rec"on*ci`la*ble*ness, n.
 -- Ir*rec"on*ci`la*bly, adv.
