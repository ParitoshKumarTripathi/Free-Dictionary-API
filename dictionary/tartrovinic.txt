TARTROVINIC
Tar`tro*vin"ic, a. Etym: [Tartro- + vinic.] (Chem.)

Defn: Of, pertaining to, or designating, a certain acid composed of
tartaric acid in combination with ethyl, and now called ethyltartaric
acid.
