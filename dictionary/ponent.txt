PONENT
Po"nent, a. Etym: [OF., fr. It. ponente, properly, setting (applied
to the setting sun), fr. L. ponens, p. pr. of ponere to set, put.]

Defn: Western; occidental. [R.]
Forth rush the levant and the ponent winds. Milton.
