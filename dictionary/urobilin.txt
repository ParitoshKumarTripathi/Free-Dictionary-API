UROBILIN
U`ro*bi"lin, n. Etym: [1st uro- + bile + -in.] (Physiol. Chem.)

Defn: A yellow pigment identical with hydrobilirubin, abundant in the
highly colored urine of fever, and also present in normal urine. See
Urochrome.
