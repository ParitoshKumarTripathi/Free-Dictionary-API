HADE
Hade, n. Etym: [Cf. heald inclined, bowed down, G. halde declivity.]

1. The descent of a hill. [Obs.]

2. (Mining)

Defn: The inclination or deviation from the vertical of any mineral
vein.
HADE

=====
Hade, v. i. (Mining)

Defn: To deviate from the vertical; -- said of a vein, fault, or
lode.
