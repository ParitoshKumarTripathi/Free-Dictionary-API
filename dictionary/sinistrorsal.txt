SINISTRORSAL
Sin`is*tror"sal, a. Etym: [L.sinistrorsus, sinistroversus, turned
toward the left side; sinister left + vertere, vortere, versum,
vorsum, to turn.]

Defn: Rising spirally from right to left (of the spectator);
sinistrorse.
