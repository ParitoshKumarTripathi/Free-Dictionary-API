REIMBURSABLE
Re`im*burs"a*ble (r`m*brs"*b'l), a. Etym: [CF. F. remboursable.]

Defn: Capable of being repaid; repayable.
A loan has been made of two millions of dollars, reimbursable in ten
years. A. Hamilton.
