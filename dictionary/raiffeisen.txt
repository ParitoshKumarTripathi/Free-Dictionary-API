RAIFFEISEN
Raif"fei`sen, a. (Economics)

Defn: Designating, or pertaining to, a form of coöperative bank
founded among the German agrarian population by Friedrich Wilhelm
Raiffeisen (1818-88); as, Raiffeisen banks, the Raiffeisen system,
etc. The banks are unlimited-liability institutions making small
loans at a low rate of interest, for a designated purpose, to worthy
members only.
