JAPANESE
Jap`a*nese", a.

Defn: Of or pertaining to Japan, or its inhabitants.
JAPANESE

=====
Jap`a*nese", n. sing. & pl.

1. A native or inhabitant of Japan; collectively, the people of
Japan.

2. sing.

Defn: The language of the people of Japan.
