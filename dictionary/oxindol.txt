OXINDOL
Ox*in"dol, n. Etym: [Oxygen + indol.] (Chem.)

Defn: A white crystalline nitrogenous substance (C8H7NO) of the indol
group, obtained by the reduction of dioxindol. It is a so-called
lactam compound.
