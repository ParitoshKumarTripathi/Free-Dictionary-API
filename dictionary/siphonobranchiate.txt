SIPHONOBRANCHIATE
Si`pho*no*bran"chi*ate, a. (Zoöl.)

Defn: Having a siphon, or siphons, to convey water to the gills;
belonging or pertaining to the Siphonobranchiata.
 -- n.

Defn: One of the Siphonobranchiata.
