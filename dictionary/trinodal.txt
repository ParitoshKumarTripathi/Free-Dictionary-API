TRINODAL
Tri*nod"al, a. Etym: [L. trinodis three-knotted; tri- (see Tri-) +
nodus knot.]

1. (Bot.)

Defn: Having three knots or nodes; having three points from which a
leaf may shoot; as, a trinodal stem.

2. (Geom.)

Defn: Having three nodal points.
