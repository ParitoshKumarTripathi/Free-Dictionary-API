SPIRIFER
Spi"ri*fer, n. Etym: [NL., fr. L. spira a coil + ferreto bear.]
(Paleon.)

Defn: Any one of numerous species of fossil brachipods of the genus
Spirifer, or Delthyris, and allied genera, in which the long
calcareous supports of the arms form a large spiral, or helix, on
each side.
