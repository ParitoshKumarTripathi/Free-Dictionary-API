FRANC-TIREUR
Franc"-ti`reur", n. [F., fr. franc free + tireur shooter, fr. tirer
to shoot.] (Mil.)

Defn: A French partisan soldier, or one belonging to a corps of
detached light troops engaged in forays, skirmishes, scouting, etc.
