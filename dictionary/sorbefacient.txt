SORBEFACIENT
Sor`be*fa"cient, a. Etym: [L. sorbere to suck in, absorb + faciens,
p.pr. of facere to make.] (Med.)

Defn: Producing absorption.
 -- n.

Defn: A medicine or substance which produces absorption.
