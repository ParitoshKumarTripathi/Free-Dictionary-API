REP
Rep (rp), n. Etym: [Prob. a corruption of rib: cf. F. reps.]

Defn: A fabric made of silk or wool, or of silk and wool, and having
a transversely corded or ribbed surface.
REP

=====
Rep, a.

Defn: Formed with a surface closely corded, or ribbed transversely; -
- applied to textile fabrics of silk or wool; as, rep silk.
