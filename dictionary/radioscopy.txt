RADIOSCOPY
Ra`di*os"co*py, n.  [Radio- + -scopy.]

Defn: Direct observation of objects opaque to light by means of some
other form of radiant energy, as the Röntgen rays. -- Ra`di*o*scop"ic
(#), *scop"ic*al (#), a.
