VOLT
Volt, n. Etym: [F. volte; cf. It. volta. See Vault.]

1. (Man.)

Defn: A circular tread; a gait by which a horse going sideways round
a center makes two concentric tracks.

2. (Fencing)

Defn: A sudden movement to avoid a thrust.
