TOLYL
Tol"yl, n. Etym: [Toluic + -yl.] (Chem.)

Defn: The hydrocarbon radical, CH3.C6H4, regarded as characteristic
of certain compounds of the aromatic series related to toluene; as,
tolyl carbinol.
