REVIVIFICATION
Re*viv`i*fi*ca"tion, n. Etym: [Cf. F. révivification.]

1. Renewal of life; restoration of life; the act of recaling, or the
state of being recalled, to life.

2. (Old Chem.)

Defn: The reduction of a metal from a state of combination to its
metallic state.
