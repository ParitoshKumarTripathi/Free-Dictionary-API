ALONELY
A*lone"ly, adv.

Defn: Only; merely; singly. [Obs.]
This said spirit was not given alonely unto him, but unto all his
heirs and posterity. Latimer.
ALONELY

=====
A*lone"ly, a.

Defn: Exclusive. [Obs.] Fabyan.
