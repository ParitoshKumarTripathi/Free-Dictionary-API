NUTCRACKER
Nut"crack`er, n.

1. An instrument for cracking nuts.

2. (Zoöl.)
(a) A European bird (Nucifraga caryocatactes), allied to the magpie
and crow. Its color is dark brown, spotted with white. It feeds on
nuts, seeds, and insects.
(b) The American, or Clarke's, nutcracker (Picicorvus Columbianus) of
Western North America.
