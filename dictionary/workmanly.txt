WORKMANLY
Work"man*ly, a.

Defn: Becoming a skillful workman; skillful; well performed;
workmanlike.
WORKMANLY

=====
Work"man*ly, adv.

Defn: In a skillful manner; in a manner becoming a skillful workman.
