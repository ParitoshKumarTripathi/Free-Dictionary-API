VITRUVIAN
Vi*tru"vi*an, a.

Defn: Of or pertaining to Vitruvius, an ancient Roman architect.
Vitruvian scroll (Arch.), a name given to a peculiar pattern of
scrollwork, consisting of convolved undulations. It is used in
classical architecture. Oxf. Gloss.
