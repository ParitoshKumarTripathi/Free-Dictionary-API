RINGER
Ring"er, n.

1. One who, or that which, rings; especially, one who rings chimes on
bells.

2. (Mining)

Defn: A crowbar. Simmonds.
RINGER

=====
Ring"er, n. (Horse Racing)

Defn: A horse that is not entitled to take part in a race, but is
fraudulently got into it.
