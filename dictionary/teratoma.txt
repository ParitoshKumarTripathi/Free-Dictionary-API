TERATOMA
Ter`a*to"ma, n. Etym: [NL., fr. Gr. -oma.] (Med.)

Defn: A tumor, sometimes found in newborn children, which is made up
of a heterigenous mixture of tissues, as of bone, cartilage and
muscle.
