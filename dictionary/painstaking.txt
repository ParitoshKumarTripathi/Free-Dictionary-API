PAINSTAKING
Pains"tak`ing, a.

Defn: Careful in doing; diligent; faithful; attentive. "Painstaking
men." Harris.
PAINSTAKING

=====
Pains"tak`ing, n.

Defn: The act of taking pains; carefulness and fidelity in
performance. Beau. & Fl.
