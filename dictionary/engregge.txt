ENGREGGE
En*greg"ge, v. t. Etym: [OF. engregier, from (assumed) LL.
ingreviare; in + (assumed) grevis heavy, for L. gravis. Cf.
Aggravate.]

Defn: To aggravate; to make worse; to lie heavy on. [Obs.] Chaucer.
