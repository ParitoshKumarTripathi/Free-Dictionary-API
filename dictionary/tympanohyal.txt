TYMPANOHYAL
Tym`pa*no*hy"al, a. (Anat.)

Defn: Of or pertaining to the tympanum and the hyoidean arch.
 -- n.

Defn: The proximal segment in the hyoidean arch, becoming a part of
the styloid process of the temporal bone in adult man.
