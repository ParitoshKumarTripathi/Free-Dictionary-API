PORTULACACEOUS
Por`tu*la*ca"ceous, a. (Bot.)

Defn: Of or pertaining to a natural order of plants (Portulacaceæ),
of which Portulaca is the type, and which includes also the spring
beauty (Claytonia) and other genera.
