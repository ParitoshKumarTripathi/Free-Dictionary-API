USURARIOUS; USURARY
U`su*ra"ri*ous, U"su*ra*ry, a. Etym: [L. usurarius that serves for
use, that pays interest. See Usurer.]

Defn: Usurious. [Obs.] "Usurarious contracts." Jer. Taylor. Bp. Hall.
