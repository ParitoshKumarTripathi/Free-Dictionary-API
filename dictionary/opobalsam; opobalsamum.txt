OPOBALSAM; OPOBALSAMUM
Op`o*bal"sam, Op`o*bal"sa*mum, n. Etym: [L. opobalsamum, Gr. (Med.)

Defn: The old name of the aromatic resinous juice of the
Balsamodendron opobalsamum, now commonly called balm of Gilead. See
under Balm.
