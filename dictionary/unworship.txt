UNWORSHIP
Un*wor"ship, v. t. Etym: [1st pref. un- + worship.]

Defn: To deprive of worship or due honor; to dishonor. [Obs.] Wyclif.
UNWORSHIP

=====
Un*wor"ship, n. Etym: [Pref. un- not + worship.]

Defn: Lack of worship or respect; dishonor. [Obs.] Gower.
