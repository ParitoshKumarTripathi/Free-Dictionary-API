SIPUNCULOID
Si*pun"cu*loid, a. Etym: [NL. Sipunculus, the typical genus + -oid.]
(Zoöl.)

Defn: Pertaining to the Sipunculoidea.
 -- n.

Defn: One of the Sipunculoidea.
