PHTHALIDE
Phthal"ide, n. Etym: [Phthalyl + anhydride.] (Chem.)

Defn: A lactone obtained by reduction of phthalyl chloride, as a
white crystalline substance; hence, by extension, any one of the
series of which phthalide proper is the type. [Written also
phthalid.]
