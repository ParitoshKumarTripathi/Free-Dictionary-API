CAUSELESS
Cause"less, a.

Defn: 1. Self-originating; uncreated.

2. Without just or sufficient reason; groundless.
My fears are causeless and ungrounded. Denham.
CAUSELESS

=====
Cause"less, adv.

Defn: Without cause or reason.
