REMURMUR
Re*mur"mur (r-mr"mr), v. t. & i. Etym: [Pref. re- + murmur: cf. F.
remurmurare.]

Defn: To murmur again; to utter back, or reply, in murmurs.
The trembling trees, in every plain and wood, Her fate remurmur to
the silver flood. Pope.
