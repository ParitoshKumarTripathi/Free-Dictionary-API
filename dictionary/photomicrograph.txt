PHOTOMICROGRAPH
Pho`to*mi"cro*graph, n. Etym: [Photo- + micro + -graph.]

1. An enlarged or macroscopic photograph of a microscopic object. See
Microphotograph.

2. A microscopically small photograph of an object.
