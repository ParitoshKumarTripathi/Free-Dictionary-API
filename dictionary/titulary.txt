TITULARY
Tit"u*la*ry, n.; pl. Titularies. Etym: [Cf. F. titulaire.]

Defn: A person invested with a title, in virtue of which he holds an
office or benefice, whether he performs the duties of it or not.
TITULARY

=====
Tit"u*la*ry, a.

1. Consisting in a title; titular.

2. Of or pertaining to a title.
