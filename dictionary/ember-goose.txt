EMBER-GOOSE
Em"ber-goose`, n. Etym: [Cf. Norw. ember, hav-imber, hav-immer, Icel.
himbrin, himbrimi.] (Zoöl.)

Defn: The loon or great northern diver. See Loon. [Written also
emmer-goose and imber-goose.]
