WEDNESDAY
Wednes"day (; 48), n. Etym: [OE. wednesdai, wodnesdei, AS. Wodnes
dæg, i. e., Woden's day (a translation of L. dies Mercurii); fr.
Woden the highest god of the Teutonic peoples, but identified with
the Roman god Mercury; akin to OS. Wodan, OHG. Wuotan, Icel. Oedhinn,
D. woensdag Wednesday, Icel. oedhinsdagr, Dan. & Sw. onsdag. See Day,
and cf. Woden, Wood, a.]

Defn: The fourth day of the week; the next day after Tuesday. Ash
Wednesday. See in the Vocabulary.
