HOMOGRAPH
Hom"o*graph, n. Etym: [Gr. "omo`grafos with the same letters; "omo`s
the same + gra`fein to write.] (Philol.)

Defn: One of two or more words identical in orthography, but having
different derivations and meanings; as, fair, n., a market, and fair,
a., beautiful.
