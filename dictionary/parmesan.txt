PARMESAN
Par`me*san", a. Etym: [F. parmesan, It. parmigiano.]

Defn: Of or pertaining to Parma in Italy. Parmesan cheese, a kind of
cheese of a rich flavor, though from skimmed milk, made in Parma,
Italy.
