UNHEAL
Un*heal", n. Etym: [Pref. un- not + heal health.]

Defn: Misfortune; calamity; sickness. [Obs.] Chaucer.
UNHEAL

=====
Un*heal", v. t.

Defn: To uncover. See Unhele. [Obs.]
