TOPOGRAPHER
To*pog"ra*pher, n. Etym: [Cf. F. topographe, Cr.

Defn: One who is skilled in the science of topography; one who
describes a particular place, town, city, or tract of land.
Dante is the one authorized topographer of the mediæval hell. Milman.
