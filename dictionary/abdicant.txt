ABDICANT
Ab"di*cant, a. Etym: [L. abdicans, p.pr. of abdicare.]

Defn: Abdicating; renouncing; -- followed by of.
Monks abdicant of their orders. Whitlock.
ABDICANT

=====
Ab"di*cant, n.

Defn: One who abdicates. Smart.
