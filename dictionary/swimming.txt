SWIMMING
Swim"ming, a.

1. That swims; capable of swimming; adapted to, or used in, swimming;
as, a swimming bird; a swimming motion.

2. Suffused with moisture; as, swimming eyes. Swimming bell (Zoöl.),
a nectocalyx. See Illust. under Siphonophora.
 -- Swimming crab (Zoöl.), any one of numerous species of marine
crabs, as those of the family Protunidæ, which have some of the
joints of one or more pairs of legs flattened so as to serve as fins.
SWIMMING

=====
Swim"ming, n.

Defn: The act of one who swims.
SWIMMING

=====
Swim"ming, a. Etym: [From Swim to be dizzy.]

Defn: Being in a state of vertigo or dizziness; as, a swimming brain.
SWIMMING

=====
Swim"ming, n.

Defn: Vertigo; dizziness; as, a swimming in the head. Dryden.
