TRIMORPHIC; TRIMORPHOUS
Tri*mor"phic, Tri*mor"phous, a. Etym: [Gr. Tri-) +

Defn: Of, pertaining to, or characterized by, trimorphism; --
contrasted with monomorphic, dimorphic, and polymorphic.
