RHIZOSTOMATA
Rhi`zo*stom"a*ta, n.pl. Etym: [NL., fr. Gr. (Zoöl.)

Defn: A suborder of Medusæ which includes very large species without
marginal tentacles, but having large mouth lobes closely united at
the edges. See Illust. in Appendix.
