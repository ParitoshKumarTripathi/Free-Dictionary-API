UNPOPE
Un*pope", v. t. Etym: [1st pref. un- + pope.]

1. To divest of the character, office, or authority of a pope.

2. To deprive of a pope. [Obs.]
Rome will never so far unpope herself as to part with her pretended
supremacy. Fuller.
