REJOLT
Re*jolt" (r-jlt"), n.

Defn: A reacting jolt or shock; a rebound or recoil. [R.]
These inward rejolts and recoilings of the mind. South.
REJOLT

=====
Re*jolt", v. t.

Defn: To jolt or shake again. Locke.
