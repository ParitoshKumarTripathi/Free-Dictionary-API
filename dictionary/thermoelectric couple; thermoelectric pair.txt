THERMOELECTRIC COUPLE; THERMOELECTRIC PAIR
Ther`mo*e*lec"tric couple or pair. (Elec.)

Defn: A union of two conductors, as bars or wires of dissimilar
metals joined at their extremities, for producing a thermoelectric
current.
