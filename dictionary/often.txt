OFTEN
Of`ten, adv. [Compar. Oftener; superl. Oftenest.] Etym: [Formerly
also ofte, fr. oft. See Oft., adv.]

Defn: Frequently; many times; not seldom.
OFTEN

=====
Of"ten, a.

Defn: Frequent; common; repeated. [R.] "Thine often infirmities." 1
Tim. v. 23.
And weary thee with often welcomes. Beau. & Fl.
