GLOSSIC
Glos"sic, n. Etym: [L. glossa a word requiring a gloss. See 3d
Gloss.]

Defn: A system of phonetic spelling based upon the present values of
English letters, but invariably using one symbol to represent one
sound only.
Ingglish Glosik konvaiA. J. Ellis.
