ETYMOLOGICAL
Et`y*mo*log"ic*al, a. Etym: [L. etymologicus, Gr. étimilogique. See
Etymology.]

Defn: Pertaining to etymology, or the derivation of words.
 -- Et`y*mo*log"ic*al*ly, adv.
