PRECONTRACT
Pre`con*tract", v. t. [imp. & p. p. Precontracted; p. pr. & vb. n.
Precontracting.]

Defn: To contract, engage, or stipulate previously.
PRECONTRACT

=====
Pre`con*tract", v. i.

Defn: To make a previous contract or agreement. Ayliffe.
PRECONTRACT

=====
Pre*con"tract, n.

Defn: A contract preceding another; especially (Law),

Defn: a contract of marriage which, according to the ancient law,
rendered void a subsequent marriage solemnized in violation of it.
Abbott.
