SERE
Sere, a.

Defn: Dry; withered. Same as Sear.
But with its sound it shook the sails That were so thin and sere.
Coleridge.
SERE

=====
Sere, n. Etym: [F. serre.]

Defn: Claw; talon. [Obs.] Chapman.
