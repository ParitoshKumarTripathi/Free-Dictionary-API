RAMSON
Ram"son, n. Etym: [AS. hramsan, pl., akin to G. rams, Sw. rams,
ramslök; cf. Gr. (Bot.)

Defn: A broad-leaved species of garlic (Allium ursinum), common in
European gardens; -- called also buckram.
