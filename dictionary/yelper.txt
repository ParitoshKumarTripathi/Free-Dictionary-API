YELPER
Yelp"er, n.

Defn: An animal that yelps, or makes a yelping noise. Specifically:
(Zoöl.)
(a) The avocet; -- so called from its sharp, shrill cry. [Prov. Eng.]
(b) The tattler. [Local, U. S.]
