TRICENNIAL
Tri*cen"ni*al, a. Etym: [L. tricennium thirty years; triginta thirty
+ annus year: cf. L. tricennalis.]

Defn: Of or pertaining to thirty years; consisting of thirty years;
occurring once in every thirty years.
