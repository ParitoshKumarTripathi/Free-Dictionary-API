OXALURIC
Ox`a*lur"ic, a. Etym: [Oxalyl + urea.] (Chem.)

Defn: Pertaining to, or designating, a complex nitrogenous acid
related to the ureids, and obtained from parabanic acid as a white
silky crystalline substance.
