VERBIGERATE
Ver*big"er*ate, v. i.  [imp. & p. p. -ated; p. pr. & vb. n. -ating.]
[L. verbigerate, -atum, to talk.]

1. To talk; chat. [Obs.]

2.  (Med.) To repeat a word or sentence, in speaking or writing,
without wishing to do so or in spite of efforts to cease. --
Ver*big`er*a"tion (#), n.
