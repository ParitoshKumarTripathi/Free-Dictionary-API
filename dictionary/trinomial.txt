TRINOMIAL
Tri*no"mi*al, n. Etym: [Pref. tri- + -nomial as in binomial: cf. F.
trinôme.] (Math.)

Defn: A quantity consisting of three terms, connected by the sign +
or -; as, x + y + z, or ax + 2b - c2.
TRINOMIAL

=====
Tri*no"mi*al, a. (Math.)

Defn: Consisting of three terms; of or pertaining to trinomials; as,
a trinomial root.
