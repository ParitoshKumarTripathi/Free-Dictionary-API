VENEW
Ven"ew, n. Etym: [F. venue, lit., an arrival, from venir, p. p. venu,
venue, to come. See Venue.]

Defn: A bout, or turn, as at fencing; a thrust; a hit; a veney.
[Obs.] Fuller.
