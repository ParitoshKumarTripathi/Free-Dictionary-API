NURL
Nurl, v. t. [imp. & p. p. Nurled; p. pr. & vb. n. Nurling.] Etym:
[Cf. Knurl.]

Defn: To cut with reeding or fluting on the edge of, as coins, the
heads of screws, etc.; to knurl.
