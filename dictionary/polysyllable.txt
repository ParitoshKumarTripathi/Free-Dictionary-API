POLYSYLLABLE
Pol"y*syl`la*ble, n. Etym: [Poly- + syllable.]

Defn: A word of many syllables, or consisting of more syllables than
three; -- words of less than four syllables being called
monosyllables, dissyllables, and trisyllables.
