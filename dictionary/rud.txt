RUD
Rud, n. Etym: [AS. rudu, akin to reád red. sq. root113. See Red, and
cf. Ruddy.]

1. Redness; blush. [Obs.]

2. Ruddle; red ocher.

3. (Zoöl.)

Defn: The rudd.
RUD

=====
Rud, v. t.

Defn: To make red. [Obs.] Spenser.
