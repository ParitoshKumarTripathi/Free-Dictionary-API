YELLOWBIRD
Yel"low*bird`, n. (Zoöl.)
(a) The American goldfinch, or thistle bird. See Goldfinch.
(b) The common yellow warbler; -- called also summer yellowbird. See
Illust. of Yellow warbler, under Yellow, a.
