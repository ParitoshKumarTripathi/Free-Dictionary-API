SHUFFLING
Shuf"fling, a.

1. Moving with a dragging, scraping step. "A shuffling nag." Shak.

2. Evasive; as, a shuffling excuse. T. Burnet.
SHUFFLING

=====
Shuf"fling, v.

Defn: In a shuffling manner.
