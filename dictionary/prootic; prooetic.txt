PROOTIC; PROOETIC
Pro*ö"tic, a. Etym: [Pref. pro- + Gr. (Anat.)

Defn: In front of the auditory capsule; -- applied especially to a
bone, or center of ossification, in the periotic capsule.
 -- n.

Defn: A proötic bone.
