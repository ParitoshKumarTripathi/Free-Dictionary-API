WIGG; WIG
Wigg, Wig, n. Etym: [Cf. D. wegge a sort of bread, G. weck, orig., a
wedge-shaped loaf or cake. See Wedge.]

Defn: A kind of raised seedcake. "Wiggs and ale." Pepys.
