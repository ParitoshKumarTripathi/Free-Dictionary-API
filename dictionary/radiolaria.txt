RADIOLARIA
Ra`di*o*la"ri*a, n. pl. Etym: [NL. See Radioli.] (Zoöl.)

Defn: Order of rhizopods, usually having a siliceous skeleton, or
shell, and sometimes radiating spicules. The pseudopodia project from
the body like rays. It includes the polycystines. See Polycystina.
