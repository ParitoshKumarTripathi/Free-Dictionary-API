SUDS
Suds, n. pl. Etym: [Akin to sodden, seethe. See Seethe.]

Defn: Water impregnated with soap, esp. when worked up into bubbles
and froth. In the suds, in turmoil or difficulty. [Colloq.] Beau. &
Fl.
