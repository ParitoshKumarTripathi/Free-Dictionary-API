GLASSFUL
Glass"ful, n.; pl. Glassfuls (.

Defn: The contents of a glass; as much of anything as a glass will
hold.
GLASSFUL

=====
Glass"ful, a.

Defn: Glassy; shining like glass. [Obs.] "Minerva's glassful shield."
Marston.
