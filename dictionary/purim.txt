PURIM
Pu"rim, n. Etym: [Heb. pur, pl. purim, a lot.]

Defn: A Jewish festival, called also the Feast of Lots, instituted to
commemorate the deliverance of the Jews from the machinations of
Haman. Esther ix. 26.
