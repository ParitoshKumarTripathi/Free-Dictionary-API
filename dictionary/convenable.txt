CONVENABLE
Con*ven"a*ble, a.

Defn: Capable of being convened or assembled.
CONVENABLE

=====
Con"ve*na*ble, a. Etym: [F. convenable, fr. convenir. See Convene.]

Defn: Consistent; accordant; suitable; proper; as, convenable
remedies. [Obs.]
With his wod his work is convenable. Spenser.
