THEOLOGICAL
The`o*log"ic*al, a. Etym: [L. theologicus, Gr. théologique.]

Defn: Of or pertaining to theology, or the science of God and of
divine things; as, a theological treatise.
 -- The`o*log"ic*al*ly, adv.
