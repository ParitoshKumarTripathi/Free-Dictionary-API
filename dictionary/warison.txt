WARISON
War"i*son, n. Etym: [OF. warison safety, supplies, cure, F. guérison
cure. See Warish, v. t.]

1. Preparation; protection; provision; supply. [Obs.]

2. Reward; requital; guerdon. [Obs. or Scot.]
Wit and wisdom is good warysoun. Proverbs of Hending.
