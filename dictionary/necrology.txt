NECROLOGY
Ne*crol"o*gy, n.; pl. Necrologies. Etym: [Gr. -logy: cf. F.
nécrologie. See Necromancy.]

Defn: An account of deaths, or of the dead; a register of deaths; a
collection of obituary notices.
