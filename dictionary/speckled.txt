SPECKLED
Spec"kled, a.

Defn: Marked or variegated with small spots of a different color from
that of the rest of the surface. Speckled Indians (Ethnol.), the
Pintos.
 -- Speckled trout. (Zoöl.) (a) The common American brook trout. See
Trout. (b) The rainbow trout.
