QUINQUEDENTATE; QUINQUEDENTATED
Quin`que*den"tate, Quin`que*den"ta*ted, a. Etym: [Quinque- + dentate,
-tated: cf. F. quinquédenté.]

Defn: Five-toothed; as, a quinquedentate leaf.
