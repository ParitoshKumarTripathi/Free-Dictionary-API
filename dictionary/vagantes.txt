VAGANTES
Va*gan"tes, p. pl. Etym: [NL., fr. L. vagans, p. pr. of vagari to
stroll or wander.] (Zoöl.)

Defn: A tribe of spiders, comprising some of those which take their
prey in a web, but which also frequently run with agility, and chase
and seize their prey.
