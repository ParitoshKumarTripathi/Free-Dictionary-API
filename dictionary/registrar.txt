REGISTRAR
Reg"is*trar (-trr), n. Etym: [LL. registrarius, or F. régistraire.
See Register.]

Defn: One who registers; a recorder; a keeper of records; as, a
registrar of births, deaths, and marriages. See Register, n., 3.
