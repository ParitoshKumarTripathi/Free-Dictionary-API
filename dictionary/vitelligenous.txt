VITELLIGENOUS
Vit`el*lig"e*nous, a. (Zoöl.)

Defn: Producing yolk, or vitelline substance; -- applied to certain
cells (also called nutritive, or yolk, cells) formed in the ovaries
of many insects, and supposed to supply nutriment to the developing
ova.
