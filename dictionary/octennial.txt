OCTENNIAL
Oc*ten"ni*al, a. Etym: [L. octennium a period of eight years; octo
eight + annus year.]

Defn: Happening every eighth year; also, lasting a period of eight
years. Johnson.
 -- Oc*ten"ni*al*ly, adv.
