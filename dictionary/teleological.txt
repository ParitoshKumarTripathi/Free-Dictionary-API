TELEOLOGICAL
Te`le*o*log"ic*al, a. Etym: [Cf. F. téléologique.] (Biol.)

Defn: Of or pertaining to teleology, or the doctrine of design.
 -- Te`le*o*log"ic*al*ly, adv.
