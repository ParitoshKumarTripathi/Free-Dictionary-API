DEBUTANT; DEBUTANTE
De`bu`tant", n.; fem. De`bu`tante" (. Etym: [F., p. pr. of débuter to
have the first throw, to make one's début. See Début.]

Defn: A person who makes his (or her) first appearance before the
public.
