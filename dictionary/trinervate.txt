TRINERVATE
Tri*nerv"ate, a. Etym: [NL. trinervatus; pref. tri- + L. nervus
nerve.] (Bot.)

Defn: Having three ribs or nerves extending unbranched from the base
to the apex; -- said of a leaf. Gray.
