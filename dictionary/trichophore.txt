TRICHOPHORE
Trich"o*phore, n. Etym: [Gr.

1. (Bot.)

Defn: The special cell in red algæ which produces or bears a
trichogyne. See Illust. of Trichogyne.

2. (Zoöl.)

Defn: One of the saclike organs from which the setæ of annelids
arise.
 -- Trich`o*phor"ic, a.
