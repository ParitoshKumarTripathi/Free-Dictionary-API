POLYAUTOGRAPHY
Pol`y*au*tog"ra*phy, n. Etym: [Poly- + autography.]

Defn: The act or practice of multiplying copies of one's own
handwriting, or of manuscripts, by printing from stone, -- a species
of lithography.
