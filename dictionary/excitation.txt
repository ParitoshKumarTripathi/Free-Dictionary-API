EXCITATION
Ex`ci*ta"tion n. Etym: [L. excitatio: cf. F. excitation.]

1. The act of exciting or putting in motion; the act of rousing up or
awakening. Bacon.

2. (Physiol.)

Defn: The act of producing excitement (stimulation); also, the
excitement produced.
