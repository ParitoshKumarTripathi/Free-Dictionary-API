SOE
Soe, n. Etym: [Scot. sae, say, saye; cf. Icel. sar a large cask, Sw.
s a tub.]

Defn: A large wooden vessel for holding water; a cowl. [Obs. or Prov.
Eng.] Dr. H. More.
