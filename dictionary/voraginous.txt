VORAGINOUS
Vo*rag"i*nous, a. Etym: [L. voraginosus, fr. vorago an abyss, fr.
vorare to swallow up.]

Defn: Pertaining to a gulf; full of gulfs; hence, devouring. [R.]
Mallet.
