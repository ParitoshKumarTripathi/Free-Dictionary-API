SEVENTEEN
Sev"en*teen`, a. Etym: [OE. seventene, AS. seofontyne, i. e., seven-
ten. Cf. Seventy.]

Defn: One more than sixteen; ten and seven added; as, seventeen
years.
SEVENTEEN

=====
Sev"en*teen`, n.

1. The number greater by one than sixteen; the sum of ten and seven;
seventeen units or objects.

2. A symbol denoting seventeen units, as 17, or xvii.
