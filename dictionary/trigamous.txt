TRIGAMOUS
Trig"a*mous, a. Etym: [L. trigamus a thrice-married man, Gr. Tri-) +
trigame.] (Bot.)

Defn: Having three sorts of flowers in the same head, -- male,
female, and hermaphrodite, or perfect, flowers.
