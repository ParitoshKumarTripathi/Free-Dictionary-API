SOPORIFIC
Sop`o*rif"ic, a. Etym: [L. sopor a heavy sleep (akin to somnus sleep)
+ facere to make. See Somnolent, Fact.]

Defn: Causing sleep; tending to cause sleep; soporiferous; as, the
soporific virtues of opium.

Syn.
 -- Somniferous; narcotic; opiate; anodyne.
SOPORIFIC

=====
Sop`o*rif"ic, n.

Defn: A medicine, drug, plant, or other agent that has the quality of
inducing sleep; a narcotic.
