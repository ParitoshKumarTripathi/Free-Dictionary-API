VANTBRACE; VANTBRASS
Vant"brace, Vant"brass, n. Etym: [F. avant fore + bras arm: cf. F.
brassard armor for the arm, brace, forearm. Cf. Vambrace.] (Anc.
Armor)

Defn: Armor for the arm; vambrace. Milton.
