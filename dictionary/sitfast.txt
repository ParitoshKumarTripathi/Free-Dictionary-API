SITFAST
Sit"fast`, a. Etym: [Sit + fast.]

Defn: Fixed; stationary; immovable. [R.]
'T is good, when you have crossed the sea and back, To find the
sitfast acres where you left them. Emerson.
SITFAST

=====
Sit"fast`, n. (Far.)

Defn: A callosity with inflamed edges, on the back of a horse, under
the saddle.
