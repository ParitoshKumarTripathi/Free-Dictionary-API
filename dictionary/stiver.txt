STIVER
Sti"ver, n. Etym: [D. stuiver; akin to G. stüber, Dan. styver, Sw.
styfver.]

Defn: A Dutch coin, and money of account, of the value of two cents,
or about one penny sterling; hence, figuratively, anything of little
worth.
