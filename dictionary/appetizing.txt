APPETIZING
Ap"pe*ti`zing, a. Etym: [Cf. F. appétissant.]

Defn: Exciting appetite; as, appetizing food.
The appearance of the wild ducks is very appetizing. Sir W. Scott.
APPETIZING

=====
Ap"pe*ti`zing, adv.

Defn: So as to excite appetite.
