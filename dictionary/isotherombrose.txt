ISOTHEROMBROSE
I*soth`er*om"brose, n. Etym: [Iso- + Gr. qe`ros summer + 'o`mbros
rain.] (Phys. Geog)

Defn: A line connecting or marking points on the earth's surface,
which have the same mean summer rainfall.
