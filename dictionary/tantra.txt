TANTRA
Tan"tra (tan"tra; tun"-), n. [Skr.] (Hinduism)

Defn: A ceremonial treatise related to Puranic and magic literature;
esp., one of the sacred works of the worshipers of Sakti. -- Tan"tric
(-trik), a.
