VESPERTILIONES
Ves`per*til`i*o"nes, n. pl. Etym: [NL.] (Zoöl.)

Defn: A tribe of bats including the common insectivorous bats of
America and Europe, belonging to Vespertilio and allied genera. They
lack a nose membrane.
