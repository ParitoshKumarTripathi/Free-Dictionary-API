FUZZ
Fuzz, v. t.

Defn: To make drunk. [Obs.] Wood.
FUZZ

=====
Fuzz, n. Etym: [Cf. Prov. E. fuzzy that ravels (of silk or cotton),
D. voos spongy, fungous, G. faser filament. E. feaze to untwist.]

Defn: Fine, light particles or fibers; loose, volatile matter. Fuzz
ball, a kind of fungus or mushroom, which, when pressed, bursts and
scatters a fine dust; a puffball.
FUZZ

=====
Fuzz, v. i.

Defn: To fly off in minute particles.
