TUBERCULOCIDIN
Tu*ber"cu*lo*ci`din, n. [Tuberculum + root of L. caedere to kill.]
(Physiol. Chem.)

Defn: A special substance contained in tuberculin, supposed to be the
active agent of the latter freed from various impurities.
