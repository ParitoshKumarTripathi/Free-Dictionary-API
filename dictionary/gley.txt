GLEY
Gley, v. i. Etym: [OE. gli, glien, gleien, to shine, to squint; cf.
Icel. glja to glitter.]

Defn: To squint; to look obliquely; to overlook things. [Scot.]
Jamieson.
GLEY

=====
Gley, adv.

Defn: Asquint; askance; obliquely.
