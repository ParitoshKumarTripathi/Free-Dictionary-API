TUP
Tup, v. t. & i. Etym: [Probably akin to top summit, head.]

1. To butt, as a ram does. [Prov. Eng.]

2. To cover; -- said of a ram. Shak.
TUP

=====
Tup, n. (Zoöl.)

Defn: A ram.
