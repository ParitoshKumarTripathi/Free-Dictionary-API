VOLUMETRIC
Vol`u*met"ric, a. Etym: [Volume + -metric.]

Defn: Of or pertaining to the measurement of volume. Volumetric
analysis (Chem.), that system of the quantitative analysis of
solutions which employs definite volumes of standardized solutions of
reagents, as measured by burettes, pipettes, etc.; also, the analysis
of gases by volume, as by the eudiometer.
