BOBTAIL
Bob"tail`, n. Etym: [Bob + tail.]

Defn: An animal (as a horse or dog) with a short tail. Rag, tag, and
bobtail, the rabble.
BOBTAIL

=====
Bob"tail`, a.

Defn: Bobtailed. "Bobtail cur." Marryat.
