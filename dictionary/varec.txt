VAREC
Var"ec, n. Etym: [F. varech; of Teutonic origin. See Wrack seaweed,
wreck.]

Defn: The calcined ashes of any coarse seaweed used for the
manufacture of soda and iodine; also, the seaweed itself; fucus;
wrack.
