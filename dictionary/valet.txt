VALET
Val"et, n. Etym: [F. valet, OF. vallet, varlet, vaslet. See Varlet,
and Vassal.]

1. A male waiting servant; a servant who attends on gentleman's
person; a body servant.

2. (Man.)

Defn: A kind of goad or stick with a point of iron. Valet de chambre
( Etym: [F.], a body servant, or personal attendant.
