VOCIFEROUS
Vo*cif"er*ous, a. Etym: [Cf. F. vocifère.]

Defn: Making a loud outcry; clamorous; noisy; as, vociferous heralds.
 -- Vo*cif"er*ous*ly, adv.
 -- Vo*cif"er*ous*ness, n.
