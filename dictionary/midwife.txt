MIDWIFE
Mid"wife`, n.; pl. Midwives. Etym: [OE. midwif, fr. AS. mid with
(akin to Gr. Meta-, and Wife.]

Defn: A woman who assists other women in childbirth; a female
practitioner of the obstetric art.
MIDWIFE

=====
Mid"wife`, v. t.

Defn: To assist in childbirth.
MIDWIFE

=====
Mid"wife`, v. i.

Defn: To perform the office of midwife.
