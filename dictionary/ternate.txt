TERNATE
Ter"nate, a. Etym: [NL. ternatus, fr. L. terni three each. See Tern,
a.]

Defn: Having the parts arranged by threes; as, ternate branches,
leaves, or flowers.
 -- Ter"nate*ly, adv.
