PEAN
Pean, n. Etym: [OF. pene, F. panne.] (Her.)

Defn: One of the furs, the ground being sable, and the spots or tufts
or.
PEAN

=====
Pe"an, n.

Defn: A song of praise and triumph. See Pæan.
