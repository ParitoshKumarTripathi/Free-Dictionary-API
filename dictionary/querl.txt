QUERL
Querl, v. t. Etym: [G. querlen, quirlen, to twirl, to turn round, fr.
querl, querl, a twirling stick. Cf. Twirl.]

Defn: To twirl; to turn or wind round; to coil; as, to querl a cord,
thread, or rope. [Local, U.S.]
QUERL

=====
Querl, n.

Defn: A coil; a twirl; as, the qwerl of hair on the fore leg of a
blooded horse. [Local, U. S.]
