VERILY
Ver"i*ly, adv. Etym: [From Very.]

Defn: In very truth; beyond doubt or question; in fact; certainly.
Bacon.
Trust in the Lord and do good; so shalt thou dwell in the verily thou
shalt be fed. Ps. xxxvii. 3.
