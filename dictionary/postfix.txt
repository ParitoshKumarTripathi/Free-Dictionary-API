POSTFIX
Post"fix, n.; pl. Postfixes. Etym: [Pref. post- + -fix, as in prefix:
cf. F. postfixe.] (Gram.)

Defn: A letter, syllable, or word, added to the end of another word;
a suffix. Parkhurst.
POSTFIX

=====
Post*fix", v. t.

Defn: To annex; specifically (Gram.), to add or annex, as a letter,
syllable, or word, to the end of another or principal word; to
suffix. Parkhurst.
