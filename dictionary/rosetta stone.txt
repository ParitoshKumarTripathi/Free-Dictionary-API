ROSETTA STONE
Ro*set"ta stone`.

Defn: A stone found at Rosetta, in Egypt, bearing a trilingual
inscription, by aid of which, with other inscriptions, a key was
obtained to the hieroglyphics of ancient Egypt. Brande & C.
