HOPPING
Hop"ping, n.

Defn: The act of one who, or that which, hops; a jumping, frisking,
or dancing. Hopping Dick (Zoöl.), a thrush of Jamaica (Merula
leucogenys), resembling the English blackbird in its familiar
manners, agreeable song, and dark plumage.
HOPPING

=====
Hop"ping, n. Etym: [See 3rd Hop.]
