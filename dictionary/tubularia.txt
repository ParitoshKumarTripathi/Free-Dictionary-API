TUBULARIA
Tu`bu*la"ri*a, n. Etym: [NL.] (Zoöl.)

Defn: A genus of hydroids having large, naked, flowerlike hydranths
at the summits of long, slender, usually simple, stems. The
gonophores are small, and form clusters at the bases of the outer
tentacles.
