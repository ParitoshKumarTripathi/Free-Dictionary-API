VOLTAMETER
Vol*tam"e*ter, n. Etym: [Voltaic + -meter.] (Physics)

Defn: An instrument for measuring the voltaic electricity passing
through it, by its effect in decomposing water or some other chemical
compound acting as an electrolyte.
