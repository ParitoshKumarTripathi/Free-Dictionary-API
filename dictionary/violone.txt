VIOLONE
Vi`o*lo"ne, n. Etym: [It. violone, augment. of viola a viol. See
Viol.] (Mus.)

Defn: The largest instrument of the bass-viol kind, having strings
tuned an octave below those of the violoncello; the contrabasso; --
called also double bass. [Written also violono.]
