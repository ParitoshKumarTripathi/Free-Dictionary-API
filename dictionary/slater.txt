SLATER
Slat"er, n.

Defn: One who lays slates, or whose occupation is to slate buildings.
SLATER

=====
Slat"er, n. (Zoöl.)

Defn: Any terrestrial isopod crustacean of the genus Porcellio and
allied genera; a sow bug.
