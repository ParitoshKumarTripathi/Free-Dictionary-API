HEY
Hey, a. Etym: [See High.]

Defn: High. [Obs.] Chaucer.
HEY

=====
Hey, interj. Etym: [OE. hei; cf. D. & G. hei.]

1. An exclamation of joy, surprise, or encouragement. Shak.

2. A cry to set dogs on. Shak.
