ROLLICHE; ROLLEJEE; ROLLICHIE
Rol"li*che, n. [Also Rol"le*jee, Rol"li*chie.] [D. rolletje a little
roll.]

Defn: A kind of sausage, made in a bag of tripe, sliced and fried,
famous among the Dutch of New Amsterdam and still known, esp. in New
Jersey.
