NULLIPORE
Nul"li*pore, n. Etym: [L. nullus none + porus pope.] (Bot.)

Defn: A name for certain crustaceous marine algæ which secrete
carbonate of lime on their surface, and were formerly thought to be
of animal nature. They are now considered corallines of the genera
Melobesia and Lithothamnion.
