RECUSE
Re*cuse" (r*kz"), v. t. Etym: [F. récuser, or L. recusare. See
Recusant.] (Law)

Defn: To refuse or reject, as a judge; to challenge that the judge
shall not try the cause. [Obs.] Sir K. Digby.
