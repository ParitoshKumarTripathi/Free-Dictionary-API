DISPROPORTIONABLE
Dis`pro*por"tion*a*ble, a.

Defn: Disproportional; unsuitable in form, size, quantity, or
adaptation; disproportionate; inadequate.
 -- Dis`pro*por"tion*a*ble*ness, n. Hammond.
 -- Dis`pro*por"tion*a*bly, adv.
