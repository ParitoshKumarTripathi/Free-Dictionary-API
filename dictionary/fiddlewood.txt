FIDDLEWOOD
Fid"dle*wood`, n. Etym: [Corrupted fr. F. bois-fidèle, lit., faithful
wood; -- so called from its durability.]

Defn: The wood of several West Indian trees, mostly of the genus
Citharexylum.
