GOMER
Go"mer, n.

Defn: A Hebrew measure. See Homer.
GOMER

=====
Go"mer, n. (Gun.)

Defn: A conical chamber at the breech of the bore in heavy ordnance,
especially in mortars; -- named after the inventor.
