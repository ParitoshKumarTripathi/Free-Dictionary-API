RIBBAND

=====
Rib"*band`, n. Etym: [Rib + band.] Etym: [Written also riband, and
ribbon.] (Shipbuilding)

Defn: A long, narrow strip of timber bent and bolted longitudinally
to the ribs of a vessel, to hold them in position, and give rigidity
to the framework. Rib-band lines, oblique longitudinal sectionss of
the hull of a vessel. Knight.
