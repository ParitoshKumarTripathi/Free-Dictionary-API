TWINING
Twin"ing, a.

Defn: Winding around something; twisting; embracing; climbing by
winding about a support; as, the hop is a twinning plant.
TWINING

=====
Twin"ing, a.

Defn: The act of one who, or that which, twines; (Bot.) the act of
climbing spirally.
