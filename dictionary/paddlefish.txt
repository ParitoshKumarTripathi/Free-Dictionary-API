PADDLEFISH
Pad"dle*fish`, n. (Zoöl)

Defn: A large ganoid fish (Polyodon spathula) found in the rivers of
the Mississippi Valley. It has a long spatula-shaped snout. Called
also duck-billed cat, and spoonbill sturgeon.
