PYROSCOPE
Pyr"o*scope, n. Etym: [Pyro- + -scope: cf. F. pyroscope.] (Physics)

Defn: An instrument for measuring the intensity of heat radiating
from a fire, or the cooling influence of bodies. It is a differential
thermometer, having one bulb coated with gold or silver leaf. [R.]
