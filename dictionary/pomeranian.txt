POMERANIAN
Pom`e*ra"ni*an, a.

Defn: Of or pertaining to Pomerania, a province of Prussia on the
Baltic Sea.
 -- n.

Defn: A native or inhabitant of Pomerania. Pomeranian dog (Zoöl.),
the loup-loup, or Spitz dog.
