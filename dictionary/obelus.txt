OBELUS
Ob"e*lus, n.; pl. Obeli. Etym: [L., fr. Gr. (Print.)

Defn: A mark [thus ---, or รท]; -- so called as resembling a needle.
In old MSS. or editions of the classics, it marks suspected passages
or readings.
