VEGETARIAN
Veg`e*ta"ri*an, n.

Defn: One who holds that vegetables and fruits are the only proper
food for man. Strict vegetarians eat no meat, eggs, or milk.
VEGETARIAN

=====
Veg`e*ta"ri*an, a.

Defn: Of or pertaining to vegetarianism; as, a vegetarian diet.
