ORBICULATE; ORBICULATED
Or*bic"u*late, Or*bic"u*la`ted, a. Etym: [L. orbiculatus. See
Orbicular.]

Defn: Made, or being, in the form of an orb; having a circular, or
nearly circular, or a spheroidal, outline. Orbiculate leaf (Bot.), a
leaf whose outline is nearly circular.
