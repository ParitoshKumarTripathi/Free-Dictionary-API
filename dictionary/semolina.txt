SEMOLINA
Sem`o*li"na, n. Etym: [It. semolino, from semola bran, L. simila the
finest wheat flour. Cf. Semoule, Simnel.]

Defn: The fine, hard parts of wheat, rounded by the attrition of the
millstones, -- used in cookery.
