PLEURODONT
Pleu"ro*dont, a. Etym: [Pleuro- + Gr. (Anat.)

Defn: Having the teeth consolidated with the inner edge of the jaw,
as in some lizards.
PLEURODONT

=====
Pleu"ro*dont, n. (Zoöl.)

Defn: Any lizard having pleurodont teeth.
