TAIL-BAY
Tail"-bay`, n.

1. (Arch.)

Defn: One of the joists which rest one end on the wall and the other
on a girder; also, the space between a wall and the nearest girder of
a floor. Cf. Case-bay.

2. The part of a canal lock below the lower gates.
