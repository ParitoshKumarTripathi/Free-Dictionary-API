OUTSTAND
Out*stand", v. i.

Defn: To stand out, or project, from a surface or mass; hence, to
remain standing out.
OUTSTAND

=====
Out*stand", v. t.

1. To resist effectually; to withstand; to sustain without yielding.
[R.] Woodward.

2. To stay beyond. "I have outstood my time." Shak.
