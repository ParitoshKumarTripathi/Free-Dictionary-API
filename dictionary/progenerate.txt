PROGENERATE
Pro*gen"er*ate, v. t. Etym: [L. progeneratus, p. p. of progenerare to
beget; pro forth, forward + generare to generate.]

Defn: To beget; to generate; to produce; to procreate; as, to
progenerate a race. [R.] Landor.
