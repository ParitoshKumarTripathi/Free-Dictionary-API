PYCNODONTINI
Pyc`no*don"ti*ni, n. pl. Etym: [NL.] (Zoöl.)

Defn: An extinct order of ganoid fishes. They had a compressed body,
covered with dermal ribs (pleurolepida) and with enameled rhomboidal
scales.
