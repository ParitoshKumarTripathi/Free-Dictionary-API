NATIVISM
Na"tiv*ism, n.

1. The disposition to favor the native inhabitants of a country, in
preference to immigrants from foreign countries.

2. (Philos.)

Defn: The doctrine of innate ideas, or that the mind possesses forms
of thought independent of sensation.
