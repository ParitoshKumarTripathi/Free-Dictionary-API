OXANILIDE
Ox*an"i*lide, n. Etym: [Oxalic + aniline + amide.] (Chem.)

Defn: a white crystalline substance, resembling oxanilamide, obtained
by heating aniline oxalate, and regarded as a double anilide of
oxalic acid; -- called also diphenyl oxamide.
