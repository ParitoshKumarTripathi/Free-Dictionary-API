EXTRAVERSION
Ex`tra*ver"sion, n. Etym: [Pref. extra + L. vertere, versum, to turn:
cf. F. extraversion.]

Defn: The act of throwing out; the state of being turned or thrown
out. [Obs.] Boyle.
