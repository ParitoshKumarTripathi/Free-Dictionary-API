ECTHLIPSIS
Ec*thlip"sis, n. Etym: [L., fr. Gr.

1. The dropping out or suppression from a word of a consonant, with
or without a vowel.

2. (Lat. Pros.)

Defn: The elision of a final m, with the preceding vowel, before a
word beginning with a vowel.
