RESOLVABLE
Re*solv"a*ble (r-zlv"-b'l), a. Etym: [See Resolve, and cf.
Resoluble.]

Defn: Admitting of being resolved; admitting separation into
constituent parts, or reduction to first principles; admitting
solution or explanation; as, resolvable compounds; resolvable ideas
or difficulties.
