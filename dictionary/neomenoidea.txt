NEOMENOIDEA
Ne`o*me*noi"de*a, n. pl. Etym: [NL., fr. Neomenia, a representative
genus (See Neomenia) + -oid.] (Zoöl.)

Defn: A division of vermiform gastropod mollusks, without a shell,
belonging to the Isopleura.
