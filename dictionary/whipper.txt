WHIPPER
Whip"per, n.

1. One who whips; especially, an officer who inflicts the penalty of
legal whipping.

2. One who raises coal or merchandise with a tackle from a chip's
hold. [Eng.]

3. (Spinning)

Defn: A kind of simple willow.
