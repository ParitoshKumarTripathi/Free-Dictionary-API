OPISTHOBRANCHIA; OPISTHOBRANCHIATA
O*pis`tho*bran"chi*a, O*pis`tho*bran`chi*a"ta, n. pl. Etym: [NL.,
from Gr. (Zoöl.)

Defn: A division of gastropod Mollusca, in which the breathing organs
are usually situated behind the heart. It includes the tectibranchs
and nudibranchs.
