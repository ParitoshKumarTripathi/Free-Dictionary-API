SCARABOID
Scar"a*boid, a. Etym: [Scarab + -oid.] (Zoöl.)

Defn: Of or pertaining to the family Scarabæidæ, an extensive group
which includes the Egyptian scarab, the tumbleding, and many similar
lamellicorn beetles.
SCARABOID

=====
Scar"a*boid, n. (Zoöl.)

Defn: A scaraboid beetle.
