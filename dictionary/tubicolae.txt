TUBICOLAE
Tu*bic"o*læ, n. pl. Etym: [L. tubus a tube + colere to inhabit.]
(Zoöl.)

Defn: A division of annelids including those which construct, and
habitually live in, tubes. The head or anterior segments usually bear
gills and cirri. Called also Sedentaria, and Capitibranchiata. See
Serpula, and Sabella.
