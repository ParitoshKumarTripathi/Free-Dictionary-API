RESECT
Re*sect" (r-skt"), v. t. [imp. & p. p. Resected;p. pr. & vb. n.
Resecting.] Etym: [L. resectus, p. p. of resecare to cut off; pref.
re- re- + secare to cut.]

Defn: To cut or pare off; to remove by cutting.
