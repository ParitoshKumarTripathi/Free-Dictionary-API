WEATHERED
Weath"ered, a.

1. (Arch.)

Defn: Made sloping, so as to throw off water; as, a weathered cornice
or window sill.

2. (Geol.)

Defn: Having the surface altered in color, texture, or composition,
or the edges rounded off by exposure to the elements.
