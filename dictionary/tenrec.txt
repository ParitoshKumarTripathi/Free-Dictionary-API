TENREC
Ten"rec, n. Etym: [From the native name: cf. F. tanrac, tanrec,
tandrec.] (Zoöl.)

Defn: A small insectivore (Centetes ecaudatus), native of Madagascar,
but introduced also into the islands of Bourbon and Mauritius; --
called also tanrec. The name is applied to other allied genera. See
Tendrac.
