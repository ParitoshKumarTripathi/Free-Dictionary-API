TARGUM
Tar"gum, n.; pl. Targums. Heb. Targumim. Etym: [Chald. targum
interpretation, fr. targem to interpret. Cf. Truchman, and Dragoman.]

Defn: A translation or paraphrase of some portion of the Old
Testament Scriptures in the Chaldee or Aramaic language or dialect.
