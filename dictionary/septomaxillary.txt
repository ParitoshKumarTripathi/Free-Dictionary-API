SEPTOMAXILLARY
Sep`to*max"il*la*ry, a. (Anat.)

Defn: Of or pertaining to the nasal septum and the maxilla; situated
in the region of these parts.
 -- n.

Defn: A small bone between the nasal septum and the maxilla in many
reptiles and amphibians.
