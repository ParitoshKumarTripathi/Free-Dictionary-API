OXAMIDE
Ox*am"ide, n, Etym: [Oxalic + amide.] (Chem.)

Defn: A white crystalline neutral substance (C2O2(NH2)2) obtained by
treating ethyl oxalate with ammonia. It is the acid amide of oxalic
acid. Formerly called also oxalamide.
