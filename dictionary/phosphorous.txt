PHOSPHOROUS
Phos"phor*ous, a. Etym: [Cf. F. phosphoreux.] (Chem.)

Defn: Of or pertaining to phosphorus; resembling or containing
phosphorus; specifically, designating those compounds in which
phosphorus has a lower valence as contrasted with phosphoric
compounds; as, phosphorous acid, H3PO3.
