PALEOZOIC
Pa`le*o*zo"ic, a. Etym: [Paleo- + Gr. (Geol.)

Defn: Of or pertaining to, or designating, the older division of
geological time during which life is known to have existed, including
the Silurian, Devonian, and Carboniferous ages, and also to the life
or rocks of those ages. See Chart of Geology.
