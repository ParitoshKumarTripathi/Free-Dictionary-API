ROTELLA
Ro*tel"la, n. Etym: [NL., dim. of rota wheel; cf. LL. rotella a
little whell.] (Zoöl.)

Defn: Any one of numerous species of small, polished, brightcolored
gastropods of the genus Rotella, native of tropical seas.
