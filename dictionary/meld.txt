MELD
Meld, v. t. & i.  [imp. & p. p. Melded; p. pr. & vb. n. Melding.] [G.
melden to announce.] (Card Playing)

Defn: In the game of pinochle, to declare or announce for a score;
as, to meld a sequence.
MELD

=====
Meld, n. (Card Playing)

Defn: Any combination or score which may be declared, or melded, in
pinochle.
