SEMASIOLOGY
Se*ma`si*ol"o*gy, n. [Gr.  signification + -logy.] (Philol.)

Defn: The science of meanings or sense development (of words); the
explanation of the development and changes of the meanings of words.
--Se*ma`si*o*log"ic*al (#), a.
