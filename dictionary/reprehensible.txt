REPREHENSIBLE
Rep`re*hen"si*ble (-hn"s-b'l), a. Etym: [L. reprehensibilis: cf. F.
répréhensible.]

Defn: Worthy of reprehension; culpable; censurable; blamable.
 -- Rep`re*hen"si*ble*ness, n.
 -- Rep`re*hen"si*bly, adv.
