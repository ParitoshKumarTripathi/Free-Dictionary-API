VELOUTE; SAUCE VELOUTE
Ve*lou`té", n., or Sauce velouté. [F. velouté, lit., velvety.]
(Cookery)

Defn: A white sauce or stock made by boiling down ham, veal, beef,
fowl, bouillon, etc., then adding soup stock, seasoning, vegetables,
and thickening, and again boiling and straining.
