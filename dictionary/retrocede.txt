RETROCEDE
Re"tro*cede, v. t. Etym: [Pref. retro- + cede: cf. F. rétrocéder.]

Defn: To cede or grant back; as, to retrocede a territory to a former
proprietor.
RETROCEDE

=====
Re"tro*cede, v. i. Etym: [L. retrocedere; retro backward, back +
cedere to go. See Cede.]

Defn: To go back.
