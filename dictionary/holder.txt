HOLDER

=====
Hold"er, n.

1. One who, or that which, holds.

2. One who holds land, etc., under another; a tenant.

3. (Com.)

Defn: The payee of a bill of exchange or a promissory note, or the
one who owns or holds it.

Note: Holder is much used as the second part of a compound; as,
shareholder, officeholder, stockholder,etc.
