RIGADOON
Rig`a*doon", n. Etym: [F. rigadon, rigaudon.]

Defn: A gay, lively dance for one couple, -- said to have been
borrowed from Provence in France. W. Irving.
Whose dancing dogs in rigadoons excel. Wolcott.
