PROVANT
Pro*vant", v. t.

Defn: To supply with provender or provisions; to provide for. [Obs.]
Nash.
PROVANT

=====
Prov"ant, a.

Defn: Provided for common or general use, as in an army; hence,
common in quality; inferior. "A poor provant rapier." B. Jonson.
