RISSOID
Ris"soid, n. Etym: [NL. Rissoa, the typical genus ( fr. A.Risso, an
Italian naturalist) + -oid.] (Zoöl.)

Defn: Any one of very numerous species of small spiral gastropods of
the genus Rissoa, or family Rissoidæ, found both in fresh and salt
water.
