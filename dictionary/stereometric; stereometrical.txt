STEREOMETRIC; STEREOMETRICAL
Ste`re*o*met"ric, Ste`re*o*met"ric*al, a. Etym: [Cf. F.
stéréométrique.]

Defn: Of or pertaining to stereometry; performed or obtained by
stereometry.
 -- Ste`re*o*met"ric*al*ly, adv.
