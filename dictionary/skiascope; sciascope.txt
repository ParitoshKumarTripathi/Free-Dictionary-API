SKIASCOPE; SCIASCOPE
Ski"a*scope, Sci"a*scope, n. [Gr.  a shadow + -scope.] (Med.)

Defn: A device for determining the refractive state of the eye by
observing the movements of the retinal lights and shadows. --
Ski*as"co*py (#), Ski*as"co*py (#), n.
