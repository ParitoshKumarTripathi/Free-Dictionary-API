PLEUROTOMA
Pleu*rot"o*ma, n.; pl. L. Pleurotomæ, E. Pleurotomas. Etym: [NL., fr.
Gr. (Zoöl.)

Defn: Any marine gastropod belonging to Pleurotoma, and ether allied
genera of the family Pleurotmidæ. The species are very numerous,
especially in tropical seas. The outer lip has usually a posterior
notch or slit.
