TRISECT
Tri*sect", v. t. [imp. & p. p. Trisected; p. pr. & vb. n.
Trisecting.] Etym: [Pref. tri- + L. sectus, p. p. of secare to cut.
See Section.]

1. To cut or divide into three parts.

2. (Geom.)

Defn: To cut or divide into three equal parts.
