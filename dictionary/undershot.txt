UNDERSHOT
Un"der*shot`, a.

1. (Zoöl.)

Defn: Having the lower incisor teeth projecting beyond the upper
ones, as in the bulldog.

2. Moved by water passing beneath; -- said of a water wheel, and
opposed to overshot; as, an undershot wheel.
