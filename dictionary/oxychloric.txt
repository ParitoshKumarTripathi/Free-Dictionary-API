OXYCHLORIC
Ox`y*chlo"ric, a. Etym: [Oxy- (a) + chloric.] (Chem.)
(a) Of, pertaining to, or designating in general, certain compounds
containing oxygen and chlorine.
(b) Formerly designating an acid now called perchloric acid. See
Perchloric.
