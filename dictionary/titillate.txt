TITILLATE
Tit"il*late, v. t. & i. [imp. & p. p. Titillated; p. pr. & vb. n.
Titillating.] Etym: [L. titillatus, p.p. of titillare.]

Defn: To tickle; as, to titillate the nose with a feather.
The pungent grains of titillating dust. Pope.
