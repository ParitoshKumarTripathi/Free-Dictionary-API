ACUATE
Ac"u*ate, v. t. Etym: [L. acus needle.]

Defn: To sharpen; to make pungent; to quicken. [Obs.] "[To] acuate
the blood." Harvey.
ACUATE

=====
Ac"u*ate, a.

Defn: Sharpened; sharp-pointed.
