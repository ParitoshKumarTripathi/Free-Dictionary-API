VITELLUS
Vi*tel"lus, n. Etym: [L., the yolk of an egg.]

1. (Biol.)

Defn: The contents or substance of the ovum; egg yolk. See Illust. of
Ovum.

2. (Bot.)

Defn: Perisperm in an early condition.
