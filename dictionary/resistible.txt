RESISTIBLE
Re*sist"i*ble (r-zst"-b'l), a. Etym: [Cf. F. résistible.]

Defn: Capable of being resisted; as, a resistible force. Sir M. Hale.
 -- Re*sist"i*ble*ness, n.
 -- Re*sist"i*bly, adv.
