NUNCHION
Nun"chion, n. Etym: [OE. nonechenche, for noneschenche, prop., a noon
drink; none noon + schenchen, schenken, skinken, to pour, AS.
scencan. See Noon, and Skink, v. i.]

Defn: A portion of food taken at or after noon, usually between full
meals; a luncheon. [Written also noonshun.] Hudibras.
