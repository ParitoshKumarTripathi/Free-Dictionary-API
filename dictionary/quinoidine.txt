QUINOIDINE
Qui*noid"ine, n. Etym: [Quinine + -oid.] (Med. (Chem.)

Defn: A brownish resinous substance obtained as a by-product in the
treatment of cinchona bark. It consists of a mixture of several
alkaloids. [Written also chinoidine.]
