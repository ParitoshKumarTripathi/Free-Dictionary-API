SPITCHCOCK
Spitch"cock`, v. t. Etym: [1st spit + cock.] (Cookery)

Defn: To split (as an eel) lengthwise, and broil it, or fry it in hot
fat.
SPITCHCOCK

=====
Spitch"cock`, n. (Cookery)

Defn: An eel split and broiled.
