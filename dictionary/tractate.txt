TRACTATE
Tract"ate, n. Etym: [L. tractatus a touching, handling, treatise. See
Tractable, and Tract a treatise, Treaty.]

Defn: A treatise; a tract; an essay.
Agreeing in substance with Augustin's, from whose fourteenth Tractate
on St. John the words are translated. Hare.
