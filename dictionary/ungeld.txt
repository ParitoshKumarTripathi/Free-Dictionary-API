UNGELD
Un*geld", n. Etym: [Pref. un- not + geld payment.] (Anglo-Sax. Law)

Defn: A person so far out of the protection of the law, that if he
were murdered, no geld, or fine, should be paid, or composition made
by him that killed him. Cowell. Burrill.
