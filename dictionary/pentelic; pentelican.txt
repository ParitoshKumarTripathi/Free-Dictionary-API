PENTELIC; PENTELICAN
Pen*tel"ic, Pen*tel"i*can, a.

Defn: Of or pertaining to Mount Pentelicus, near Athens, famous for
its fine white marble quarries; obtained from Mount Pentelicus; as,
the Pentelic marble of which the Parthenon is built.
