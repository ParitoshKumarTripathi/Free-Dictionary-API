SULPHOARSENIC
Sul`pho*ar*sen"ic, a. (Chem.)

Defn: Of, pertaining to, or containing, sulphur and arsenic; -- said
of an acid which is the same as arsenic acid with the substitution of
sulphur for oxygen.
