RUPICOLA
Ru*pic"o*la, n. Etym: [NL., fr. L. rupes, gen. rupis, a rock + colere
to inhabit.] (Zoöl.)

Defn: A genus of beautiful South American passerine birds, including
the cock of the rock.

Note: The species are remarkable for having an elevated fan-shaped
crest of feathers on the head, and for the beautiful color of their
plumage, which is mostly some delicate shade of yellow or orange.
