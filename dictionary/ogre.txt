OGRE
O"gre, n. Etym: [F., fr. Sp. ogro, fr. L. Orcus the god of the
infernal regions; also, the lower world, hell.]

Defn: An imaginary monster, or hideous giant of fairy tales, who
lived on human beings; hence, any frightful giant; a cruel monster.
His schoolroom must have resembled an ogre's den. Maccaulay.
