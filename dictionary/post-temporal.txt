POST-TEMPORAL
Post-tem"po*ral, a. Etym: [Pref. post- + temporal.] (Anat.)

Defn: Situated back of the temporal bone or the temporal region of
the skull; -- applied especially to a bone which usually connects the
supraclavicle with the skull in the pectoral arch of fishes.
 -- n.

Defn: A post-temporal bone.
