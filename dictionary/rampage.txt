RAMPAGE
Ramp"age, n. Etym: [See Ramp, v.]

Defn: Violent or riotous behavior; a state of excitement, passion, or
debauchery; as, to be on the rampage. [Prov. or Low.] Dickens.
RAMPAGE

=====
Ramp"age, v. i.

Defn: To leap or prance about, as an animal; to be violent; to rage.
[Prov. or Low]
