RATCH
Ratch, n. (Zoöl.)

Defn: Same as Rotche.
RATCH

=====
Ratch, n. Etym: [See Rack the instrument, Ratchet.]

Defn: A ratchet wheel, or notched bar, with which a pawl or chick
works.
