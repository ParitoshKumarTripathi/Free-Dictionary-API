UNCHARNEL
Un*char"nel, v. t. [imp. & p. p. Uncharneled; p. pr. & vb. n.
Uncharneling.] Etym: [1st pref. un- + charnel.]

Defn: To remove from a charnel house; to raise from the grave; to
exhume. Byron.
