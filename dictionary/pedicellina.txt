PEDICELLINA
Ped`i*cel*li"na, n. Etym: [NL. See Pedicel.] (Zoöl.)

Defn: A genus of Bryozoa, of the order Entoprocta, having a bell-
shaped body supported on a slender pedicel. See Illust. under
Entoprocta.
