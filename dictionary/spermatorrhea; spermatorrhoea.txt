SPERMATORRHEA; SPERMATORRHOEA
Sper`ma*tor*rhe"a, Sper`ma*tor*rhoe"a, (, n. Etym: [NL., fr. Gr.
(Med.)

Defn: Abnormally frequent involuntary emission of the semen without
copulation.
