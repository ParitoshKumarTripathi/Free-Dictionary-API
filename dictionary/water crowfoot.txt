WATER CROWFOOT
Wa"ter crow"foot`. (Bot.)

Defn: An aquatic kind of buttercup (Ranunculus aquatilis), used as
food for cattle in parts of England. Great water crowfoot, an
American water plant (Ranunculus multifidus), having deep yellow
flowers.
