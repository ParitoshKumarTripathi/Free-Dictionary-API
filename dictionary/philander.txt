PHILANDER
Phi*lan"der, v. i. Etym: [Gr.

Defn: To make love to women; to play the male flirt.
You can't go philandering after her again. G. Eliot.
PHILANDER

=====
Phi*lan"der, n.

Defn: A lover. [R.] Congreve.
PHILANDER

=====
Phi*lan"der, n. (Zoöl.)
(a) A South American opossum (Didelphys philander).
(b) An Australian bandicoot (Perameles lagotis).
