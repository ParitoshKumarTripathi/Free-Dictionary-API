PERCLOSE
Per*close", n. Etym: [OF. parclose an inclosed place; L. per through
+ claudere, clausum, to shut.]

1. (Eccl. Arch.)

Defn: Same as Parclose.

2. Conclusion; end. [Obs.] Sir W. Raleigh.
