NUDGE
Nudge, v. t. [imp. & p. p. Nudge; p. pr. & vb. n. Nudging.] Etym:
[Cf. Prov. G. knütschen to squeeze, pinch, E. Knock.]

Defn: To touch gently, as with the elbow, in order to call attention
or convey intimation.
NUDGE

=====
Nudge, n.

Defn: A gentle push, or jog, as with the elbow.
