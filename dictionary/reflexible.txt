REFLEXIBLE
Re*flex"i*ble (r*flks"*b'l), a. Etym: [CF. F. réflexible.]

Defn: Capable of being reflected, or thrown back.
The light of the sun consists of rays differently refrangible and
reflexible. Cheyne.
