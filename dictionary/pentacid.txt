PENTACID
Pen*tac"id ( Etym: [Penta- + acid.] (Chem.)

Defn: Capable of neutralizing, or combining with, five molecules of a
monobasic acid; having five hydrogen atoms capable of substitution by
acid residues; -- said of certain complex bases.
