TRULL
Trull, n. Etym: [G. trolle, trulle; cf. OD. drol a jester, Dan. trold
an elf, imp, Sw. troll a goblin, Icel. troll, tröll, a giant, fiend,
demon. Cf. Droll, Troll.]

1. A drab; a strumpet; a harlot; a trollop. Shak.

2. A girl; a wench; a lass. [Obs.]
