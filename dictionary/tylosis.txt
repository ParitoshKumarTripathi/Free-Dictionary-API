TYLOSIS
Ty*lo"sis, n.; pl. Tyloses. Etym: [NL., fr. Gr. (Bot.)

Defn: An intrusion of one vegetable cell into the cavity of another,
sometimes forming there an irregular mass of cells. Goodale.
