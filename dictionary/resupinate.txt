RESUPINATE
Re*su"pi*nate, a. Etym: [L. resupinatus, p. p. of resupinare to bend
back. See Resupine.]

Defn: Inverted in position; appearing to be upside down or reversed,
as the flowers of the orchis and the leaves of some plants.
