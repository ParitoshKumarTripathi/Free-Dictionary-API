SPINDLEWORM
Spin"dle*worm`, n. (Zoöl.)

Defn: The larva of a noctuid mmoth (Achatodes zeæ) which feeds inside
the stalks of corn (maize), sometimes causing much damage. It is
smooth, with a black head and tail and a row of black dots across
each segment.
