UNNATURAL
Un*nat"u*ral, a.

Defn: Not natural; contrary, or not conforming, to the order of
nature; being without natural traits; as, unnatural crimes.

Syn.
 -- See Factitious.
 -- Un*nat"u*ral*ly, adv.
 -- Un*nat"u*ral*ness, n.
