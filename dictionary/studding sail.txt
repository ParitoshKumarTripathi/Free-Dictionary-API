STUDDING SAIL
Stud"ding sail`. (Naut.)

Defn: A light sail set at the side of a principal or square sail of a
vessel in free winds, to increase her speed. Its head is bent to a
small spar which is called the studding-sail boom. See Illust. of
Sail. Toten.
