ORGANOLEPTIC
Or`ga*no*lep"tic, a. Etym: [F. organoleptique, fr. Gr. (Physiol.)

Defn: Making an impression upon an organ; plastic; -- said of the
effect or impression produced by any substance on the organs of
touch, taste, or smell, and also on the organism as a whole.
