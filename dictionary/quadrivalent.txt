QUADRIVALENT
Quad*riv"a*lent, a. Etym: [Quadri- + L. valens, -entis, p. pr. See
Valence.] (Chem.)

Defn: Having a valence of four; capable of combining with, being
replaced by, or compared with, four monad atoms; tetravalent; -- said
of certain atoms and radicals; thus, carbon and silicon are
quadrivalent elements.
