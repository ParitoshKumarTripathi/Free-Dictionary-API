RETIRADE
Ret`i*rade", n. Etym: [F.; cf. Sp. retirada retreat. See Retire.]
(Fort.)

Defn: A kind of retrenchment, as in the body of a bastion, which may
be disputed inch by inch after the defenses are dismantled. It
usually consists of two faces which make a reëntering angle.
