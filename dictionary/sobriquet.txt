SOBRIQUET
So`bri`quet" (so`bre`kay"), n.Etym: [F. sobriquet, OF. soubzbriquet,
soubriquet, a chuck under the chin, hence, an affront, a nickname; of
uncertain origin; cf. It. sottobecco a chuck under the chin.]

Defn: An assumed name; a fanciful epithet or appellation; a nickname.
[Sometimes less correctly written soubriquet.]
