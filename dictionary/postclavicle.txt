POSTCLAVICLE
Post*clav"i*cle, n. Etym: [Pref. post- + clavicle.] (Anat.)

Defn: A bone in the pectoral girdle of many fishes projecting
backward from the clavicle.
 -- Post`*cla*vic"u*lar, a.
