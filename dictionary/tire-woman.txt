TIRE-WOMAN
Tire"-wom`an, n.; pl. Tire-women. Etym: [See Tire attire, Attire.]

1. A lady's maid.
Fashionableness of the tire-woman's making. Locke.

2. A dresser in a theater. Simmonds.
