SCRAGGY
Scrag"gy, a. [Compar. Scragger; superl. Scraggiest.]

1. Rough with irregular points; scragged. "A scraggy rock." J.
Philips.

2. Lean and rough; scragged. "His sinewy, scraggy neck." Sir W.
Scott.
