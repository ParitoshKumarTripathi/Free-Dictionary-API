SINISTRAL
Sin"is*tral, a.

1. Of or pertaining to the left, inclining to the left; sinistrous; -
- opposed to dextral.

2. (Zoöl.)

Defn: Having the whorls of the spire revolving or rising to the left;
reversed; -- said of certain spiral shells.
