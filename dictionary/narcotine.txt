NARCOTINE
Nar"co*tine, n. Etym: [Cf. F. narcotine. Cf. Cotarnine.] (Chem.)

Defn: An alkaloid found in opium, and extracted as a white
crystalline substance, tasteless and less poisonous than morphine; --
called also narcotia.
