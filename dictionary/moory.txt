MOORY
Moor"y, a.

Defn: Of or pertaining to moors; marshy; fenny; boggy; moorish.
Mortimer.
As when thick mists arise from moory vales. Fairfax.
MOORY

=====
Moor"y, n.

Defn: A kind of blue cloth made in India. Balfour (Cyc of India).
