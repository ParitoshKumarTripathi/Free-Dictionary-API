TOON
Toon, obs.

Defn: pl. of Toe. Chaucer.
TOON

=====
Toon, n. Etym: [Hind. tun, tun, Skr. tunna.] (Bot.)

Defn: The reddish brown wood of an East Indian tree (Cedrela Toona)
closely resembling the Spanish cedar; also. the tree itself.
