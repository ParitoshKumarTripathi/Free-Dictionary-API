PHOCENIC
Pho*cen"ic, a. Etym: [Gr. (Chem.)

Defn: Of or pertaining to dolphin oil or porpoise oil; -- said of an
acid (called also delphinic acid) subsequently found to be identical
with valeric acid. Watts.
