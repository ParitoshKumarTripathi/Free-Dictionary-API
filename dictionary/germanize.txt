GERMANIZE
Ger"man*ize, v. t. [imp. & p. p. Germanized; p. pr. & vb. n.
Germanizing.]

Defn: To make German, or like what is distinctively German; as, to
Germanize a province, a language, a society.
GERMANIZE

=====
Ger"man*ize, v. i.

Defn: To reason or write after the manner of the Germans.
