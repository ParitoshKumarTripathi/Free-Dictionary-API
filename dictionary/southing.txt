SOUTHING
South"ing, n.

1. Tendency or progress southward; as, the southing of the sun.
Emerson.

2. The time at which the moon, or other heavenly body, passes the
meridian of a place.

3. (Astron.)

Defn: Distance of any heavenly body south of the equator; south
declination; south latitude.

4. (Surv. & Navigation)

Defn: Distance southward from any point departure or of reckoning,
measured on a meridian; -- opposed to northing.
