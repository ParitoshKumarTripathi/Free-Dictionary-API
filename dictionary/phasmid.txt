PHASMID
Phas"mid, n. Etym: [See Phasm. Probably so called from its mimicking,
or appearing like, inanimate objects.] (Zoöl.)

Defn: Any orthopterous insect of the family Phasmidæ, as a leaf
insect or a stick insect.
