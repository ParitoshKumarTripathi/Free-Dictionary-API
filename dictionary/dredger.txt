DREDGER
Dredg"er, n.

1. One who fishes with a dredge.

2. A dredging machine.
DREDGER

=====
Dredg"er, n. (Cookery)

Defn: A box with holes in its lid; -- used for sprinkling flour, as
on meat or a breadboard; -- called also dredging box, drudger, and
drudging box.
