RECOLLET
Rec"ol*let (rk"l*lt; F. r`k`l"), n. Etym: [F. récollet, fr. L.
recollectus, p.p. of recolligere to gather again, to gather up; NL.,
to collect one's self, esp. for religious contemplation.] (Eccl.)

Defn: Same as Recollect, n.
