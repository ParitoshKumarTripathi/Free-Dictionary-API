WILLEMITE
Wil"lem*ite, n. Etym: [From Willem I., king of the Netherlands.]
(Min.)

Defn: A silicate of zinc, usually occurring massive and of a greenish
yellow color, also in reddish crystals (troostite) containing
manganese.
