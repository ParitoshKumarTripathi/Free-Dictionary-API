SHEETING
Sheet"ing, n.

1. Cotton or linen cloth suitable for bed sheets. It is sometimes
made of double width.

2. (Hydraul. Engin.)

Defn: A lining of planks or boards (rarely of metal) for protecting
an embankment.

3. The act or process of forming into sheets, or flat pieces; also,
material made into sheets.
