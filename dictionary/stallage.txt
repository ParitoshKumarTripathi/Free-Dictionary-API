STALLAGE
Stall"age, n. Etym: [Cf. OF.estallange, of German origin. See Stall,
n. ]

1. (Eng. Law)

Defn: The right of erecting a stalls in fairs; rent paid for a stall.

2. Dung of cattle or horses, mixed with straw. [Obs.]
