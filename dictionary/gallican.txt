GALLICAN
Gal"li*can, a. Etym: [L. Gallicanus: cf. F. gallican.]

Defn: Of or pertaining to Gaul or France; Gallic; French; as, the
Gallican church or clergy.
GALLICAN

=====
Gal"li*can, n.

Defn: An adherent to, and supporter of, Gallicanism. Shipley.
