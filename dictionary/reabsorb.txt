REABSORB
Re`ab*sorb", v. t.

Defn: To absorb again; to draw in, or imbibe, again what has been
effused, extravasated, or thrown off; to swallow up again; as, to
reabsorb chyle, lymph, etc.; -- used esp. of fluids.
