SPECTROBOLOMETER
Spec`tro*bo*lom"e*ter, n. (Physics)

Defn: A combination of spectroscope and bolometer for determining the
distribution of energy in a spectrum. --Spec`tro*bo`lo*met"ric (#),
a.
