TERMINABLE
Ter"mi*na*ble, a. Etym: [See Terminate.]

Defn: Capable of being terminated or bounded; limitable.
 -- Ter"mi*na*ble*ness, n. Terminable annuity, an annuity for a
stated, definite number of years; -- distinguished from life annuity,
and perpetual annuity.
