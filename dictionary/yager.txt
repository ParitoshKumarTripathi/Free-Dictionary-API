YAGER
Ya"ger, n. Etym: [G. jäger a hunter, from jagen to chase, hunt.]
(Mil.)

Defn: In the German army, one belonging to a body of light infantry
armed with rifles, resembling the chasseur of the French army.
[Written also jager.]
