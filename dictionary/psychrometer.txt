PSYCHROMETER
Psy*chrom"e*ter, n. Etym: [Gr. psychro`s cold + -meter: cf. F.
psychromètre.]

Defn: An instrument for measuring the tension of the aqueous vapor in
the atmosphere, being essentially a wet and dry bulb hygrometer.
