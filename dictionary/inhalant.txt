INHALANT
In*hal"ant, a. Etym: [Cf. F. inhalant.]

Defn: Inhaling; used for inhaling.
INHALANT

=====
In*hal"ant, n.

Defn: An apparatus also called an inhaler (which see); that which is
to be inhaled.
