PHARYNGOGNATHI
Phar`yn*gog"na*thi, n. pl. Etym: [NL. See Pharynx, and Gnathic.]
(Zoöl.)

Defn: A division of fishes in which the lower pharyngeal bones are
united. It includes the scaroid, labroid, and embioticoid fishes.
