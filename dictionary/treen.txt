TREEN
Treen, a. Etym: [AS. treówen.]

1. Made of wood; wooden. [Obs.] " Treen cups." Camden.

2. Relating to, or drawn from, trees. [Obs.] Spenser.
Treen liquors, especially that of the date. Evelyn.
TREEN

=====
Treen, obs.

Defn: pl. of Tree. " The shady treen." Fairfax.
