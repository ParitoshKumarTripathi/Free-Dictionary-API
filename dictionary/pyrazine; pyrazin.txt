PYRAZINE; PYRAZIN
Pyr"a*zine, n. Also -zin. [Pyridine + Gr.  not +  life.] (Org. Chem.)

Defn: A feebly basic solid, C4H4N2, obtained by distilling piperazine
with zinc dust, and in other ways. Also, by extension, any of various
derivatives of the same.
