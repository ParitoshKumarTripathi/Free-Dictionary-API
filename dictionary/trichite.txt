TRICHITE
Trich"ite, n. Etym: [Gr.

1. (Min.)

Defn: A kind of crystallite resembling a bunch of hairs, common in
obsidian. See Illust. of Crystallite.

2. (Zoöl.)

Defn: A delicate, hairlike siliceous spicule, found in certain
sponges. Trichite sheaf (Zoöl.), one of the small sheaflike fascicles
of slender setæ characteristic of certain sponges. See Illust. under
Spicule.
