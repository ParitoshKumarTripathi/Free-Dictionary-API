RINDERPEST
Rin"der*pest, n. Etym: [G., fr. rind, pl. rinder, cattle + pest pest,
plague.]

Defn: A highly contagious distemper or murrain, affecting neat
cattle, and less commonly sheep and goats; -- called also cattle
plague, Russian cattle plague, and steppe murrain.
