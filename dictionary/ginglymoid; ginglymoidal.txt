GINGLYMOID; GINGLYMOIDAL
Gin"gly*moid, Gin`gly*moid"al, a. Etym: [Gr. ginglymoide,
ginglymoïdal.] (Anat.)

Defn: Pertaining to, or resembling, a ginglymus, or hinge joint;
ginglyform.
