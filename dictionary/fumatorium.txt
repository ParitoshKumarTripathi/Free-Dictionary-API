FUMATORIUM
Fu`ma*to"ri*um, n.; L. pl. -ria (#). [NL., fr. L. fumare, fumatum, to
smoke.]

Defn: An air-tight compartment in which vapor may be generated to
destroy germs or insects; esp., the apparatus used to destroy San
José scale on nursery stock, with hydrocyanic acid vapor.
