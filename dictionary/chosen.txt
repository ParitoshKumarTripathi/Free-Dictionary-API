CHOSEN
Cho"sen, p. p. of Choose.

Defn: Selected from a number; picked out; choice.
Seven hundred chosen men left-handed. Judg. xx. 16.
CHOSEN

=====
Cho"sen, n.

Defn: One who, or that which is the object of choice or special
favor.
