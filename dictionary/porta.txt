PORTA
Por"ta, n.; pl. Portæ. Etym: [L., a gate. See Port a hole.] (Anat.)
(a) The part of the liver or other organ where its vessels and nerves
enter; the hilus.
(b) The foramen of Monro. B. G. Wilder.
