THIGMOTAXIS
Thig`mo*tax"is, n. [NL., fr. Gr.  touch +  an arranging.] (Physiol.)

Defn: The property possessed by living protoplasm of contracting, and
thus moving, when touched by a solid or fluid substance.

 When the movement is away from the touching body, it is negative
thigmotaxis; when towards it, positive thigmotaxis.
