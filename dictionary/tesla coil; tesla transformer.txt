TESLA COIL; TESLA TRANSFORMER
Tes"la coil, Tes"la trans*form"er. [After N. Tesla, American
electrician.] (Elec.)

Defn: A transformer without iron, for high frequency alternating or
oscillating currents; an oscillation transformer.
