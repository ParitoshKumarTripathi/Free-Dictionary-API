NONRESIDENT
Non*res"i*dent, a.

Defn: Not residing in a particular place, on one's own estate, or in
one's proper place; as, a nonresident clergyman or proprietor of
lands.
NONRESIDENT

=====
Non*res"i*dent, n.

Defn: A nonresident person; one who does not reside in the State or
jurisdiction.
