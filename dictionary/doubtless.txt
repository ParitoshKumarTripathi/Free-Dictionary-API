DOUBTLESS
Doubt"less, a.

Defn: Free from fear or suspicion. [Obs.]
Pretty child, sleep doubtless and secure. Shak.
DOUBTLESS

=====
Doubt"less, adv.

Defn: Undoubtedly; without doubt.
