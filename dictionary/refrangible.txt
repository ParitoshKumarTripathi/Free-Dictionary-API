REFRANGIBLE
Re*fran"gi*ble (-frn"j*b'l), a. Etym: [Cf. F. réfrangible. See
Refract.]

Defn: Capable of being refracted, or turned out of a direct course,
in passing from one medium to another, as rays of light.
 -- Re*fran"gi*ble*ness, n.
