OUTCROP
Out"crop`, n. (Geol.)
(a) The coming out of a stratum to the surface of the ground. Lyell.
(b) That part of inclined strata which appears at the surface;
basset.
OUTCROP

=====
Out*crop", v. i. (Geol.)

Defn: To come out to the surface of the ground; -- said of strata.
