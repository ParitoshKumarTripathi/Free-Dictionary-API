DOGGER
Dog"ger, n. Etym: [D., fr. dogger codfish, orig. used in the catching
of codfish.] (Naut.)

Defn: A two-masted fishing vessel, used by the Dutch.
DOGGER

=====
Dog"ger, n.

Defn: A sort of stone, found in the mines with the true alum rock,
chiefly of silica and iron.
