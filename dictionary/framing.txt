FRAMING
Fram"ing, n.

1. The act, process, or style of putting together a frame, or of
constructing anything; a frame; that which frames.

2. (Arch. & Engin.)

Defn: A framework, or a sy Framing chisel (Carp.), a heavy chisel
with a socket shank for making mortises.
