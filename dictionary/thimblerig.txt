THIMBLERIG
Thim"ble*rig`, n.

Defn: A sleight-of-hand trick played with three small cups, shaped
like thimbles, and a small ball or little pea.
THIMBLERIG

=====
Thim"ble*rig`, v. t. [imp. & p. p. Thimblerigged; p. pr. & vb. n.
Thimblerigging.]

Defn: To swindle by means of small cups or thimbles, and a pea or
small ball placed under one of them and quickly shifted to another,
the victim laying a wager that he knows under which cup it is; hence,
to cheat by any trick.
