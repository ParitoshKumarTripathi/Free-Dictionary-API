PERCOLATION
Per`co*la"tion, n. Etym: [L. percolatio.]

Defn: The act or process of percolating, or filtering; filtration;
straining. Specifically (Pharm.), the process of exhausting the
virtues of a powdered drug by letting a liquid filter slowly through
it.
