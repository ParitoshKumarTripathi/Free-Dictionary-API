PERCESOCES
Per*ces"o*ces, n. pl. Etym: [NL., fr. L. perca a perch + esox, -ocis,
a pike.] (Zoöl.)

Defn: An order of fishes including the gray mullets (Mugil), the
barracudas, the silversides, and other related fishes. So called from
their relation both to perches and to pikes.
