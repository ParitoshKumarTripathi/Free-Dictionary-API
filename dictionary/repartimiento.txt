REPARTIMIENTO
Re`par*ti`mi*en"to (r`pr-t`m-n"t), n. Etym: [Sp., fr. repartir to
divide.]

Defn: A partition or distribution, especially of slaves; also, an
assessment of taxes. W. Irving.
