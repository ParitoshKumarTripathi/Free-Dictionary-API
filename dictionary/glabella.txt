GLABELLA
Gla*bel"la, n.; pl. Glabell. Etym: [NL., fr. L. glabellus hairless,
fr. glaber bald.] (Anat.)

Defn: The space between the eyebrows, also including the
corresponding part of the frontal bone; the mesophryon.
 -- Gla*bel"lar, a.
