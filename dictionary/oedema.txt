OEDEMA
OE*de"ma, n. Etym: [NL., from Gr. (Med.)

Defn: A swelling from effusion of watery fluid in the cellular tissue
beneath the skin or mucous membrance; dropsy of the subcutaneous
cellular tissue. [Written also edema.]
