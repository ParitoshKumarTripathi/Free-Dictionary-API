TRIDACTYL; TRIDACTYLE
Tri*dac"tyl Tri*dac"tyle, a. Etym: [Gr. Tri-) + tridactyle.] (Biol.)

Defn: Having three fingers or toes, or composed of three movable
parts attached to a common base.
