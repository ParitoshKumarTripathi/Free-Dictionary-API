FODIENT
Fo"di*ent, a. Etym: [L. fodiens, p. pr. of fodere to dig.]

Defn: Fitted for, or pertaining to, digging.
FODIENT

=====
Fo"di*ent, n. (Zoöl.)

Defn: One of the Fodientia.
