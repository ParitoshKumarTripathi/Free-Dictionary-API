TRODE
Trode, archaic

Defn: imp. of Tread.
On burnished hooves his war-horse trode. Tennyson.
TRODE

=====
Trode, n. Etym: [AS. trod, fr. tredan to tread. See Tread.]

Defn: Tread; footing. [Written also troad.][Obs.] Spenser.
