SUDORIFEROUS
Su`dor*if"er*ous, a. Etym: [L. sudor sweat + -ferous.] (Physiol.)

Defn: Producing, or secreting, sweat; sudoriparous. Sudoriferous
glands (Anat.), small convoluted tubular glands which are situated in
the subcutaneous tissues and discharge by minute orifices in the
surface of the skin; the sweat glands.
