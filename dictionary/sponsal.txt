SPONSAL
Spon"sal, a. Etym: [L. sponsalis, fr. sponsus a betrothal, fr.
spondere, sponsum, to betroth. See Spouse, and cf. Esousal, Spousal.]

Defn: Relating to marriage, or to a spouse; spousal.
