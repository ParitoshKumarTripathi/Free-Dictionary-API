FRATRICIDE
Frat"ri*cide, n. Etym: [L. fratricidium a brother's murder, fr.
fratricida a brother's murderer; frater, fratris, brother + caedere
to kill: cf. F. fratricide.]

1. The act of one who murders or kills his own brother.

2. Etym: [L. fratricida: cf. F. fratricide.]

Defn: One who murders or kills his own brother.
