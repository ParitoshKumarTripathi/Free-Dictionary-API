Were, v. t. & i.

Defn: To wear. See 3d Wear. [Obs.] Chaucer.
WERE

=====
Were, n.

Defn: A weir. See Weir. [Obs.] Chaucer. Sir P. Sidney.
WERE

=====
Were, v. t. Etym: [AS. werian.]

Defn: To guard; to protect. [Obs.] Chaucer.
WERE

=====
Were. Etym: [AS. wre (thou) wast, w (we, you, they) were, w imp.
subj. See Was.]

Defn: The imperfect indicative plural, and imperfect subjunctive
singular and plural, of the verb be. See Be.
