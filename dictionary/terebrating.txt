TEREBRATING
Ter"e*bra`ting, a.

1. (Zoöl.)

Defn: Boring; perforating; -- applied to molluskas which form holes
in rocks, wood, etc.

2. (Med.)

Defn: Boring; piercing; -- applied to certain kinds of pain,
especially to those of locomotor ataxia.
