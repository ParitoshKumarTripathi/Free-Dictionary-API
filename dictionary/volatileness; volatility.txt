VOLATILENESS; VOLATILITY
Vol"a*tile*ness, Vol`a*til"i*ty, n. Etym: [Cf. F. volatilité.]

Defn: Quality or state of being volatile; disposition to evaporate;
changeableness; fickleness.

Syn.
