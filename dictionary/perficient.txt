PERFICIENT
Per*fi"cient, a. Etym: [L. perficiens, p.pr. of perficere to perform.
See Perfect.]

Defn: Making or doing throughly; efficient; effectual. [R.]
Blackstone.
PERFICIENT

=====
Per*fi"cient, n.

Defn: One who performs or perfects a work; especially, one who endows
a charity. [R.]
