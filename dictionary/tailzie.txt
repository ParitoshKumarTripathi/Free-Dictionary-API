TAILZIE
Tail"zie (-zî or -yî), n. Etym: [F. tailler to cut. See Tail a
limitation.] (Scots Law)

Defn: An entailment or deed whereby the legal course of succession is
cut off, and an arbitrary one substituted. [Written also tailzee.]
