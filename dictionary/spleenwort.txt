SPLEENWORT
Spleen"wort`, n. Etym: [Spleen + wort; cf. L. splenium, asplenium,
Gr. (Bot.)

Defn: Any fern of the genus Asplenium, some species of which were
anciently used as remedies for disorders of the spleen.
