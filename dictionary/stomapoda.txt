STOMAPODA
Sto*map"o*da, n. pl. Etym: [NL. See Stoma, and -poda.] (Zoöl.)

Defn: An order of Crustacea including the squillas. The maxillipeds
are leglike in form, and the large claws are comblike. They have a
large and elongated abdomen, which contains a part of the stomach and
heart; the abdominal appendages are large, and bear the gills. Called
also Gastrula, Stomatopoda, and Squilloidea.
