QUADRIFARIOUS
Quad`ri*fa"ri*ous, a. Etym: [L. quadrifarius fourfold, fr. quattuor
four: cf. F. quadrifarié. Cf. Multifarious.]

Defn: Arranged in four rows or ranks; as, quadrifarious leaves.
Loudon.
