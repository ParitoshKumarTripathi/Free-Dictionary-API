WEST INDIA; WEST INDIAN
West` In"di*a, West` In"di*an.

Defn: Belonging or relating to the West Indies. West India tea
(Bot.), a shrubby plant (Capraria biflora) having oblanceolate
toothed leaves which are sometimes used in the West Indies as a
substitute for tea.
