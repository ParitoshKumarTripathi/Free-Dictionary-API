WANHOPE
Wan"hope`, n. Etym: [AS. wan, won, deficient, wanting + hopa hope:
cf. D. wanhoop. . See Wane, and Hope.]

Defn: Want of hope; despair; also, faint or delusive hope; delusion.
[Obs.] Piers Plowman. "Wanhope and distress." Chaucer.
