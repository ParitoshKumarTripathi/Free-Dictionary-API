RABDOLOGY
Rab*dol"o*gy (-dol"o*jy), n. [Gr. "ra`bdos rod, stick + -logy: cf. F.
rabdologie.]

Defn: The method or art of performing arithmetical operations by
means of Napier's bones. See Napier's bones. [Written also
rhabdology.]
