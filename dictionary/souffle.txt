SOUFFLE
Souf"fle, n. Etym: [F.] (Med.)

Defn: A murmuring or blowing sound; as, the uterine souffle heard
over the pregnant uterus.
SOUFFLE

=====
Souf"flé, n. Etym: [F., fr. soufflé, p.p. of souffler to puff.]
(Cookery)

Defn: A side dish served hot from the oven at dinner, made of eggs,
milk, and flour or other farinaceous substance, beaten till very
light, and flavored with fruits, liquors, or essence.
