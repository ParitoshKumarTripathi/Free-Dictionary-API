ORBITOSPHENOID
Or`bi*to*sphe"noid, a. Etym: [Orbit + sphenoid.] (Anat.)

Defn: Of or pertaining to the sphenoid bone and the orbit, or to the
orbitosphenoid bone.
 -- n.

Defn: The orbitosphenoid bone, which is situated in the orbit on
either side of the presphenoid. It generally forms a part of the
sphenoid in the adult.
