TURGENT
Tur"gent, a. Etym: [L. turgens, -entis, p. pr. of turgere to swell.]

1. Rising into a tumor, or a puffy state; swelling; tumid; as,
turgent humors.

2. Inflated; bombastic; turgid; pompous.
Recompensed with turgent titles. Burton.
