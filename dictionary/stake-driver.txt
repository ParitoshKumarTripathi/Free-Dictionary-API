STAKE-DRIVER
Stake"-driv`er, n. (Zoöl.)

Defn: The common American bittern (Botaurus lentiginosus); -- so
called because one of its notes resembles the sound made in driving a
stake into the mud. Called also meadow hen, and Indian hen.
