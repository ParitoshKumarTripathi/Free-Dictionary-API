SANDPAPER
Sand"pa`per, n.

Defn: Paper covered on one side with sand glued fast, -- used for
smoothing and polishing.
SANDPAPER

=====
Sand"pa`per, v. t.

Defn: To smooth or polish with sandpaper; as, to sandpaper a door.
