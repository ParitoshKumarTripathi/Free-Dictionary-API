PARTITIVE
Par"ti*tive, a. Etym: [Cf. F. partitif.] (Gram.)

Defn: Denoting a part; as, a partitive genitive.
PARTITIVE

=====
Par"ti*tive, n. (Gram.)

Defn: A word expressing partition, or denoting a part.
