SUFFERING
Suf"fer*ing, n.

Defn: The bearing of pain, inconvenience, or loss; pain endured;
distress, loss, or injury incurred; as, sufferings by pain or sorrow;
sufferings by want or by wrongs. "Souls in sufferings tried." Keble.
SUFFERING

=====
Suf"fer*ing, a.

Defn: Being in pain or grief; having loss, injury, distress, etc.
 -- Suf"fer*ing*ly, adv.
