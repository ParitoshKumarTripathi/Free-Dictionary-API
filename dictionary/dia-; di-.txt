DIA-; DI-
Di"a-, Di-. Etym: [Gr. Two, and cf. 1st Di-.]

Defn: A prefix denoting through; also, between, apart, asunder,
across. Before a vowel dia- becomes di-; as, diactinic; dielectric,
etc.
