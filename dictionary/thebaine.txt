THEBAINE
The*ba"ine, n. Etym: [So called from a kind of Egyptian opium
produced at Thebes.] (Chem.)

Defn: A poisonous alkaloid, C19H21NO3, found in opium in small
quantities, having a sharp, astringent taste, and a tetanic action
resembling that of strychnine.
