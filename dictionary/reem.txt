REEM

=====
Reem, v. t. Etym: [Cf. Ream to make a hole in.] (Naut.)

Defn: To open (the seams of a vessel's planking) for the purpose of
calking them. Reeming iron (Naut.), an iron chisel for reeming the
seams of planks in calking ships.
