OEIL-DE-PERDRIX
Oil`-de-per`drix", a. [F., lit., eye of a partridge.]

1. (Ornamental Art) Characterized by, or decorated with, small round
points, spots, or rings; as, oil-de-perdrix pattern.

2.  Having a brownish red color; -- used esp. of light-colored red
wine.
