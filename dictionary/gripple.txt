GRIPPLE
Grip"ple, n.

Defn: A grasp; a gripe. [Obs.] Spenser.
GRIPPLE

=====
Grip"ple, a. Etym: [Dim. fr. gripe.]

Defn: Griping; greedy; covetous; tenacious. [Obs.] Spenser.
