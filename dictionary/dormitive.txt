DORMITIVE
Dor"mi*tive, a. Etym: [Cf. F. dormitif, fr. dormire to sleep.]

Defn: Causing sleep; as, the dormitive properties of opium. Clarke.
 -- n. (Med.)

Defn: A medicine to promote sleep; a soporific; an opiate.
