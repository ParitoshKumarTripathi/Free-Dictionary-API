SCAPHITE
Scaph"ite, n. Etym: [L. scapha a boat, fr. Gr. (Paleon.)

Defn: Any fossil cephalopod shell of the genus Scaphites, belonging
to the Ammonite family and having a chambered boat-shaped shell.
Scaphites are found in the Cretaceous formation.
