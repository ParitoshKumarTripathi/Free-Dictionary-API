RESIANT
Res"i*ant (-ant), a. Etym: [OF. reseant, resseant, L. residens. See
Resident.]

Defn: Resident; present in a place. [Obs.]
In which her kingdom's throne is chiefly resiant. Spenser.
RESIANT

=====
Res"i*ant, n.

Defn: A resident. [Obs.] Sir T. More.
