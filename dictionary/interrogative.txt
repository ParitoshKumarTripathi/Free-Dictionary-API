INTERROGATIVE
In`ter*rog"a*tive (, a. Etym: [L. interrogativus: cf. F.
interrogatif.]

Defn: Denoting a question; expressed in the form of a question; as,
an interrogative sentence; an interrogative pronoun.
INTERROGATIVE

=====
In`ter*rog"a*tive, n. (Gram.)

Defn: A word used in asking questions; as, who which why
