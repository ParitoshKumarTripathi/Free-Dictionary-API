CENTUPLE
Cen"tu*ple, a. Etym: [L. centuplex; centum + plicare to fold; cf. F.
centuple.]

Defn: Hundredfold.
CENTUPLE

=====
Cen"tu*ple, v. t.

Defn: To increase a hundredfold.
