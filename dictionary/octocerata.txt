OCTOCERATA
Oc`to*cer"a*ta, n.pl. Etym: [NL., fr. Gr. (Zoöl.)

Defn: A suborder of Cephalopoda including Octopus, Argonauta, and
allied genera, having eight arms around the head; -- called also
Octopoda.
