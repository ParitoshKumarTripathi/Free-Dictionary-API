VITRIOLIC
Vit`ri*ol"ic, a. Etym: [Cf. F. vitriolique.] (Chem.)

Defn: Of or pertaining to vitriol; derived from, or resembling,
vitriol; vitriolous; as, a vitriolic taste. Cf. Vitriol. Vitriolic
acid (Old Chem.), (a) sulphuric acid. See Vitriol (b). [Colloq.]
