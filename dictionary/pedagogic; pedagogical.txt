PEDAGOGIC; PEDAGOGICAL
Ped`a*gog"ic, Ped`a*gog"ic*al, a. Etym: [Gr. pédagogique. See
Pedagogue.]

Defn: Of or pertaining to a pedagogue; suited to, or characteristic
of, a pedagogue.
