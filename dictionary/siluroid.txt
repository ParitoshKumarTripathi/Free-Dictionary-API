SILUROID
Si*lu"roid, n. Etym: [Silurus + -oid.] (Zoöl.)

Defn: Belonging to the Siluroidei, or Nematognathi, an order of
fishes including numerous species, among which are the American
catfishes and numerous allied fresh-water species of the Old World,
as the sheatfish (Silurus glanis) of Europe.
 -- n.

Defn: A siluroid fish.
