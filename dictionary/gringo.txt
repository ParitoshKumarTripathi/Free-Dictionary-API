GRINGO
Grin"go, n. [Amer. Sp., fr. Sp. gringo gibberish; cf. griego Greek,
F. grigou wretch.]

Defn: Among Spanish Americans, a foreigner, esp. an Englishman or
American; -- often used as a term of reproach.
