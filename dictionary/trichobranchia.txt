TRICHOBRANCHIA
Trich`o*bran"chi*a, n. Etym: [NL., fr. Gr. branchia.] (Zoöl.)

Defn: The gill of a crustacean in which the branchial filaments are
slender and cylindrical, as in the crawfishes.
