NOSOPHEN
Nos"o*phen, n.  [Nose + phenol; orig. used for affections of the
nose.] (Pharm.)

Defn: An iodine compound obtained as a yellowish gray, odorless,
tasteless powder by the action of iodine on phenolphthalein.
