HOCK
Hock, n. Etym: [So called from Hochheim, in Germany.]

Defn: A Rhenish wine, of a light yellow color, either sparkling or
still. The name is also given indiscriminately to all Rhenish wines.
HOCK

=====
Hock, v. t.

Defn: To disable by cutting the tendons of the hock; to hamstring; to
hough.
