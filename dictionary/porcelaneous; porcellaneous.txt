PORCELANEOUS; PORCELLANEOUS
Por`ce*la"ne*ous, Por`cel*la"ne*ous, a.

1. Of or pertaining to porcelain; resembling porcelain; as,
porcelaneous shells.

2. (Zoöl.)

Defn: Having a smooth, compact shell without pores; -- said of
certain Foraminifera.
