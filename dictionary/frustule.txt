FRUSTULE
Frus"tule, n. Etym: [L. frustulum, dim. fr. frustum a piece: cf. F.
frustule.] (Bot.)

Defn: The siliceous shell of a diatom. It is composed of two valves,
one overlapping the other, like a pill box and its cover.
