SPREAD-EAGLE
Spread"-ea`gle, a.

Defn: Characterized by a pretentious, boastful, exaggerated style;
defiantly or extravagantly bombastic; as, a spread-eagle orator; a
spread-eagle speech. [Colloq.& Humorous]
SPREAD-EAGLE

=====
Spread"-ea`gle v. i.

Defn: To assume a spread-eagled position; -- it may be done
reclining, for relaxation, or momentarily, as an exhibitionary
maneuver in a sport.
