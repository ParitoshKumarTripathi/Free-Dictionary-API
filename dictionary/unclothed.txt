UNCLOTHED
Un*clothed", a.

1. Etym: [Properly p. p. of unclothe.]

Defn: Divested or stripped of clothing. Byron.

2. Etym: [Pref. un- not + clothed.]

Defn: Not yet clothed; wanting clothes; naked.
 -- Un*cloth"ed*ly, adv. [Obs.] Bacon.
