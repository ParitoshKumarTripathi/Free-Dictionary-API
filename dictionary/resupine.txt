RESUPINE
Re`su*pine", a. Etym: [L. resupinus; pref.re- re- + supinus bent
backward, supine.]

Defn: Lying on the back; supine; hence, careless. Sir K. Digby.
He spake, and, downward swayed, fell resupine, With his huge neck
aslant. Cowper.
