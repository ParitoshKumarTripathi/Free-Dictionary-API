UNSOFT
Un*soft", a.

Defn: Not soft; hard; coarse; rough. [Obs.] "Bristles of his beard
unsoft." Chaucer.
UNSOFT

=====
Un*soft", adv. Etym: [AS. uns. See Un- not, and Soft.]

Defn: Not softly. [Obs.]
Great climbers fall unsoft. Spenser.
