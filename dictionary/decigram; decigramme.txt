DECIGRAM; DECIGRAMME
Dec"i*gram, Dec"i*gramme, n. Etym: [F. décigramme; pref. déci- tenth
(fr. L. decimus) + gramme.]

Defn: A weight in the metric system; one tenth of a gram, equal to
1.5432 grains avoirdupois.
