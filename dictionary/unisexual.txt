UNISEXUAL
U`ni*sex"u*al, a. Etym: [Uni- + sexual: cf. F. unisexuel.] (Biol.)

Defn: Having one sex only, as plants which have the male and female
flowers on separate individuals, or animals in which the sexes are in
separate individuals; dioecious; -- distinguished from bisexual, or
hermaphrodite. See Dioecious.
