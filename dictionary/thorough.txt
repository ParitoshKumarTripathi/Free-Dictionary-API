THOROUGH
Thor"ough, prep. Etym: [See Through.]

Defn: Through. [Obs.] Spenser. Shak.
THOROUGH

=====
Thor"ough, a.

1. Passing through; as, thorough lights in a house. [Obs.]

2. Passing through or to the end; hence, complete; perfect; as, a
thorough reformation; thorough work; a thorough translator; a
thorough poet.
THOROUGH

=====
Thor"ough, adv.

1. Thoroughly. [Obs. or Colloq.] Chaucer.

2. Through. [Obs.] Shak.
THOROUGH

=====
Thor"ough, n.

Defn: A furrow between two ridges, to drain off the surface water.
[Prov. Eng.] Halliwell.
