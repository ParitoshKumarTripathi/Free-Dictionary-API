SCHEMATISM
Sche"ma*tism, n. Etym: [Cf. F. schématisme (cf. L. schematismos
florid speech), fr. Gr. Scheme.]

1. (Astrol.)

Defn: Combination of the aspects of heavenly bodies.

2. Particular form or disposition of a thing; an exhibition in
outline of any systematic arrangement. [R.]
