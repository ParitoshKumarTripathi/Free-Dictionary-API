PHRENITIS
Phre*ni"tis, n. Etym: [L., fr. Gr.

1. (Med.)

Defn: Inflammation of the brain, or of the meninges of the brain,
attended with acute fever and delirium; -- called also cephalitis.

2. See Frenzy.
