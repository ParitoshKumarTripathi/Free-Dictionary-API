THEOREMATIC; THEOREMATICAL
The`o*re*mat"ic, The`o*re*mat"ic*al, a. Etym: [Cf. Gr.

Defn: Of or pertaining to a theorem or theorems; comprised in a
theorem; consisting of theorems.
