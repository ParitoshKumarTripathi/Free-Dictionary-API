TASTY
Tast"y, a. [Compar. Tastier; superl. Tastiest.]

1. Having a good taste; -- applied to persons; as, a tasty woman. See
Taste, n., 5.

2. Being in conformity to the principles of good taste; elegant; as,
tasty furniture; a tasty dress.
