SIGHT-SEEING
Sight"-see`ing, a.

Defn: Engaged in, or given to, seeing sights; eager for novelties or
curiosities.
SIGHT-SEEING

Sight"-see`ing, n.

Defn: The act of seeing sights; eagerness for novelties or
curiosities.
