TIRRALIRRA
Tir"ra*lir`ra, n.

Defn: A verbal imitation of a musical sound, as of the note of a lark
or a horn.
The lark, that tirra lyra chants. Shak.
"Tirralira, " by the river, Sang Sir Lancelot. Tennyson.
