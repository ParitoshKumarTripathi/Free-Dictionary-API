VINUM
Vi"num, n.; pl. Vina (#). [L. See Wine.]

Defn: Wine, --chiefly used in Pharmacy in the name of solutions of
some medicinal substance in wine; as: vina medicata, medicated wines;
vinum opii, wine of opium.
