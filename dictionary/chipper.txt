CHIPPER
Chip"per, v. i. Etym: [Cf. Cheep, Chirp.]

Defn: To chirp or chirrup. [ Prov. Eng.] Forby.
CHIPPER

=====
Chip"per, a.

Defn: Lively; cheerful; talkative. [U. S.]
