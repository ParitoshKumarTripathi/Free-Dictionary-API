FRAISE
Fraise, n. Etym: [See Froise.]

Defn: A large and thick pancake, with slices of bacon in it. [Obs.]
Johnson.
FRAISE

=====
Fraise, n. Etym: [F. fraise, orig., a ruff, cf. F. frise frieze, E.
frieze a coarse stuff.]

1. (Fort.)

Defn: A defense consisting of pointed stakes driven into the ramparts
in a horizontal or inclined position.

2. (Mech.)

Defn: A fluted reamer for enlarging holes in stone; a small milling
cutter.
FRAISE

=====
Fraise, v. t. (Mil.)

Defn: To protect, as a line of troops, against an onset of cavalry,
by opposing bayonets raised obliquely forward. Wilhelm.
