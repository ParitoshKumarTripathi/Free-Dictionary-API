THEOBROMIC
The`o*bro"mic, a. (Chem.)

Defn: Of, pertaining to, or designating, an acid extracted from cacao
butter (from the Theobroma Cacao), peanut oil (from Arachis hypogæa),
etc., as a white waxy crystalline substance.
