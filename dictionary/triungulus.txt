TRIUNGULUS
Tri*un"gu*lus, n.; pl. Triunguli. Etym: [NL. See Tri-, and Ungulate.]
(Zoöl.)

Defn: The active young larva of any oil beetle. It has feet armed
with three claws, and is parasitic on bees. See Illust. of Oil
beetle, under Oil.
