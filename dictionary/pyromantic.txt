PYROMANTIC
Pyr"o*man"tic, a.

Defn: Of or pertaining to pyromancy.
PYROMANTIC

=====
Pyr`o*man"tic, n. Etym: [Cf. Gr.

Defn: One who pretends to divine by fire. Sir T. Herbert.
