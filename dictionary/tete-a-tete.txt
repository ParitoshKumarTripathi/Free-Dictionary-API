TETE-A-TETE
Tête`-à-tête", n. Etym: [F., head to head. See Tester a covering,
Test a cupel.]

1. Private conversation; familiar interview or conference of two
persons.

2. A short sofa intended to accomodate two persons.
TETE-A-TETE

=====
Tête`-à-tête", a.

Defn: Private; confidential; familiar.
She avoided tête-à-tête walks with him. C. Kingsley.
TETE-A-TETE

=====
Tête`-à-tête", adv.

Defn: Face to face; privately or confidentially; familiarly. Prior.
