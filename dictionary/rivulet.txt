RIVULET
Riv"u*let, n. Etym: [Earlier rivolet, It. rivoletto, a dim. fr.
rivolo, L. rivulus, dim. of rivus a brook. CF. Rival, Rite.]

Defn: A small stream or brook; a streamlet.
By fountain or by shady rivulet He sought them. Milton.
