FRINGILLA
Frin*gil"la, a. Etym: [NL., fr. L. fringilla a chaffinch.] (Zoöl.)

Defn: A genus of birds, with a short, conical, pointed bill. It
formerly included all the sparrows and finches, but is now restricted
to certain European finches, like the chaffinch and brambling.
