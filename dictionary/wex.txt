WEX
Wex, v. t. & i.

Defn: To grow; to wax. [Obs.] Chaucer. "Each wexing moon." Dryden.
WEX

=====
Wex, obs. imp. of Wex.

Defn: Waxed. Chaucer.
WEX

=====
Wex, n.

Defn: Wax. [Obs.] "Yelwe as wex." Chaucer.
