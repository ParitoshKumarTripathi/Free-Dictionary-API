WEAKLY
Weak"ly, adv.

Defn: In a weak manner; with little strength or vigor; feebly.
WEAKLY

=====
Weak"ly, a. [Compar. Weaklier; superl. Weakliest.]

Defn: Not strong of constitution; infirm; feeble; as, a weakly woman;
a man of a weakly constitution.
