TRIQUADRANTAL
Tri`quad*ran"tal, a. Etym: [Pref. tri- + quadrantal.] (Spherical
Trig.)

Defn: Having three quadrants; thus, a triquadrantal triangle is one
whose three sides are quadrants, and whose three angles are
consequently right angles.
