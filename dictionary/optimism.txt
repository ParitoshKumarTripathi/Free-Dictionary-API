OPTIMISM
Op"ti*mism, n. Etym: [L. optimus the best; akin to optio choice: cf.
F. optimisme. See Option.]

1. (Metaph.)

Defn: The opinion or doctrine that everything in nature, being the
work of God, is ordered for the best, or that the ordering of things
in the universe is such as to produce the highest good.

2. A disposition to take the most hopeful view; -- opposed to
pessimism.
