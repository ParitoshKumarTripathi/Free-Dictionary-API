STAMINATE
Stam"i*nate, a. Etym: [L. staminatus consisting of threads, fr.
stamen thread: cf. F. staminé.] (Bot.)
(a) Furnished with stamens; producing stamens.
(b) Having stamens, but lacking pistils.
STAMINATE

=====
Stam"i*nate, v. t.

Defn: To indue with stamina. [R.]
