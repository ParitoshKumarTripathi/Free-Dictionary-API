VERBENACEOUS
Ver`be*na"ceous, a. (Bot.)

Defn: Of or pertaining to a natural order (Verbenaceæ) of
gamopetalous plants of which Verbena is the type. The order includes
also the black and white mangroves, and many plants noted for
medicinal use or for beauty of bloom.
