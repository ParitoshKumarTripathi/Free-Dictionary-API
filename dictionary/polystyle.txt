POLYSTYLE
Pol"y*style, a. Etym: [Gr. polystyle.] (Arch.)

Defn: Having many columns; -- said of a building, especially of an
interior part or court; as, a polystyle hall.
 -- n.

Defn: A polystyle hall or edifice.
