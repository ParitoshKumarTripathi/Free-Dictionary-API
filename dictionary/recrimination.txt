RECRIMINATION
Re*crim`i*na"tion (-n"shn), n. Etym: [F. récrimination, LL.
recriminatio.]

Defn: The act of recriminating; an accusation brought by the accused
against the accuser; a counter accusation.
Accusations and recriminations passed back ward and forward between
the contending parties. Macaulay.
