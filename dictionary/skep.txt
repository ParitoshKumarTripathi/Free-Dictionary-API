SKEP
Skep, n. Etym: [Icel. skeppa a measure, bushel; cf. Gael. sgeap a
basket, a beehive.]

1. A coarse round farm basket. [Obs. or Prov. Eng. & Scot.] Tusser.

2. A beehive. [Prov. Eng. & Scot.]
