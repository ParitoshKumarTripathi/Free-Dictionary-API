NARES
Na"res, n. pl. Etym: [L., pl. of naris nostril.] (Anat.)

Defn: The nostrils or nasal openings, -- the anterior nares being the
external or proper nostrils, and the posterior nares, the openings of
the nasal cavities into the mouth or pharynx.
