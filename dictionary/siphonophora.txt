SIPHONOPHORA
Si`pho*noph"o*ra, n. pl. Etym: [NL., fr. Gr. (Zoöl.)

Defn: An order of pelagic Hydrozoa including species which form
complex free-swimming communities composed of numerous zooids of
various kinds, some of which act as floats or as swimming organs,
others as feeding or nutritive zooids, and others as reproductive
zooids. See Illust. under Physallia, and Porpita.
