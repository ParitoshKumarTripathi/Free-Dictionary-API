QUOTHA
Quoth"a, interj. Etym: [For quoth'a, said he, 'a being corrupted from
he.]

Defn: Indeed; forsooth.
To affront the blessed hillside drabs and thieves With mended morals,
quotha, -- fine new lives ! Mrs. Browning.
