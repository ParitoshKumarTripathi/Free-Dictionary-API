NEPHRITIC; NEPHRITICAL
Ne*phrit"ic, Ne*phrit"ic*al, a. Etym: [L. nephriticus, gr.
néphrétique. See Nephritis.]

1. Of or pertaining to the kidneys or urinary organs; renal; as, a
nephritic disease.

2. (Med.)
(a) Affected with a disease of the kidneys; as, a nephritic patient.
(b) Relieving disorders of the kidneys; affecting the kidneys; as, a
nephritic medicine. Nephritic stone (Min.), nephrite; jade. See
Nephrite.
