TORET
Tor"et, n. Etym: [Probably dim. fr. tore, torus.]

Defn: A Turret. [Obs.]
TORET

=====
Tor"et, n.

Defn: A ring for fastening a hawk's leash to the jesses; also, a ring
affixed to the collar of a dog, etc. [Obs.] Chaucer.
