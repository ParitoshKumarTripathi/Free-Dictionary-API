RESPERSE
Re*sperse" (r*sprs"), v. t. Etym: [L. respersus, p. p. of respergere;
pref. re- re- + spargere to srew, sprinkle.]

Defn: To sprinkle; to scatter. [Obs.] Jer. Taylor.
