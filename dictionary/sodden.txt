SODDEN
Sod"den, a. [p. p. of Seethe.]

Defn: Boiled; seethed; also, soaked; heavy with moisture; saturated;
as, sodden beef; sodden bread; sodden fields.
SODDEN

=====
Sod"den, v. i.

Defn: To be seethed; to become sodden.
SODDEN

=====
Sod"den, v. t.

Defn: To soak; to make heavy with water.
