JUTTY
Jut"ty, n. Etym: [See Jetty, Jut, Jet.]

Defn: A projection in a building; also, a pier or mole; a jetty.
Shak.
JUTTY

=====
Jut"ty, v. t. & i.

Defn: To project beyond. [Obs.] Shak.
