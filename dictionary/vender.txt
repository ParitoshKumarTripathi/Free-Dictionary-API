VENDER
Vend"er, n. Etym: [From Vend: cf. F. vendeur, OF. vendeor. Cf.
Vendor.]

Defn: One who vends; one who transfers the exclusive right of
possessing a thing, either his own, or that of another as his agent,
for a price or pecuniary equivalent; a seller; a vendor.
