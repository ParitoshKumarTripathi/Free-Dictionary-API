KIKE
Kike, v. i. Etym: [Cf. D. kijken, Sw. kika.]

Defn: To gaze; to stare. [Obs.] Chaucer.
KIKE

=====
Kike, v. t. & i.

Defn: To kick [Obs.] Chaucer.
