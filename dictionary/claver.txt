CLAVER
Clav"er, n. [Obs.]

Defn: See Clover. Holland.
CLAVER

=====
Clav"er, n.

Defn: Frivolous or nonsensical talk; prattle; chattering. [Scot. &
North of Eng.]
Emmy found herself entirely at a loss in the midst of their clavers.
Thackeray.
