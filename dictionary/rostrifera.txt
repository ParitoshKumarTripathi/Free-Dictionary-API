ROSTRIFERA
Ros*trif"e*ra, n. pl. Etym: [NL., fr. L. rostrum beak + ferre to
bear.] (Zoöl.)

Defn: A division of pectinibranchiate gastropods, having the head
prolonged into a snout which is not retractile.
