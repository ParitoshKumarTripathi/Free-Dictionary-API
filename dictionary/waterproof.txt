WATERPROOF
Wa"ter*proof`, a.

Defn: Proof against penetration or permeation by water; impervious to
water; as, a waterproof garment; a waterproof roof.
WATERPROOF

=====
Wa"ter*proof`, n.

1. A substance or preparation for rendering cloth, leather, etc.,
impervious to water.

2. Cloth made waterproof, or any article made of such cloth, or of
other waterproof material, as rubber; esp., an outer garment made of
such material.
WATERPROOF

=====
Wa"ter*proof`, v. t.

Defn: To render impervious to water, as cloth, leather, etc.
