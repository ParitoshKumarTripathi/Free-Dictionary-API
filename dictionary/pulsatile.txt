PULSATILE
Pul"sa*tile, a. Etym: [Cf. It. pulsatile, Sp. pulsatil.]

1. Capable of being struck or beaten; played by beating or by
percussion; as, a tambourine is a pulsatile musical instrument.

2. Pulsating; throbbing, as a tumor.
