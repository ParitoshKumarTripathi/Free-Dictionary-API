TORREFACTION
Tor`re*fac"tion, n. Etym: [L.torrefacere,torrefactum, to torrefy: cf.
F. torréfaction. See Torrefy.]

Defn: The act or process of torrefying, or the state of being
torrefied. Bp. Hall.
