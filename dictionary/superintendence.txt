SUPERINTENDENCE
Su`per*in*tend"ence, n. Etym: [Cf. F. superintendance.]

Defn: The act of superintending; care and oversight for the purpose
of direction; supervision. Barrow.

Syn.
 -- Inspection; oversight; care; direction; control; guidance.
