TORULA
Tor"u*la, n.; pl. Torulæ ( Etym: [NL., dim. of L. torus a
semicircular molding.] (Biol.)
(a) A chain of special bacteria. (b) A genus of budding fungi. Same
as Saccharomyces. Also used adjectively.
