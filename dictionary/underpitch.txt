UNDERPITCH
Un`der*pitch", v. t. [imp. Underpight.] Etym: [OE. underpicchen. See
Under, and Pitch to throw, fix.]

Defn: To fill underneath; to stuff. [Obs.]
He drank and well his girdle underpight. Chaucer.
