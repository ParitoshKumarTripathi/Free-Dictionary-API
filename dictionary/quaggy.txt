QUAGGY
Quag"gy, a.Etym: [See Quag, Quagmire.]

Defn: Of the nature of a quagmire; yielding or trembling under the
foot, as soft, wet earth; spongy; boggy. "O'er the watery strath, or
quaggy moss." Collins.
