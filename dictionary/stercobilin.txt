STERCOBILIN
Ster`co*bi"lin, n. Etym: [L. stercus dung + E. bilin.] (Physiol.
Chem.)

Defn: A coloring matter found in the fæces, a product of the
alteration of the bile pigments in the intestinal canal, -- identical
with hydrobilirubin.
