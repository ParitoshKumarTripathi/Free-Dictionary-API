UPPERMOST
Up"per*most`, a. Etym: [From Up, Upper; formed like aftermost. Cf.
Upmost.]

Defn: Highest in place, position, rank, power, or the like; upmost;
supreme.
Whatever faction happens to be uppermost. Swift.
