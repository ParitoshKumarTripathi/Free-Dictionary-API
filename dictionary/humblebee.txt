HUMBLEBEE
Hum"ble*bee`, n. Etym: [OE. humbilbee, hombulbe; cf. D. hommel, G.
hummel, OHG. humbal, Dan. humle, Sw. humla; perh. akin to hum. sq.
root15. Cf. Bumblebee.] (Zoöl.)

Defn: The bumblebee. Shak.
