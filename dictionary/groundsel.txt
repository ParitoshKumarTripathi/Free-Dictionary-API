GROUNDSEL
Ground"sel, n. Etym: [OE. grundswilie, AS. gpundeswylige,
grundeswelge, earlier gundiswilge; gund matter, pus + swelgan to
swallow. So named as being good for a running from the eye. See
Swallow, v.] (Bot.)

Defn: An annual composite plant (Senecio vulgaris) one of the most
common, and widely distributed weeds on the globe.
