TERMES
Ter"mes, n.; pl. Termities. Etym: [L. termes, tarmes, -itis, a
woodworm. Cf. Termite.] (Zoöl.)

Defn: A genus of Pseudoneuroptera including the white ants, or
termites. See Termite.
