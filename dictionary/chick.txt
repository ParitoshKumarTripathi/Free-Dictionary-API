CHICK
Chick, v. i. Etym: [OE. chykkyn, chyke, chicken.]

Defn: To sprout, as seed in the ground; to vegetate. Chalmers.
CHICK

=====
Chick, n.

1. A chicken.

2. A child or young person; -- a term of endearment. Shak.
