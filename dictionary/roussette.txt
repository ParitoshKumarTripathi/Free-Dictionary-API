ROUSSETTE
Rous*sette", n. Etym: [F.; -- so called in allusion to the color. See
Russet.]

1. (Zoöl.)

Defn: A fruit bat, especially the large species (Pieropus vulgaris)
inhabiting the islands of the Indian ocean. It measures about a yard
across the expanded wings.

2. (Zoöl.)

Defn: Any small shark of the genus Scyllium; -- called also dogfish.
See Dogfish.
