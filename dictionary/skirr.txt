SKIRR
Skirr, v. t. Etym: [Cf. Scur, Scurry.]

Defn: To ramble over in order to clear; to scour. [Archaic] Shak.
SKIRR

=====
Skirr, v. i.

Defn: To scour; to scud; to run. [Archaic]
SKIRR

=====
Skirr, n. (Zoöl.)

Defn: A tern. [Prov. Eng.]
