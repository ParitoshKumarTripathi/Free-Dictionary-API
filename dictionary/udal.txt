UDAL
U"dal (u"dal), n. Etym: [Icel. oedhal allodium, an hereditary estate;
akin to Sw. odal allodial, Dan. odel.]

Defn: In Shetland and Orkney, a freehold; property held by udal, or
allodial, right.
UDAL

=====
U"dal, a.

Defn: Allodial; -- a term used in Finland, Shetland, and Orkney. See
Allodial. Burrill.
