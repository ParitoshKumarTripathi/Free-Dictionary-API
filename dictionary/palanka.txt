PALANKA
Pa*lan"ka, n. Etym: [Cf. It., Pg., & Sp. palanca, fr.L. palanga,
phalanga a pole, Gr. (Mil.)

Defn: A camp permanently intrenched, attached to Turkish frontier
fortresses.
