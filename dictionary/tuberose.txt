TUBEROSE
Tube"rose`, n. Etym: [Cf. G. tuberose, F. tubéreuse, NL. Polianthes
tuberosa. See Tuberous.] (Bot.)

Defn: A plant (Polianthes tuberosa) with a tuberous root and a
liliaceous flower. It is much cultivated for its beautiful and
fragrant white blossoms.
