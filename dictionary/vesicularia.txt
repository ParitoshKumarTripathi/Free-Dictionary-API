VESICULARIA
Ve*sic`u*la"ri*a, n. Etym: [NL. See Vesicle.] (Zoöl.)

Defn: Any one of numerous species of marine Bryozoa belonging to
Vesicularia and allied genera. They have delicate tubular cells
attached in clusters to slender flexible stems.
