TRADUCT
Tra*duct", v. t. Etym: [L. traducere, traductum. See Traduce.]

Defn: To derive or deduce; also, to transmit; to transfer. [Obs.]
Fotherby.
TRADUCT

=====
Tra*duct", n.

Defn: That which is traducted; that which is transferred; a
translation. [Obs.] Howell.
