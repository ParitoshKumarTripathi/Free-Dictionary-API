UNBALLASTED
Un*bal"last*ed, a.

1. Etym: [Properly p. p. unballast.]

Defn: Freed from ballast; having discharged ballast.

2. Etym: [Pref. un- not + ballasted.]

Defn: Not furnished with ballast; not kept steady by ballast;
unsteady; as, unballasted vessels; unballasted wits.
Unballasted by any sufficient weight of plan. De Quincey.
