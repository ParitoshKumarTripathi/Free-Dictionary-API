ROUNCEVAL
Roun"ce*val, a. Etym: [F. Ronceval, Roncevaux, a town at the foot of
the foot of the Pyrenees, Sp. Roncesvalles.]

Defn: Large; strong; -- from the gigantic bones shown at
Roncesvalles, and alleged to be those of old heroes. [Obs.]
ROUNCEVAL

=====
Roun"ce*val, n.

Defn: A giant; anything large; a kind of pea called also marrowfat.
[Obs.]
