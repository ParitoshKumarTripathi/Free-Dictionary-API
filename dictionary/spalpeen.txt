SPALPEEN
Spal"peen, n. Etym: [Ir. spailpin, fr. spailp a beau, pride, self-
conceit.]

Defn: A scamp; an Irish term for a good-for-nothing fellow; -- often
used in good-humored contempt or ridicule. [Colloq.]
