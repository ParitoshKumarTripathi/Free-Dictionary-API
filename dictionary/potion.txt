POTION
Po"tion, n. Etym: [L. potio, from potare to drink: cf. F. potion. See
Poison.]

Defn: A draught; a dose; usually, a draught or dose of a liquid
medicine. Shak.
POTION

=====
Po"tion, v. t.

Defn: To drug. [Obs.] Speed.
