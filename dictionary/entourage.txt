ENTOURAGE
En`tou`rage" (äN`too`razh"), n. [F.]

Defn: Surroundings; specif., collectively, one's attendants or
associates.

The entourage and mode of life of the mikados were not such as to
make of them able rulers.
B. H. Chamberlain.
