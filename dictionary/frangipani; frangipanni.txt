FRANGIPANI; FRANGIPANNI
Fran`gi*pan"i, Fran`gi*pan"ni, n. Etym: [Another spelling of
frangipane.]

Defn: A perfume derived from, or imitating the odor of, the flower of
the red jasmine, a West Indian tree of the genus Plumeria.
