ISOHYETOSE
I`so*hy"e*tose`, a. Etym: [Iso- + Gr. "yeto`s rain.] (Phys. Geog.)

Defn: Of or pertaining to lines connecting places on the earth's
surface which have a mean annual rainfall.
 -- n.

Defn: An isohyetose line.
