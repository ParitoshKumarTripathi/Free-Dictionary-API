NEPHRALGIA; NEPHRALGY
Ne*phral"gi*a, Ne*phral"gy, n. Etym: [NL. nephralgia, fr. Gr.
néphralgie.] (Med.)

Defn: Neuralgia of the kidneys; a disease characterized by pain in
the region of the kidneys without any structural lesion of the
latter. Quain.
