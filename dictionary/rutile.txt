RUTILE
Ru"tile, n. Etym: [L. rutilus red, inclining to golden yellow.]
(Min.)

Defn: A mineral usually of a reddish brown color, and brilliant
metallic adamantine luster, occurring in tetragonal crystals. In
composition it is titanium dioxide, like octahedrite and brooklite.
