WATER PORE
Wa"ter pore`

1. (Zoöl.)

Defn: A pore by which the water tubes of various invertebrates open
externally.

2. (Bot.)

Defn: One of certain minute pores in the leaves of some plants. They
are without true guardian cells, but in other respects closely
resemble ordinary stomata. Goodale.
