RICINUS
Ric"i*nus (, n. Etym: [L., the castor-oil plant.] (Bot.)

Defn: A genus of plants of the Spurge family, containing but one
species (R. communis), the castor-oil plant. The fruit is three-
celled, and contains three large seeds from which castor oil iss
expressed. See Palma Christi.
