ULIGINOSE; ULIGINOUS
U*lig"i*nose`, U*lig"i*nous, a. Etym: [L. uliginosus, fr. uligo, -
inis, moisture, fr. uvere to be moist.]

Defn: Muddy; oozy; slimy; also, growing in muddy places. [R.]
Woodward.
