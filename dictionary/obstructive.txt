OBSTRUCTIVE
Ob*struct"ive, a. Etym: [Cf.F. obstrictif.]

Defn: Tending to obstruct; presenting obstacles; hindering; causing
impediment.
 -- Ob*struct"ive*ly, adv.
OBSTRUCTIVE

=====
Ob*struct"ive, n.

Defn: An obstructive person or thing.
