QUEACH
Queach, n. Etym: [Cf. Quick.]

Defn: A thick, bushy plot; a thicket. [Obs.] Chapman.
QUEACH

=====
Queach, v. i. Etym: [Cf. E. quich, v. i., quick, v. i.; or AS.
cweccan to shake.]

Defn: To stir; to move. See Quick, v. i. [Obs.]
