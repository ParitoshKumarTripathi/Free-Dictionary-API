MATURATIVE
Mat"u*ra*tive, a. Etym: [Cf. F. maturatif.]

Defn: Conducing to ripeness or maturity; hence, conducing to
suppuration.
MATURATIVE

=====
Mat"u*ra*tive, n. (Med.)

Defn: A remedy promoting maturation; a maturant.
