PRIMAL
Pri"mal, a. Etym: [LL. primalis, fr. L. primus the first. See Prime,
a.]

Defn: First; primary; original; chief.
It hath the primal eldest curse upon it. Shak.
The primal duties shine aloft like stars. Wordsworth.
