TEREK
Ter"ek, n. [Because found on the Terek River in the Caucasus.]

Defn: A sandpiper (Terekia cinerea) of the Old World, breeding in the
far north of eastern Europe and Asia and migrating to South Africa
and Australia. It frequents rivers.
