VOCATIVE
Voc"a*tive, a. Etym: [L. vocativus, fr. vocare to call.]

Defn: Of or pertaining to calling; used in calling; specifically
(Gram.), used in address; appellative; -- said of that case or form
of the noun, pronoun, or adjective, in which a person or thing is
addressed; as, Domine, O Lord.
