UNCARNATE
Un*car"nate, a.

Defn: Not fleshy; specifically, not made flesh; not incarnate. [R.]
Sir T. Browne.
UNCARNATE

=====
Un*car"nate, v. t. Etym: [1st pref. un- + carnate.]

Defn: To divest of flesh.
