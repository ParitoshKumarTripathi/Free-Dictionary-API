TIRL
Tirl, v. i. [Cf. Twirl, Thirl.] [Scot. & Prov. Eng.]

1. To quiver; to vibrate; to veer about.

2.  To make a ratting or clattering sound by twirling or shaking; as,
to tirl at the pin, or latch, of a door.
