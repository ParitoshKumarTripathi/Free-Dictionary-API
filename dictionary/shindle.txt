SHINDLE
Shin"dle, n. Etym: [See 2d Shingle.]

Defn: A shingle; also, a slate for roofing. [Obs.] Holland.
SHINDLE

=====
Shin"dle, v. t.

Defn: To cover or roof with shindles. [Obs.]
