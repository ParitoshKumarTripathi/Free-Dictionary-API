POSTHASTE
Post`haste", n.

Defn: Haste or speed in traveling, like that of a post or courier.
Shak.
POSTHASTE

=====
Post`haste, adv.

Defn: With speed or expedition; as, he traveled posthaste; to send
posthaste. Shak.
