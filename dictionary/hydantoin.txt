HYDANTOIN
Hy*dan"to*in, n. Etym: [Hydrogen + allantion.] (Chem.)

Defn: A derivative of urea, C3H4N2O2, obtained from allantion, as a
white, crystalline substance, with a sweetish taste; -- called also
glycolyl urea.
