YERN
Yern, v. i.

Defn: See 3d Yearn. [Obs.]
YERN

=====
Yern, a. Etym: [OE. ýern, ýeorne, AS. georn desirous, eager. See
Yearn to long.]

Defn: Eager; brisk; quick; active. [Obs.] "Her song . . . loud and
yern." Chaucer.
