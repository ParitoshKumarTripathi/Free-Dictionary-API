SYMPOSIAC
Sym*po"si*ac, a. Etym: [L. symposiacus, Gr.

Defn: Of or pertaining to compotations and merrymaking; happening
where company is drinking together; as, symposiac meetings.
Symposiac disputations amongst my acquaintance. Arbuthnot.
SYMPOSIAC

=====
Sym*po"si*ac, n.

Defn: A conference or conversation of philosophers at a banquet;
hence, any similar gathering.
