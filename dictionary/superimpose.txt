SUPERIMPOSE
Su`per*im*pose", v. t. [imp. & p. p. Superimposed; p. pr. & vb. n.
Superimposing.]

Defn: To lay or impose on something else; as, a stratum of earth
superimposed on another stratum.
 -- Su`per*im`po*si"tion, n.
