SEMICUBIUM; SEMICUPIUM
Sem`i*cu"bi*um, Sem`i*cu"pi*um, n. Etym: [LL., fr. L. semi half +
cupa tub, cask.]

Defn: A half bath, or one that covers only the lewer extremities and
the hips; a sitz-bath; a half bath, or hip bath.
