PERCHROMIC
Per*chro"mic, a. Etym: [Pref. per- + chromic.] (Chem.)

Defn: Pertaining to, or designating, a certain one of the highly
oxidized compounds of chromium, which has a deep blue color, and is
produced by the action of hydrogen peroxide.
