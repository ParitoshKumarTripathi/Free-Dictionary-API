PHOTOLITHOGRAPH
Pho`to*lith"o*graph, n. Etym: [Photo- + lithograph.]

Defn: A lithographic picture or copy from a stone prepared by the aid
of photography.
PHOTOLITHOGRAPH

=====
Pho`to*lith"o*graph, v. t.

Defn: To produce (a picture, a copy) by the process of
photolithography.
