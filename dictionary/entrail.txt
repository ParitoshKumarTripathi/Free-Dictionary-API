ENTRAIL
En*trail", v. t. Etym: [Pref. en- + OF. treiller to grate, lattice,
F. treille vine, arbor. See Trellis.]

Defn: To interweave; to intertwine. [Obs.] Spenser.
ENTRAIL

=====
En*trail", n.

Defn: Entanglement; fold. [Obs.] Spenser.
