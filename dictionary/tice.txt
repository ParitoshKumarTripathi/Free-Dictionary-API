TICE
Tice, v. t. Etym: [Aphetic form of entice.]

Defn: To entice. [Obs.] The Coronation.
TICE

=====
Tice, n. (Cricket)

Defn: A ball bowled to strike the ground about a bat's length in
front of the wicket.
