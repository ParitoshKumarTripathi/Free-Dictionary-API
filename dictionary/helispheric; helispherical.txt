HELISPHERIC; HELISPHERICAL
Hel`i*spher"ic, Hel`i*spher"ic*al (, a. Etym: [Helix + spheric,
spherical.]

Defn: Spiral. Helispherical line (Math.). the rhomb line in
navigation. [R.]
