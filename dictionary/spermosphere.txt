SPERMOSPHERE
Sper"mo*sphere, n. Etym: [Spermo- + sphere.] (Physiol.)

Defn: A mass or ball of cells formed by the repeated division of a
male germinal cell (spermospore), each constituent cell (spermoblast)
of which is converted into a spermatozoid; a spermatogemma.
