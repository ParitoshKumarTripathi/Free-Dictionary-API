FRIST
Frist, v. t. Etym: [OE. fristen, firsten, to lend, give respite,
postpone, AS. firstan to give respite to; akin to first time, G.
frist, Icel. frest delay.]

Defn: To sell upon credit, as goods. [R.] Crabb.
