WAWE
Wawe, n. Etym: [See Woe.]

Defn: Woe. [Obs.]
WAWE

=====
Wawe, n. Etym: [OE. wawe, waghe; cf. Icel. vagr; akin to E. wag; not
the same word as wave.]

Defn: A wave. [Obs.] Chaucer. Spenser.
