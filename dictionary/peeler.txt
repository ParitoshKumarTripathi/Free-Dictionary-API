PEELER
Peel"er, n.

Defn: One who peels or strips.
PEELER

=====
Peel"er, n. Etym: [See Peel to plunder.]

Defn: A pillager.
PEELER

=====
Peel"er, n.

Defn: A nickname for a policeman; -- so called from Sir Robert Peel.
