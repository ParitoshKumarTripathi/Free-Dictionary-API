ROCOCO
Ro*co"co, n. Etym: [F.; of uncertain etymology.]

Defn: A florid style of ornamentation which prevailed in Europe in
the latter part of the eighteenth century.
ROCOCO

=====
Ro*co"co, a.

Defn: Of or pertaining to the style called rococo; like rococo;
florid; fantastic.
