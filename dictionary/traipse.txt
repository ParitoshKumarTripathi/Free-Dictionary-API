TRAIPSE
Traipse, v. i. Etym: [Cf. G. trapsen, trappsen, trappen, to tread
noisily, to walk stamping. See Trample, Trape.]

Defn: To walk or run about in a slatternly, careless, or thoughtless
manner. [Colloq.] Pope.
