NAVEW
Na"vew, n. Etym: [OE. navel, naveau, a dim. fr. L. napus navew. Cf.
Napiform.] (Bot.)

Defn: A kind of small turnip, a variety of Brassica campestris. See
Brassica. [Writen also naphew.]
