REISSNER'S MEMBRANE
Reiss"ner's mem"brane (rs"nrz mm"brn). Etym: [Named from E. Reissner,
A German anatomist.] (Anat.)

Defn: The thin membrane which separates the canal of the cochlea from
the vestibular scala in the internal ear.
