HYP
Hyp, n.

Defn: An abbreviation of hypochonaria; -- usually in plural.
[Colloq.]
Heaven send thou hast not got the hyps. Swift.
HYP

=====
Hyp, v. t.

Defn: To make melancholy. [Colloq.] W. Irving.
