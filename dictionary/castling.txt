CASTLING
Cast"ling, n.

Defn: That which is cast or brought forth prematurely; an abortion.
Sir T. Browne.
CASTLING

=====
Cas"tling, n. (Chess)

Defn: A compound move of the king and castle. See Castle, v. i.
