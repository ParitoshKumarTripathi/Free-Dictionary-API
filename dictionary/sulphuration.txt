SULPHURATION
Sul`phu*ra"tion, n. Etym: [Cf. F. sulfuration, L. sulphuratio,
sulfuratio, a vein of sulphur.]

Defn: The act or process of combining or impregnating with sulphur or
its compounds; also, the state of being so combined or impregnated.
