PLATYPTERA
Pla*typ"te*ra, n. pl. Etym: [NL., fr. Gr. (Zoöl.)

Defn: A division of Pseudoneuroptera including the species which have
four broad, flat wings, as the termites, or white-ants, and the stone
flies (Perla).
