HUZZA
Huz*za", interj. Etym: [Cf. G. hussa, husa, interj., hurrah, huzza.
sq. root43. Cf. Hurrah.]

Defn: A word used as a shout of joy, exultation, approbation, or
encouragement.
HUZZA

=====
Huz"za, n.

Defn: A shout of huzza; a cheer; a hurrah.
They made a great huzza or shout. Evelyn.
HUZZA

=====
Huz*za", v. i. [imp. & p. p. Huzzaed; p. pr. & vb. n. Huzzaing.]

Defn: To shout huzza; to cheer.
HUZZA

=====
Huz*za", v. t.

Defn: To receive or attend with huzzas.
He was huzzaed into the court. Addison.
