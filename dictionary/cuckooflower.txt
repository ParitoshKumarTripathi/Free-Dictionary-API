CUCKOOFLOWER
Cuck"oo*flow`er (-flou`r), n. (Bot.)

Defn: A species of Cardamine (C. pratensis), or lady's smock. Its
leaves are used in salads. Also, the ragged robin (Lychnis Flos-
cuculi).
