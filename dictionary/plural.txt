PLURAL
Plu"ral, a Etym: [L. pluralis, from plus, pluris, more; cf. F.
pluriel, OF. plurel. See Plus.]

Defn: Relating to, or containing, more than one; designating two or
more; as, a plural word.
Plural faith, which is too much by one. Shak.
Plural number (Gram.), the number which designates more than one. See
Number, n., 8.
PLURAL

=====
Plu"ral, n. (Gram.)

Defn: The plural number; that form of a word which expresses or
denotes more than one; a word in the plural form.
