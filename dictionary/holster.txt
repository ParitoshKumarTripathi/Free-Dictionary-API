HOLSTER
Hol"ster, n. Etym: [D. holster; skin to AS. heolstor den, cave,
fr.helan to conceal, and to Icel. hulstr case, Goth.hulistr covering,
veil, huljan to cover. sq. root17. See Hele to cover, Hell, and cf.
Housing, Houss.]

Defn: A leather case for a pistol, carried by a horseman at the bow
of his saddle.
