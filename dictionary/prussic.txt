PRUSSIC
Prus"sic, a. Etym: [Cf. F. prussique.] (Old Chem.)

Defn: designating the acid now called hydrocyanic acid, but formerly
called prussic acid, because Prussian blue is derived from it or its
compounds. See Hydrocyanic.
