PHALANGER
Pha*lan"ger, n. Etym: [Cf. F. phalanger. See Phalanx.] (Zoöl.)

Defn: Any marsupial belonging to Phalangista, Cuscus, Petaurus, and
other genera of the family Phalangistidæ. They are arboreal, and the
species of Petaurus are furnished with lateral parachutes. See Flying
phalanger, under Flying.
