SCALA
Sca"la, n.; pl. Scalæ. Etym: [L., a ladder.]

1. (Surg.)

Defn: A machine formerly employed for reducing dislocations of the
humerus.

2. (Anat.)

Defn: A term applied to any one of the three canals of the cochlea.
