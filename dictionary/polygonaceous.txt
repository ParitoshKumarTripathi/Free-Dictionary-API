POLYGONACEOUS
Pol`y*go*na"ceous, a. Etym: [See Polygonum.] (Bot.)

Defn: Of or pertaining to a natural order of apetalous plants
(Polygonaceæ), of which the knotweeds (species of Polygonum) are the
type, and which includes also the docks (Rumex), the buckwheat,
rhubarb, sea grape (Coccoloba), and several other genera.
