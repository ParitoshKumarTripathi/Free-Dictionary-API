SCHILLER
Schil"ler, n. Etym: [G., play of colors.] (Min.)

Defn: The peculiar bronzelike luster observed in certain minerals, as
hypersthene, schiller spar, etc. It is due to the presence of minute
inclusions in parallel position, and in sometimes of secondary
origin. Schiller spar (Min.), an altered variety of enstatite,
exhibiting, in certain positions, a bronzelike luster.
