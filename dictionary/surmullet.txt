SURMULLET
Sur*mul"let, n. Etym: [F. surmulet; saur, saure, brownish yellow, red
+ mulet a mullet. See Sorrel, a., and Mullet.] (Zoöl.)

Defn: Any one of various species of mullets of the family Millidæ,
esp. the European species (Millus surmulletus), which is highly
prized as a food fish. See Mullet.
