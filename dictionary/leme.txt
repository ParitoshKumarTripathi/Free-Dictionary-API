LEME
Leme, n. Etym: [OE. leem, leme, leam, AS. leóma light, brightness;
akin to E. light, n. sq. root122.]

Defn: A ray or glimmer of light; a gleam. [Obs.] Chaucer.
LEME

=====
Leme, v. i.

Defn: To shine. [Obs.] Piers Plowman.
