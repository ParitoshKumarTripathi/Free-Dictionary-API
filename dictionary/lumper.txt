LUMPER
Lump"er, n. Etym: [Cf. Lamper eel.] (Zoöl.)

Defn: The European eelpout; -- called also lumpen.
LUMPER

=====
Lump"er, n.

1. One who lumps.

2. A laborer who is employed to load or unload vessels when in
harbor.
