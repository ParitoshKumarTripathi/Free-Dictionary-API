TRIONYCHOIDEA
Tri*on`y*choi"de*a, n. pl. Etym: [NL. See Trionyx, and -old.] (Zoöl.)

Defn: A division of chelonians which comprises Trionyx and allied
genera; -- called also Trionychoides, and Trionychina.
