WOLLASTON'S DOUBLET
Wol"las*ton's dou"blet. [After W. H. Wollaston, English physicist.]
(Optics)

Defn: A magnifying glass consisting of two plano-convex lenses. It is
designed to correct spherical aberration and chromatic dispersion.
