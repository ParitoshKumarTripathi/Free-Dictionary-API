STUPENDOUS
Stu*pen"dous, a. Etym: [L. stupendus astonishing, p. future pass. of
stupere to be astonished at. Cf. Stupid.]

Defn: Astonishing; wonderful; amazing; especially, astonishing in
magnitude or elevation; as, a stupendous pile. "A stupendous sum."
Macaulay.
All are but parts of one stupendous whole. Pope.
-- Stu*pen"dous*ly, adv.
 -- Stu*pen"dous*ness, n.
