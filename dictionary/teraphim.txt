TERAPHIM
Ter"a*phim, n. pl. Etym: [Heb. teraphim.]

Defn: Images connected with the magical rites used by those
Israelites who added corrupt practices to the patriarchal religion.
Teraphim were consulted by the Israelites for oracular answers. Dr.
W. Smith (Bib. Dict.).
