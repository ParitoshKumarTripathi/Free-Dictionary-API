ALTERANT
Al"ter*ant, a. Etym: [LL. alterans, p. pr.: cf. F. altérant.]

Defn: Altering; gradually changing. Bacon.
ALTERANT

=====
Al"ter*ant, n.

Defn: An alterative. [R.] Chambers.
