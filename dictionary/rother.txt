ROTHER
Roth"er, a. Etym: [AS. hryedher; cf. D. rund.] (Zoöl.)

Defn: Bovine.
 -- n.

Defn: A bovine beast. [Obs.] Shak. Rother beasts, cattle of the
bovine genus; black cattle. [Obs.] Golding.
 -- Rother soil, the dung of rother beasts.
ROTHER

=====
Roth"er, n. Etym: [OE. See Rudder.]

Defn: A rudder. Rother nail, a nail with a very full head, used for
fastening the rudder irons of ships; -- so called by shipwrights.
