HINDOOSTANEE; HINDUSTANI
Hin"doo*sta"nee, Hin"du*sta"ni, a. Etym: [Hind. Hindustani an Indian,
fr. Hind. and Per. Hindustan India.]

Defn: Of or pertaining to the Hindoos or their language.
 -- n.

Defn: The language of Hindostan; the name given by Europeans to the
most generally spoken of the modern Aryan languages of India. It is
Hindi with the addition of Persian and Arabic words.
