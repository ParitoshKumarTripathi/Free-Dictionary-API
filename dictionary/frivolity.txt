FRIVOLITY
Fri*vol"i*ty, n.; pl. Frivolities. Etym: [Cg. F. frivolité. See
Frivolous.]

Defn: The condition or quality of being frivolous; also, acts or
habits of trifling; unbecoming levity of disposition.
