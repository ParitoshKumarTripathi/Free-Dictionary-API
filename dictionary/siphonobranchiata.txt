SIPHONOBRANCHIATA
Si`pho*no*bran`chi*a"ta, n. pl. Etym: [NL. See Siphon, and Branchia.]
(Zoöl.)

Defn: A tribe of gastropods having the mantle border, on one or both
sides, prolonged in the form of a spout through which water enters
the gill cavity. The shell itself is not always siphonostomatous in
this group.
