HAVIOR
Hav"ior, n. Etym: [OE. havour, a corruption of OF. aveir, avoir, a
having, of same origin as E. aver a work horse. The h is due to
confusion with E. have.]

Defn: Behavior; demeanor. [Obs.] Shak.
