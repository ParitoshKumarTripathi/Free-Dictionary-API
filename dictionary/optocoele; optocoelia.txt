OPTOCOELE; OPTOCOELIA
Op"to*coele, Op`to*coe"li*a, n. Etym: [NL. optocoelia, fr. Gr.
(Anat.)

Defn: The cavity of one of the optic lobes of the brain in many
animals. B. G. Wilder.
