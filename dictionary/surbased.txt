SURBASED
Sur"based`, a. (Arch.)
(a) Having a surbase, or molding above the base. (b) Etym: [F.
surbaissé.]

Defn: Having the vertical height from springing line to crown less
than the half span; -- said of an arch; as, a segmental arch is
surbased.
