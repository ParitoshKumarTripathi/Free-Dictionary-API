PLYER
Ply"er, n.

Defn: One who, or that which, plies; specifically: (a) pl.

Defn: A kind of balance used in raising and letting down a
drawbridge. It consists of timbers joined in the form of a St.
Andrew's cross. (b) pl.

Defn: See Pliers.
