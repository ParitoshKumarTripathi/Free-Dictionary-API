BLEATING
Bleat"ing, a.

Defn: Crying as a sheep does.
Then came the shepherd back with his bleating flocks from the
seaside. Longfellow.
BLEATING

=====
Bleat"ing, n.

Defn: The cry of, or as of, a sheep. Chapman.
