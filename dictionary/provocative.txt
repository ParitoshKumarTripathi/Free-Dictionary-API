PROVOCATIVE
Pro*vo"ca*tive, a. Etym: [L. provocativus: cf. OF. provocatif.]

Defn: Serving or tending to provoke, excite, or stimulate; exciting.
PROVOCATIVE

=====
Pro*vo"ca*tive, n.

Defn: Anything that is provocative; a stimulant; as, a provocative of
appetite.
