UNDIRECT
Un`di*rect", v. t. Etym: [1st pref. un- + direct, v. t.]

Defn: To misdirect; to mislead. [Obs.]
who make false fires to undirect seamen in a tempest. Fuller.
UNDIRECT

=====
Un`di*rect", a. Etym: [Pref. un- not + direct.]

Defn: Indirect.
