SHILL
Shill, v. t.

Defn: To shell. [Obs. or Prov. Eng.]
SHILL

=====
Shill, v. t. Etym: [Cf. Sheal.]

Defn: To put under cover; to sheal. [Prov.ng.] Brockett.
