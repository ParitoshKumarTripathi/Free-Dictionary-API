TERCENTENARY
Ter*cen"te*na*ry, a. Etym: [L. ter thirce + E. centenary.]

Defn: Including, or relating to, an interval of three hundred years.
 -- n.

Defn: The three hundredth anniversary of any event; also, a
celebration of such an anniversary.
