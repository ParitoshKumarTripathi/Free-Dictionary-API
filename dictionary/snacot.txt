SNACOT
Snac"ot, n. Etym: [Said to be corrupted fr. NL. syngnathus, fr. Gr.
sy`n together + gna`qos jaw, because the jaws can be only slightly
separated.] (Zoöl.)

Defn: A pipefish of the genus Syngnathus. See Pipefish.
