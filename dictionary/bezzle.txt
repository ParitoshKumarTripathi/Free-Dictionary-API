BEZZLE
Bez"zle, v. t. [imp. & p. p. Bezzled; p. pr. & vb. n. Bezzling.]
Etym: [OF. besillier, besiler, to maltreat, pillage; or shortened fr.
embezzle. Cf. Embezzle.]

Defn: To plunder; to waste in riot. [Obs.]
BEZZLE

=====
Bez"zle, v. i.

Defn: To drink to excess; to revel. [Obs.]
