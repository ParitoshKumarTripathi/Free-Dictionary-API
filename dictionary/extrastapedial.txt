EXTRASTAPEDIAL
Ex`tra*sta*pe"di*al, a. (Anat.)

Defn: Pertaining to a part of the columella of the ear, which, in
many animals, projects beyond the connection with the stapes.
 -- n.

Defn: The extrastapedial part of columella.
