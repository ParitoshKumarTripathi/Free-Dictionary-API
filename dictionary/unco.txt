UNCO
Un"co, a. Etym: [Scot. The same word as E. uncouth.]

Defn: Unknown; strange, or foreign; unusual, or surprising; distant
in manner; reserved. [Scot.]
UNCO

=====
Un"co, adv.

Defn: In a high degree; to a great extent; greatly; very. [Prov. Eng.
& Scot.]
UNCO

=====
Un"co, n.

Defn: A strange thing or person. [Scot.]
