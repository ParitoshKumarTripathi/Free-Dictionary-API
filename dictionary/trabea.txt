TRABEA
Tra"be*a, n.; pl. Trabeæ. Etym: [L.] (Rom. Antiq.)

Defn: A toga of purple, or ornamented with purple horizontal stripes.
 -- worn by kings, consuls, and augurs. Dr. W. Smith.
