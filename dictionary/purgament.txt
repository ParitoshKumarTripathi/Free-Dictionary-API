PURGAMENT
Pur"ga*ment, n. Etym: [L. purgamentum offscourings, washings,
expiatory sacrifice. See Purge.]

1. That which is excreted; excretion. [Obs.]

2. (Med.)

Defn: A cathartic; a purgative. [Obs.] Bacon.
