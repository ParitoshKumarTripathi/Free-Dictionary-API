SECRETORY
Se*cre"to*ry, a. Etym: [Cf. F. sécrétoire. See Secrete.] (Physiol.)

Defn: Secreting; performing, or connected with, the office secretion;
secernent; as, secretory vessels, nerves.
 -- n.

Defn: A secretory vessel; a secernent.
