REVOLUTIONARY
Rev`o*lu"tion*a*ry, a. Etym: [Cf. F. révolutionnaire.]

Defn: Of or pertaining to a revolution in government; tending to, or
promoting, revolution; as, revolutionary war; revolutionary measures;
revolutionary agitators.
REVOLUTIONARY

=====
Rev`o*lu"tion*a*ry, n.

Defn: A revolutionist. [R.]
Dumfries was a Tory town, and could not tolerate a revolutionary.
Prof. Wilson.
