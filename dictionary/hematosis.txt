HEMATOSIS
Hem`a*to"sis, n. Etym: [NL., fr. Gr. a"ima`twsis.] (Physiol.)
(a) Sanguification; the conversion of chyle into blood.
(b) The arterialization of the blood in the lungs; the formation of
blood in general; hæmatogenesis.
