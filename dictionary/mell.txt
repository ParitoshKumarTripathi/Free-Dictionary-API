MELL
Mell, v. i. & t. Etym: [F. mêler, OF. meller, mester. See Meddle.]

Defn: To mix; to meddle. [Obs.] Chaucer.
MELL

=====
Mell, n. Etym: [See Mellifluous.]

Defn: Honey. [Obs.] Warner.
MELL

Mell, n.

Defn: A mill. [Obs.] Chaucer.
