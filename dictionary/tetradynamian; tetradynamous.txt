TETRADYNAMIAN; TETRADYNAMOUS
Tet`ra*dy*na"mi*an, Tet`ra*dyn"a*mous, a. (Bot.)

Defn: Belonging to the order Tetradynamia; having six stamens, four
of which are uniformly longer than the others.
