RUBEFACIENT
Ru`be*fa"cient, a. Etym: [L. rubefaciens, p.pr. of rubefacere to make
red; rubere to be red + facere to make.]

Defn: Making red.
 -- n. (Med.)

Defn: An external application which produces redness of the skin.
