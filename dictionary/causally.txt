CAUSALLY
Caus"al*ly, adv.

Defn: According to the order or series of causes; by tracing effects
to causes.
CAUSALLY

=====
Caus"al*ly, n. (Mining.)

Defn: The lighter, earthy parts of ore, carried off washing.
