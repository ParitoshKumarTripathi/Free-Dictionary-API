TROPOLOGIC; TROPOLOGICAL
Trop`o*log"ic, Trop`o*log"ic*al, a. Etym: [Gr. tropologique. See
Tropology.]

Defn: Characterized by tropes; varied by tropes; tropical. Burton.
 -- Trop`o*log"ic*al*ly, adv.
