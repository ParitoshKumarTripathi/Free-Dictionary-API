TULIP-SHELL
Tu"lip-shell`, n. (Zoöl.)

Defn: A large, handsomely colored, marine univalve shell (Fasciolaria
tulipa) native of the Southern United States. The name is sometimes
applied also to other species of Fasciolaria.
