TRANSPASS
Trans*pass", v. t. Etym: [Pref. trans- + pass: cf. LL. transpassare.
Cf. Trespass.]

Defn: To pass over; as, Alexander transpassed the river. [Obs.] J.
Gregory.
TRANSPASS

=====
Trans*pass", v. i.

Defn: To pass by; to pass away. [Obs.]
