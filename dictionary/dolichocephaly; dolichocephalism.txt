DOLICHOCEPHALY; DOLICHOCEPHALISM
Dol`i*cho*ceph"a*ly, Dol`i*cho*ceph"a*lism, n. Etym: [Cf. F.
dolichcéphalie.]

Defn: The quality or condition of being dolichocephalic.
