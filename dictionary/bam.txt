BAM
Bam, n. Etym: [Prob. a contr. of bamboozle.]

Defn: An imposition; a cheat; a hoax. Garrick.
To relieve the tediumbams. Prof. Wilson.
BAM

=====
Bam, v. t.

Defn: To cheat; to wheedle. [Slang] Foote.
