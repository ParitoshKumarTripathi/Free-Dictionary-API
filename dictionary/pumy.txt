PUMY
Pu"my, a. Etym: [Cf. Prov. E. pummer big, large, and E. pomey
pommel.]

Defn: Large and rounded. [Obs.]
A gentle stream, whose murmuring wave did play Amongst the pumy
stones. Spenser.
