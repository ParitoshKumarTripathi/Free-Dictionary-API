QUADRILLION
Quad*ril"lion, n. Etym: [F., fr. L. quater four times, akin to
quattuor four, E. four; -- formed like million. See Four, Million.]

Defn: According to the French notation, which is followed also upon
the Continent and in the United States, a unit with fifteen ciphers
annexed; according to the English notation, the number produced by
involving a million to the fourth power, or the number represented by
a unit with twenty-four ciphers annexed. See the Note under
Numeration.
