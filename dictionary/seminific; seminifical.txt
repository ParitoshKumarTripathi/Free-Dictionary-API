SEMINIFIC; SEMINIFICAL
Sem`i*nif"ic, Sem`i*nif"ic*al, a. Etym: [L. semen, seminis, seed +
facere to make.] (Biol.)

Defn: Forming or producing seed, or the male generative product of
animals or of plants.
