REASCEND
Re`as*cend", v. i.

Defn: To rise, mount, or climb again.
REASCEND

=====
Re`as*cend", v. t.

Defn: To ascend or mount again; to reach by ascending again.
He mounts aloft, and reascends the skies. Addison.
