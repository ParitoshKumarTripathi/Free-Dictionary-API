TUESDAY
Tues"day, n. Etym: [OE. Tewesday, AS. Tiwes dæg the day of Tiw the
god of war; akin to OHG. Zio, Icel. T, L. Jupiter, Gr. Ziostac
Tuesday, G. Dienstag, Icel. T. See Deity, Day, and cf. Jovial.]

Defn: The third day of the week, following Monday and preceding
Wednesday.
