TABLESPOONFUL
Ta"ble*spoon`ful, n.; pl. Tablespoonfuls (.

Defn: As much as a tablespoon will hold; enough to fill a tablespoon.
It is usually reckoned as one half of a fluid ounce, or four fluid
drams.
