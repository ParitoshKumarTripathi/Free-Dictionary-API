PAGANISM
Pa"gan*ism, n. Etym: [L. paganismus: cf. F. paganisme. See Pagan, and
cf. Painim.]

Defn: The state of being pagan; pagan characteristics; esp., the
worship of idols or false gods, or the system of religious opinions
and worship maintained by pagans; heathenism.
