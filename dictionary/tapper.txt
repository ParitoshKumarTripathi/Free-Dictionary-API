TAPPER
Tap"per, n. (Zoöl.)

Defn: The lesser spotted woodpecker (Dendrocopus minor); -- called
also tapperer, tabberer, little wood pie, barred woodpecker, wood
tapper, hickwall, and pump borer. [Prov. Eng.]
