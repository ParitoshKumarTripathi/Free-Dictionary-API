TRIPINNATIFID
Tri`pin*nat"i*fid, a. Etym: [Pref. tri- + pinnatifid.] (Bot.)

Defn: Thrice pinnately cleft; -- said of a pinnatifid leaf when its
segments are pinnatifid, and the subdivisions of these also are
pinnatifid.
