DETECTIVE
De*tect"ive, a.

Defn: Fitted for, or skilled in, detecting; employed in detecting
crime or criminals; as, a detective officer.
DETECTIVE

=====
De*tect"ive, n.

Defn: One who business it is so detect criminals or discover matters
of secrecy.
