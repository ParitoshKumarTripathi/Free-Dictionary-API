PROTONEMA
Pro`to*ne"ma, n.; pl. Protonemata. Etym: [NL., fr. Gr. (Bot.)

Defn: The primary growth from the spore of a moss, usually consisting
of branching confervoid filaments, on any part of which stem and leaf
buds may be developed.
