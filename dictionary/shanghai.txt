SHANGHAI
Shang`hai", v. t. [imp. & p. p. Shanghaied; p. pr. & vb. n.
Shanghaiing.]

Defn: To intoxicate and ship (a person) as a sailor while in this
condition. [Written also shanghae.] [Slang, U.S.]
SHANGHAI

=====
Shang`hai", n. (Zoöl.)

Defn: A large and tall breed of domestic fowl.
