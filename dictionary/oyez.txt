OYEZ
O"yez` (oyês; 277), interj. Etym: [Anglo-F. oyez hear ye. See Oyer.]

Defn: Hear; attend; -- a term used by criers of courts to secure
silence before making a proclamation. It is repeated three times.
[Written also oyes.]
