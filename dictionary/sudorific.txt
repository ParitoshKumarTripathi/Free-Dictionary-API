SUDORIFIC
Su`dor*if"ic, a. Etym: [L. sudor sweat (akin to E. sweat) + facere to
make.]

Defn: Causing sweat; as, sudorific herbs.
 -- n.

Defn: A sudorific medicine. Cf. Diaphoretic.
