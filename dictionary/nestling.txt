NESTLING
Nes"tling. n.

1. A young bird which has not abandoned the nest. Piers Plowman.

2. A nest; a receptacle. [Obs.] Bacon.
NESTLING

=====
Nes"tling, a.

Defn: Newly hatched; being yet in the nest.
