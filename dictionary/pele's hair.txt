PELE'S HAIR
Pe"le's hair. [After a Hawaiian goddess associated with the crater
Kilauea.]

Defn: Glass threads or fibers formed by the wind from bits blown from
frothy lava or from the tips of lava jets or from bits of liquid lava
thrown into the air. It often collects in thick masses resembling
tow.
