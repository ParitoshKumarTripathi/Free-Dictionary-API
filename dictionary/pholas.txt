PHOLAS
Pho"las, n.; pl. Pholades. Etym: [NL., fr. Gr. (Zoöl.)

Defn: Any one of numerous species of marine bivalve mollusks of the
genus Pholas, or family Pholadidæ. They bore holes for themselves in
clay, peat, and soft rocks.
