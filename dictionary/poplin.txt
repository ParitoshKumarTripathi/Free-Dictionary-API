POPLIN
Pop"lin, n. Etym: [F. popeline, papeline.]

Defn: A fabric of many varieties, usually made of silk and worsted, -
- used especially for women's dresses. Irish poplin, a fabric with
silk warp and worsted weft, made in Ireland.
