SUPERHEAT
Su`per*heat", v. t. [imp. & p. p. Superheated; p. pr. & vb. n.
Superheating.]

1. To heat too much, to overheat; as, to superheat an oven.

2. (Steam Engine)

Defn: To heat, as steam, apart from contact with water, until it
resembles a perfect gas.
SUPERHEAT

=====
Su"per*heat`, n.

Defn: The increase of temperature communicated to steam by
superheating it.
