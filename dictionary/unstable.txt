UNSTABLE
Un*sta"ble, a. Etym: [Cf. Instable.]

Defn: Not stable; not firm, fixed, or constant; subject to change or
overthrow.
 -- Un*sta"ble*ness, n. Chaucer. Unstable equilibrium. See Stable
equilibrium, under Stable.
