RET
Ret, v. t.

Defn: See Aret. [Obs.] Chaucer.
RET

=====
Ret, v. t. Etym: [Akin to rot.]

Defn: To prepare for use, as flax, by separating the fibers from the
woody part by process of soaking, macerating, and other treatment.
Ure.
