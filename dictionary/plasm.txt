PLASM
Plasm, n. Etym: [L. plasma anything formed or molded, that which is
molded, Gr. plasme. Cf. Plasma.]

1. A mold or matrix in which anything is cast or formed to a
particular shape. [R.] Woodward.

2. (Biol.)

Defn: Same as Plasma.
