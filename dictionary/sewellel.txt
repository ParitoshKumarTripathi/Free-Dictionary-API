SEWELLEL
Se*wel"lel, n. Etym: [Of American Indian origin.] (Zoöl.)

Defn: A peculiar gregarious burrowing rodent (Haplodon rufus), native
of the coast region of the Northwestern United States. It somewhat
resembles a muskrat or marmot, but has only a rudimentary tail. Its
head is broad, its eyes are small and its fur is brownish above, gray
beneath. It constitutes the family Haplodontidæ. Called also boomer,
showt'l, and mountain beaver.
